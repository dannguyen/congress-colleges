BOREN, Daniel David, (1973 - )

Image, Congressional Pictorial Directory, 109th.

BOREN, Daniel David, (grandson of Lyle Boren and son of David Boren), (grandson of Lyle Boren and son of David Boren), a Representative from Oklahoma; born in Shawnee, Pottawatomie County, Okla., August 2, 1973; United States Capitol Page School, 1989; B.S., Texas Christian University, Fort Worth, Tex., 1997; M.B.A., University of Oklahoma, 2001; staff, United States Representative Wesley Watkins of Oklahoma; education administrator; bank teller; aide, Oklahoma Corporation Commission; member of the Oklahoma state house of representatives, 2002-2004; elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Ninth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2005-January 3, 2013); was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

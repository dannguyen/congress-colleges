BUERKLE, Ann Marie, (1951 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

BUERKLE, Ann Marie, a Representative from New York; born in Auburn, Cayuga County, N.Y., May 8, 1951; R.N., St. Joseph’s Hospital School of Nursing, Syracuse, N.Y., 1972; B.S., Le Moyne College, Syracuse, N.Y., 1977; J.D., Syracuse University, Syracuse, N.Y., 1994; nurse; member of the Syracuse common council, 1994-1995; assistant New York state attorney general, 1997-2010; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2013); unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

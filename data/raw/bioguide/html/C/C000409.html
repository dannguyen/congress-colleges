CLANCY, Donald Daniel, (1921 - 2007)

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

CLANCY, Donald Daniel, a Representative from Ohio; born in Cincinnati, Hamilton County, Ohio, July 24, 1921; graduated from Elder High School; attended Xavier University; graduated from Cincinnati Law School in 1948; admitted to the bar in 1948; member of city council, Cincinnati, 1952-1960; mayor of Cincinnati, Ohio,1958-1960; chairman of Cincinnati Planning Commission, 1958-1960; elected as a Republican to the Eighty-seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1961-January 3, 1977); unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1976 to the Ninety-fifth Congress; died on June 12, 2007, in Cincinnati, Ohio.

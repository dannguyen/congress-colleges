DOOLEY, Calvin M., (1954 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

DOOLEY, Calvin M., a Representative from California; born in Visalia, Tulare County, Calif., January 11, 1954; B.S., University of California, Davis, Calif., 1977; M.A., Stanford University, Stanford, Calif., 1987; farmer; administrative assistant to California state Senator Rose Ann Vuich, 1987-1989; elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Second and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1991-January 3, 2005); not a candidate for reelection in 2004.

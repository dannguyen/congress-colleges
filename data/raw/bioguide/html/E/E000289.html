ELLSWORTH, Brad, (1958 - )

Image courtesy of the Honorable Brad Ellsworth

ELLSWORTH, Brad, a Representative from Indiana; born in Jasper, Dubois County, Ind., September 11, 1958; B.S., Indiana State University, Evansville, Ind., 1981; M.S., Indiana State University, Evansville, Ind., 1993; police officer; sheriff, Vanderburgh County, Ind., 1999-2007; elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2011); not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress, but was an unsuccessful candidate for the United States Senate in 2010.

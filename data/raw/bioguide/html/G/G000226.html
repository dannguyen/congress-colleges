GINN, Ronald Bryan (Bo), (1934 - 2005)

GINN, Ronald Bryan (Bo), a Representative from Georgia; born in Morgan, Calhoun County, Ga., May 31, 1934; educated in the public schools of Morgan; Abraham Baldwin Agricultural College, Tifton, Ga., 1951-1953; Georgia Southern College, Statesboro, Ga., 1953-1956; teacher; businessman; cattle farmer; former administrative assistant to Senator Herman E. Talmadge and Representative G. Elliott Hagan; elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-third and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1973-January 3, 1983); was not a candidate in 1982 for reelection, but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination for governor of Georgia; chairman of the board of a governmental relations firm, Alexandria, Va.; died on January 6, 2005, in Augusta, Ga.

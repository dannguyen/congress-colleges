GRIFFIN, Tim, (1968 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

GRIFFIN, Tim, a Representative from Arkansas; born in Charlotte, Mecklenberg County, North Carolina, on August 21, 1968; B.A., Hendrix College, Conway, Ark., 1990; attended Pembroke College, Oxford University, 1991; J.D., Tulane University School of Law, New Orleans, La., 1994; United States Army Reserve, 1996-present; business owner; lawyer, private practice; United States attorney for the Eastern District of Arkansas, 2006-2007; special assistant, White House, 2005; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2015); was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014; lieutenant governor of Arkansas, 2015-present.

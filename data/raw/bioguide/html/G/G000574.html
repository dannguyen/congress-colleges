GALLEGO, Ruben, (1979 - )

Image courtesy of the Member

GALLEGO, Ruben, a Representative from Arizona; born in Chicago, Cook County, Ill., November 20, 1979; A.B., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 2004; United States Marine Corps, 2002-2006; public affairs consultant, 2007-2008; delegate, Democratic National Convention, 2008; vice-chair, Ariz. Democratic party, 2009; member of the Arizona state house of representatives, 2010-2014; assistant minority leader, Arizona state house of representatives, 2012-2014; elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

HAYES, Philip Harold, (1940 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

HAYES, Philip Harold, a Representative from Indiana; born in Battle Creek, Calhoun County, Mich., September 1, 1940; attended Rensselaer (Ind.) Elementary School; graduated from Rensselaer High School, 1958; B.A., Indiana University, 1963; J.D., Indiana University Law School, 1967; admitted to the Indiana bar in 1967 and District of Columbia bar in 1977; lawyer, private practice; deputy prosecuting attorney, Vanderburgh County, Ind., 1967-1968; member, Indiana State senate, 1971-1974; elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-fourth Congress (January 3, 1975-January 3, 1977); was not a candidate for reelection in 1976, but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate; county attorney, Vanderburgh County, Ind., 2001-2002; is a resident of Evansville, Ind.

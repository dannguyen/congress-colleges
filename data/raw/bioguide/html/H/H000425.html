HEALY, Joseph, (1776 - 1861)

HEALY, Joseph, a Representative from New Hampshire; born in Newton, Middlesex County, Mass., August 21, 1776; completed preparatory studies; was a hotel keeper and also engaged in agricultural pursuits; member of the State senate in 1824; elected as an Adams candidate to the Nineteenth and Twentieth Congresses (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1829); member of the State executive council 1829-1832; resumed agricultural pursuits and the hotel business; died in Washington, Sullivan County, N.H., October 10, 1861; interment in the Old Cemetery.

HOLLENBECK, Harold Capistran, (1938 - )

HOLLENBECK, Harold Capistran, a Representative from New Jersey; born in Passaic, Passaic County, N.J., December 29, 1938; attended the public schools of East Rutherford, New Jersey; B.A., Fairleigh Dickinson University, Rutherford, 1961; LL.B., University of Virginia, 1964; admitted to the New Jersey bar in 1965 and commenced practice in Ridgewood; member, East Rutherford Borough Council, 1967-1969, New Jersey assembly, 1968-1971; New Jersey senate, 1972-1973; delegate, Republican National Convention, 1968; elected as a Republican to the Ninety-fifth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1977-January 3, 1983); unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1982 to the Ninety-eighth Congress; appointed by Governor Thomas Kean judge, superior court of New Jersey and sworn in July 1, 1987; is a resident of Ridgewood, N.J.

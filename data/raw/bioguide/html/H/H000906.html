HUDNUT, William Herbert, III, (1932 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

HUDNUT, William Herbert, III, a Representative from Indiana; born in Cincinnati, Hamilton County, Ohio, October 17, 1932; graduated from the Darrow School, New Lebanon, N.Y., 1946-1950; A.B., Princeton University, Princeton, N.J., 1954; B.D., Union Theological Seminary, New York, N.Y., 1957; clergyman, ordained in Rochester, N.Y., 1957; member, Indianapolis, Ind., board of public safety, 1970-1972; elected as a Republican to the Ninety-third Congress (January 3, 1973-January 3, 1975); unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Ninety-fourth Congress in 1974; mayor, Indianapolis, Ind., 1976-1991; mayor, Chevy Chase, Md., 2004-2006.

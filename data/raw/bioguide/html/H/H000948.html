HULSHOF, Kenny, (1958 - )

Image, Congressional Pictorial Directory, 109th.

HULSHOF, Kenny, a Representative from Missouri; born in Sikeston, Scott County, Mo., May 22, 1958; graduated from Thomas W. Kelly High School, Benton, Mo.; B.S., University of Missouri, 1980; J.D., University of Mississippi Law School, 1983; assistant public defender, 1983-1986; assistant prosecuting attorney, Cape Girardeau, Mo., 1986-1989; assistant attorney general, Office of the Missouri state attorney general, 1989-1996; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2009); not a candidate for reelection, but was an unsuccessful candidate for Governor of Missouri in 2008.

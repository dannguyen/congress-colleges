HUNTER, Narsworthy, (Birth date unknown - 1802)

HUNTER, Narsworthy, a Delegate from Mississippi Territory; born in Virginia, birth date unknown; captain in the militia organization of the district formed, 1793; commissioned inspector of the military posts on the east side of the Mississippi River; elected to the Seventh Congress (March 4, 1801-March 11, 1802); died on March 11, 1802, in Washington, D.C.; interment in the Congressional Cemetery.

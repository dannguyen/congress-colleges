HUNTSMAN, Adam, (1786 - 1849)

Image courtesy of the East Tennessee Historical Society, Knoxville, Tennessee

HUNTSMAN, Adam, a Representative from Tennessee; born in Charlotte County, Va.,, February 11, 1786; moved to Jackson, Tenn.; elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1837); unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1836 to the Twenty-fifth Congress; died August 23, 1849; interment in Old Salem Cemetery, Madison County, Tenn.

HAYWORTH, Nan, (1959 - )

Image courtesy of the Honorable Nan Hayworth

HAYWORTH, Nan, a Representative from New York; born in Chicago, Cook County, Ill., December 14, 1959; graduated from Munster High School, Munster, Ind., 1977; A.B., Princeton University, Princeton, N.J., 1981; M.D., Cornell University Medical College, Ithaca, N.Y., 1985; physician, 1989-2005; businesswoman; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2013); unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012; unsuccessful candidate for election to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

JAMES, Craig T., (1941 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

JAMES, Craig T., a Representative from Florida; born in Augusta, Richmond County, Ga., May 5, 1941; graduated, Deland High School, Fla., 1959; B.S., Stetson University, 1963; served in the National Guard and United States Army Reserve, 1963-1969; J.D., Stetson University law School, 1967; admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Deland; commissioner, Deland Housing Authority, 1971-1975; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred First and One Hundred Second Congresses (January 3, 1989-January 3, 1993); was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Third Congress; is a resident of Deland, Fla.

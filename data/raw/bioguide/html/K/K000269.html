KLINGENSMITH, John, Jr., (1786 - 1854)

KLINGENSMITH, John, Jr., a Representative from Pennsylvania; born in Westmoreland County, Pa., on March 26, 1786; sheriff, Westmoreland County, Pa., 1819-1822, 1828-1831; elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress and reelected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1839); member of the Pennsylvania state senate, 1831-1835; secretary of the land office of Pennsylvania, 1839-1842; died on February 8, 1854, in Westmoreland County, Pa.; interment in West Newton Cemetery, Westmoreland County, Pa.

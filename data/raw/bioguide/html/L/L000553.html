LaTOURETTE, Steven C., (1954 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

LaTOURETTE, Steven C., a Representative from Ohio; born in Cleveland, Cuyahoga County, Ohio, July 22, 1954; graduated from Cleveland Heights High School, Cleveland, Ohio, 1972; B.A., University of Michigan, 1976; J.D., Cleveland State University, Cleveland, Ohio, 1979; lawyer, private practice; public defender, Lake County, Ohio, 1980-1983; prosecuting attorney, Lake County, Ohio, 1989-1995; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-January 3, 2013); was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

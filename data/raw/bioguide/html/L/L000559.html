LANGEVIN, James, (1964 - )

Image courtesy of the Member

LANGEVIN, James, a Representative from Rhode Island; born in Providence, Providence County, R.I., April 22, 1964; B.A., Rhode Island College, Providence, R.I., 1990; M.A., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1994; member of the Rhode Island state house of representatives, 1988-1994; Rhode Island secretary of state, 1994-2001; elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present).

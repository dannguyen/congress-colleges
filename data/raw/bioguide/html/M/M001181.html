MEEHAN, Patrick, (1955 - )

Image courtesy of the Member

MEEHAN, Patrick, a Representative from Pennsylvania; born in Cheltenham, Montgomery County, Pa., October 20, 1955; graduated from Chestnut Hill Academy, Philadelphia, Pa., 1974; B.A., Bowdoin College, Brunswick, Maine, 1978; J.D., Temple University, Philadelphia, Pa., 1986; lawyer, private practice; United States Attorney for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania, 2001-2008; staff, United States Senator Arlen Specter, 1991-1994; Delaware County, Pa., district attorney, 1996-2001; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

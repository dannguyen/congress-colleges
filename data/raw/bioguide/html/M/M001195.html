MOONEY, Alex X., (1971 - )

Image courtesy of the Member

MOONEY, Alex X., a Representative from West Virginia; born in Washington, D.C., June 5, 1971; graduated from Frederick High School, Frederick, Md.; A.B., Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H., 1993; unsuccessful candidate for the New Hampshire state house of representatives, 1992; staff, United States Representative Roscoe Bartlett of Maryland, 1993-1995; member of the Maryland state house of delegates, 1999-2010; chairman, Maryland Republican Party, 2010-2013; member, Republican National Committee, 2011-2013; staff, House Republican Conference, 1995; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

NORCROSS, Donald, (1958 - )

Image courtesy of the Member

NORCROSS, Donald, a Representative from New Jersey; born in Camden, Camden County, N.J., December 13, 1958; graduated from Pennsauken High School, Pennsauken, N.J., 1977; A.S., Camden County College, Camden, N.J., 1979; electrician; union representative; member of the New Jersey state assembly, 2010; member of the New Jersey state senate, 2010-2014; elected simultaneously as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and One Hundred Fourteenth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Robert Andrews (November 4, 2014-present).

PRYOR, Mark, (1963 - )
Senate Years of Service: 2003-2015
Party: Democrat


Courtesy U.S. Senate Historical Office

PRYOR, Mark, (son of David H. Pryor), a Senator from Arkansas; born in Fayetteville, Arkansas, on January 10, 1963; B.A., University of Arkansas 1985; J.D., University of Arkansas 1988; Arkansas State house of representatives 1991-1994; Arkansas State attorney general 1999-2002; elected as a Democrat to the U.S. Senate in 2002; reelected in 2008, and served from January 3, 2003, to January 3, 2015; was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 2014.

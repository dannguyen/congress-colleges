ROTH, Toby A., (1938 - )

Image, Congressional Pictorial Directory, 104th.

ROTH, Toby A., a Representative from Wisconsin; born in Strasburg, Emmons County, N.Dak., October 10, 1938; graduated from St. Mary’s High School, Menasha, Wis., 1957; B.A., Marquette University, Milwaukee, Wis., 1961; served in the United States Army Reserve, 1962-1969 with rank of lieutenant; realtor; professional advocate; served in the Wisconsin Legislature, 1972-1978; elected as a Republican to the Ninety-sixth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1997); was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress; is a resident of Great Falls, Va.

ROTHMAN, Steven, (1952 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

ROTHMAN, Steven, a Representative from New Jersey; born in Englewood, Bergen County, N.J., October 14, 1952; graduated from Tenafly High School, Tenafly, N.J., 1970; B.A., Syracuse University, Syracuse, N.Y., 1974; J.D., Washington University School of Law, St. Louis, Mo., 1977; mayor, Englewood, N.J., 1983-1989; Bergen County, N.J., surrogate court judge, 1993-1996; elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2013); unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

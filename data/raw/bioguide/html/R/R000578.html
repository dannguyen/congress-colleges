REICHERT, David G., (1950 - )

Image, Congressional Pictorial Directory, 109th.

REICHERT, David G., a Representative from Washington; born in Detroit Lakes, Becker County, Minn., August 29, 1950; graduated, Kent Meridian High School, Kent, Wash., 1968; A.A., Concordia Lutheran College, Portland, Oreg., 1970; United States Air Force reserve, 1971-1976; United States Air Force, 1976; police officer, King County, Wash., 1972-1997; sheriff, King County, Wash., 1997- 2004; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Ninth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2005-present).

SCARBOROUGH, Charles Joseph, (1963 - )

Image, Congressional Pictorial Directory, 107th.

SCARBOROUGH, Charles Joseph, a Representative from Florida; born in Atlanta, Fulton County, Ga., April 9, 1963; graduated from Catholic High School, Pensacola, Fla.; B.A., University of Alabama, 1985; J.D., University of Florida School of Law, Gainesville, Fla., 1990; admitted to the Florida State bar, 1991; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-September 5, 2001); resigned on September 5, 2001.

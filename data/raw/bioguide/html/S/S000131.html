SCHMIDHAUSER, John Richard, (1922 - )

SCHMIDHAUSER, John Richard, a Representative from Iowa; born in the Bronx, Queens County, N.Y., on January 3, 1922; B.A., University of Delaware, Newark, Del., 1949; M.A., University of Virginia, Charlottesville, Va., 1952; Ph.D. University of Virginia, Charlottesville, Va., 1954; United States Navy, 1941-1945; professor, University of Iowa, Iowa City, Iowa, 1954-1965 and 1967-1973; elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-ninth Congress (January 3, 1965-January 3, 1967); unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Ninetieth Congress in 1966; unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the Ninety-third Congress in 1972; professor, University of Southern California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1973-1992, University of Virginia, Charlottesville, Va., 1982-1983, Simon Fraser University, Burnaby, British Columbia, 1984, professor emeritus, University of Southern California, Los Angeles, Ca. 1992 to present.

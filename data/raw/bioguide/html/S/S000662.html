SNOWBARGER, Vincent K., (1949 - )

Image, Congressional Pictorial Directory, 105th.

SNOWBARGER, Vincent K., a Representative from Kansas; born in Kankakee, Ill., September 16, 1949; graduated from Shawnee Mission South High School, Overland Park, Kans., 1967; B.A., Southern Nazarene University, Bethany, Okla., 1971; M.A., University of Illinois, Champagne-Urbana, Ill., 1974; J.D., University of Kansas, Lawrence, Kans., 1977; instructor, MidAmerica Nazarene College, 1973-1977; Republican precinct committeeman, 1978-1984; member of the Olathe City, Ill., planning commission, 1982-1984; member of the Kansas state house of representatives, 1985-1996; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth Congress (January 3, 1997-January 3, 1999); unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Sixth Congress in 1998.

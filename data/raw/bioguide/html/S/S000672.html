SNYDER, Victor F., (1947 - )

Image, Congressional Pictorial Directory, 109th.

SNYDER, Victor F., a Representative from Arkansas; born in Medford, Jackson County, Oreg., September 27, 1947; B.A., Willamette University, Salem, Oreg., 1975; M.D., University of Oregon, Portland, Oreg., 1979; J.D., University of Arkansas, Little Rock, Ark., 1988; United States Marine Corps, 1967-1969; physician; member of the Arkansas state senate, 1991-1996; elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2011); was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

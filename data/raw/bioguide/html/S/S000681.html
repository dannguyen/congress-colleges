SOULE, Nathan, (ca. 1790 - 1860)

SOULE, Nathan, a Representative from New York; born in Duchess County, N.Y., ca. 1790; resided at Fort Plain; completed preparatory studies; elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-second Congress (March 4, 1831-March 3, 1833); member of the New York state assembly, 1837; died in Clay, Onondaga County, N.Y. on January 9, 1860; interment in Pine Plains Cemetery, Clay, Onondaga County, N.Y.

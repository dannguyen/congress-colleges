STOCKMAN, Steve, (1956 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

STOCKMAN, Steve, a Representative from Texas; born in Bloomfield Hills, Mich., November 14, 1956; graduated from Dondero High School, Royal Oak, Mich., 1975; B.S., University of Houston, Houston, Tex., 1990; accountant; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth Congress (January 3, 1995-January 3, 1997); unsuccessful candidate for reelection the One Hundred Fifth Congress in 1996; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress (January 3, 2013-January 3, 2015); was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014, but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate.

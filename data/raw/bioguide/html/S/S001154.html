SHUSTER, William (Bill), (1961 - )

Image courtesy of the Member

SHUSTER, William (Bill), (son of E. G. "Bud" Shuster), a Representative from Pennsylvania; born in McKeesport, Allegheny County, Pa., January 10, 1961; graduated from Everett High School, Everett. Pa.; B.A., Dickinson College, Carlisle, Pa., 1983; M.B.A., American University, Washington, D.C.; business owner; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative E. G. “Bud” Shuster of Pennsylvania and reelected to the seven succeeding Congresses (May 15, 2001-present); chair, Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure (One Hundred Thirteenth and One Hundred Fourteenth Congresses).

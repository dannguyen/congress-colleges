SÁNCHEZ, Linda T., (1969 - )

Image courtesy of the Member

SÁNCHEZ, Linda T., (sister of Loretta Sanchez), a Representative from California; born in Orange, Orange County, Calif., January 28, 1969; B.A., University of California, Berkeley, Calif., 1991; J.D., University of California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1995; lawyer, private practice; elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

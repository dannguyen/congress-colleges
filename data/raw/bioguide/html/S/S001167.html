SALI, William, (1954 - )

SALI, William, a Representative from Idaho; born in Portsmouth, Scioto County, Ohio, February 17, 1954; graduated from Capital High School, Boise, Idaho, 1972; B.B.A., Boise State University, Boise, Idaho, 1981; J.D., University of Idaho, Moscow, Idaho,1984; lawyer, private practice; member of the Idaho state house of representatives, 1990-2006; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Tenth Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2009); unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

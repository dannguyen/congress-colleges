TAUSCHER, Ellen O'Kane, (1951 - )

Collection of the U.S. House of Representatives

TAUSCHER, Ellen O'Kane, a Representative from California; born in Newark, Essex County, N.J., November 15, 1951; B.S., Seton Hall University, South Orange, N.J., 1974; investment banker, New York Stock Exchange, 1977-1979; founder, Child Care Registry, Inc., 1992; elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the six succeeding Congresses until her resignation on June 26, 2009 (January 3, 1997-June 26, 2009); Under Secretary for Arms Control and International Security, U. S. Department of State, 2009-2012; Special Envoy for Strategic Stability and Missile Defense, U. S. Department of State, 2012-present.

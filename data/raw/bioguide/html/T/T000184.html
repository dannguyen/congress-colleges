THOMAS, Robert Lindsay, (1943 - )

THOMAS, Robert Lindsay, a Representative from Georgia; born in Patterson, Pierce County, Ga., November 20, 1943; graduated from Patterson High School, Patterson, Ga., 1961; B.A., University of Georgia, Athens, Ga., 1965; Georgia National Guard, 1966-1972; investment banker; farmer; elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-eighth and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-January 3, 1993); was not a candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992; is a resident of Atlanta, and Savannah, Ga.

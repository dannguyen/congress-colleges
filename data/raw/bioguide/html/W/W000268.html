WELDON, Wayne Curtis (Curt), (1947 - )

Image, Congressional Pictorial Directory, 109th.

WELDON, Wayne Curtis (Curt), a Representative from Pennsylvania; born in Marcus Hook, Delaware County, Pa., July 22, 1947; B.A., West Chester State College, West Chester, Pa., 1969; teacher; volunteer fire chief; mayor of Marcus Hook, Pa., 1977-1982; member of the Delaware County, Pa., county council, 1981-1986; unsuccessful candidate for election to the Ninety-Ninth Congress in 1984; elected as a Republican to the One Hundredth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1987-January 3, 2007); unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Tenth Congress in 2006.

ABBOTT, Nehemiah, a Representative from Maine
born in Sidney, Maine, March 29, 1804
studied law at the Litchfield (Conn.) Law School
was admitted to the bar in 1836 and commenced practice at Calais, Maine
moved to Columbus, Miss., in 1839 and continued the practice of law
returned to Maine in 1840 and settled in Belfast, Waldo County, where he resumed the practice of law
member of the State house of representatives in 1842, 1843, and 1845
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1859)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1858
engaged in the practice of his profession until his death
mayor of Belfast in 1865 and 1866
died in Belfast, Maine, July 26, 1877
interment in Grove Cemetery.

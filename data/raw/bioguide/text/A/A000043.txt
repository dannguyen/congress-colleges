ADAMS, Robert Huntington, a Senator from Mississippi
born in Rockbridge County, Va., in 1792
apprenticed to the cooperÂ’s trade
graduated from Washington College (now Washington and Lee University) at Lexington, Va., in 1806
studied law
admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Knoxville, Tenn.
moved to Natchez, Miss., in 1819
member of the State house of representatives in 1828
elected as a Jacksonian to the United States Senate to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Thomas B. Reed and served from January 6, 1830, until his death in Natchez, Miss., July 2, 1830
interment in Natchez City Cemetery.

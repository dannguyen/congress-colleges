ADDABBO, Joseph Patrick, a Representative from New York
born in Ozone Park, Queens County, N.Y., March 17, 1925
attended Public School 59, Boys High School, Brooklyn, and City College of New York
graduated from St. Johns Law School in 1946 and commenced the practice of law in Ozone Park, N.Y., in 1947
president of Ozone Park Mens Association, 1948-1959 and Ferrini Welfare League of Catholic Charities, 1956-1958
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-seventh and to the twelve succeeding Congresses and served from January 3, 1961, until his death in Washington, D.C., April 10, 1986
interment in St. Johns Cemetery, Queens, N.Y.

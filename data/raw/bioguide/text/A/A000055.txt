ADERHOLT, Robert, a Representative from Alabama
born in Haleyville, Winston County, Ala., July 22, 1965
B.A., Birmingham Southern College, Birmingham, Ala., 1987
J.D., Samford University, Birmingham, Ala., 1990
lawyer, private practice
Haleyville, Ala., municipal judge, 1992-1995
staff, Alabama Governor Fob James, 1995-1996
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-present).

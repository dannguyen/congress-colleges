ALLEN, John, (father of John William Allen), a Representative from Connecticut
born in Great Barrington, Mass., June 12, 1763
attended the common schools
studied law at the Litchfield Law School
was admitted to the bar in 1786 and commenced practice in Litchfield, Conn.
member of the State house of representatives 1793-1796, serving as clerk in 1796
elected as a Federalist to the Fifth Congress (March 4, 1797-March 3, 1799)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1798
member of the State council and of the supreme court of errors 1800-1806
continued the practice of law in Litchfield, Conn., until his death on July 31, 1812
interment in East Cemetery.

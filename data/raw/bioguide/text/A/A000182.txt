ANDERSON, Carl Carey, a Representative from Ohio
born in Bluffton, Allen County, Ohio, December 2, 1877
moved to Sandusky County in 1881 with his parents, who settled in Fremont
attended the common schools
became employed as a traveling salesman
moved to Fostoria, Seneca County, and engaged in the manufacture of underwear
elected mayor of Fostoria, Ohio, in 1905 and again in 1907, on each occasion for a term of two years
president of the city hospital board and director in a number of manufacturing enterprises
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-first and Sixty-second Congresses and served from March 4, 1909, until his death in an automobile accident near Fostoria, Ohio, October 1, 1912
interment in Oakwood Cemetery, Fremont, Ohio.

ANDERSON, George Alburtus, a Representative from Illinois
born in Botetourt County, Va., March 11, 1853
moved to Illinois in 1855 with his parents, who settled in Hancock County
attended the common schools
was graduated from Carthage (Ill.) College in 1876
studied law in Lincoln, Nebr., and Sedalia, Mo.
was admitted to the bar in 1878 and commenced practice in Quincy, Ill., in 1880
unsuccessful candidate for city attorney of Quincy in 1883
elected city attorney in 1884 and again in 1885
elected as a Democrat to the Fiftieth Congress (March 4, 1887-March 3, 1889)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1888
engaged in the practice of law until his death in Quincy, Ill., January 31, 1896
interment in Woodlawn Cemetery.

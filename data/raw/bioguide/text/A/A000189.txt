ANDERSON, Glenn Malcolm, a Representative from California
born in Hawthorne, Los Angeles County, Calif., February 21, 1913
B.A., University of California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1936
United States Army, 1943-1945
real estate developer
Mayor of Hawthorne, Calif., 1940-1942
member of the California state assembly, 1942-1943, 1945-1948
lieutenant governor of California, 1959-1967
chairman and member, Calif. state lands commission, 1959-1967
member, board of trustees of California state colleges, 1961-1967
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-first and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1969-January 3, 1993)
chair, Committee on Public Works and Transportation (One Hundredth through One Hundred First Congresses)
was not a candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992
died on December 13, 1994, in Los Angeles, Calif.
interment in Green Hills Cemetery, Rancho Palos Verdes, Calif.

ANDREWS, Elizabeth Bullock, (wife of George William Andrews), a Representative from Alabama
born Leslie Elizabeth Bullock in Geneva, Ala., February 12, 1911
attended Geneva public schools
B.S., Montevallo College, Montevallo, Ala., 1932
teacher
elected as a Democrat by special election to the Ninety-second Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of her husband, United States Representative George W. Andrews (April 4, 1972-January 3, 1973)
was not a candidate for reelection to the Ninety-third Congress in 1972
died on December 2, 2002, in Birmingham, Ala.
interment in Oak Hill Cemetery, Union Springs, Ala.

ANTHONY, Beryl Franklin, Jr., a Representative from Arkansas
born in El Dorado, Union County, Ark., February 21, 1938
attended the Union County public schools
graduated, El Dorado High School, 1956
B.S., B.A., University of Arkansas, Fayetteville, 1961
J.D., same university, 1963
admitted to the Arkansas bar in 1963 and commenced practice in El Dorado
assistant attorney general, 1964-1965
deputy prosecuting attorney, Union County, Ark., 1966-1970
prosecuting attorney, 13th Judicial District, 1971-1976
legal counsel, Anthony Forest Products Co., 1977
private practice of law, 1977
delegate to Arkansas State Democratic conventions, 1964-1978
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-sixth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1993)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
is a resident of El Dorado, Ark.

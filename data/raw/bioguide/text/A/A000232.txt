ANDERSON, Simeon H., (father of William Clayton Anderson), a Representative from Kentucky
born near Lancaster, Garrard County, Ky., March 2, 1802
pursued preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1823 and commenced practice in Lancaster, Ky.
member of the State house of representatives 1828, 1829, 1832, and 1836-1838
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-sixth Congress and served from March 4, 1839, until his death near Lancaster, Garrard County, Ky., August 11, 1840
interment in the Anderson family cemetery.

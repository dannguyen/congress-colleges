ANTHONY, Joseph Biles, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Philadelphia, Pa., June 19, 1795
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
member of the State senate 1830-1833
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-third and Twenty-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1833-March 3, 1837)
appointed judge of the Nichelson court
engaged in the sale of titles to large tracts of lands in Pennsylvania
was elected president judge of the eighth district in 1844 and served until his death in Williamsport, Lycoming County, Pa., January 10, 1851
interment in Williamsport Cemetery.

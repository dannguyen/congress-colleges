ARNOLD, Benedict, (brother-in-law of Matthias J. Bovee), a Representative from New York
born in Amsterdam, Montgomery County, N.Y., October 5, 1780
attended the common schools
engaged in mercantile pursuits and also was an extensive landowner and philanthropist
supervisor of Amsterdam 1813-1816
member of the State assembly in 1816 and 1817
elected as an Anti-Jacksonian to the Twenty-first Congress (March 4, 1829-March 3, 1831)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1830
president of the board of trustees of the village of Amsterdam in 1832
did not resume active business pursuits, but lived in retirement until his death in Amsterdam, N.Y., March 3, 1849
interment in Green Hill Cemetery.

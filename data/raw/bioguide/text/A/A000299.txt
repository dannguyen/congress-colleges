ARNOLD, William Carlile, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Luthersburg, Clearfield County, Pa., July 15, 1851
attended the public schools and Phillips Academy, Andover, Mass.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in Clearfield County, Pa., June 18, 1875, and practiced in Curwensville and Du Bois, Clearfield County, Pa.
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-fourth and Fifty-fifth Congresses (March 4, 1895-March 3, 1899)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1898 to the Fifty-sixth Congress
resumed the practice of law in Clearfield County, Pa.
died in Muskegon, Mich., while on a business trip to that city, March 20, 1906
interment in Oak Hill Cemetery, Curwensville, Pa.

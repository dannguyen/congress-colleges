ATHERTON, Charles Humphrey, (father of Charles Gordon Atherton), a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Amherst, Hillsborough County, N.H., August 14, 1773
attended the common schools and was graduated from Harvard University in 1794
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1797 and commenced practice in Amherst
register of probate 1798-1807
elected as a Federalist to the Fourteenth Congress (March 4, 1815-March 3, 1817)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1816
member of the State house of representatives 1823-1839
resumed the practice of law
died in Amherst, N.H., January 8, 1853
interment in the Old Cemetery.

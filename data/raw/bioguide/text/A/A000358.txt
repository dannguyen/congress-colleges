AKIN, W. Todd, a Representative from Missouri
born in New York, N.Y., July 5, 1947
B.S., Worcester Polytechnic Institute, Worcester, Mass., 1971
M.Div., Covenant Seminary, St. Louis, Mo., 1984
United States Army, 1971-1980
member of the Missouri state house of representatives, 1988-2001
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012, but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate.

ALEXANDER, Lamar, a Senator from Tennessee
born in Maryville, Tennessee, on July 3, 1940
B.A., Vanderbilt University 1962
J.D., New York University Law School 1965
governor of Tennessee 1979-1987
chairman of the National Governors Association 1985-1986
U.S. secretary of education 1991-1993
elected as a Republican to the U.S. Senate in 2002
reelected in 2008, and again in 2014 for the term ending January 3, 2021
chair, Republican Conference (2008-2012)
chair, Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions (One Hundred Fourteenth Congress).

ALTMIRE, Jason, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Kittanning, Armstrong County, Pa., March 7, 1968
B.S., Florida State University, Tallahassee, Fla., 1990
M.H.S.A., George Washington University, Washington, D.C., 1998
staff, United States Representative Pete Peterson of Florida, 1991-1996
professional advocate
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

AUSTRIA, Steve, a Representative from Ohio
born in Cincinnati, Hamilton County, Ohio, October 12, 1958
graduated from Carroll High School, Riverside, Ohio, 1977
B.A., Marquette University, Milwaukee, Wis., 1982
financial advisor
member, Ohio state house of representatives, 1999-2000
member, Ohio state senate, 2001-2008, majority whip, 2005-2008
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

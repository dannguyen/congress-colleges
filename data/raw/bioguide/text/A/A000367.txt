AMASH, Justin, a Representative from Michigan
born in Grand Rapids, Kent County, Mich., April 18, 1980
graduated from Grand Rapids Christian High School, Grand Rapids, Mich., 1998
A.B., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 2002
J.D., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 2005
lawyer, private practice
member of the Michigan state house of representatives, 2008-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

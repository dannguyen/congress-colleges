AGUILAR, Peter Rey, a Representative from California
born in Fontana, San Bernadino County, Calif., June 19, 1979
B.S., University of Redlands, Redlands, Calif., 2001
business owner
interim director & deputy director, Inland Empire regional office of the Governor of California, 2001
member of the Redlands, Calif., city council, 2006-2014
Mayor of Redlands, Calif., 2010-2014
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

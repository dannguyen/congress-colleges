ASHFORD, John Bradley, a Representative from Nebraska
born in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebr., November 10, 1949
graduated from Westside High School, Omaha, Nebr., 1967
B.A., Colgate University, Hamilton, N.Y., 1971
J.D., Creighton University, Omaha, Nebr., 1974
staff, United States Senator Roman Hruska of Nebraska
lawyer, private practice
business executive
attorney, Federal Highway Administration, 1974-1975
hearing examiner, Nebr. department of education, 1983-1984
judge, Nebr. court of industrial relations, 1984-1986
member of the Nebraska state legislature, 1987-1994
chief executive officer, Omaha, Nebr., housing authority, 1998-2003
member of the Nebraska state legislature, 2006-2014
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015)

BABCOCK, William, a Representative from New York
born in Hinsdale, Westmoreland County, N.H., in 1785
moved to Penn Yan, N.Y., in 1813 and engaged in mercantile pursuits
upon the formation of Yates County was appointed by the Governor as the first county treasurer
elected as an Anti-Masonic candidate to the Twenty-second Congress (March 4, 1831-March 3, 1833)
resumed mercantile pursuits and also was engaged as a hotel keeper
died in Penn Yan, Yates County, N.Y., October 20, 1838
interment in City Hill Cemetery in Torrey Township, near Penn Yan.

BAILEY, R. Wendell, a Representative from Missouri
born in Willow Springs, Mo., July 30, 1940
graduated from Willow Springs High School, Willow Springs, Mo.
B.S., Southwest Missouri State University, Springfield, Mo., 1962
automobile dealer
member, Willow Springs, Mo., city council, 1969-1971
mayor, Willow Springs, Mo., 1971-1972
member of the Missouri state house of representatives, 1972-1980
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-seventh Congress (January 3, 1981-January 3, 1983)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Ninety-eighth Congress in 1982
Missouri state treasurer, 1985-1993
unsuccessful candidate for nomination for Governor of Missouri in 1992
unsuccessful candidate for election as lieutenant governor of Missouri in 2000.

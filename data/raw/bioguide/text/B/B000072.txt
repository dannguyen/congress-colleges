BAKER, Richard Hugh, a Representative from Louisiana
born in New Orleans, Orleans Parish, La., May 22, 1948
graduated from University High School
B.A., Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge, La., 1971
member of the Louisiana state house of representatives, 1972-1986
elected as a Republican to the One Hundredth and to the ten succeeding Congresses until his resignation February 2, 2008 (January 3, 1987-February 2, 2008).

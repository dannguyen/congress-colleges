BALDACCI, John Elias, a Representative from Maine
born in Bangor, Penobscot County, Maine, January 30, 1955
graduated from Bangor High School, Bangor, Maine, 1973
B.A., University of Maine, Orono, 1986
member of Bangor, Maine, city council, 1978-1981
member of the Maine state senate, 1982-1994
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fourth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-January 3, 2003)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in 2002
Governor of Maine, 2003 to 2011.

BANNON, Henry Towne, a Representative from Ohio
born near Portsmouth, Scioto County, Ohio, June 5, 1867
attended the public schools of Portsmouth, Ohio State University at Columbus in 1885 and 1886, and was graduated from the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor in 1889
studied law
was admitted to the Ohio bar in 1891 and practiced in Portsmouth, Ohio
prosecuting attorney of Scioto County 1897-1902
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-ninth and Sixtieth Congresses (March 4, 1905-March 3, 1909)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1908
resumed the practice of law
delegate to the Republican National Conventions in 1924, 1928, 1932, 1936, and 1940
served as a director of the First National Bank, National Bank of Portsmouth, Oak Hill Savings Bank, and the Selby Shoe Co.
also engaged in literary pursuits
died in Portsmouth, Ohio, September 6, 1950
interment in Greenlawn Cemetery.

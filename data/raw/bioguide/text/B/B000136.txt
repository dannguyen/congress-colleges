BARCLAY, David, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Punxsutawney, Jefferson County, Pa., in 1823
attended Washington (now Washington and Jefferson) College, Washington, Pa.
studied law in Pittsburgh
was admitted to the bar and practiced in Punxsutawney, Brookville, and Kittanning, Pa.
one of the editors and publishers of the Pittsburgh Union and Legal Journal 1850-1855
while a resident of Brookville was elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1855-March 3, 1857)
resumed the practice of law
died in Freeport, Armstrong County, Pa., September 10, 1889
interment in Freeport Cemetery.

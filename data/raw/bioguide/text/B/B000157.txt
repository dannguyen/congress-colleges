BARNES, George Thomas, a Representative from Georgia
born in a suburb (now called Summerville) of Augusta, Richmond County, Ga., August 14, 1833
attended private schools, Richmond Academy, and Franklin College
was graduated from the University of Georgia at Athens in 1853
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1855 and commenced practice in Augusta
during the Civil War served in the Confederate Army in the Washington Light Artillery Company of Augusta, Ga., as second lieutenant and major brevet
member of the State house of representatives 1860-1865
member of the Democratic National Committee 1876-1884
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-ninth, Fiftieth, and Fifty-first Congresses (March 4, 1885-March 3, 1891)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1890 to the Fifty-second Congress
resumed the practice of law
died in Augusta, Ga., October 24, 1901
interment in the City Cemetery.

BARNEY, John, a Representative from Maryland
born in Baltimore, Md., January 18, 1785
appointed a captain and assistant district quartermaster general in the United States Army August 15, 1814, and served until June 15, 1815, when he was honorably discharged
unsuccessful candidate for election in 1822 to the Eighteenth Congress
elected as an Adams to the Nineteenth and Twentieth Congresses (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1829)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1828 to the Twenty-first Congress
engaged in literary pursuits until his death in Washington, D.C., January 26, 1857
interment in Greenmount Cemetery, Baltimore, Md.

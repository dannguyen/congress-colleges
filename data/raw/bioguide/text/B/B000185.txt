BARROW, Washington, a Representative from Tennessee
born in Davidson County, Tenn., October 5, 1807
received a classical education
lawyer, private practice
Minister to Portugal, 1841-1844
newspaper editor
elected as a Whig to the Thirtieth Congress (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1849)
was not a candidate for renomination to the Thirty-first Congress in 1848
businessman
member of the Tennessee state senate, 1860-1861
died in St. Louis, Mo., October 19, 1866
interment in the vault of Dr. John Shelby on a private estate in East Nashville, Tenn.

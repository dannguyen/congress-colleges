BARTLETT, Harry Stephen (Steve), a Representative from Texas
born in Los Angeles, Calif., September 19, 1947
attended public schools of Lockhart, Tex.
graduated, Kimball High School, Dallas, Tex., 1966
B.A., University of Texas, Austin, 1971
businessman
president and founder of manufacturing company
member, city council, Dallas, Tex., 1977-1981
delegate, Texas State Republican conventions, 1972-1982
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-eighth and to the four succeeding Congresses and served from January 3, 1983, until his resignation March 11, 1991
is a resident of Dallas, Tex.

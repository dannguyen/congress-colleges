BARTON, Richard Walker, a Representative from Virginia
born at Shady Oak, near Winchester, Frederick County, Va., in 1800
pursued academic studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Winchester, Va.
member of the State assembly in 1823-1824, 1832-1835 and 1839
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1843)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1842 to the Twenty-eighth Congress
resumed the practice of his profession in Winchester, Va.
died on his estate, Springdale, near Winchester, Frederick County, Va., March 15, 1859
interment in the family burying ground at Springdale.

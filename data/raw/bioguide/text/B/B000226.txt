BASSETT, Richard, (grandfather of Richard Henry Bayard and James Asheton Bayard, Jr.), a Senator from Delaware
born in Cecil County, Md., April 2, 1745
pursued preparatory studies
studied law
admitted to the bar and practiced in Delaware
captain of a Delaware troop during the Revolutionary War
member of the State constitutional conventions in 1776 and 1792
member, State senate 1782
member, State house of representatives 1786
delegate to the Federal Constitutional Convention in Philadelphia in 1787 and one of the signers of the Constitution
member of the Delaware convention which ratified the Federal Constitution in 1787
elected to the United States Senate and served from March 4, 1789, to March 3, 1793
chief justice of the court of common pleas 1793-1799
Governor of Delaware 1799-1801
appointed United States circuit judge by President John Adams in 1801
died on his estate, Bohemia Manor, in Cecil County, Md., August 15, 1815
interment Brandywine Cemetery, Wilmington, Del.

BAXTER, Portus, a Representative from Vermont
born in Brownington, Orleans County, Vt., December 4, 1806
attended the common schools, Norwich Military Academy, and the University of Vermont at Burlington
moved to Derby Line, Orleans County, Vt., in 1828
presidential elector on the Whig ticket in 1852 and on the Republican ticket in 1856
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-seventh, Thirty-eighth, and Thirty-ninth Congresses (March 4, 1861-March 3, 1867)
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Department of the Navy (Thirty-eighth Congress)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1866
died in Washington, D.C., March 4, 1868
interment in Strafford Cemetery, Strafford, Orange County, Vt.

BEALE, Charles Lewis, a Representative from New York
born in Canaan, Columbia County, N.Y., March 5, 1824
was graduated from Union College, Schenectady, N.Y., in 1844
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1849 and commenced practice in Canaan, N.Y.
moved to Kinderhook, N.Y., in 1852 and continued the practice of law
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1859-March 3, 1861)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1860 to the Thirty-seventh Congress
delegate to the Union National Convention at Philadelphia in 1866
resumed the practice of law
died in Hudson, N.Y., on January 30, 1899
interment in Kinderhook Cemetery, Kinderhook, N.Y.

BELCHER, Hiram, a Representative from Maine
born in Hallowell, Maine, February 23, 1790
attended the rural schools and the local academy at Hallowell 1805-1807
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Farmington, Kennebec County, Maine, in 1812
elected town clerk of Farmington and served from 1814 to 1819
member of the State house of representatives in 1822, 1829, and 1832
served in the State senate in 1838 and 1839
elected as a Whig to the Thirtieth Congress (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1849)
chairman, Committee on Mileage (Thirtieth Congress)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1848 to the Thirty-first Congress
engaged in the practice of his profession until his death in Farmington, Maine, May 6, 1857
interment in Center Meeting House Cemetery.

BENNETT, Charles Edward, a Representative from Florida
born in Canton, St. Lawrence County, N.Y., December 2, 1910
attended the Tampa schools
J.D., University of Florida, Gainesville, Fla., 1934
lawyer, private practice
member of the Florida state house of representatives, 1941
United States Army, 1942-1947
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-first and to the twenty-one succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1949-January 3, 1993)
chair, Committee on Standards of Official Conduct (Ninety-sixth Congress)
was not a candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992
died on September 6, 2003, in Jacksonville, Fla.

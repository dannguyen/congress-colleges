BENNETT, David Smith, a Representative from New York
born on a farm near Camillus, Onondaga County, N.Y., May 3, 1811
attended the common schools and the local academy in Onondaga
engaged in agricultural pursuits
moved to Syracuse and engaged in the produce business, afterwards extending his business to New York City
moved to Buffalo in 1853 and built and operated several grain elevators
also purchased the original Dart grain elevator
elected a member of the State senate in 1865
elected as a Republican to the Forty-first Congress (March 4, 1869-March 3, 1871)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1870
resumed his former business pursuits in Buffalo, N.Y., where he died November 6, 1894
interment in Oakwood Cemetery, Syracuse, N.Y.

BENNETT, Joseph Bentley, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Greenup County, Ky., April 21, 1859
attended the common schools and Greenup Academy, Greenup, Ky.
taught in the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1878 and commenced practice in 1880
entered the mercantile business in 1885
judge of Greenup County 1894-1897
reelected in 1897 and served until 1901
member of the Republican State central committee in 1900 and 1904
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-ninth, Sixtieth, and Sixty-first Congresses (March 4, 1905-March 3, 1911)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1910 to the Sixty-second Congress
continued the practice of his profession until his death in Greenup, Greenup County, Ky., November 7, 1923
interment in Riverview Cemetery.

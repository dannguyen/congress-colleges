BENTLEY, Henry Wilbur, a Representative from New York
born in DeRuyter, Madison County, N.Y., September 30, 1838
moved with his parents to Morrisville, N.Y.
attended Union School, Yates Polytechnic Institute at Chittenango, and JuddÂ’s private school at Berkshire
taught school for several years
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1861 and commenced practice in Boonville, N.Y.
chairman of the Oneida County Building Commission
president of Boonville in 1874, 1889-1891, and 1899
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-second Congress (March 4, 1891-March 3, 1893)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1892 to the Fifty-third Congress
continued the practice of law in Boonville, Oneida County, N.Y., until his death there on January 27, 1907
interment in Boonville Cemetery.

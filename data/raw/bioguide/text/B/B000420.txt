BERRY, Robert Marion, a Representative from Arkansas
born in Stuttgart, Arkansas County, Ark., August 27, 1942
graduated from DeWitt High School, DeWitt, Ark.
B.S., University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences, 1965
farmer
Gillett, Ark., city council, 1976-1980
Arkansas Soil and Water Conservation Commission, 1986-1994
White House Domestic Policy Council, 1993-1996
special assistant to President William Clinton for Agricultural Trade and Food Assistance, 1993-1996
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2011)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

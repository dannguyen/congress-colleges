BICKNELL, Bennet, a Representative from New York
born in Mansfield, Conn., November 14, 1781
attended the public schools
moved to Morrisville, N.Y., in 1808
served in the War of 1812
member of the State assembly in 1812
served in the State senate 1814-1818
clerk of Madison County, N.Y., 1821-1825
editor of the Madison Observer
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1837-March 3, 1839)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1838 to the Twenty-sixth Congress
died in Morrisville, Madison County, N.Y., September 15, 1841
interment in Morrisville Rural Cemetery.

BINGAMAN, Jesse Francis, Jr. (Jeff), a Senator from New Mexico
born in El Paso, Tex., October 3, 1943
attended public schools of Silver City, N.Mex.
graduated, Harvard College 1965 and Stanford Law School 1968
served in the United States Army Reserve 1968-1974
admitted to the New Mexico bar 1968
assistant New Mexico attorney general 1969
counsel to State constitutional convention 1969
commenced private practice in 1970
elected New Mexico attorney general 1979-1982
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 1982
reelected in 1988, 1994, 2000, and 2006, and served from January 3, 1983, to January 3, 2013
chairman, Senate Impeachment Trial Committee (1989-1991), Committee on Energy and Natural Resources (One Hundred Seventh Congress [January 3-20, 2001
June 6, 2001-January 3, 2003], One Hundred Tenth to One Hundred Twelfth Congresses)
was not a candidate for reelection to the Senate in 2012.

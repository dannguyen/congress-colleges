BIRDSALL, James, a Representative from New York
born in that State in 1783
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1806 and was the first lawyer to settle in Norwich, Chenango County, N.Y.
surrogate of Chenango County, N.Y., in 1811
elected as a Republican to the Fourteenth Congress (March 4, 1815-March 3, 1817)
member of the State assembly in 1827
one of the incorporators of the Bank of Chenango
moved to Fenton, Genesee County, Mich., in 1839 and later to Flint, Mich., where he died July 20, 1856
interment in Glenwood Cemetery.

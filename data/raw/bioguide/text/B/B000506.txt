BLACKBURN, Benjamin Bentley, a Representative from Georgia
born in Atlanta, Fulton County, Ga., February 14, 1927
attended the public schools in Atlanta, Ga.
graduated from the University of North Carolina in 1947, and from Emory University School of Law in 1954
during the Second World War served in the United States Navy, 1944-1946
during the Korean conflict again served in the United States Navy, 1950-1952
was retired as a lieutenant commander in the United States Naval Reserve
served in the State attorney generalÂ’s office, 1955-1957
admitted to the bar in 1954 and commenced private practice in Atlanta, Ga., after service with the State attorney general
elected as a Republican to the Ninetieth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1967-January 3, 1975)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1974 to the Ninety-fourth Congress
is a resident of Atlanta, Ga.

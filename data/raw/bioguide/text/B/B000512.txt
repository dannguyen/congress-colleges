BLACKLEDGE, William Salter, (son of William Blackledge), a Representative from North Carolina
born in Pitt County, N.C., in 1793
moved to Craven County, N.C., and settled in New Bern
graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 1813
member of the State house of commons in 1820
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Jesse Slocumb
reelected as a Republican to the Seventeenth Congress and served from February 7, 1821, until March 3, 1823
died in New Bern, Craven County, N.C., November 21, 1856
interment in New Bern Cemetery.

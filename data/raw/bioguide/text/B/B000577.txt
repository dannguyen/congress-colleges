BOARDMAN, Elijah, (father of William Whiting Boardman), a Senator from Connecticut
born in New Milford, Conn., March 7, 1760
educated under private tutors
served in the Revolutionary War
employed as clerk in a mercantile establishment
engaged in mercantile pursuits 1781-1812
member, State house of representatives 1803-1805 and again in 1816
member, State upper house 1817-1819
member, State senate 1819-1821
elected as a Democratic Republican to the United States Senate and served from March 4, 1821, until his death while on a visit to Boardman, Ohio, August 18, 1823
interment in the Center Cemetery, New Milford, Conn.

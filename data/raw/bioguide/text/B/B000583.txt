BODEN, Andrew, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Carlisle, Cumberland County, Pa., birth date unknown
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
also engaged in the real estate business
elected as a Republican to the Fifteenth Congress and reelected to the Sixteenth Congress (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1821)
resumed the practice of law
died in Carlisle, Pa., December 20, 1835.

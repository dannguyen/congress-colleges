BODLE, Charles, a Representative from New York
born near Poughkeepsie, Dutchess County, N.Y., in 1787
was a wagon maker by trade
justice of the peace
held several political offices in Bloomingburg, Sullivan County
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-third Congress (March 4, 1833-March 3, 1835)
died in New York City October 31, 1835
interment in Bloomingburg Cemetery, Bloomingburg, N.Y.

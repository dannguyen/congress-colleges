BONILLA, Henry, a Representative from Texas
born in San Antonio, Bexar County, Tex., January 2, 1954
graduated from South San Antonio High School, San Antonio, Tex., 1972
B.J., University of Texas, Austin, Tex., 1976
journalist
broadcasting executive
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Third and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2007)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Tenth Congress.

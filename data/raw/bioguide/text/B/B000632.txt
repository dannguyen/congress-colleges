BOOTHMAN, Melvin Morella, a Representative from Ohio
born near Bryan, Williams County, Ohio, October 16, 1846
attended the public schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
enlisted in Company H, Thirty-eighth Regiment, Ohio Volunteer Infantry, January 4, 1864
served through the Atlanta campaign
was graduated from the law department of Michigan University at Ann Arbor in 1871
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Bryan, Ohio
elected treasurer of Williams County in 1871 and reelected in 1873
elected as a Republican to the Fiftieth and Fifty-first Congresses (March 4, 1887-March 3, 1891)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1890
resumed the practice of law in Bryan, Ohio, and died there March 5, 1904
interment in Fountain City Cemetery.

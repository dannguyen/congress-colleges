BORSKI, Robert Anthony, Jr., a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Philadelphia, Philadelphia County, Pa., October 20, 1948
graduated from Frankford High School, Philadelphia, Pa., 1966
B.A., University of Baltimore, Baltimore, Md., 1971
stockbroker
member of the Pennsylvania state house of representatives, 1976-1982
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-eighth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-January 3, 2003)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in 2002.

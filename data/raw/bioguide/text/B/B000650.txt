BOSS, John Linscom, Jr., a Representative from Rhode Island
born in Charleston, S.C., September 7, 1780
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Newport, R.I.
held many important local offices
member of the State house of representatives from 1806 to 1815
elected as a Federalist to the Fourteenth and Fifteenth Congresses (March 4, 1815-March 3, 1819)
died in Newport, R.I., August 1, 1819
interment in the Common Burial Ground.

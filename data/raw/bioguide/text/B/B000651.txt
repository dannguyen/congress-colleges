BOSSIER, Pierre Jean Baptiste Evariste, a Representative from Louisiana
born in Natchitoches, La., March 22, 1797
received a classical education
engaged as a sugar and cotton planter
member of the State senate 1833-1843
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-eighth Congress and served from March 4, 1843, until his death in Washington, D.C., on April 24, 1844
interment in the Congressional Cemetery, Washington, D.C.
reinterment in the Catholic Cemetery, Natchitoches, La.

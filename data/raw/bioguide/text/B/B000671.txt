BOURNE, Shearjashub, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Barnstable, Mass., June 14, 1746
was graduated from Harvard College in 1764
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Boston
member of the State house of representatives 1782-1785 and 1788-1790
member of the convention in 1788 which ratified the Constitution
elected as a Pro-Administration candidate to the Second and Third Congresses (March 4, 1791-March 3, 1795)
served as justice of the court of common pleas of Suffolk County from 1799 until his death in Boston, Mass., March 11, 1806.

BOWEN, David Reece, a Representative from Mississippi
born in Houston, Chickasaw County, Miss., October 21, 1932
graduated, Cleveland (Miss.) High School, 1950
attended University of Missouri, 1950-1952
A.B., Harvard University, 1954
M.A., Oxford University, Oxford, England, 1956
served in the United States Army, private first class, 1957-1958
assistant professor of political science and history, Mississippi College, 1958-1959, and Millsaps College, 1959-1964
employed by U.S. Office of Economic Opportunity, 1966-1967
U.S. Chamber of Commerce, 1967-1968
first coordinator of federal-state programs, State of Mississippi, 1968-1972
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-third and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1973-January 3, 1983)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1982 to the Ninety-eighth Congress
visiting lecturer, Mississippi State University, 1985-1987
is a resident of McLean, Va.

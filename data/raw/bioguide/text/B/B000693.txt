BOWERSOCK, Justin De Witt, a Representative from Kansas
born near Columbiana, Columbiana County, Ohio, September 19, 1842
moved to Iowa City, Iowa, in 1860 and engaged in mercantile pursuits and grain shipping
moved to Lawrence, Kans., in 1877 and engaged in banking and in the manufacture of flour, paper, and barbed wire
mayor of Lawrence 1881-1885
elected to the Kansas house of representatives in 1887
member of the State senate in 1895
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-sixth and to the three succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1899-March 3, 1907)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1906
interested in banking and manufactures in Lawrence, Kans., until his death there on October 27, 1922
interment in Oak Hill Cemetery.

BRADBURY, George, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Falmouth, Mass., October 10, 1770
was graduated from Harvard University in 1789
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Portland, Maine (until 1820 a district of Massachusetts)
member of the Massachusetts house of representatives 1806-1812
elected as a Federalist to the Thirteenth and Fourteenth Congresses (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1817)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1816
resumed the practice of law
associate clerk of the Portland Court 1817-1820
member of the State senate in 1820
died in Portland, Maine, November 7, 1823
interment in Eastern Cemetery.

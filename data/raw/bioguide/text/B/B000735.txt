BRADBURY, Theophilus, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Newbury, Mass., November 13, 1739
was graduated from Harvard College in 1757
taught school and studied law in Portland, Maine
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Portland in 1761
moved to Newburyport, Mass., in 1764 and continued the practice of law
member of the State senate 1791-1794
elected as a Federalist to the Fourth and Fifth Congresses and served from March 4, 1795, until July 24, 1797, when he resigned
appointed judge of the supreme court of Massachusetts in 1797, which position he held until his death
member of the electoral college in 1800
died in Newburyport, Mass., September 6, 1803
interment in Newburyport Cemetery.

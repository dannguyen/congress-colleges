BRADY, Kevin Patrick, a Representative from Texas
born in Vermillion, Clay County, S. Dak., April 11, 1955
graduated from Rapid City Central High School, Rapid City, S. Dak
B.A., University of South Dakota, Vermillion, S. Dak., 1990
member of the Texas state house of representatives, 1990-1996
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-present)
chair, Joint Economic Committee (One Hundred Thirteenth Congress)
chair, Ways and Means Committee (One Hundred Fourteenth Congress).

BRAND, Charles, a Representative from Ohio
born in Urbana, Champaign County, Ohio, November 1, 1871
attended the graded schools of his native city and Ohio Wesleyan University, Delaware, Ohio
engaged in agricultural pursuits, manufacturing, and banking at Urbana
member and president of the Urbana City Council 1911-1912
member of the State senate in 1921 and 1922
served as a member of the advisory committee of the War Finance Corporation in 1921
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-eighth and to the four succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1923-March 3, 1933)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1932
resumed former business pursuits until his retirement
died in Melbourne Beach, Fla., May 23, 1966
interment in Melbourne Cemetery.

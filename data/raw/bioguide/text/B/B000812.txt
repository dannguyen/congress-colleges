BREWER, Willis, a Representative from Alabama
born near Livingston, Sumter County, Ala., March 15, 1844
attended the common schools
entered the Confederate Army at the age of eighteen years
journalist, author, and planter
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1870 and commenced practice at Haynesville, Ala.
treasurer of Lowndes County in 1871
State auditor 1876-1880
member of the State house of representatives 1880-1882
served in the State senate 1882-1890
again a member of the State house of representatives 1890-1894
again served in the State senate 1894-1897
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-fifth and Fifty-sixth Congresses (March 4, 1897-March 3, 1901)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1900
resumed the practice of law and continued his work as an author until his death in Montgomery, Ala., on October 30, 1912
interment in the family mausoleum on Cedars plantation, near Montgomery, Ala.

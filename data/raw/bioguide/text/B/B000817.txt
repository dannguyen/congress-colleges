BREWSTER, William K., a Representative from Oklahoma
born in Ardmore, Carter County, Okla., November 8, 1941
attended public schools in Oklahoma and Texas
B.S., Southwestern Oklahoma State University, 1968
member, United States Army Reserves, 1966-1971
pharmacist
rancher
owner of a real estate company
member, Oklahoma house of representatives, 1983-1990
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Second and to the two succeeding congresses (January 3, 1991-January 3, 1997)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress in 1996.

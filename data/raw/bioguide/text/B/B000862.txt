BRODHEAD, William McNulty, a Representative from Michigan
born in Cleveland, Cuyahoga County, Ohio, September 12, 1941
graduated from St. Ignatius High School, Cleveland, Ohio, 1959
A.B., Wayne State University, Detroit, Mich., 1965
J.D., University of Michigan Law School, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1967
admitted to the Michigan Bar in 1968
lawyer, private practice
member of the Michigan state house of representatives, 1970
reelected in 1972
delegate to Michigan state Democratic conventions, 1968-1974
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-fourth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1975-January 3, 1983)
was not a candidate for reelection to the Ninety-eighth Congress in 1982
resumed the practice of law
is a resident of West Bloomfield, Mich..

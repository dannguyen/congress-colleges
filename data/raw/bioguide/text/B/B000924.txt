BROWN, Jason Brevoort, a Representative from Indiana
born in Dillsboro, Dearborn County, Ind., February 26, 1839
attended the common schools and Wilmington Academy, Dearborn County, Ind.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1860 and commenced practice in Brownstown, Ind.
member of the State house of representatives 1862-1866
member of the State senate in 1870
secretary of the Territory of Wyoming 1873-1875
moved to Seymour, Ind., in 1875
again a member of the State senate 1880-1883
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-first, Fifty-second, and Fifty-third Congresses (March 4, 1889-March 3, 1895)
chairman, Committee on Elections (Fifty-third Congress)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1894
resumed the practice of law in Seymour, Jackson County, Ind., and died there March 10, 1898
interment in Riverview Cemetery.

BROWN, John Brewer, a Representative from Maryland
born in Philadelphia, Pa., May 13, 1836
attended Centerville (Maryland) Academy and Dickinson College, Carlisle, Pa.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1857 and practiced in Centerville, Queen AnneÂ’s County, Md.
member of the State house of delegates in 1870
served in the State senate 1888-1892
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-second Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of Henry Page and served from November 8, 1892, to March 3, 1893
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1892
resumed the practice of law
died in Centerville May 16, 1898
interment in Chesterfield Cemetery.

BROWN, William, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Frederick County, Va., April 19, 1779
attended the common schools
moved with his father to Bourbon County, Ky., in 1784 and to Cynthiana, Harrison County, Ky., about 1795
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
served as a colonel in the War of 1812
member of the State house of representatives
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth Congress (March 4, 1819-March 3, 1821)
moved to Jacksonville, Morgan County, Ill., in 1832, where he died October 6, 1833.

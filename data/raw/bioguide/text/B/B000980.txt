BRUNNER, David B., a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Amity, Berks (now Washington) County, Pa., March 7, 1835
attended the common schools
learned the carpenterÂ’s trade
taught school from 1853 to 1856, during which time he studied the classics
was graduated from Dickinson College, Carlisle, Pa., in 1860
principal of Reading Classical Academy, Reading, Pa., 1860-1869
established the Reading Business College in 1880
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-first and Fifty-second Congresses (March 4, 1889-March 3, 1893)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1892
taught at the Reading Business College
died in Reading, Pa., on November 29, 1903
interment in Amityville Cemetery, Berks County, Pa.

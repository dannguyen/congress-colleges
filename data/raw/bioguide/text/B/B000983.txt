BRUSH, Henry, a Representative from Ohio
born in Dutchess County, N.Y., in June 1778
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1803 and commenced practice in Chillicothe, Ohio
member of the State house of representatives in 1810
served in the State senate in 1814
moved to London, Ohio
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth Congress (March 4, 1819-March 3, 1821)
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Department of War (Sixteenth Congress)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1820 to the Seventeenth Congress
judge of the supreme court of Ohio in 1828
retired to his farm near London, Madison County, Ohio, where he died January 19, 1855
interment in Oak Hill Cemetery.

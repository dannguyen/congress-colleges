BRYAN, Henry Hunter, (brother of Joseph Hunter Bryan), a Representative from Tennessee
born in Martin County, N.C., February 23, 1786
attended grammar and high schools
moved to Tennessee and held several local offices
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth Congress (March 4, 1819-March 3, 1821)
had been reelected to the Seventeenth Congress but did not qualify
died in Montgomery County, Tenn., May 7, 1835.

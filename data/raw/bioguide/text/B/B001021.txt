BUCKINGHAM, William Alfred, a Senator from Connecticut
born in Lebanon, Conn., May 28, 1804
attended the common schools and Bacon Academy, Colchester, Conn.
engaged in mercantile pursuits and in manufacturing
mayor of Norwich, Conn. 1849-1850, 1856-1857
Governor of Connecticut 1858-1866
resumed his former business pursuits
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate and served from March 4, 1869, until his death in Norwich, Conn., February 5, 1875
chairman, Committee on Engrossed Bills (Forty-first and Forty-second Congresses), Committee on Investigation and Retrenchment (Forty-second Congress), Committee on Indian Affairs (Forty-third Congress)
interment in Yantic Cemetery, Norwich, Conn.

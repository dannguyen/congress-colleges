BUCKLER, Richard Thompson, a Representative from Minnesota
born on a farm near Oakland, Coles County, Ill., October 27, 1865
attended the public schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits in Cole County, Ill.
moved to Andover Township, Polk County, Minn., in 1904 and continued agricultural pursuits
active in Farm Bureau and FarmersÂ’ Union organizations
held numerous township and local school district offices
served in the State senate 1915-1919, 1923-1927, and 1931-1933
elected on the Farmer-Labor ticket to the Seventy-fourth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1935-January 3, 1943)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1942
resumed agricultural pursuits
died in Crookston, Minn., January 23, 1950
interment in Oakdale Cemetery.

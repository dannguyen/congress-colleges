BUECHNER, John William (Jack), a Representative from Missouri
born in Kirkwood, Mo., June 4, 1940
attended parochial schools
A.B., Benedictine College, Atchison, Kans., 1962
J.D., St. Louis University Law School, St. Louis, Mo.,1965
admitted to the Missouri State bar in 1965 and commenced practice in St. Louis County
real estate developer
member, Missouri house of representatives, 1972-1982
delegate to the Republican National Conventions, 1964, 1980, 1988
elected as a Republican to the One Hundredth and One Hundred First Congresses (January 3, 1987-January 3, 1991)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1990 to the One Hundred Second Congress
president, National Republican Institute for International Affairs
is a resident of St. Louis, Mo.

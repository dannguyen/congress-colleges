BUFFETT, Howard Homan, a Representative from Nebraska
born in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebr., August 13, 1903
attended the public schools, and was graduated from the University of Nebraska at Lincoln in 1925
engaged in the investment business in 1926
member of the Omaha Board of Education 1939-1942
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-eighth, Seventy-ninth, and Eightieth Congresses (January 3, 1943-January 3, 1949)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1948 to the Eighty-first Congress
elected to the Eighty-second Congress (January 3, 1951-January 3, 1953)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1952
resumed former business pursuits
was a resident of Omaha, Nebr., until his death there on April 30, 1964
interment in Forest Lawn Memorial Park.

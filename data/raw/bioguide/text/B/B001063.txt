BUNN, Jim, a Representative from Oregon
born in McMinniville, Oreg., December 12, 1956
graduated Dayton Union High School
A.A., Chemeketa Community College, 1977
B.A., NW Nazarene College, 1979
2nd lieutenant, Oregon National Guard Reserve
member, State senate, 1987-1995, GOP whip 1990-1995
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth Congress (January 3, 1995-January 3, 1997)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress.

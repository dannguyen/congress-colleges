BURD, George, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in 1793
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1810 at Carlisle, Cumberland County, Pa., and practiced
elected as an Anti-Jacksonian to the Twenty-second and Twenty-third Congresses (March 4, 1831-March 3, 1835)
moved to Mercer County in 1843
died in Bedford, Bedford County, Pa., on January 13, 1844
interment in Bedford Cemetery.

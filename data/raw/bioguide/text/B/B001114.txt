BURNELL, Barker, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Nantucket, Mass., January 30, 1798
member of the State house of representatives in 1819
member of the Massachusetts Constitutional convention in 1820
served in the State senate in 1824 and 1825
delegate to the Whig National Convention in 1840
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-seventh and Twenty-eighth Congresses and served from March 4, 1841, until his death in Washington, D.C., June 15, 1843
interment in Congressional Cemetery
reinterment in Prospect Hill Cemetery, Nantucket, Mass., in 1844.

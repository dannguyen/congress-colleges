BURNES, Daniel Dee, a Representative from Missouri
born in Ringgold, Platte County, Mo., January 4, 1851
received his early schooling at Weston, Mo.
was graduated from St. Louis University, St. Louis, Mo., in 1873 and from the law department of Harvard University in 1874
went to Germany and studied at Heidelberg University
returned to the United States and settled in St. Joseph, Mo., where he engaged in the practice of law
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-third Congress (March 4, 1893-March 3, 1895)
declined to be a candidate for reelection in 1894
resumed the practice of law
died on his estate, Ayr Lawn, at St. Joseph, Buchanan County, Mo., November 2, 1899
interment in Mount Mora Cemetery.

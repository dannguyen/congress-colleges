BURNS, Conrad, a Senator from Montana
born in Gallatin, Davies County, Mo., January 25, 1935
attended the Gallatin public schools
attended the University of Missouri 1953-1954
United States Marine Corps 1955-1957
farm broadcaster and auctioneer
Yellowstone (Mont.) County Commissioner 1986
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 1988
reelected in 1994 and 2000, and served from January 3, 1989, to January 3, 2007
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 2006
was a resident of Billings, Mont., until his death on April 28, 2016.

BURR, Richard M., a Senator and a Representative from North Carolina
born in Charlottesville, Albemarle County, Va., November 30, 1955
graduated from Reynolds High School, Winston-Salem, N.C., 1974
B.A., Wake Forest University, Winston-Salem, N.C., 1978
unsuccessful candidate for election to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-January 3, 2005)
was not a candidate for reelection to the House of Representatives, but was elected to the United States Senate in 2004
reelected in 2010 for the term ending January 3, 2017
chair, Select Committee on Intelligence (One Hundred Fourteenth Congress).

BUTLER, Mounce Gore, a Representative from Tennessee
born in Gainesboro, Jackson County, Tenn., May 11, 1849
attended the common schools, Old Philomath Academy, and the law department of Cumberland University, Lebanon, Tenn.
was admitted to the bar in 1871 and commenced the practice of law in Gainesboro
delegate to all Democratic State conventions from 1872 to 1916
attorney general for the fifth judicial circuit of Tennessee 1894-1902
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1905-March 3, 1907)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1906
resumed the practice of his profession in Gainesboro, Jackson County, Tenn., and died there February 13, 1917
interment in Gainesboro Cemetery.

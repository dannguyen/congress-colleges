born in Rensselaer, Jasper County, Ind., November 26, 1958
graduated from North White High School, Monon, Ind., 1976
B.S., The Citadel, Charlestown, S.C., 1980
J.D., Valparaiso University School of Law, Valparaiso, Ind., 1984
lawyer, private practice
United States Army, 1984-1987
United States Army Reserve, 1980-2010
Indiana state deputy attorney general, 1987-1988
legal counsel for the 22nd Theater Army in Operation Desert Shield and Desert Storm
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Third and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2011)
one of the managers appointed by the House of Representatives in 1998 to conduct the impeachment proceedings of President William Jefferson Clinton

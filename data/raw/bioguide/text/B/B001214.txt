BYRNE, William Thomas, a Representative from New York
born in the town of Florida, Montgomery County, N.Y., March 6, 1876
attended the public schools
was graduated from Albany (N.Y.) Law School (branch of Union College) in 1904
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Albany, N.Y.
member of the State senate 1923-1936
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-fifth and to the seven succeeding Congresses and served from January 3, 1937, until his death in Troy, N.Y., January 27, 1952

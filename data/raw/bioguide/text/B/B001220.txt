BYRON, Beverly Barton Butcher, (wife Goodloe Edgar Byron and daughter-in-law of William Devereux Byron and Katharine Edgar Byron), a Representative from Maryland
born Beverly Barton Butcher in Baltimore, Md., July 27, 1932
graduated from National Cathedral School for Girls, Washington, D.C., 1950
attended Hood College, Frederick, Md., 1961-1962
treasurer, Maryland Young Democrats, 1962 and 1965
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-sixth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1993)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992
appointed to the Base Closure and Realignment Commission, 1993
is a resident of Frederick, Md.

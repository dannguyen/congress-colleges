BAIRD, Brian, a Representative from Washington
born in Chama, Rio Arriba County, N.Mex., March 7, 1956
B.S., University of Utah, Provo, Utah, 1977
M.S., University of Wyoming, Laramie, Wyo., 1980
Ph.D., University of Wyoming, Laramie, Wyo., 1984
clinical psychologist
professor, Pacific Lutheran University, Tacoma, Wash., 1986-1998
unsuccessful candidate for election to the One Hundred Fifth Congress in 1996
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Sixth Congress and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1999-January 3, 2011)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

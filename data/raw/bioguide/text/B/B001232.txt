BIGGERT, Judy Borg, a Representative from Illinois
born in Chicago, Cook County, Ill., August 15, 1937
attended New Trier High School, Winnetka, Ill.
B.A., Stanford University, Stanford, Calif., 1959
J.D., Northwestern University School of Law, Evanston, Ill., 1963
clerk to Judge Luther M. Swygert, U.S. Court of Appeals, 7th Circuit, 1963-1964
president, board of education, Hinsdale Township High School district 86, Hinsdale Township, Ill., 1983-1986
member, Illinois state house of representatives, 1993-1998
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Sixth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1999-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

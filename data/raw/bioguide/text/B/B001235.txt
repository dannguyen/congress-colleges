BROWN, Henry Edward, Jr., a Representative from South Carolina
born in Bishopville, Lee County, S.C., December 20, 1935
graduated from Berkeley High School, Moncks Corner, S.C., 1953
attended Baptist College (now Charleston Southern University), Charleston, S.C.
attended The Citadel, Charleston, S.C.
South Carolina National Guard, 1953-1962
business executive
member of the South Carolina state general assembly, 1985-2000
member of the Hanahan, S.C., city council, 1996-2000
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2011)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

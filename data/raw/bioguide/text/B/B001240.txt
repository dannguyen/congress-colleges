BEAUPREZ, Bob, a Representative from Colorado
born in Lafayette, Boulder County, Colo., September 22, 1948
B.S., University of Colorado, 1970
farmer
business owner
chair, Republican State Central Committee of Colorado, 1999-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2003-January 3, 2007)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Tenth Congress in 2006, but was an unsuccessful candidate for Governor of Colorado
unsuccessful candidate for Governor of Colorado in 2014.

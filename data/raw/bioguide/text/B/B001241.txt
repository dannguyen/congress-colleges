BELL, Chris, a Representative from Texas
born in Texas, November 23, 1959
B.J., University of Texas, Austin, Tex., 1982
J.D., South Texas University, Houston, Tex., 1992
journalist
lawyer, private practice
member, Houston, Tex., city council, 1997-2001
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth Congress (January 3, 2003-January 3, 2005)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 2004.

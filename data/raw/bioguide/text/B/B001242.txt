BISHOP, Timothy H., a Representative from New York
born in Southampton, Suffolk County, N.Y., June 1, 1950
graduated from Southampton High School, Southampton, N.Y., 1968
A.B., Holy Cross College, Worcester, Mass., 1972
M.A., Long Island University, Long Island, N.Y., 1981
administrator, Southampton College, Southampton, N.Y., 1973-2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

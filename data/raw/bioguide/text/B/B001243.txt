BLACKBURN, Marsha, a Representative from Tennessee
born in Laurel, Miss., June 6, 1952
graduated from Northeast Jones High School, Laurel, Miss.
B.S. Mississippi State University, Starkville, Miss., 1973
business owner
private advocate
unsuccessful candidate for election to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992
member of the Tennessee state senate, 1998-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

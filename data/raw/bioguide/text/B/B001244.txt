BONNER, Jr., Josiah Robins (Jo), a Representative from Alabama
born in Selma, Dallas County, Ala., November 19, 1959
B.A., University of Alabama, 1982
attended University of Alabama Law School
staff, United States Representative Sonny Callahan of Alabama, 1985-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the five succeeding Congresses until his resignation on August 2, 2013 (January 3, 2003-August 2, 2013)
chair, Committee on Ethics (One Hundred Twelfth Congress).

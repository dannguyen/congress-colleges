BORDALLO, Madeleine, a Delegate from Guam
born in Graceville, Big Stone County, Minn., May 31, 1933
graduated from George Washington High School, Mangilao, Guam, 1951
attended St. Mary’s College, South Bend, Ind.
attended, St. Katherine’s College, St. Paul, Minn., 1953
member of the Guam legislature, 1981-1982, 1987-1994
unsuccessful candidate for Governor of Guam in 1990
lieutenant governor of Guam, 1995-2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth Congress and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

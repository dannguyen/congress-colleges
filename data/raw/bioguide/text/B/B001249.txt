BURNS, Max, a Representative from Georgia
born in Millen, Jenkins County, Ga., on November 8, 1948
B.A., Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, Ga., 1973
M.B.A, Georgia State University, Atlanta, Ga., 1977
Ph.D., Georgia State University, Atlanta, Ga., 1987
United States Army Reserve, 1973-1981
Screven County, Ga., Commission, 1993-1998
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth Congress (January 3, 2003-January 3, 2005)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 2004.

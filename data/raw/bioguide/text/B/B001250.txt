BISHOP, Robert (Rob), a Representative from Utah
born in Kaysville, Utah, July 13, 1951
B.A., University of Utah, Salt Lake City, Utah, 1974
teacher
professional advocate
member of the Utah state house of representatives, 1979-1994, speaker, 1992-1994
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present)
chair, Committee on Natural Resources (One Hundred Fourteenth Congress).

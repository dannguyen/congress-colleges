BEAN, Melissa L., a Representative from Illinois
born in Chicago, Cook County, Ill., January 22, 1962
graduated, Maine East High School, Park Ridge, Ill., 1980
A.A., Oakton Community College, Des Plaines, Ill., 1982
B.A., Roosevelt University, Chicago, Ill., 2002
consultant
businesswoman
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Ninth Congress and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2005-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for re-election to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

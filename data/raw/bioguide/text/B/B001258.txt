BOYDA, Nancy, a Representative from Kansas
born in St. Louis, St. Louis County, Mo., August 2, 1955
B.S., William Jewell College, Liberty, Mo., 1977
chemist
teacher
unsuccessful candidate for election to One Hundred and Ninth Congress in 2004
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2009)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

BRALEY, Bruce, a Representative from Iowa
born in Grinnell, Poweshiek County, Iowa, October 30, 1957
graduated from B.G.M. High School, Brooklyn, Iowa
B.A., Iowa State University, 1980
J.D., University of Iowa, 1983
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2015)
not a candidate for reelection, but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate in 2014.

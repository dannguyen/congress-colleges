BROUN, Paul C., Jr., a Representative from Georgia
born in Atlanta, Fulton County, Ga., May 14, 1946
graduated from Athens High School, Athens, Ga., 1963
United States Marine Corps Reserve, 1964-1967
B.S., University of Georgia, Athens, Ga., 1967
M.D., Medical College of Georgia, Augusta, Ga., 1971
physician
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Tenth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of United States Representative Charles Norwood, and to the three succeeding Congresses (July 17, 2007-January 3, 2015)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate in 2014.

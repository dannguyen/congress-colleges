BOCCIERI, John A., a Representative from Ohio
born in Youngstown, Mahoning County, Ohio, October 5, 1969
graduated from Ursuline High School, Youngstown, Ohio, 1988
B.S., St. Bonaventure University, St. Bonaventure, N.Y., 1992
M.A., Webster University, St. Louis, Mo., 1996
M.P.A., Webster University, St. Louis, Mo., 1996
United States Air Force, 1994-1998
Ohio Air National Guard, 1998-2000
Air Force Reserve, 2000-present
member, Ohio state house of representatives, 2001-2007
member, Ohio state senate, 2007-2008
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

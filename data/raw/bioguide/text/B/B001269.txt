BARLETTA, Lou, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Hazleton, Luzerne County, Pa., January 28, 1956
graduated from Hazleton High School, Hazleton, Pa., 1974
attended Bloomsburg University, Bloomsburg, Pa.
professional athlete
business owner
member of the Hazleton, Pa., city council, 1998-2000
Mayor of Hazleton, Pa., 2000-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

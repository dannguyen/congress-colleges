BENISHEK, Dan, a Representative from Michigan
born in Iron River, Iron County, Mich., April 20, 1952
graduated from West Iron County High School, Iron River, Mich., 1970
B.S., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1974
M.D., Wayne State University Medical School, Detroit, Mich., 1978
physician
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

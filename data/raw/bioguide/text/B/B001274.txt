BROOKS, Mo, a Representative from Alabama
born in Charleston, Charleston County, S.C., April 29, 1954
graduated from Grissom High School, Huntsville, Ala., 1972
B.A., Duke University, Durham, N.C., 1975
J.D., University of Alabama School of Law, Tuscaloosa, Ala., 1978
lawyer, private practice
Tuscaloosa County, Ala. prosecutor, 1978-1980
clerk, Circuit Court Judge John Snodgrass, 1980-1982
member of the Alabama house of representatives, 1982-1992
Madison County, Ala. district attorney, 1991-1993
special assistant attorney general, state of Alabama, 1995-2002
commissioner, Madison County, Ala., board of commissions, 1996-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

BUCSHON, Larry, a Representative from Indiana
born in Taylorville, Bartholomew County, Ill., May 31, 1962
B.S., University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, Ill., 1984
M.D., University of Illinois, Chicago, Ill., 1988
physician

elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

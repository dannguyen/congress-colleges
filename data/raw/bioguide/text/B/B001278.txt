BONAMICI, Suzanne, a Representative from Oregon
born in Detroit, Wayne County, Mich., October 14, 1954
A.A., Lane Community College, Eugene, Oreg. 1978
B.A., University of Oregon, Eugene, Oreg., 1980
J.D., University of Oregon, Eugene, Oreg., 1983
lawyer, Federal Trade Commission, Washington, D.C.
lawyer, private practice
staff, Oregon state house of representatives, 2001-2006
member of Oregon state house of representatives 2007-2008
member of Oregon state senate, 2008-2011
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative David Wu, and reelected to the two succeeding Congresses (January 31, 2012-present).

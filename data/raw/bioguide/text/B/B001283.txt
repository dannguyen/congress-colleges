BRIDENSTINE, Jim, a Representative from Oklahoma
born in Ann Arbor, Washtenaw County, Mich., June 15, 1975
graduated from Jenks High School, Tulsa, Okla., 1993
B.A., Rice University, Houston, Texas, 1998
M.B.A., Cornell University, Ithaca, N.Y., 2009
United States Navy, 1998-2007
United States Navy Reserve, 2010-present
defense consultant
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

BUSTOS, Cheri, a Representative from Illinois
born in Springfield, Sangamon County, Ill., October 17, 1961
graduated from Springfield High School, Springfield, Ill., 1979
attended Illinois College, Jacksonville, Ill.
B.A., University of Maryland, College Park, Md., 1983
M.A., University of Illinois, Springfield, Ill., 1985
journalist
businesswoman
member of the East Moline, Ill., city council, 2007-2011
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

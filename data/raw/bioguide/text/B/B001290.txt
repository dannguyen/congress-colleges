BRAT, David A., a Representative from Virginia
born in Detroit, Wayne County, Mich., July 27, 1964
graduated from Park Center Senior High School, Brooklyn, Minn., 1982
B.A., Hope College, Holland, Mich., 1986
M.Div., Princeton Theological Seminary, Princeton, N.J., 1990
Ph.D., American University, Washington, D.C., 1995
accountant
professor, Randolph-Macon College, Ashland, Va., 1996-present
elected simultaneously as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth and One Hundred Fourteenth Congresses, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Eric Cantor (November 4, 2014-present).

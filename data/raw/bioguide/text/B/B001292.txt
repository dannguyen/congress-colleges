BEYER, Donald Sternoff Jr., a Representative from Virginia
born in Trieste, Italy, June 20, 1950
graduated from Gonzaga College High School, Washington, D.C., 1968
B.A., Williams College, Williamstown, Mass., 1972
automobile dealer
lieutenant governor of Virginia, 1990-1998
unsuccessful candidate for Governor of Virginia in 1997
United States Ambassador to Switzerland and Liechtenstein, 2009-2013
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

BLUM, Rod, a Representative from Iowa
born in Dubuque, Dubuque County, Iowa, April 26, 1955
graduated from Dubuque Senior High School, Dubuque, Iowa, 1973
B.A., Loras College, Dubuque, Iowa, 1977
M.B.A., University of Dubuque, Dubuque, Iowa, 1989
business executive
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 2011
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

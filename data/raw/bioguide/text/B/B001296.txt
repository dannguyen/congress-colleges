BOYLE, Brendan Francis, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Philadelphia, Philadelphia County, Pa., on February 6, 1977
graduated from Cardinal Dougherty High School, Philadelphia, Pa., 1995
B.A., University of Notre Dame, South Bend, Ind., 1999
M.A., Harvard University School of Public Policy, Cambridge, Mass., 2005
radio broadcaster
management consultant
adjunct professor, Drexel University, Philadelphia, Pa.
member of the Pennsylvania state house of representatives, 2008-2014
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

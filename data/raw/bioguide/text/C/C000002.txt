CABELL, Earle, a Representative from Texas
born on a farm, south of Trinity River in Dallas County, Tex., October 27, 1906
graduated from North Dallas High School in 1925
attended Texas A. & M. and Southern Methodist University
in 1932 with two brothers organized CabellÂ’s, Inc. (dairies and convenience stores) and became president and chairman of the board
engaged in banking and investments
elected mayor of Dallas May 1961 and reelected in 1963, serving until his resignation February 3, 1964, to be a candidate for Congress
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-ninth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1965-January 3, 1973)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1972 to the Ninety-third Congress
retired and returned to Dallas where he died September 24, 1975
interment in Restland Memorial Park.

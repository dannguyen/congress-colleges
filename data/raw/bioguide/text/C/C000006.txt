CABLE, Benjamin Taylor, a Representative from Illinois
born in Georgetown, Scott County, Ky., August 11, 1853
moved with his parents to Rock Island, Ill., in September 1856
attended the public schools and Racine College, Racine, Wis.
was graduated from the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor in 1876
engaged in agricultural pursuits and also became interested in various manufacturing enterprises
chairman of the western branch of the Democratic National Committee in 1892
chairman of the Democratic executive committee in 1902
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1904
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-second Congress (March 4, 1891-March 3, 1893)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1892
engaged in agricultural pursuits as joint owner of a ranch near San Antonio, Tex.
died in Rock Island, Ill., on December 13, 1923
interment in Chippiannock Cemetery.

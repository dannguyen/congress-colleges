CALDWELL, Greene Washington, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Belmont, Gaston County, N.C., April 13, 1806
pursued academic studies
was graduated from the medical department of the University of Pennsylvania at Philadelphia in 1831 and practiced
assistant surgeon in the United States Army 1832
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced in Charlotte, N.C.
member of the State house of commons 1836-1841
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1843)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1842
appointed superintendent of the United States Mint at Charlotte in 1844
participated in the war with Mexico as captain of Infantry
commissioned captain of the Third Dragoons April 9, 1847, and was mustered out July 20, 1848
member of the State senate in 1849
unsuccessful candidate for election in 1850 to the Thirty-second Congress
resumed the practice of medicine
died in Charlotte, N.C., July 10, 1864
interment in the Old Cemetery.

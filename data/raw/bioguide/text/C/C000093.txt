CAMPBELL, John Hull, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in York, York County, Pa., October 10, 1800
studied law
was admitted to the bar in Philadelphia, Pa., in 1823 and commenced practice in that city
member of the State house of representatives in 1831
elected as a candidate of the American Party to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1846
resumed the practice of law
died in Philadelphia, Pa., on January 19, 1868
interment in Monument Cemetery.

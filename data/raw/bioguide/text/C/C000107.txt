CANADY, Charles Terrance, a Representative from Florida
born in Lakeland, Polk County, Fla., June 22, 1954
B.A., Haverford College, 1976
J.D., Yale University School of Law, 1979
admitted to the bar in 1979 and commenced the practice of law in Lakeland
served as counsel to the Central Florida Regional Planning Council, 1983-1984
member, Florida house of representatives, 1984-1990
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Third and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2001)
one of the managers appointed by the House of Representatives in 1998 to conduct the impeachment proceedings of President William Jefferson Clinton
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Seventh Congress.

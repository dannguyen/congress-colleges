CANNON, Arthur Patrick (Pat), a Representative from Florida
born in Powder Springs, Cobb County, Ga., May 22, 1904
moved to Laurens County, S.C.
attended the public schools, Wofford College, Spartanburg, S.C., and John B. Stetson University, De Land, Fla.
was graduated from the law college of the University of Miami, Miami, Fla., in 1931
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Miami
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-sixth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1939-January 3, 1947)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1946
resumed the practice of law
elected circuit judge of Dade County, Fla., in 1952, reelected in 1954, and again in 1960 for a six-year term
was a resident of Miami, Fla., until his death there on January 23, 1966
interment in Woodlawn Park Cemetery, Miami, Fla.

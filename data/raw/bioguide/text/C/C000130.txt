CAPERTON, Allen Taylor, (son of Hugh Caperton), a Senator from West Virginia
born near Union, Monroe County, Va. (now West Virginia), November 21, 1810
attended the public schools of Virginia and Huntsville, Ala., and the University of Virginia at Charlottesville
graduated from Yale College in 1832
studied law in Staunton, Va.
admitted to the bar and practiced
member, Virginia house of delegates 1841-1842
member, State senate 1844-1848
delegate to the State constitutional conventions in 1850 and 1861
member, State house of delegates 1857-1861
elected by the legislature of Virginia a member of the Confederate States Senate and served until 1865
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate from West Virginia and served from March 4, 1875, until his death in Washington, D.C., July 26, 1876
interment in Green Hill Cemetery, Union, W.Va.

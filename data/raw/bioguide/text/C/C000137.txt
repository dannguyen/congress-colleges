CAPUTO, Bruce Faulkner, a Representative from New York
born in New York City, August 7, 1943
graduated from Deerfield (Mass.) Academy, 1961
B.A., 1965, M.B.A., 1967, Harvard University
J.D., Georgetown Law School, Washington, D.C., 1971
employed in Office of the Secretary, United States Department of Defense, 1967-1969
member, New York state assembly, 1973-1976
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-fifth Congress (January 3, 1977-January 3, 1979)
was not a candidate for reelection to the Ninety-sixth Congress in 1978 but was an unsuccessful candidate for the Republican nomination for lieutenant governor of New York
resumed the practice of law in New York City
is a resident of Bronxville, N.Y.

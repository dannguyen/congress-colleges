CARLETON, Peter, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Haverhill, Mass., September 19, 1755
attended the public schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
served in a Massachusetts regiment during the Revolutionary War
moved to Landaff, Grafton County, N.H., about 1789
member of the State constitutional convention in 1790
member of the State house of representatives in 1803 and 1804
served in the State senate in 1806 and 1807
elected as a Republican to the Tenth Congress (March 4, 1807-March 3, 1809)
died in Landaff, N.H., on April 29, 1828
interment in the City Cemetery.

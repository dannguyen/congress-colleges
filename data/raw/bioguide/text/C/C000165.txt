CARNEY, William, a Representative from New York
born in Brooklyn, Kings County, N.Y., July 1, 1942
received preliminary education at St. Catherine of Genoa, Brooklyn, N.Y.
graduated from Delahanty High School, Queens, N.Y., 1960
attended Florida State University, Tallahassee, Fla., 1960-1961
United States Army Medical Corps, 1961-1964
sales representative for heavy equipment firm, 1972-1976
member, Suffolk County, N.Y., legislature, 1976-1979
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-sixth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1987)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1986
private advocate
is a resident of Washington, D.C.

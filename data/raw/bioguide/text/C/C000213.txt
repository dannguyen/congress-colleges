CARY, George Booth, a Representative from Virginia
born at Bonny Doon, near Courtland, Southampton County, Va., in 1811
received a liberal education
engaged in planting
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1843)
resumed agricultural pursuits
died in Bethlehem, Va., March 5, 1850
interment in the family cemetery on his estate, Bonny Doon, near Courtland, Southampton County, Va.

CASSEDY, George, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Hackensack, Bergen County, N.J., September 16, 1783
attended the common schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1809 and commenced practice in Hackensack
postmaster of Hackensack from June 10, 1805, to January 1, 1806
elected as a Republican to the Seventeenth Congress, reelected as a Jackson Republican to the Eighteenth Congress, and reelected as a Jacksonian to the Nineteenth Congress (March 4, 1821-March 3, 1827)
died in Hackensack, December 31, 1842
interment in the cemetery of the First Reformed Church.

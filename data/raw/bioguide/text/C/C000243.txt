CASTLE, Michael Newbold, a Representative from Delaware
born in Wilmington, New Castle County, Del., July 2, 1939
graduate of Tower Hill School, Wilmington, Del., 1957
B.S., Hamilton College, Clinton, N.Y., 1961
LL.B., Georgetown University School of Law, Washington, D.C., 1964
lawyer, private practice
deputy attorney general of Delaware, 1965-1966
member of the Delaware state house of representatives, 1966-1967
member of the Delaware state senate, 1968-1976
Lieutenant Governor of Delaware, 1981-1985
Governor of Delaware, 1985-1992
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Third and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2011)
not a candidate for relection in 2010, but was an unsuccessful nominee to the United States Senate.

CATLIN, George Smith, a Representative from Connecticut
born in Harwinton, Conn., August 24, 1808
attended the common schools, Amherst (Mass.) College, and the Litchfield (Conn.) Law School
was admitted to the bar in 1828 and practiced in Windham, Conn., 1829-1851
member of the State house of representatives in 1831 and again in 1846
secretary to the Governor 1831-1833
prosecuting attorney for Windham County in 1842 and 1843
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1845)
unsuccessful Democratic candidate for Governor of Connecticut in 1848
served in the State senate in 1850
judge of the Windham County Court in 1850 and 1851
died in Windham, Conn., December 26, 1851
interment in Windham Cemetery.

CAUSEY, John Williams, a Representative from Delaware
born in Milford, Kent County, Del., September 19, 1841
attended a private school and Albany Academy, New York, and was graduated from the Pennsylvania Agricultural College
engaged in agricultural pursuits
member of the State senate 1875-1877
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1884
appointed internal-revenue collector for Delaware by President Cleveland in 1885 and served until 1887
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-second and Fifty-third Congresses (March 4, 1891-March 3, 1895)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1894
resumed agricultural pursuits
president of an insurance company
died in Milford, Del., October 1, 1908
interment in Odd Fellows Cemetery.

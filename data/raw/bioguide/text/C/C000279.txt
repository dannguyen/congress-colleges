CHAMBERLAIN, John Curtis, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Worcester, Mass., June 5, 1772
was graduated from Harvard College in 1793
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1796 and commenced practice in Alstead, Cheshire County, N.H.
member of the State house of representatives 1802-1804
moved to Charlestown, N.H., in 1804
elected as a Federalist to the Eleventh Congress (March 4, 1809-March 3, 1811)
resumed the practice of law
again a member of the State house of representatives in 1818
moved to Honeoye Falls, Monroe County, N.Y., in 1826, and thence to Utica, N.Y., where he died December 8, 1834
interment in Mount Albion Cemetery, Albion, Orleans County, N.Y.

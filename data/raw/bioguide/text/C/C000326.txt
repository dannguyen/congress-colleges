CHASE, George William, a Representative from New York
born in the town of Maryland, Otsego County, N.Y., birth date unknown
attended the common schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
also engaged in mercantile and milling pursuits at Schenevus, Otsego County, N.Y.
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
resumed his former agricultural and business pursuits
died in Chaseville, Maryland Township, N.Y., April 17, 1867
interment in the Chase vault in Schenevus Cemetery, Schenevus, N.Y.

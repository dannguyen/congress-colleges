CHRYSLER, Dick, a Representative from Michigan
born in St. Paul, Minn., April 29, 1942
graduated Brighton High School
vice-president, Hurst Performance
founder and president Cars and Concepts and RCI
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth Congress (January 3, 1995-January 3, 1997)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress.

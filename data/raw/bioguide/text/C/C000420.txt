CLARK, Ambrose Williams, a Representative from New York
born near Cooperstown, N.Y., on February 19, 1810
attended the public schools
publisher of the Otsego Journal 1831-1836, of the Northern Journal in Lewis County 1836-1844, and of the Northern New York Journal at Watertown 1844-1860
surrogate for five years
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-seventh and Thirty-eighth Congresses (March 4, 1861-March 3, 1865)
appointed consul at Valparaiso by President Lincoln and served from 1865 to 1869
acted as Chargé dAffaires in Chile in the absence of the Minister in 1869
died in Watertown, N.Y., October 13, 1887
interment in Brookside Cemetery.

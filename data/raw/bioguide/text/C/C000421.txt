CLARK, Amos, Jr., a Representative from New Jersey
born in Brooklyn, N.Y., November 8, 1828
engaged in business in New York City, with residence in Elizabeth, where he was largely interested in real estate
member of the city council of Elizabeth in 1865 and 1866
served in the State senate 1866-1869
elected as a Republican to the Forty-third Congress (March 4, 1873-March 3, 1875)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1874 to the Forty-fourth Congress
retired to his residence in Norfolk County, Mass., but retained business interests in Elizabeth, N.J.
died in Boston, Mass., October 31, 1912
interment in Evergreen Cemetery, Elizabeth, N.J.

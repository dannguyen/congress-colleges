CLARK, Franklin, a Representative from Maine
born in Wiscasset, Lincoln County, Maine, August 2, 1801
attended the common schools
engaged in the lumber and shipping business in Wiscasset
member of the State senate in 1847
elected as a Democrat to the Thirtieth Congress (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1849)
engaged in the manufacture of lumber
an executive councilor in 1855
died in Brooklyn, N.Y., on August 24, 1874
interment in Greenwood Cemetery.

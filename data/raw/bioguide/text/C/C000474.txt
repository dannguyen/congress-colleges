CLASSON, David Guy, a Representative from Wisconsin
born in Oconto, Oconto County, Wis., September 27, 1870
attended the public schools, and was graduated from the law department of the University of Wisconsin at Madison in 1891
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Oconto, Wis.
judge of Oconto County 1894-1898
mayor of Oconto 1898-1900
city attorney 1900-1906
president of the board of education in 1912 and 1913
president of the board of fire and police commissioners in 1915 and 1916
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-fifth, Sixty-sixth, and Sixty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1917-March 3, 1923)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1922
resumed the practice of law in Oconto, Wis.
served as circuit judge of the twentieth judicial circuit 1928-1930
died in Oconto, Wis., September 6, 1930
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

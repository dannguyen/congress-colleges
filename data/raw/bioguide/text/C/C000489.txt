CLAYPOOL, Harold Kile, (son of Horatio Clifford Claypool and cousin of John Barney Peterson), a Representative from Ohio
born in Bainbridge, Ross County, Ohio, June 2, 1886
attended the public schools and Ohio State University at Columbus
engaged in the publishing business at Columbus, Ohio, and published Hunter and Trader Magazine
deputy probate judge of Ross County, Ohio
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-fifth, Seventy-sixth, and Seventy-seventh Congresses (January 3, 1937-January 3, 1943)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1942 to the Seventy-eighth Congress
resumed the publishing and office supply business
United States marshal for the southern district of Ohio 1944-1953
died in Chillicothe, Ohio, August 2, 1958
interment in Grandview Cemetery.

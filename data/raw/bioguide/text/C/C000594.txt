COGHLAN, John Maxwell, a Representative from California
born in Louisville, Ky., December 8, 1835
moved with his parents to Illinois in 1847, and in 1850 they moved to California and settled in Suisun City
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced in Suisun City
member of the State assembly in 1865 and 1866
elected as a Republican to the Forty-second Congress (March 4, 1871-March 3, 1873)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1872 to the Forty-third Congress
engaged in the practice of law until his death in Oakland, Calif., March 26, 1879
interment in Mountain View Cemetery.

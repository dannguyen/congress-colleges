COLERICK, Walpole Gillespie, a Representative from Indiana
born in Fort Wayne, Ind., August 1, 1845
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1872 and commenced practice at Fort Wayne, Ind.
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-sixth and Forty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1879-March 3, 1883)
supreme court commissioner from 1883 to 1885
again engaged in the practice of law at Fort Wayne, Ind., until his death there on January 11, 1911
interment in Lindenwood Cemetery.

COLLIN, John Francis, a Representative from New York
born in Hillsdale, N.Y., April 30, 1802
attended the common schools and Lenox Academy, Massachusetts
engaged in agricultural pursuits
member of the State assembly in 1834
supervisor of Hillsdale
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Department of the Navy (Twenty-ninth Congress)
resumed agricultural pursuits
died in Hillsdale, Columbia County, N.Y., September 16, 1889
interment in Hillsdale Rural Cemetery.

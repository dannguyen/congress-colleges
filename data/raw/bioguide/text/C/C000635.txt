COLLINS, Ela, (father of William Collins), a Representative from New York
born in Meriden, Conn., February 14, 1786
attended Clinton Academy
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Lowville, N.Y., in 1807
member of the State assembly in 1815
district attorney for Lewis, Jefferson, and St. Lawrence Counties 1815-1818, and for Lewis County from June 11, 1818, to March 24, 1840
delegate to the State constitutional convention in 1821
elected as one of the Crawford Republicans to the Eighteenth Congress (March 4, 1823-March 3, 1825)
resumed the practice of law
died in Lowville, Lewis County, N.Y., November 23, 1848
interment in Jackson Street Cemetery.

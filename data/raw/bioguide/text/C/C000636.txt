COLLINS, Francis Dolan, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Saugerties, Ulster County, N.Y., March 5, 1841
attended St. Josephs College, near Montrose, Susquehanna County
moved with his parents to Dinsmore, Lackawanna County, Pa.
attended Wyoming Seminary at Kingston, Pa.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1866 and commenced practice in Scranton, Pa.
elected district attorney of the mayors court district in 1869
served in the State senate 1872-1874
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-fourth and Forty-fifth Congresses (March 4, 1875-March 3, 1879)
resumed the practice of his profession
died in Scranton, Lackawanna County, Pa., November 21, 1891
interment in Cathedral Cemetery, Hyde Park (Scranton).

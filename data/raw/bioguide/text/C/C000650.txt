COLSTON, Edward, a Representative from Virginia
born near Winchester, Va., December 25, 1786
studied under private teachers, and was graduated from Princeton College in 1806
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
served in the War of 1812
member of the State house of delegates 1812-1814, 1816-1817, 1823-1828, and 1833-1835
high sheriff of Berkeley County 1844 and 1845
elected as a Federalist to the Fifteenth Congress (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1819)
died at Honeywood, Berkeley County, Va. (now West Virginia), April 23, 1852
interment in the family burying ground on his estate, ``Honeywood,'' near Hedgesville, Berkeley County, W.Va.

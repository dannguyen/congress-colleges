COMBS, Jesse Martin, a Representative from Texas
born in Center, Shelby County, Tex., July 7, 1889
attended the public schools
was graduated from Southwest Texas State TeachersÂ’ College in 1912
was admitted to the bar in 1918 and commenced practice in Kountze, Tex.
county judge of Hardin County, Tex., in 1919 and 1920
district judge of the seventy-fifth district 1923-1925
associate justice of the ninth court of civil appeals 1933-1943
member and president of the board of trustees of South Park Schools 1926-1940
president of the board of trustees of Lamar College 1940-1944
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-ninth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1945-January 3, 1953)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1952
returned to Beaumont, Tex., where he died August 21, 1953
interment in Magnolia Cemetery.

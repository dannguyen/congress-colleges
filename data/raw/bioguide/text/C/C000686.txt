CONNELL, Richard Edward, a Representative from New York
born in Poughkeepsie, Dutchess County, N.Y., November 6, 1857

reporter and editor on the Poughkeepsie News Press 1887-1910
police commissioner of Poughkeepsie in 1892
unsuccessful candidate for election to the Fifty-fifth Congress in 1896
unsuccessful candidate for member of the State assembly in 1898 and 1900
inheritance tax appraiser 1907-1909
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1900 and 1904
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-second Congress and served from March 4, 1911, until his death
had been nominated in 1912 as the Democratic candidate for reelection to the Sixty-third Congress
died in Poughkeepsie, N.Y., on October 30, 1912
interment in St. Peter's Cemetery.

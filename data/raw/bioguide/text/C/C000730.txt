COOKE, Edmund Francis, a Representative from New York
born in Prescott, Yavapai County, Ariz., April 13, 1885
moved with his parents to Alden, Erie County, N.Y., in 1887
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1910 and commenced practice in Alden, N.Y.
served as a member of the New York assembly 1923-1928
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-first and Seventy-second Congresses (March 4, 1929-March 3, 1933)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1932 to the Seventy-third Congress
resumed the practice of law in Buffalo, N.Y.
was a resident of Alden, N.Y., until his death there on May 13, 1967
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

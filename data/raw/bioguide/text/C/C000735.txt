COOKSEY, John, a Representative from Louisiana
born in Alexandria, Rapides Parish, La., August 20, 1941
graduated from LaSalle High School, Olla, La.
B.S., Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge, La.,1962
M.D., Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge, La., 1966
M.B.A., University of Texas, Austin, Tex.,1994
United States Air Force, 1967-1969
Louisiana Air National Guard, 1970-1971
physician
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2003)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in 2002, but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate
is a resident of Monroe, La.

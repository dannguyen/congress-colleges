COOLEY, Wes, a Representative from Oregon
born in Los Angeles, Calif., March 28, 1932
graduated from Leuzinger High School, Lawndale, Calif., 1949
B.S., University of Southern California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1958
served in the United States Army, 1952-1954
owner, Vitamin Supplements Company
rancher
member of the Oregon state senate, 1992-1995
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth Congress (January 3, 1995-January 3, 1997)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress in 1996
unsuccessful candidate for the Republican nomination to the One Hundred Sixth Congress in 1998
died on February 4, 2015, in Bend, Oreg.

COOPER, Thomas, a Representative from Delaware
born in Little Creek Hundred, Sussex County, Del., in 1764
completed preparatory studies
member of the State house of representatives 1803-1808
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1805 and practiced
served in the State senate in 1808
elected as a Federalist to the Thirteenth and Fourteenth Congresses (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1817)
resumed the practice of law in Georgetown, Del., where he died in 1829
interment in the Cooper family cemetery, near Laurel, Del.

COOPER, Thomas Buchecker, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Coopersburg, Pa., December 29, 1823
attended the public schools and Pennsylvania College at Gettysburg
was graduated from the medical department of the University of Pennsylvania at Philadelphia in 1843 and commenced practice in Coopersburg
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-seventh Congress and served from March 4, 1861, until his death in Coopersburg, Pa., on April 4, 1862
interment in Woodland Cemetery.

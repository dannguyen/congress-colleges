COOPER, William Craig, a Representative from Ohio
born in Mount Vernon, Knox County, Ohio, on December 18, 1832
attended the public schools and Mount Vernon Academy
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1852 and commenced practice in Mount Vernon, Ohio
prosecuting attorney of Knox County 1859-1863
mayor of Mount Vernon 1862-1864
member of the State house of representatives 1872-1874
judge advocate general of Ohio 1879-1884
member and president of the board of education of Mount Vernon
elected as a Republican to the Forty-ninth, Fiftieth, and Fifty-first Congresses (March 4, 1885-March 3, 1891)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1890
resumed the practice of law in Mount Vernon, Ohio, where he died on August 29, 1902
interment in Mound View Cemetery.

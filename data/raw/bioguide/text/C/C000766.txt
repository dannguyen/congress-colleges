COOPER, William Raworth, a Representative from New Jersey
born near Bridgeport, Gloucester County, N.J., February 20, 1793
attended the local schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
member of the State general assembly 1839-1841
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1839-March 3, 1841)
resumed agricultural pursuits until his death near Bridgeport, N.J., on September 22, 1856
interment in the Cooper family burying ground, near Bridgeport, N.J.

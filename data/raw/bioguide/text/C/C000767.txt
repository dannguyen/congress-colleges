COPPERSMITH, Samuel G., a Representative from Arizona
born in Johnstown, Cambria County, Pa., May 22, 1955
A.B., Harvard University, 1976
foreign service officer, United States Department of State, 1977-1979
J.D., Yale University, 1982
admitted to the bar in 1982
law clerk to Judge William C. Canby, Jr., United States Court of Appeals, Ninth Circuit, 1982-1983
assistant to Mayor Terry Goddard of Phoenix, 1984
member and chair, Phoenix Board of Adjustment, 1989-1992
director, Arizona Community Service Legal Assistance Foundation, 1986-1990
practiced business and real estate law in Phoenix
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third Congress (January 3, 1993-January 3, 1995)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1994, but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate.

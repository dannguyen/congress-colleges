CORNING, Parker, (grandson of Erastus Corning), a Representative from New York
born in Albany, N.Y., January 22, 1874
attended the public schools, the Boys Academy in Albany, and St. Pauls School, Concord, N.H.
was graduated from Yale University in 1895
engaged in the manufacture of steel and woolens
also interested in banking
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1923-January 3, 1937)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1936
resumed his former pursuits
died in Albany, N.Y., May 24, 1943
interment in the Rural Cemetery, Menands, Albany County, N.Y.

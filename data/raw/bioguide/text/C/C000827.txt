COWLES, George Washington, a Representative from New York
born in Otisco, Onondaga County, N.Y., December 6, 1823
attended the common schools, and was graduated from Hamilton College, Clinton, N.Y., in 1845
taught school until 1853
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1854 and commenced practice in Clyde, Wayne County, N.Y.
judge of the Wayne County court from January 1, 1864, to October 30, 1869
elected as a Republican to the Forty-first Congress (March 4, 1869-March 3, 1871)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1870
resumed the practice of law
again judge of Wayne County court from January 1, 1874, to January 1, 1880, and from January 1, 1886, until his death in Clyde, N.Y., January 20, 1901
interment in Maple Grove Cemetery.

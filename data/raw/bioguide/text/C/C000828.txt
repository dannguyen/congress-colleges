COWLES, Henry Booth, a Representative from New York
born in Hartford, Conn., March 18, 1798
moved with his father to Dutchess County, N.Y., in 1809
was graduated from Union College, Schenectady, N.Y., in 1816
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Putnam County
member of the State assembly 1826-1828
elected as an Anti-Jacksonian to the Twenty-first Congress (March 4, 1829-March 3, 1831)
moved to New York City in 1834 and practiced law until his death there on May 17, 1873
interment in Rhinebeck Cemetery, Rhinebeck, Dutchess County, N.Y.

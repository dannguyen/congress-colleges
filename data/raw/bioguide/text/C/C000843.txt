COXE, William, Jr., a Representative from New Jersey
born in Burlington, N.J., May 3, 1762
served as a member of the State general assembly 1796-1804, 1806-1809, and again in 1816 and 1817
served as speaker 1798-1800 and again in 1802
elected as a Federalist to the Thirteenth Congress (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1815)
author
died in Burlington, Burlington County, N.J., on February 25, 1831
interment in St. Mary's Churchyard.

COYNE, William Joseph, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Pittsburgh, Allegheny County, Pa., August 24, 1936
graduated from Central Catholic High School, Pittsburgh, Pa., 1954
B.S., Robert Morris College, Pittsburgh, Pa., 1965
United States Army, 1955-1957
accountant
member of the Pennsylvania state legislature, 1970-1972
member of the Pittsburgh, Pa., city council, 1974-1980
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-seventh and to the ten succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1981-January 3, 2003)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in 2002
died on November 3, 2013, in Pittsburgh, Pa.

CREELY, John Vaudain, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Philadelphia, Pa., November 14, 1839
received a classical education
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1862 and practiced in Philadelphia
during the Civil War served with the Union Army as an officer of Light Artillery
member of the Philadelphia city council for four years
elected as an Independent Republican to the Forty-second Congress (March 4, 1871-March 3, 1873)

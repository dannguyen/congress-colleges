CRIST, Henry, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Fredericksburg, Spotsylvania County, Va., October 20, 1764
moved with his father to Pennsylvania, where he attended the public schools
moved to Kentucky and engaged in the surveying of lands
moved to Bullitt County, Ky., in 1788 and engaged in the manufacture of salt
member of the State house of representatives in 1795 and 1806
served in the State senate 1800-1804
elected as a Republican to the Eleventh Congress (March 4, 1809-March 3, 1811)
was a Whig after the organization of that party
died near Shepherdsville, Bullitt County, Ky., August 11, 1844
interment in State Cemetery, Frankfort, Ky.

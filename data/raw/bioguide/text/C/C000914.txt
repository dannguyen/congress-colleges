CROCHERON, Henry, (brother of Jacob Crocheron), a Representative from New York
born on Staten Island, Richmond County, N.Y., December 26, 1772
attended the common schools
engaged in mercantile pursuits in Northfield
supervisor of Northfield 1808-1814
elected as a Republican to the Fourteenth Congress (March 4, 1815-March 3, 1817)
captain of militia in 1818
died in New Springville, Richmond County, N.Y., on November 8, 1819

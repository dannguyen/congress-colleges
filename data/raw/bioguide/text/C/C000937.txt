CROW, Charles Augustus, a Representative from Missouri
born on a farm near Sikeston, Scott County, Mo., March 31, 1873
attended the common schools
moved to a farm near Bernie, Stoddard County, Mo., in August 1896 and engaged in agricultural pursuits
moved to Caruthersville, Pemiscot County, in 1901 and engaged in the real estate and insurance business
postmaster of Caruthersville from May 19, 1902, to January 14, 1909
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-first Congress (March 4, 1909-March 3, 1911)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1910 to the Sixty-second Congress
moved to Campbell, Dunklin County, Mo., in 1911 and resumed agricultural pursuits
also engaged in the real estate and insurance business
died in Campbell, Mo., March 20, 1938
interment in Woodlawn Cemetery.

CROWLEY, Miles, a Representative from Texas
born in Boston, Mass., February 22, 1859
attended the common schools
employed as a longshoreman
moved to Galveston in the seventies
assistant chief of the Galveston Fire Department
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1892 and commenced practice
member of the State house of representatives in 1892
served in the State senate in 1893 and 1894
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1895-March 3, 1897)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1896
resumed the practice of law in Galveston, Tex.
prosecuting attorney of Galveston County 1904-1912
elected judge of Galveston County Court in 1920, in which capacity he was serving at the time of his death in Galveston, Tex., on September 22, 1921
interment in Calvary Cemetery.

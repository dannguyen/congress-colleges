CULBERTSON, William Constantine, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Edinboro, Erie County, Pa., November 25, 1825
attended the common schools of his native town
engaged in lumbering on the Allegheny River in Jefferson County, Pa.
also operated a mill and a factory at Covington, Ky.
moved to Girard, Pa., in 1863
purchased extensive tracts of timberland in Michigan, Wisconsin, and other States
later became interested in agricultural pursuits in Minnesota and in his native county
president of the CitizensÂ’ National Bank of Corry, Pa.
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-first Congress (March 4, 1889-March 3, 1891)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1890
resumed his former business activities
died in Girard, Erie County, Pa., on May 24, 1906
interment in Girard Cemetery.

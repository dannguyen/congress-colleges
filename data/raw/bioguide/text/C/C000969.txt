CULLEN, Elisha Dickerson, a Representative from Delaware
born in Millsboro, Sussex County, Del., April 23, 1799
attended Princeton College
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1821 and commenced practice in Georgetown, Del
elected as the candidate of the American Party to the Thirty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1855-March 3, 1857)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1856 to the Thirty-fifth Congress
resumed the practice of law
died in Georgetown, Del., on February 8, 1862
interment in the Presbyterian Churchyard, Lewes, Del.

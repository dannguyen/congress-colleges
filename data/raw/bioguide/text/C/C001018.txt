CUSHMAN, Francis Wellington, a Representative from Washington
born in Brighton, Washington County, Iowa, May 8, 1867
attended the public schools in Brighton and Pleasant Plain Academy in Pleasant Plain, Jefferson County, Iowa
moved to Albany County, Wyo., in 1885
employed as a ranch hand and as a teacher
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1889 and commenced practice in Bassett, Rock County, Nebr.
moved to Tacoma, Wash., in 1891 and continued the practice of law
member of Troop B, First Cavalry, Washington National Guard, 1896-1903
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-sixth and to the five succeeding Congresses and served from March 4, 1899, until his death in New York City July 6, 1909
the remains were cremated and the ashes interred in Tacoma Cemetery, Tacoma, Wash.

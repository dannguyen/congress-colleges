CLELAND, Joseph Maxwell (Max), a Senator from Georgia
born in Atlanta, Ga., August 24, 1942
grew up in Lithonia and graduated from Lithonia High School 1960
graduated from Stetson University, DeLand, Fla. 1964
received a MasterÂ’s degree from Emory University 1968
served in the U.S. Army, attaining the rank of captain 1965-1968
wounded in combat in Vietnam
member of the Georgia state senate 1971-1975
unsuccessful candidate for lieutenant governor 1974
consultant to Senate Committee on Veterans Affairs 1975, professional Senate staff member 1975-1977
appointed administrator of United States Veterans Administration by President Jimmy Carter in 1977, and served until 1981
Georgia Secretary of State 1982-1996
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 1996, and served from January 3, 1997 to January 3, 2003
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 2002.

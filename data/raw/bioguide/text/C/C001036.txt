CAPPS, Lois, (wife of Walter Capps), a Representative from California
born in Ladysmith, Rusk County, Wis., January 10, 1938
graduated from Flathead County High School, Kalispell, Mont., 1955
B.S., Pacific Lutheran University, Tacoma, Wash., 1959
M.A., Yale University, New Haven, Conn., 1964
M.A., University of California, Santa Barbara, Calif., 1990
nursing instructor
nurse administrator, Yale Hospital, New Haven, Conn.
director, Teenage Pregnancy and Parenting Project and the Parent and Child Enrichment Center, Santa Barbara County, Calif.
instructor, Santa Barbara City College, Santa Barbara, Calif.
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of her husband, United States Representative Walter Capps, and reelected to the nine succeeding Congresses (March 10, 1998-present).

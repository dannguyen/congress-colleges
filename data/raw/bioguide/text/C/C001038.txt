CROWLEY, Joseph, a Representative from New York
born in New York, New York County, N.Y., March 16, 1962
graduated from Power Memorial High School, New York, N.Y., 1981
B.A., Queens College, City University of New York, Flushing, N.Y., 1985
member of the New York state assembly, 1986-1998
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Sixth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1999-present).

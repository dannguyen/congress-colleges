CARSON, Brad, a Representative from Oklahoma
born in Winslow, Navajo County, Ariz., March 11, 1967
B.A., Baylor University, Waco, Tex.
Rhodes Scholar, M.A., Oxford University, Oxford, England
J.D., University of Oklahoma, Norman, Okla, 1994.
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2005)
not a candidate for reelection in 2004, but was an unsuccessful candidate to the United States Senate in 2004.

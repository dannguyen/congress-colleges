CRENSHAW, Ander, a Representative from Florida
born in Jacksonville, Duval County, Fla., September 1, 1944
graduated from Robert E. Lee High School, Jacksonville, Fla., 1962
B.A., University of Georgia, Athens, Ga., 1966
J.D., University of Florida, Gainesville, Fla., 1969
member of the Florida state house of representatives, 1972-1978
member of the Florida state senate, 1986-1994
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present).

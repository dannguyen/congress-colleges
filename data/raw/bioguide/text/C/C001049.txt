CLAY, William Lacy, Jr., (son of William Lacy Clay, Sr.), a Representative from Missouri
born in St. Louis, Mo., July 27, 1956
graduated from Springbrook High School, Silver Spring, Md., 1974
B.A., University of Maryland, College Park, Md., 1983
member of the Missouri state house of representatives, 1983-1991
member of the Missouri state senate, 1991-2001
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present).

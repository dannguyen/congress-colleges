CARDOZA, Dennis A., a Representative from California
born in Merced, Merced County, Calif., March 31, 1959
B.A., University of Maryland, College Park, Md., 1982
businessman
Atwater, Calif., city council, 1984-1987
member of the California general assembly, 1996-2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth and to the four succeeding Congresses until his resignation on August 15, 2012 (January 3, 2003-August 15, 2012).

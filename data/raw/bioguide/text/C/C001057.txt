COLEMAN, Norm, a Senator from Minnesota
born in Brooklyn, New York, on August 17, 1949
B.A., Hofstra University
J.D., University of Iowa 1976
attorney
chief prosecutor for Minnesota state attorney general
Minnesota state solicitor general
mayor of St. Paul, Minnesota 1993-1998
elected as a Republican to the U.S. Senate in 2002, and served from January 3, 2003, to January 3, 2009
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 2008, having failed in his bid to challenge the results of the election.

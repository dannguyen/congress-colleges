COSTA, Jim, a Representative from California
born in Fresno, Fresno County, Calif., April 13, 1952
B.A., California State University, Fresno, Calif., 1974
member of the California state assembly, 1978-1994
member of the California state senate, 1994-2002
professional advocate
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Ninth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2005-present).

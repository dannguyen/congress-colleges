CAMPBELL, John (of California), a Representative from California
born in Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, Calif., July 19, 1955
graduated from the Harvard School, North Hollywood, Calif., 1973
B.A., University of California, Los Angeles, Calif.
M.B.T., University of Southern California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1977
certified public accountant
auto dealer
member of the California state assembly, 2000-2004
member of the California state senate, 2004-2005
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Ninth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Christopher Cox, and reelected to the four succeeding Congresses (December 6, 2005-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

CARNEY, Chris, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Cedar Rapids, Linn County, Iowa, March 2, 1959
B.S.S., Cornell College, Mount Vernon, Iowa, 1981
M.A., University of Wyoming, Laramie, Wyo., 1983
Ph.D., University of Nebraska, Lincoln, Nebr.,1993
United States Navy Reserve, 1995 to present
professor
intelligence analyst
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

CASTOR, Kathy, a Representative from Florida
born in Miami, Miami-Dade County, Fla., August 20, 1966
B.A., Emory University, Atlanta, Ga., 1988
J.D., Florida State University, Tallahassee, Fla., 1991
lawyer, private practice
member of the Hillsborough County (Fla.) board of commissioners, 2002 to 2006
unsuccessful candidate for election to the Florida state senate in 2000
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2007-present).

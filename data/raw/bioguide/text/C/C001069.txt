COURTNEY, Joe, a Representative from Connecticut
born in West Hartford, Tolland County, Conn., April 6, 1953
B.A., Tufts University, Medford, Mass, 1975
J.D., University of Connecticut, Storrs, Conn., 1978
lawyer, private practice
member of the Connecticut state house of representatives, 1987-1994
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2007-present).

CHAFFETZ, Jason, a Representative from Utah
born in Los Gatos, Santa Clara County, Calif., March 26, 1967
B.A., Brigham Young University, Provo, Utah, 1989
business executive
staff, Utah Governor John Huntsman, 2004
member of the Utah Valley board of trustees, 2007-2008
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2009-present)
chair, Committee on Oversight and Government Reform (One Hundred Fourteenth Congress).

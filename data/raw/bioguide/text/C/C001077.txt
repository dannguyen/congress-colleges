COFFMAN, Mike, a Representative from Colorado
born in Fort Leonard Wood, Pulaski County, Mo., March 19, 1955
attended Aurora Central High School, Aurora, Colo.
received graduation equivalency degree through the United States Army
B.A., University of Colorado, Boulder, Colo., 1979
United States Army, 1972-1974
United States Army Reserve, 1975-1978
United States Marine Corps, 1979-1982
United States Marine Corps Reserve, 1983-1994, 2005-2006
small business owner
member of the Colorado state house of representatives, 1989-1994
member of the Colorado state senate, 1994-1998
Colorado state treasurer, 1999-2007
Colorado secretary of state, 2007-2008
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2009-present).

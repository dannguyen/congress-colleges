CRITZ, Mark S., a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Irwin, Westmoreland County, Pa.
January 5, 1962
B. S., Indiana University of Pennsylvania, Indiana, Pa., 1987
business manager
staff, United States Representative John Patrick Murtha, Jr., 1998-2010
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress, by special election to fill the vacancy caused by the death of United States Representative John Patrick Murtha, Jr. and reelected to the succeeding Congress (May 18, 2010-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

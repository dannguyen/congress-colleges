CRAVAACK, Chip, a Representative from Minnesota
born in Charleston, Kanawha County, W.Va., December 29, 1959
graduated from St. Xavier High School, Finneytown, Ohio, 1977
B.S., United States Naval Academy, Annapolis, Md., 1981
M.Ed., University of West Florida, Pensacola, Fla., 1989
United States Navy, 1981-1990
United States Navy Reserve, 1990-2005
pilot
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

CURSON, David Alan, a Representative from Michigan
born in Toledo, Lucas County, Ohio, November 4, 1948
attended University of Toledo, Toledo, Ohio
United States Marine Corps
auto worker
union representative
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Thaddeus George McCotter (November 6, 2012-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for election to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

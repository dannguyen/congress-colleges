CARTWRIGHT, Matt, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Erie, Erie County, Pa., May 1, 1961
A.B., Hamilton College, Clinton, N.Y., 1983
attended Temple University School of Law, Philadelphia, Pa., 1984
J.D., University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, Pa., 1986
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

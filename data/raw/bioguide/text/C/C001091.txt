CASTRO, Joaquin, a Representative from Texas
born in San Antonio, Bexar County, Tex., September 16, 1974
graduated from Thomas Jefferson High School, San Antonio, Tex., 1992
B.A., Stanford University, Stanford, Calif., 1996
J.D., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 2000
lawyer, private practice
member of the Texas state house of representatives, 2002-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

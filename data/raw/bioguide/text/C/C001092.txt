COLLINS, Chris, a Representative from New York
born in Schenectady, Schenectady County, N.Y., May 20, 1950
B.S., North Carolina State University, Raleigh, N.C., 1972
M.B.A., University of Alabama, Birmingham, Ala., 1975
businessman
Erie County, N.Y. executive, 2007-2011
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 1998
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

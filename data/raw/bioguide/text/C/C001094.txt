COOK, Paul, a Representative from California
born in Meriden, New Haven County, Conn., March 3, 1943
B.S., Southern Connecticut State University, New Haven, Conn., 1966
M.P.A., California State University, San Bernardino, Calif., 1996
M.A., University of California, Riverside, Calif., 2000
United States Marine Corps, 1966-1992
professor
member of the Yucca Valley, Calif. town council, 1998-2006
Mayor of Yucca Valley, Calif.
member of the California state assembly, 2006-2012
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

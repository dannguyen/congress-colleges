CHIESA, Jeffrey Scott, a Senator from New Jersey
born in Bound Brook. N.J., June 22, 1965
graduated University of Notre Dame, B.A., 1987
graduated The Catholic University of America, J.D., 1990
lawyer
assistant U.S. attorney 2002-2009
chief counsel to New Jersey Governor Chris Christie 2010-2011
attorney general of New Jersey 2012-2013
appointed as a Republican to the United States Senate on June 6, 2013, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Frank Lautenberg, took the oath of office on June 10, 2013
and served until October 30, 2013, a successor having been chosen in a special election
not a candidate for election to the unexpired portion of the term..

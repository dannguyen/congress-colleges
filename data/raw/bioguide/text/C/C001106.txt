COSTELLO, Ryan, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Chester County, Pa., on September 7, 1976
graduated from Owen J. Roberts High School, Pottstown, Pa., 1995
B.A., Ursinus College, Collegeville, Pa., 1999
LL.B., Villanova University, Villanova, Pa., 2002
lawyer, private practice
member of the board of commissioners, Chester County, Pa., 2011-2013
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

DAMRELL, William Shapleigh, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Portsmouth, N.H., November 29, 1809
attended the public schools
learned the art of printing and became the proprietor of a large printing establishment in Boston
elected as the candidate of the American Party to the Thirty-fourth Congress and as a Republican to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1855-March 3, 1859)
suffered a paralytic stroke before the expiration of his term
was not a candidate for renomination in 1858
resumed business activities
died in Dedham, Norfolk County, Mass., May 17, 1860
interment in Forest Hills Cemetery.

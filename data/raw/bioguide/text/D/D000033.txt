DANIEL, Henry, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Louisa County, Va., March 15, 1786
attended the public schools
moved to Kentucky
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Mount Sterling, Montgomery County, Ky.
member of the State house of representatives in 1812
served in the War of 1812 as captain of the Eighth Regiment, United States Infantry, 1813-1815
again a member of the State house of representatives in 1819 and 1826
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twentieth, Twenty-first, and Twenty-second Congresses (March 4, 1827-March 3, 1833)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1832 to the Twenty-third Congress
resumed the practice of law
died in Mount Sterling, Ky., October 5, 1873
interment in Macphelah Cemetery.

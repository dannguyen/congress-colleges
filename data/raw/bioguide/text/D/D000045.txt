DANNER, Joel Buchanan, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Liberty, Md., in 1804
engaged in the hardware business and carriage building at Gettysburg, Pa.
justice of the peace
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-first Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Henry Nes and served from December 2, 1850, to March 3, 1851
resumed his former business pursuits in Gettysburg, Pa., where he died July 29, 1885
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

DANNER, Patsy Ann (Pat), a Representative from Missouri
born in Louisville, Ky., January 13, 1934
attended public schools in Bevier, Mo.
B.A., Northeast Missouri State University, 1972
vice chair, Ninth Congressional District Democratic Committee, northeast Missouri, 1970-1972
chair, Macon County Democratic Committee, 1970-1972
district assistant to Representative Jerry L. Litton, 1973-1976
federal co-chair, Ozarks Regional Commission, 1977-1981
member, State senate, 1983-1993
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2001)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Seventh Congress.

DARBY, Ezra, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Scotch Plains, N.J., June 7, 1768
attended the common schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
held offices as chosen freeholder, assessor, and justice of the peace from 1800 to 1804
member of the State house of assembly 1802-1804
elected as a Republican to the Ninth and Tenth Congresses and served from March 4, 1805, until his death in Washington, D.C., January 27, 1808
interment in Congressional Cemetery.

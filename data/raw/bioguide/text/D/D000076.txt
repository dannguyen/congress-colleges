DAVENPORT, John, a Representative from Ohio
born near Winchester, Jefferson County, Va., January 9, 1788
attended the common schools
moved to Ohio in 1818 and engaged in mercantile pursuits
member of the State house of representatives in 1824, 1827, and 1830
member of the State senate in 1825 and 1826
elected as an Adams candidate to the Twentieth Congress (March 4, 1827-March 3, 1829)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1828 to the Twenty-first Congress
twice elected by the legislature as judge of the Monroe judicial circuit
died in Woodsfield, Monroe County, Ohio, July 18, 1855
interment in Green Mount Cemetery, Barnesville, Ohio.

DAVIDSON, Alexander Caldwell, a Representative from Alabama
born near Charlotte, Mecklenburg County, N.C., December 26, 1826
attended the public schools of Marengo County, Ala., and was graduated from the University of Alabama at Tuscaloosa July 11, 1848
studied law in Mobile, Ala., but never practiced
engaged in cotton planting near Uniontown, Perry County, Ala.
member of the State house of representatives in 1880 and 1881
served in the State senate 1882-1885
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-ninth and Fiftieth Congresses (March 4, 1885-March 3, 1889)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1888
resumed agricultural pursuits
died at Westwood, near Uniontown, Ala., November 6, 1897
interment in the Holy Cross Cemetery of Davidson Memorial Church, Uniontown, Perry County, Ala.

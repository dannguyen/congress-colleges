DAVIES, Edward, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Churchtown, Caernarvon Township, Lancaster County, Pa., in November 1779
attended the rural schools
engaged in agricultural and mercantile pursuits
member of the Pennsylvania state house of representatives, 1834-1835
elected as an Anti-Masonic candidate to the Twenty-fifth and to the succeeding Congress (March 4, 1837-March 3, 1841)
resumed his former business activities
died on May 17, 1853, in Churchtown, Pa.
interment in Bangor Episcopal Churchyard, Churchtown, Pa.

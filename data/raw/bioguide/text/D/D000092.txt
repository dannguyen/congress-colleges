DAVIS, Amos, (brother of Garrett Davis), a Representative from Kentucky
born in Mount Sterling, Ky., August 15, 1794
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Mount Sterling
was sheriff of Montgomery County, Ky.
member of the State house of representatives in 1819, 1825, 1827, and 1828
unsuccessful candidate for election to the Twentieth and Twenty-second Congresses
elected as an Anti-Jacksonian to the Twenty-third Congress (March 4, 1833-March 3, 1835)
was a candidate for reelection, but died in Owingsville, Ky., while campaigning, June 11, 1835
interment in the City Cemetery, Mount Sterling, Ky.

DAVIS, Jim, a Representative from Florida
born in Tampa, Hillsborough County, Fla., October 11, 1957
graduated from Jesuit High School, Tampa, Fla.
B.A., Washington and Lee University, Lexington, Va., 1979
J.D., University of Florida, Gainesville, Fla., 1982
lawyer, private practice
member of the Florida state house of representatives, 1988-1996, majority leader, 1994-1996
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2007)
not a candidate for reelection, but was an unsuccessful candidate for Governor of Florida in 2006.

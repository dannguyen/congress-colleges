DAVIS, Roger, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Charlestown Village, Chester County, Pa., October 2, 1762
studied medicine at the University of Pennsylvania and commenced practice about 1785 in Charlestown
member of the State house of representatives 1809-1811
elected as a Republican to the Twelfth and Thirteenth Congresses (March 4, 1811-March 3, 1815)
resumed the practice of medicine in Charlestown, where he died November 20, 1815
interment in Great Valley Presbyterian Churchyard.

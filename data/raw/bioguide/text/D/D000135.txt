DAVIS, Thomas, a Representative from Rhode Island
born in Dublin, Ireland, December 18, 1806
attended private schools
immigrated to the United States and located in Providence, R.I., in 1817
engaged in manufacturing jewelry
member of the State senate 1845-1853
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1854 to the Thirty-fourth Congress
resumed his former manufacturing pursuits
unsuccessful candidate for election to the Thirty-sixth, Forty-second, Forty-third, and Forty-sixth Congresses
again served in the State senate in 1877 and 1878
member of the State house of representatives 1887-1890
member of the Providence school committee
died in Providence, R.I., July 26, 1895
interment in Swan Point Cemetery.

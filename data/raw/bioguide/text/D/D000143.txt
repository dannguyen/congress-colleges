DAVIS, William Morris, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Keene Valley, Essex County, N.Y., August 16, 1815
moved to Pennsylvania and became a sugar refiner in Philadelphia
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1861-March 3, 1863)
died in Keene Valley, N.Y., August 5, 1891
interment in Friends Fair Hill Burial Ground, Germantown, Philadelphia, Pa.

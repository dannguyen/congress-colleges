DAWSON, William, a Representative from Missouri
born in New Madrid, New Madrid County, Mo., March 17, 1848
was graduated from Christian BrothersÂ’ College, St. Louis, Mo., in 1869
elected sheriff and collector of New Madrid County in 1870 and 1872
served as a member of the State house of representatives 1878-1884
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1885-March 3, 1887)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1886
engaged in the land business in New Madrid, Mo.
served as clerk of the circuit court of New Madrid County 1915-1927
died in New Madrid, Mo., October 12, 1929
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

DeCONCINI, Dennis Webster, a Senator from Arizona
born in Tucson, Pima County, Ariz., May 8, 1937
attended the public schools of Tucson and Phoenix
graduated, University of Arizona 1959 and from that universitys law school 1963
admitted to the Arizona bar in 1963 and commenced practice in Tucson
served in the United States Army 1959-1960
Army Reserve 1960-1967
member, Arizona Governors staff 1965-1967
Pima County attorney 1973-1976
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 1976
reelected in 1982 and again in 1988 and served from January 3, 1977, to January 3, 1995
not a candidate for reelection in 1994
chairman, Select Committee on Intelligence (One Hundred Third Congress).

de la GARZA, Eligio, II (Kika), a Representative from Texas
born in Mercedes, Hidalgo County, Tex., September 22, 1927
educated at Mission (Tex.) High School, Edinburgh (Tex.) Junior College, and St. Marys University, San Antonio, Tex., LL.B., 1952
at age 17 enlisted in the United States Navy and served from 1945 to 1946
served as an officer in the United States Army, Thirty-seventh Division Artillery, 1950-1952
graduated as a second lieutenant, St. Marys ROTC, in 1951 and from the Artillery School, Fort Sill, Okla., in 1952
was admitted to the bar in 1952 and began practice in Mission, Tex.
member of the State house of representatives 1952-1964
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-ninth and to the fifteen succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1965-January 3, 1997)
chairman, Committee on Agriculture (Ninety-seventh through One Hundred Third Congresses)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress.

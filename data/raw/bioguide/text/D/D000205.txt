DE LA MONTANYA, James, a Representative from New York
born in New York City March 20, 1798
resided in Haverstraw, Rockland County, N.Y.
supervisor of Haverstraw in 1832 and 1833
member of the State assembly in 1833
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1839-March 3, 1841)
died in New York City April 29, 1849
interment in the Barnes family burial ground, Stony Point, Rockland County, N.Y.

DeLAY, Thomas Dale, a Representative from Texas
born in Laredo, Webb County, Tex., April 8, 1947
graduated from Calallan High School, Corpus Christi, Tex., 1965
attended Baylor University, Waco, Tex., 1967
B.S., University of Houston, Houston, Tex., 1970
business owner
member of the Texas state house of representatives, 1979-1984
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-ninth and to the ten succeeding Congresses until his resignation on June 9, 2006 (January 3, 1985-June 9, 2006)
majority whip (One Hundred Fourth Congress to One Hundred Seventh Congress)
majority leader (One Hundred Eighth and One Hundred Ninth Congress).

DENNISON, David Short, a Representative from Ohio
born in Poland, Mahoning County, Ohio, July 29, 1918
graduated from Western Reserve Academy, Hudson, Ohio, 1936
Williams College, Williamstown, Mass., 1940
Western Reserve University Law School (now Case Western Reserve University), Cleveland, Ohio, 1945
lawyer, private practice
American Field Service, 1942-1943
special counsel for Warren, Ohio, 1950-1951
special assistant to Ohio state attorney general, 1953-1956
elected as a Republican to the Eighty-fifth Congress (January 3, 1957-January 3, 1959)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Eighty-sixth Congress in 1958 and for election to the Eighty-seventh Congress in 1960
consultant to the Civil Rights Commission, Washington, D.C., 1959
member of the Federal Trade Commission, 1970-1974
business executive
died on September 21, 2001, in Warren, Ohio.

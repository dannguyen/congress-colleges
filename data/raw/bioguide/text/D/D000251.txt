DENNY, Walter McKennon, a Representative from Mississippi
born in Moss Point, Jackson County, Miss., October 28, 1853
attended the common schools and Roanoke College, Salem, Va.
was graduated from the law department of the University of Mississippi at Oxford in 1874
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Pascagoula, Jackson County, Miss.
clerk of the circuit and chancery courts of Jackson County, Miss., from November 1883 until January 1, 1895
delegate to the State constitutional convention in 1890
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1895-March 3, 1897)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1896
joined the Republican Party in 1896
resumed the practice of law at Pascagoula, Miss., and for fifteen years was legal adviser to the Jackson County Board of Supervisors
died in Pascagoula November 5, 1926
interment in Machpelah Cemetery.

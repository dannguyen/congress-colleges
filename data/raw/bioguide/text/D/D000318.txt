DICKINSON, David W., (nephew of William Hardy Murfree), a Representative from Tennessee
born in Franklin, Tenn., June 10, 1808
completed preparatory studies and was graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-third Congress (March 4, 1833-March 3, 1835)
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1845)
was unable to attend the last session of Congress on account of his failing health
died at Grantland, his fathers home, near Murfreesboro, Rutherford County, Tenn., on April 27, 1845
interment in the family burying ground on the estate.

DICKINSON, Rodolphus, a Representative from Ohio
born in Hatfield, Mass., December 28, 1797
attended the public schools and Williams College, Williamstown, Mass., 1818-1821
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Tiffin, Ohio
appointed prosecuting attorney for Seneca County in 1824, for Williams County in 1826, and for Sandusky County in 1827
moved to Lower Sandusky, Ohio, in 1826
served as a member of the Board of Public Works of Ohio 1836-1845
elected as a Democrat to the Thirtieth and Thirty-first Congresses and served from March 4, 1847, until his death in Washington, D.C., on March 20, 1849
interment in Washington, D.C.
reinterment in Oakwood Cemetery, Fremont, Sandusky County, Ohio.

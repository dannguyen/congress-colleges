DICKS, Norman DeValois, a Representative from Washington
born in Bremerton, Kitsap County, Wash., December 16, 1940
graduated from West Bremerton High School, Bremerton, Wash., 1959
B.A., University of Washington, Seattle, Wash., 1963
J.D., University of Washington School of Law, Seattle, Wash., 1968
lawyer, private practice
staff, United States Senator Warren G. Magnuson of Washington, 1968-1976
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-fifth and to the seventeen succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1977-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

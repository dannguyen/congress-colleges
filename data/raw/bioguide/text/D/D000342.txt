DIETZ, William, a Representative from New York
born in Schoharie, N.Y., June 28, 1778
attended the district schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
town clerk in 1804 and 1805
supervisor of Schoharie in 1812
served in the State assembly in 1814, 1815, and 1823
elected as a Jacksonian to the Nineteenth Congress (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1827)
member of the State senate 1830-1833
resumed agricultural pursuits
colonel of the militia
died in Schoharie, Schoharie County, N.Y., on August 24, 1848
interment in St. Paul's Lutheran Cemetery.

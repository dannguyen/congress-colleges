DIMOCK, Davis, Jr., a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Exeter, near Wilkes-Barre, Luzerne County, Pa., September 17, 1801
attended the schools of the pioneer settlement of Montrose, Pa., and the Susquehanna County Academy at Montrose
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1833 and commenced practice in Montrose
also engaged in editorial work
appointed county treasurer in 1834
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-seventh Congress and served from March 4, 1841, until his death in Montrose, Pa., January 13, 1842
interment in Montrose Cemetery.

DOE, Nicholas Bartlett, a Representative from New York
born in New York City on June 16, 1786
was graduated from Phillips Exeter Academy, Exeter, N.H.
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
settled in Saratoga County, N.Y.
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-sixth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Anson Brown
took his seat on December 7, 1840, and served until March 3, 1841
resumed the practice of law
trustee of the village of Waterford, Saratoga County, in 1841
died at Saratoga Springs, N.Y., December 6, 1856
interment in Greenridge Cemetery.

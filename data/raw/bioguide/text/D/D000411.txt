DONDERO, George Anthony, a Representative from Michigan
born in Greenfield Township, Wayne County, Mich., on December 16, 1883
attended the public schools
served as village clerk of Royal Oak in 1905 and 1906, as town treasurer in 1907 and 1908, and as village assessor in 1909
was graduated from the Detroit College of Law, Detroit, Mich., in 1910
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Royal Oak, Mich.
village attorney 1911-1921
assistant prosecuting attorney for Oakland County, Mich., in 1918 and 1919
mayor of Royal Oak in 1921 and 1922
member of the board of education 1910-1928
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-third and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1933-January 3, 1957)
chairman, Committee on Public Works (Eightieth and Eighty-third Congresses)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1956
resumed the practice of law
died in Royal Oak, Mich., January 29, 1968
interment in Oakview Cemetery.

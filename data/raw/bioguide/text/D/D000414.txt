DONNELL, Forrest C., a Senator from Missouri
born in Quitman, Nodaway County, Mo., August 20, 1884
attended the public schools
graduated from the University of Missouri at Columbia in 1904 and from its law school in 1907
admitted to the bar in 1907 and commenced practice in St. Louis, Mo.
city attorney of Webster Groves, Mo.
Governor of Missouri 1941-1945
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate and served from January 3, 1945, to January 3, 1951
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1950
resumed the practice of law in St. Louis, Mo., where he died March 3, 1980
interment in Bellefontaine Cemetery.

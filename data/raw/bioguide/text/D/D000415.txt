DONNELL, Richard Spaight, (grandson of Richard Dobbs Spaight), a Representative from North Carolina
born in New Bern, N.C., September 20, 1820
attended New Bern Academy and Yale College
was graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 1839
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1840 and commenced practice in New Bern, N.C.
elected as a Whig to the Thirtieth Congress (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1849)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1848
resumed the practice of law in Washington, N.C.
delegate to the State secession convention in 1861 and to the State constitutional convention in 1865
member of the State house of commons in 1862 and 1864, and served as speaker
died in New Bern, N.C., June 3, 1867
interment in Cedar Grove Cemetery.

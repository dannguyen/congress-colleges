DOWNING, Charles, a Delegate from Florida
born in Virginia, birth date unknown
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced in St. Augustine, Fla.
member of the legislative council of the Territory of Florida, 1837
elected to the Twenty-fifth and Twenty-sixth Congresses (March 4, 1837-March 3, 1841)
died in St. Augustine, Fla., in 1845.

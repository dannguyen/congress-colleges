DOWSE, Edward, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Charlestown, Mass., October 22, 1756
moved to Dedham, Mass.
after the Revolution was a shipmaster and engaged in the East Indian and China carrying trade
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth Congress and served from March 4, 1819, until May 26, 1820, when he resigned
died in Dedham, Mass., September 3, 1828
interment in the Old Cemetery.

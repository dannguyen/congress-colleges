DOYLE, Michael F., a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Swissvale, Allegheny County, Pa., August 5, 1953
graduated from Swissvale Area High School, Swissvale, Pa., 1971
B.S., Pennsylvania State University, University Park, Pa., 1975
member of the Swissvale, Pa., borough council, 1977-1981
staff, Pennsylvania state senator Frank Pecora, 1979-1994
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fourth and to the ten succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-present).

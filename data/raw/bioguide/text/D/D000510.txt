DU BOSE, Dudley McIver, a Representative from Georgia
born in Shelby County, Tenn., October 28, 1834
attended the University of Mississippi at Oxford, and was graduated from the Lebanon (Tenn.) Law School in 1856
was admitted to the bar in 1857 and commenced the practice of law in Memphis, Tenn.
moved to Augusta, Ga., in 1860
served in the Confederate Army during the Civil War as colonel of the Fifteenth Regiment, Georgia Volunteer Infantry, and subsequently became brigadier general in the Western Army
moved to Washington, Wilkes County, Ga.
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-second Congress (March 4, 1871-March 3, 1873)
resumed the practice of law
died in Washington, Ga., March 2, 1883
interment in Rest Haven Cemetery.

DUFF, James Henderson, a Senator from Pennsylvania
born in Mansfield (now Carnegie), Allegheny County, Pa., January 21, 1883
graduated from Princeton University in 1904
student at the University of Pennsylvania 1904-1906
graduated from the law school of the University of Pittsburgh in 1907
admitted to the bar the same year and commenced the practice of law in Pittsburgh, Pa.
attorney general of Pennsylvania 1943-1947
Governor of Pennsylvania 1947-1951
member of the Pennsylvania Pardon Board
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 1950 for the term commencing January 3, 1951, and served until January 3, 1957
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1956
engaged in the practice of law in Washington, D.C., until his death there December 20, 1969
interment in Chartiers Cemetery, Carnegie, Pa.

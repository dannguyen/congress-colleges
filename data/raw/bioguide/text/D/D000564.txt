DURBOROW, Allan Cathcart, Jr., a Representative from Illinois
born in Philadelphia, Pa., November 10, 1857
moved to Indiana in 1862 with his parents, who settled in Williamsport, Warren County
attended the public schools
entered Wabash College, Crawfordsville, Ind., in the fall of 1872
was graduated from Indiana University at Bloomington in 1877
after residing in Indianapolis moved to Chicago in 1880 and in 1887 became business manager of the Western Electrician, a trade magazine
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-second and Fifty-third Congresses (March 4, 1891-March 3, 1895)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1894
engaged in the insurance business
unsuccessful candidate for election in 1902 to the Fifty-eighth Congress
died in Chicago, Ill., March 10, 1908
interment in Graceland Cemetery.

DURELL, Daniel Meserve, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Lee, N.H., July 20, 1769
was graduated from Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H., in 1794
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1797 and commenced practice in Dover, N.H.
elected as a Republican to the Tenth Congress (March 4, 1807-March 3, 1809)
member of the State house of representatives in 1816
chief justice of the district court of common pleas 1816-1821
United States attorney for the district of New Hampshire 1830-1834
resumed the practice of law
died in Dover, Strafford County, N.H., April 29, 1841
interment in Pine Hill Cemetery.

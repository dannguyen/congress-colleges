DWYER, Bernard James, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Perth Amboy, Middlesex County, N.J., January 24, 1921
attended the public schools
attended Rutgers University, Newark, N.J.
United States Navy, 1940-1945
insurance broker
member, Edison (N.J.) Township Council, 1958-1969
mayor, Edison, 1969-1973
served in the New Jersey senate, 1974-1980
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1981-January 3, 1993)
was not a candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992
died on October 31, 1998
interment in St. Gertrude's Cemetery, Colonia, N.J.

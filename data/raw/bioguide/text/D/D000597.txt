DAVIS, Jo Ann, a Representative from Virginia
born in Rowan County, N.C., June 29, 1950
attended Hampton Roads Business College, Hampton Roads, Va.
business owner
member of the Virginia state general assembly, 1997-2001
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the three succeeding Congresses, (January 3, 2001-October 6, 2007)
died on October 6, 2007, in Gloucester County, Va.

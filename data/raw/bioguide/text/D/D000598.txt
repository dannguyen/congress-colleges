DAVIS, Susan A., a Representative from California
born in Cambridge, Middlesex County, Mass., April 13, 1944
B.A., University of California, Berkeley, Calif., 1965
M.S.W., University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, N.C., 1968
member of the San Diego, Calif., board of education, 1983-1992, president, San Diego, Calif., board of education 1989-1992
executive director, Aaron Price Fellows, 1990-1994
member of the California state assembly, 1994-2000
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present).

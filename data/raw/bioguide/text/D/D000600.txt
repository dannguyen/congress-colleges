DIAZ-BALART, Mario, (brother of Lincoln Diaz-Balart), a Representative from Florida
born in Fort Lauderdale, Broward County, Fla., September 25, 1961
attended the University of South Florida, Tampa, Fla.
marketing executive
member of the Florida state house of representatives, 1988-1992, 2000-2002
member of the Florida state senate, 1992-2000
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

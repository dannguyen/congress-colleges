DOLE, Elizabeth Hanford, (wife of Robert J. Dole), a Senator from North Carolina
born in Salisbury, Rowan County, North Carolina, on July 29, 1936
B.A., Duke University 1958
M.A., Harvard University 1960
J.D., Harvard University 1965
U.S. secretary of transportation 1983-1987
U.S. secretary of labor 1989-1990
president, American Red Cross 1991-2000
unsuccessful candidate for the Republican presidential nomination in 2000
elected as a Republican to the U.S. Senate in 2002, and served from January 3, 2003, to January 3, 2009
chair, National Republican Senatorial Committee (2005-2007)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 2008.

DRAKE, Thelma D., a Representative from Virginia
born in Elyria, Lorain County, Ohio, November 20, 1949
graduated from Elyria High School, Elyria, Ohio, 1967
realtor
business owner
member of the Virginia state house of delegates, 1996-2004
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Ninth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2005-January 3, 2009)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

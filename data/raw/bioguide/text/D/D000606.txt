DAVIS, David, a Representative from Tennessee
born in Johnson City, Washington County, Tenn., November 6, 1959
A.S., California College for Health Sciences, National City, Calif., 1983
B.S., Milligan College, Milligan College, Tenn., 1991
business owner
unsuccessful candidate for nomination for the 105th Congress in 1996
member of the Tennessee state general assembly, 1999-2006
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Tenth Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2009)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

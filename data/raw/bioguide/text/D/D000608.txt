DAHLKEMPER, Kathleen A. (Kathy), a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Erie, Erie County, Pa., December 10, 1957
B. S., Edinboro State College, Edinboro, Pa., 1982
business owner
director, Lake Erie Arboretum at Frontier Park, Pa.
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010
Erie County, Pa., executive, 2014-present.

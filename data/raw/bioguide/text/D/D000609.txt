DRIEHAUS, Steve, a Representative from Ohio
born in Cincinnati, Hamilton County, Ohio, June 24, 1966
graduated from Elder High School, Cincinnati, Ohio, 1984
B.A., Miami University, Oxford, Ohio, 1988
M.P.A., Indiana University, Bloomington, Ind., 1995
community outreach program director
United States Peace Corps, 1988-1990
member, Ohio state house of representatives, 2001-2008, minority whip, 2005-2007
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2010)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010
country director, Mbabane, Swaziland, United States Peace Corps, 2011 to present.

DEUTCH, Theodore E. (Ted), a Representative from Florida
born in Bethlehem, Northampton County, Pa., May 7, 1966
graduated from Liberty High School, Bethlehem, Pa., 1984
A.B., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1988
J.D., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1990
lawyer, private practice
member of the Florida state senate, 2007-2010
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Robert Wexler, and reelected to the three succeeding Congresses (April 13, 2010-present).

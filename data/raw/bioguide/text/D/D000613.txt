DOLD, Robert, a Representative from Illinois
born in Evanston, Cook County, Ill., June 23, 1969
graduated from New Trier High School, Winnetka, Ill., 1987
B.A., Denison University, Granville, Ohio, 1991
J.D., Indiana University, Bloomington, Ind., 1996
M.B.A., Northwestern University Kellogg School of Management, Evanston, Ill., 2000
business owner
staff, United States Vice President James Danforth Quayle, 1991-1993
staff, United States House of Representatives Committee on Reform and Oversight, 1997-1999
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

DAINES, Steve, a Senator and a Representative from Montana
born in Van Nuys, Los Angeles County, Calif., August 20, 1962
graduated from Bozeman High School, Bozeman, Mont.
B.S., Montana State University, Bozeman, Mont., 1984
businessman
delegate, Republican National Convention, 1984
unsuccessful candidate for lieutenant governor of Montana in 2008
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress (January 3, 2013-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the House of Representatives but was elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 2014 for the term ending January 3, 2021.

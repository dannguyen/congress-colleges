DAVIS, Rodney, a Representative from Illinois
born in Des Moines, Polk County, Iowa, January 5, 1970
attended the public schools in Taylorville, Ill.
B.A., Millikin University, Decatur, Ill., 1992
staff, Illinois secretary of state, 1992-1996
unsuccessful candidate for the Illinois state house of representatives in 1996
staff, United States Representative John Shimkus of Illinois, 1997-2012
unsuccessful candidate for mayor of Taylorville, Ill., in 2001
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

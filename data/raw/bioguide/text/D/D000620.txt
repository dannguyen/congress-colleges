DELANEY, John, a Representative from Maryland
born in Wood-Ridge, Bergen County, N.J., April 16, 1963
B.S., Columbia University, New York, N.Y., 1985
J.D., Georgetown University, Washington, D.C., 1988
lawyer, private practice
business owner
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

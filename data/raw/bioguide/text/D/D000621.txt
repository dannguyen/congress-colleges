DeSANTIS, Ron, a Representative from Florida
born in Jacksonville, Duval County, Fla., September 14, 1978
graduated from Dunedin High School, Dunedin, Fla., 1997
B.A., Yale University, New Haven, Conn., 2001
J.D., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 2005
United States Navy, 2004-2010
United States Navy Reserve, 2010-present
author
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

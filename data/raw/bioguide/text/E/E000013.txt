EARLY, Joseph Daniel, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Worcester, Mass., Worcester County, January 31, 1933
attended parochial schools in Worcester
B.S., College of the Holy Cross, Worcester, 1955
served in United States Navy, 1955-1957
taught high school in Shrewsbury and Spencer, Mass., 1959-1963
served six terms in Massachusetts house of representatives, 1963-1974
delegate to Massachusetts State Democratic conventions, 1964-1970
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-fourth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1975-January 3, 1993)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
died on November 9, 2012, in Worcester, Mass.

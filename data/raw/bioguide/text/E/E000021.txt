EASTMAN, Nehemiah, (uncle of Ira Allen Eastman), a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Gilmanton, Belknap County, N.H., June 16, 1782
attended the local academy in Gilmanton
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1807 and practiced in Farmington, N.H.
member of the State house of representatives in 1813
served in the State senate 1820-1825
elected as an Adams candidate to the Nineteenth Congress (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1827)
resumed the practice of law
died in Farmington, N.H., January 11, 1856
interment in Farmington Cemetery.

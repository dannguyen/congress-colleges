EATON, Lewis, a Representative from New York
born in Duanesburg, Schenectady County, New York, 1789
farmer
Duanesburg town supervisor, 1819-1820
sheriff of Schenectady County, 1821-1822
elected as Crawford Republican to the Eighteenth Congress (March 4, 1823-March 3, 1825)
member of the State senate, 1829-1832
engaged in banking
Special Agent of the U.S. Postal Service
died in Buffalo, N.Y., on August 22, 1857.

ECTON, Zales Nelson, a Senator from Montana
born in Weldon, Decatur County, Iowa, April 1, 1898
moved to Gallatin County, Mont., in 1907
attended the public schools of Gallatin County, Montana State College at Bozeman, and the University of Chicago Law School
rancher with interests in grain and livestock 1921-1946
member, State house of representatives 1933-1937
member, State senate 1937-1946
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 1946, and served from January 3, 1947, to January 3, 1953
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1952
resumed ranching activities
died at Bozeman, Mont., March 3, 1961
interment in Sunset Hills Cemetery.

EFNER, Valentine, a Representative from New York
born in Blenheim Hill, near Blenheim, Schoharie County, N.Y., May 5, 1776
completed preparatory studies
engaged in agricultural pursuits
commissioned as major in the War of 1812
member of the State assembly in 1829
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1837)
resumed agricultural pursuits
died in Blenheim Hill, N.Y., November 20, 1865
interment in Blenheim Hill Cemetery.

EGBERT, Joseph, a Representative from New York
born near Bull Head, Staten Island, N.Y., April 10, 1807
attended the common schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1843)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1842
resumed agricultural pursuits
supervisor of Southfield, Richmond County, in 1855 and 1856
county clerk of Richmond County in 1869
died at his home near New Dorp, N.Y., July 7, 1888
interment in the Moravian Cemetery, New Dorp, Staten Island, N.Y.

ELLIOTT, John, a Senator from Georgia
born in St. Johns Parish, now Liberty County, Ga., October 24, 1773
completed preparatory studies
graduated from Yale College in 1794
studied law
admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Sunbury, Liberty County, Ga., in 1797
held several local offices
elected as a Democratic Republican (later Crawford Republican) to the United States Senate and served from March 4, 1819, to March 3, 1825
died in Sunbury, Ga., August 9, 1827
interment in Old Midway Cemetery in Liberty County.

ELLSWORTH, Samuel Stewart, a Representative from New York
born in Pownal, Vt., October 13, 1790
attended the common schools
moved to Penn Yan, N.Y., in 1819 and engaged in mercantile pursuits
supervisor of Milo, Yates County, 1824-1828
judge of Yates County 1824-1829
served in the State assembly in 1840
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
died in Penn Yan, N.Y., June 4, 1863
interment in Lake View Cemetery.

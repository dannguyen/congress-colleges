ELSAESSER, Edward Julius, a Representative from New York
born in Buffalo, Erie County, N.Y., March 10, 1904
attended the public schools
was graduated from the law department of the University of Buffalo, Buffalo, N.Y., in 1926
was admitted to the bar in 1927 and commenced practice in Buffalo, N.Y.
Republican State committeeman 1937-1945
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-ninth and Eightieth Congresses (January 3, 1945-January 3, 1949)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1948 to the Eighty-first Congress
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the Eighty-second Congress in 1950
resumed the practice of law
was a resident of Williamsville, N.Y., until his death there on January 7, 1983
interment in Williamsville Cemetery.

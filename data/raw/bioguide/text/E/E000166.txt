ELY, John, a Representative from New York
born in Saybrook, Conn., October 8, 1774
completed preparatory studies
studied medicine, and practiced in Coxsackie, N.Y.
member of the State assembly in 1806 and 1812
one of the organizers of the New York State and Greene County Medical Societies in 1807 and also of the Albany Female Academy
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1839-March 3, 1841)
resumed the practice of medicine
died in Coxsackie, N.Y., August 20, 1849
interment in Old Coxsackie Cemetery.

EMERSON, Louis Woodard, a Representative from New York
born in Warrensburg, Warren County, N.Y., July 25, 1857
attended the district school and was graduated from Warrensburg Academy
engaged in the lumber, banking, and manufacturing business
delegate to the Republican National Conventions in 1888, 1892, and 1896
member of the State senate 1890-1893
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-sixth and Fifty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1899-March 3, 1903)
resumed former business activities in Warrensburg, N.Y., and died there June 10, 1924
interment in the City Cemetery.

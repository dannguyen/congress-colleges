ENGEL, Eliot Lance, a Representative from New York
born in New York, N.Y., February 18, 1947
attended Bronx public schools
B.A., Hunter-Lehman College, City University of New York, New York, N.Y., 1969
M.A., Herbert H. Lehman College, City University of New York, New York, N.Y., 1973
J.D., New York Law School, New York, N.Y., 1987
teacher
member of the New York state assembly, 1977-1988
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred First and to the thirteen succeeding Congresses (January 3,1989-present).

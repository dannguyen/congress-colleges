ERDMAN, Constantine Jacob, (grandson of Jacob Erdman), a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Upper Saucon Township, near Allentown, Lehigh County, Pa., September 4, 1846
attended the common schools of the district and a classical school in Quakerstown, Pa.
was graduated from Pennsylvania College, Gettysburg, Pa., in 1865
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1867 and practiced in Allentown, Pa.
elected district attorney in 1874
adjutant of the Fourth Regiment, National Guard of Pennsylvania, during the riots at Reading in 1877
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-third and Fifty-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1893-March 3, 1897)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1896
resumed the practice of law in Allentown
trustee of Muhlenberg College at Allentown
president of the Coplay Cement Manufacturing Co., the Allentown & Coopersburg Turnpike Co., and the Allen Fire Insurance Co. for many years
died in Allentown, Pa., January 15, 1911
interment in Fairview Cemetery.

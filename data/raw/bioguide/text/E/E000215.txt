ESHOO, Anna Georges, a Representative from California
born in New Britain, Hartford County, Conn., December 13, 1942
A.A., Canada College, Redwood City, Calif., 1975
Democratic National Committeewoman from California, 1980-1992
staff, speaker pro tempore of the California state assembly, 1981-1982
member of the San Mateo County, Calif., board of supervisors, 1983-1992
president, San Mateo County, Calif. board of supervisors, 1986
member, California Democratic State Central Executive Committee
member of the Democratic National Commission on Presidential Nominations, 1982
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 1988
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-present).

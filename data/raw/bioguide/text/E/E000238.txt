EVANS, David Reid, a Representative from South Carolina
born in Westminster, England, February 20, 1769
immigrated to the United States in 1784 with his father, who settled in South Carolina
attended Mount Zion College
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1796 and commenced practice in Winnsboro
member of the State house of representatives 1802-1805
solicitor of the middle judicial circuit 1804-1811
elected as a Republican to the Thirteenth Congress (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1815)
declined to be a candidate for reelection and returned to his plantation
member of the State senate 1818-1826
first president of the Fairfield Bible Society
died in Winnsboro, Fairfield County, S.C., March 8, 1843
interment at a private residence in Winnsboro.

EVANS, James La Fayette, a Representative from Indiana
born in Clayville, Harrison County, Ky., March 27, 1825
attended the public schools
moved to Indiana, with his parents, who settled in Hancock County in 1837
moved to Marion, Ind., in 1845 and engaged in mercantile pursuits
moved to Hamilton County, Ind.
settled in Noblesville in 1850 and continued mercantile pursuits
also engaged in the grain-elevator business and in the pork-packing business
elected as a Republican to the Forty-fourth and Forty-fifth Congresses (March 4, 1875-March 3, 1879)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1878
resumed the grain-elevator business
died in Noblesville, Ind., May 28, 1903
interment in Crownland Cemetery.

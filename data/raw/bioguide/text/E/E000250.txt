EVANS, Lane Allen, a Representative from Illinois
born in Rock Island, Rock Island County, Ill., August 4, 1951
attended Sacred Heart School, Rock Island, Ill.
graduated from Alleman High School, Rock Island, Ill., 1969
B.A., Augustana College, Rock Island, Ill., 1974
J.D., Georgetown University Law Center, Washington, D.C., 1977
United States Marine Corps, 1969-1971
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-eighth and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-January 3, 2007)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Tenth Congress in 2006
died on November 5, 2014, in East Moline, Ill.

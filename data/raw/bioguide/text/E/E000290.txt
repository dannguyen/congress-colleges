EDWARDS, Donna F., a Representative from Maryland
born in Yanceyville, Caswell County, N.C., June 28, 1958
B.A., Wake Forest University, Winston-Salem, N.C., 1980
J.D., Franklin Pierce Law Center, Concord, N. H., 1989
lawyer, private practice
clerk, District of Columbia Superior Court Judge
executive director, National Network to End Domestic Violence
executive director, Center for a New Democracy
executive director, The Arca Foundation
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Albert Russell Wynn, and reelected to the four succeeding Congresses (June 17, 2008-present).

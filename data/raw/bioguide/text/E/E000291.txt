ELLMERS, Renee, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Ironwood, Gogebic County, Mich., February 9, 1964
graduated from Madison High School, Madison Heights, Mich.
B.S., Oakland University, Rochester, Mich., 1990
registered nurse
hospital administrator
member of the Dunn, N.C., planning board, 2006-2010
chair, Dunn, N.C., planning board, 2008-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

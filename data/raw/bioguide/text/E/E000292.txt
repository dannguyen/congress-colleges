ENYART, Bill, a Representative from Illinois
born in Pensacola, Escambia County, Fla., September 22, 1949
attended University of Illinois, Springfield, Ill.
B.A., Southern Illinois University, Edwardsville, Ill., 1974
J.D., Southern Illinois University, Edwardsville, Ill., 1979
M.S.S., United States Army War College, Carlisle, Pa., 2000
United States Air Force, 1969-1973
United States Air Force Reserve, 1973-1975
attorney
businessman
Illinois Army National Guard, 1982-2012
adjutant general, Illinois Army National Guard, 2007-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress (January 3, 2013-January 3, 2015)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

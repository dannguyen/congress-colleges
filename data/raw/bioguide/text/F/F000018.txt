FARLEY, Ephraim Wilder, a Representative from Maine
born in Newcastle, Maine, August 29, 1817
attended the common schools and was graduated from Bowdoin College, Brunswick, Maine, in 1836
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Newcastle
member of the State house of representatives in 1843 and 1851-1853
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1854 to the Thirty-fourth Congress
member of the State senate in 1856
died in Newcastle, Maine, April 3, 1880
interment in a tomb on the family estate.

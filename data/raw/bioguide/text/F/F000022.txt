FARLIN, Dudley, a Representative from New York
born in Norwich, New London County, Conn., September 2, 1777
moved to Dutchess County, N.Y., in early youth, and later to Warren County
engaged in the lumber and grain business
supervisor of the town of Warrensburg 1818-1820, 1827, and 1828
sheriff of Warren County in 1821, 1822, and again in 1828
member of the State assembly in 1824
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1837)
resumed his former business pursuits
died in Warrensburg, Warren County, N.Y., on September 26, 1837
interment in Warrensburg Cemetery.

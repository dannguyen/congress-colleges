FARNSLEY, Charles Rowland Peaslee, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Louisville, Jefferson County, Ky., March 28, 1907
attended Male High School, Louisville
University of Louisville, A.B., 1930, and LL.B., 1942
was admitted to the bar in 1930 and began practice in Louisville
served in the State house of representatives, 1936-1940
mayor of Louisville, 1948-1953
delegate to Democratic National Convention, 1952
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-ninth Congress (January 3, 1965-January 3, 1967)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1966 to the Ninetieth Congress
served as publisher and president of Lost Cause Press
was a resident of Glenview, Ky., until his death in Louisville on June 19, 1990
interment in Cave Hill Cemetery, Louisville, Jefferson County, Ky.

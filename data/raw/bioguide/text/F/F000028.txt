FARR, Evarts Worcester, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Littleton, Grafton County, N.H., October 10, 1840
attended the common schools and Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H.
during the Civil War entered the Union Army as first lieutenant of Company G, Second Regiment, New Hampshire Volunteer Infantry and served as major in the Eleventh Regiment, New Hampshire Volunteer Infantry
assistant assessor of internal revenue 1865-1869
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1867 and commenced practice in Littleton
assessor of internal revenue 1869-1873
solicitor for Grafton County 1873-1879
member of the executive council of New Hampshire in 1876
elected as a Republican to the Forty-sixth and Forty-seventh Congresses and served from March 4, 1879, until his death in Littleton, N.H., November 30, 1880
interment in Glenwood Cemetery.

FEELY, John Joseph, a Representative from Illinois
born on a farm near Wilmington, Will County, Ill., August 1, 1875
attended the public schools
was graduated from Niagara (N.Y.) University in 1895 and from the law department of Yale University in 1897
was admitted to the bar in Connecticut in 1897
moved to Chicago, Ill., in 1898 and engaged in the practice of law
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1901-March 3, 1903)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1902
engaged in the practice of his profession until his death in Chicago, Ill., February 15, 1905
interment in Mount Olivet Cemetery, Joilet, Ill.

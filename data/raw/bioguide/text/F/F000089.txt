FERRELL, Thomas Merrill, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Glassboro, Gloucester County, N.J., June 20, 1844
attended the common schools and completed an academic course
elected a member of the township committee in 1872 and 1873
president of Hollow Ware GlassworkersÂ’ Association 1878-1883
member of the school board 1885-1890, serving as its president in 1887
member of the State house of assembly in 1879 and 1880
member of the State senate in 1880 and 1881
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1883-March 3, 1885)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1884 to the Forty-ninth Congress
employed as a glassware salesman
died in Glassboro, N.J., October 20, 1916
interment in Methodist Episcopal Cemetery.

FIESINGER, William Louis, a Representative from Ohio
born in Willard, Huron County, Ohio, October 25, 1877
educated in the public schools of Norwalk, Ohio
was graduated from the law department of Baldwin-Wallace University, Berea, Ohio, in 1901
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Sandusky, Ohio
served as city solicitor of Sandusky 1903-1909
judge of the common pleas court of Erie County 1925-1931
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-second, Seventy-third, and Seventy-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1931-January 3, 1937)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1936
resumed the practice of law in Sandusky, Ohio
died in Cleveland, Ohio, September 11, 1953
interment in Oakland Cemetery, Sandusky, Ohio.

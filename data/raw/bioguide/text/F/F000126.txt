FINE, Sidney Asher, a Representative from New York
born in New York City, September 14, 1903
attended public schools
was graduated from College of the City of New York in 1923 and from the law school of Columbia University in 1926
was admitted to the bar in 1926 and commenced practice in New York City
member of the State assembly in 1945 and 1946 and the State senate 1947-1950
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-second, Eighty-third, and Eighty-fourth Congresses and served from January 3, 1951, until his resignation January 2, 1956
New York State Supreme Court, 1956-1975
resided in New York City until his death there on April 23, 1982
interment at Old Montefiore Cemetery, Queens, N.Y.

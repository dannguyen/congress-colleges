FISHER, Spencer Oliver, a Representative from Michigan
born in Camden, Hillsdale County, Mich., February 3, 1843
attended the public schools and Albion and Hillsdale Colleges in Michigan
engaged in lumbering and banking in West Bay City, Mich.
mayor of West Bay City 1881-1884
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1884
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-ninth and Fiftieth Congresses (March 4, 1885-March 3, 1889)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1888 to the Fifty-first Congress
resumed his former business pursuits in Bay City, Mich., where he died June 1, 1919
interment in Elmlawn Cemetery.

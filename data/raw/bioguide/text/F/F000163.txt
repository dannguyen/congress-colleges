FITZGERALD, Frank Thomas, a Representative from New York
born in New York City May 4, 1857
was graduated from the College of St. Francis Xavier, New York City, from St. MaryÂ’s College, Niagara Falls, N.Y., in 1876, and from the Columbia Law School, New York City, in 1878
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in New York City in 1879
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-first Congress
served from March 4, 1889, until November 4, 1889, when he resigned, having been elected register of New York County and held that office until 1892
delegate to the State constitutional convention in 1893
elected surrogate of New York County in 1892 for a term of fourteen years
reelected in 1906 and served in this capacity until his death in New York City November 25, 1907
interment Calvary Cemetery, Long Island City, N.Y.

FLAKE, Floyd Harold, a Representative from New York
born in Los Angeles, Calif., January 30, 1945
attended public schools in Houston, Tex.
B.A., Wilberforce University, Wilberforce, Ohio, 1970
attended Northeastern University, Boston, Mass., 1974-1976, and St. JohnÂ’s University, Jamaica, N.Y., 1980-1984
M. Div., United Theological Seminary, Dayton, Ohio, 1995
pastor of Allen A.M.E. Church, Jamaica, N.Y., 1976-1986
unsuccessful candidate in 1986 for the vacancy in the Ninety-ninth Congress caused by the death of Joseph P. Addabbo
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundredth and to the five succeeding Congresses and served from January 3, 1987, until his resignation November 17, 1997, to return full time to his duties as pastor of Allen A.M.E. Church.

FOLEY, John Robert, a Representative from Maryland
born in Wabasha, Wabasha County, Minn., October 16, 1917
graduated from St. Felix High School, Wabasha, Minn., 1935
B.A., St. Thomas College, St. Paul, Minn., 1940
United States Army, 1941-1946
LL.B., Georgetown University Law School, Washington, D.C., 1947
L.L.M., Catholic University Law School, Washington, D.C., 1950
lawyer, private practice
professor
elected judge of the OrphanÂ’s (Probate) Court, Montgomery County, Md., 1954-1958
unsuccessful Democratic candidate for election to the Eighty-fifth Congress in 1956
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-sixth Congress (January 3, 1959-January 3, 1961)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Eighty-seventh Congress in 1960 and for election to the Eighty-eighth Congress in 1962
died on November 11, 2001, in Kensington, Md.

FOLGER, Alonzo Dillard, (brother of John Hamlin Folger), a Representative from North Carolina
born in Dobson, Surry County, N.C., July 9, 1888
attended the public schools
was graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 1912 and from its law department in 1914
was admitted to the bar in 1914 and commenced practice in Dobson, N.C.
moved to Mount Airy, N.C., and continued the practice of law
also interested in banking
trustee of the University of North Carolina 1932-1938
served as judge of the State superior court in 1937, resigning after two monthsÂ’ service to become a Democratic national committeeman
member of the Democratic National Committee 1936-1941
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-sixth and Seventy-seventh Congresses and served from January 3, 1939, until his death in an automobile accident in Mount Airy, N.C., April 30, 1941
interment in Dobson Cemetery, Dobson, N.C.

FOLGER, Walter, Jr., a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Nantucket, Mass., June 12, 1765
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
member of the State senate 1809-1815 and in 1822
elected as a Republican to the Fifteenth Congress and reelected to the Sixteenth Congress (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1821)
resumed the practice of law
died in Nantucket, Mass., September 8, 1849
interment in Friends Burying Ground.

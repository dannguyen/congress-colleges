FORESTER, John B., a Representative from Tennessee
born in McMinnville, Warren County, Tenn., birth date unknown
received a limited schooling
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-third Congress and reelected as an Anti-Jacksonian candidate to the Twenty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1833-March 3, 1837)
died August 31, 1845.

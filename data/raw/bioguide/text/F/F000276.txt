FORNANCE, Joseph, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Lower Merion Township, Montgomery County, Pa., October 18, 1804
attended the public schools and the Old Academy at Lower Merion
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1832 and commenced practice in Norristown, Pa.
president of the council of the borough of Norristown
member of the State house of representatives in 1834
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth and Twenty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1839-March 3, 1843)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1842
resumed the practice of his profession
died in Norristown, Montgomery County, Pa., on November 24, 1852
interment in Montgomery Cemetery.

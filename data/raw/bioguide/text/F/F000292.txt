FOSDICK, Nicoll, a Representative from New York
born in New London, Conn., November 9, 1785
completed preparatory studies
moved to Norway, N.Y.
presidential elector on the Monroe ticket in 1816
member of the State assembly in 1818 and 1819
elected as an Adams candidate to the Nineteenth Congress (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1827)
returned to New London, Conn., in 1843
collector of customs 1849-1853
engaged in mercantile pursuits
died in New London, Conn., May 7, 1868
interment in Cedar Grove Cemetery.

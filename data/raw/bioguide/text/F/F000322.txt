FOWLER, John, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Virginia in 1755
attended the common schools
served as captain in the Revolutionary War
member of the convention held in Danville, Fayette County, Va. (now Kentucky), in 1787
served in the Virginia house of delegates in 1787
member of the Virginia convention which ratified the Constitution
moved to Lexington, Ky.
elected as a Republican to the Fifth and to the four succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1797-March 3, 1807)
postmaster of Lexington 1814-1822
died in Lexington, Fayette County, Ky., August 22, 1840
interment in the Old Episcopal Cemetery.

FRANKLIN, Meshack, (brother of Jesse Franklin), a Representative from North Carolina
born in Surry County, N.C., in 1772
member of the State house of commons in 1800 and 1801
served in the State senate in 1828, 1829, and 1838
elected as a Republican to the Tenth and to the three succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1807-March 3, 1815)
died in Surry County, N.C., December 18, 1839.

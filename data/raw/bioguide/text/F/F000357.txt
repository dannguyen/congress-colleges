FREDERICK, Benjamin Todd, a Representative from Iowa
born in Fredericktown, Columbiana County, Ohio, October 5, 1834
attended the district schools
completed preparatory studies
engaged in the foundry and machine business Marshalltown, Iowa, 1865-1888
went to Marysville, Calif., in 1857 and engaged in placer mining
returned to Marshalltown, Iowa, in 1859
member of the city council of Marshalltown 1874-1877
member of the school board three terms
successfully contested as a Democrat the election of James Wilson to the Forty-eighth Congress and took his seat the last day of that Congress, March 3, 1885
reelected to the Forty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1885-March 3, 1887)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1886
moved to San Diego, Calif., in 1887 and engaged in the real estate business
collector of internal revenue 1893-1902
died in San Diego, Calif., November 3, 1903
interment in Mount Hope Cemetery.

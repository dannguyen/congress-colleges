FRICK, Henry, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Northumberland, Pa., March 17, 1795
attended the public schools
apprenticed to a printer in Philadelphia
served in the War of 1812
settled in Milton, Pa., in 1816
established the Miltonian, a political journal, with which he was connected for over twenty years
member of the State house of representatives 1828-1831
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-eighth Congress and served from March 4, 1843, until his death in Washington, D.C., March 1, 1844
interment in the Congressional Cemetery.

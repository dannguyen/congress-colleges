FRIES, George, a Representative from Ohio
born in Pennsylvania in 1799
attended the common schools
studied medicine and commenced practice in Hanoverton, Ohio, in 1833
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-ninth and Thirtieth Congresses (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1849)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1848
moved to Cincinnati, Ohio, and resumed the practice of medicine
treasurer of Hamilton County 1860-1862
died in Cincinnati, Ohio, on November 13, 1866
interment in the Catholic Cemetery.

FRISA, Dan, a Representative from New York
born in Queens, N.Y., April 27, 1955
attended East Meadow Public schools
B.S., St. Johns University
served in the New York State Assembly, 1985-1992
marketing representative for Johnson and Johnson
retail executive Fortunoff
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth Congress (January 3, 1995-January 3, 1997)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress.

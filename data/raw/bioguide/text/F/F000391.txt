FROST, Joel, a Representative from New York
born in Westchester County, N.Y., September 28, 1765
attended the public schools
member, Westchester County Board of Supervisors, 1803
member of the State assembly, 1806-1808
first surrogate of Putnam County in 1812, and served in 1813, 1815-1819, 1821, and 1822
member of the State constitutional convention, 1821
judge, Court of Common Pleas
moved to Schenectady
elected as a Crawford Republican to the Eighteenth Congress (March 4, 1823-March 3, 1825)
died September 11, 1827
interment in Gilead Cemetery at Carmel, N.Y.

FULLER, Benoni Stinson, a Representative from Indiana
born near Boonville, Warrick County, Ind., November 13, 1825
attended the common schools
taught school in Warrick County
sheriff of Warrick County in 1856 and 1858
served in the State senate in 1862, 1870, and 1872
member of the State house of representatives 1866-1868
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-fourth and Forty-fifth Congresses (March 4, 1875-March 3, 1879)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1878
engaged in agricultural pursuits in Warrick County
died in Boonville, Ind., April 14, 1903
interment in Old Boonville Cemetery.

FAIRCLOTH, Duncan McLauchlin (Lauch), a Senator from North Carolina
born in Sampson County, N.C., January 14, 1928
attended Roseboro High School, Sampson County, N.C.
engaged in farming, commercial real estate, heavy construction and auto dealerships
chairman, North Carolina Highway Commission 1969-1972
secretary, North Carolina Department of Commerce 1977-1983
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 1992, and served from January 3, 1993, to January 3, 1999
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1998.

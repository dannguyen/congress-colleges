FOSSELLA, Vito John, (great grandson of James O'Leary), a Representative from New York
born in Staten Island, Richmond County, N.Y., March 9, 1965
graduated from Monsignor Farrell High School, Staten Island, N.Y.
B.S., Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, Pa., 1987
J.D., Fordham University Law School, New York, N.Y., 1994
lawyer, private practice
New York, N.Y., city council member
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth Congress, by special election to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Susan Molinari, reelected to the five succeeding Congresses (November 4, 1997-January 3, 2009)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

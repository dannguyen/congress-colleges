FITZGERALD, Peter G., a Senator from Illinois
born on October 20, 1960, in Elgin, Illinois, and raised in Inverness, Illinois
attended St. Theresa Catholic Elementary School in Palatine, Illinois, and Portsmouth Abbey, a Catholic secondary school in Portsmouth, Rhode Island
A.B., Dartmouth College 1982, graduating cum laude
Rotary Scholar, Aristotelian University, Greece 1982-1983
J.D., University of Michigan School of Law 1986
practiced as a corporate lawyer in Chicago
general counsel for Suburban Bancorp, Inc.
Illinois state senator 1993-1998
elected as a Republican to the U.S. Senate in 1998 and served from January 3, 1999, to January 3, 2005
was not a candidate for reelection in 2004
currently serves on the North American Advisory Board of the Transurban Group and on the Advisory Board to Financial Services Partners Fund I, LLC
currently serves as Chairman of the Board of the Chain Bridge Bank in McLean, Virginia
is a resident of McLean, Virginia.

FERGUSON, Michael, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Ridgewood, Bergen County, N.J., June 22, 1970
graduated from Delbarton School, Morristown, N.J.
B.A., University of Notre Dame, Notre Dame, Ind.
M.P.A., Georgetown University, Washington, D.C.
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2009)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

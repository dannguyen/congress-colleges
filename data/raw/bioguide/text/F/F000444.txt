FLAKE, Jeff, a Senator and a Representative from Arizona
born in Snowflake, Navajo County, Ariz., December 31, 1962
graduated from Brigham Young University, Provo, Utah, 1986
M.A. Brigham Young University, Provo, Utah, 1987
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for reelection to the House of Representatives but was elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 2012 for the term ending January 3, 2019.

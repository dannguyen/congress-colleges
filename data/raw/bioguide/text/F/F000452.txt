FORTUÃ‘O, Luis G., a Resident Commissioner from Puerto Rico
born in San Juan, P.R., October 31, 1960
B.S.F.S., Georgetown University, Washington, D.C., 1982
J.D., University of Virginia, Charlottesville, Va., 1985
lawyer, private practice
executive director, Puerto Rico Tourism Company, 1993-1996
secretary, Puerto Rico Economic Development and Commerce, 1994-1996
elected as a New Progressive to the One Hundred Ninth Congress to a four-year term (January 3, 2005-January 3, 2009)
not a candidate for reelection in 2008, but was a successful candidate for governor of Puerto Rico
Governor of Puerto Rico, 2009-2013.

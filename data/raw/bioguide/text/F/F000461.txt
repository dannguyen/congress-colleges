FLORES, Bill, a Representative from Texas
born in Cheyenne, Laramie County, Wyo., February 25, 1954
B.B.A., Texas A&M University, College Station, Tex., 1976
M.B.A., Houston Baptist University, Houston, Tex., 1985
certified public accountant
energy company executive
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

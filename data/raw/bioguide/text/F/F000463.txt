FISCHER, Debra (Deb), a Senator from Nebraska
born in Lincoln, Neb., March 1, 1951
graduated University of Nebraska, B.S., 1988
rancher
Valentine Rural High School Board of Education 1990-2004
Nebraska Coordinating Commission for Postsecondary Education 2000-2004
Nebraska Unicameral Legislature 2005-2012
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 2012 for the term ending January 3, 2019.

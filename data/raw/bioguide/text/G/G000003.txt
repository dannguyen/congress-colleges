GAGE, Joshua, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Harwich, Mass., on August 7, 1763
completed preparatory studies
in 1795 moved to Augusta, Maine (until 1820 a district of Massachusetts)
was a master mariner, and subsequently became engaged in mercantile pursuits
member of the Massachusetts house of representatives in 1805 and 1807
served in the State senate in 1813 and 1815
treasurer of Kennebec County, Maine, 1810-1831
elected as a Republican to the Fifteenth Congress (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1819)
member of the Maine executive council in 1822 and 1823
died in Augusta, Maine, January 24, 1831
interment in Augusta, Maine.

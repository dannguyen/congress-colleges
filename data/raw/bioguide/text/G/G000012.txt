GALE, George, (father of Levin Gale), a Representative from Maryland
born in Somerset County, Md., June 3, 1756
attended the common schools
served during the Revolutionary War
member of the Maryland convention which ratified the Federal Constitution in 1788
elected as a Pro-Administration candidate to the First Congress (March 4, 1789-March 3, 1791)
appointed by President Washington on March 4, 1791, supervisor of distilled liquors for the district of Maryland
died at Brookland, Cecil County, Md., January 2, 1815
interment in the family burying ground on his estate.

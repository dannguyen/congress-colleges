GANSKE, Greg, a Representative from Iowa
born in New Hampton, Chickasaw County, Iowa, March 31, 1949
B.A., University of Iowa, Iowa City, Iowa, 1972
M.D., University of Iowa Medical School, Iowa City, Iowa, 1976
general surgery training, University of Colorado Medical Center, Denver, Colo., and Oregon Health Science Center, Portland, Oreg., 1976-1982
plastic surgery training, Harvard Medical School, Cambridge, Mass., 1982-1984
surgeon
United States Army Reserve, 1986-2001
farm manager
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-January 3, 2003)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in 2002, but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate
is a resident of Des Moines, Iowa.

GARDENIER, Barent, a Representative from New York
born in Kinderhook, Columbia County, N.Y, July 28, 1776
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
held several local offices
elected as a Federalist to the Tenth and Eleventh Congresses (March 4, 1807-March 3, 1811)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1810
engaged in the practice of law in Ulster and Columbia Counties
district attorney of the first district, March 1813-April 1815
newspaper publisher
died in Kingston, N.Y., January 10, 1822
interment beneath the First Reformed Dutch Church of that city.

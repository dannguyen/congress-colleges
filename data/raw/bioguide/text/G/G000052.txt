GARDNER, Francis, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Leominster, Mass., on December 27, 1771
was graduated from Harvard University in 1793
studied law
was admitted to the bar in Cheshire County, N.H., in 1796 and commenced practice at Walpole, N.H.
moved to Keene, N.H., in 1806
solicitor of Cheshire County 1807-1820
elected as a Republican to the Tenth Congress (March 4, 1807-March 3, 1809)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1808
died in Roxbury, Mass., June 25, 1835.

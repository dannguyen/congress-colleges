GASQUE, Elizabeth Hawley, (Mrs. A.J. Van Exem) (wife of Allard Henry Gasque), a Representative from South Carolina
born Elizabeth Mills Hawley on February 26, 1886, near Blythewood, on Rice Creek Plantation, Richland County, S.C.
attended South Carolina Coeducational Institute, Edgefield, S.C.
graduated from Greenville Female College, Greenville, S.C., 1907
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-fifth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of her husband, Allard H. Gasque, (September 13, 1938-January 3, 1939)
was not a candidate for election to the Seventy-sixth Congress
author
lecturer
died on November 2, 1989, in Cedar Tree Plantation, Ridgeway, S.C.

GATLIN, Alfred Moore, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Edenton, N.C., April 20, 1790
pursued classical studies at New Bern, N.C.
graduated from the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, N.C., 1808
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Crawford Republican to the Eighteenth Congress (March 4, 1823-March 3, 1825)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Nineteenth Congress in 1824
died on February 23, 1841, in Tallahassee, Fla.
interment in St. John's Episcopal Cemetery, Tallahassee, Fla.

GERRY, James, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born near Rising Sun, Cecil County, Md., August 14, 1796
pursued an academic course and was graduated from West Nottingham Academy
studied medicine at the University of Maryland, Baltimore, Md., and commenced practice in Shrewsbury, Pa., in 1824
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth and Twenty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1839-March 3, 1843)
continued the practice of medicine until 1870, when he retired
died in Shrewsbury, York County, Pa., July 19, 1873
interment in Lutheran Cemetery.

GIBBONS, James A., a Representative from Nevada
born in Sparks, Washoe County, Nev., December 16, 1944
B.S., University of Nevada, Reno, Nev., 1967
M.S., University of Nevada, Reno, Nev., 1973
J.D. Southwestern Law School, Los Angeles, Calif., 1979
post-graduate studies, University of Southern California, Los Angeles, Calif.
United States Air Force, 1967-1971
Nevada Air National Guard, 1975-1996
lawyer, private practice
commercial airline pilot
hydrologist, Office of Federal Water Master
member of Nevada state assembly, 1989-1993
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth and to the four succeeding Congresses until his resignation on December 31, 2006 (January 3, 1997-December 31, 2006)
was not a candidate for reelection to the United States House of Representatives in 2006
Governor of Nevada, 2007-2010.

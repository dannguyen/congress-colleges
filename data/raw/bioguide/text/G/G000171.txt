GILBERT, Abijah, a Senator from Florida
born in Gilbertsville, Otsego County, N.Y., June 18, 1806
attended Gilbertsville Academy, and graduated from Hamilton College, Clinton, N.Y., in 1822
engaged in mercantile pursuits in New York City 1822-1850
moved to St. Augustine, Fla., in 1865
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate and served from March 4, 1869, to March 3, 1875
retired from business and political life
died in Gilbertsville, N.Y., November 23, 1881
interment in Brookside Cemetery.

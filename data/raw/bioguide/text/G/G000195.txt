GILLESPIE, Eugene Pierce, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Greenville, Mercer County, Pa., September 24, 1852
attended the public schools, Allegheny College, Meadville, Pa., and St. MichaelÂ’s College, Toronto, Canada
studied law
was admitted to the bar in August 1874 and commenced practice in Greenville, Pa.
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-second Congress (March 4, 1891-March 3, 1893)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1892 to the Fifty-third Congress

GILLESPIE, James, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Kenansville, Duplin County, N.C., ca. 1747
pursued classical studies
North Carolina militia, Revolutionary War
member of the North Carolina state constitutional convention, 1776
member of the North Carolina state house of commons, 1779-1783
member of the North Carolina state senate, 1784-1786
elected as an Anti-Administration candidate to the Third Congress and reelected as a Republican to the Fourth and Fifth Congresses (March 4, 1793-March 3, 1799)
elected as Republican to the Eighth Congress (March 4, 1803-January 11, 1805)
died on January 11, 1805, in Washington, D.C.
interment in Congressional Cemetery, Washington, D.C.

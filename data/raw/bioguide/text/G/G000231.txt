GLASGOW, Hugh, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Nottingham, Chester County, Pa., September 8, 1769
attended the public schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
judge of York County from July 1, 1800, to March 29, 1813
elected as a Republican to the Thirteenth and Fourteenth Congresses (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1817)
died at Peach Bottom, York County, Pa., January 31, 1818
interment in Slate Ridge Burying Ground.

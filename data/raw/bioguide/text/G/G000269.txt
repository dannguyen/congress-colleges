GOLDZIER, Julius, a Representative from Illinois
born in Vienna, Austria, January 20, 1854
attended the public schools of Vienna
immigrated to the United States in 1866 and settled in New York
studied law and was admitted to the bar
moved to Chicago in 1872 and commenced the practice of law
member of the city council of Chicago 1890-1892
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-third Congress (March 4, 1893-March 3, 1895)
unsuccessful for reelection in 1894 to the Fifty-fourth Congress
practiced law in Chicago, Ill.
again a member of the Chicago city council in 1899
died in Chicago, January 20, 1925
interment in Graceland Cemetery.

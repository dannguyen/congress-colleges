GOODE, Samuel, a Representative from Virginia
born in Whitby, Chesterfield County, Va., March 21, 1756
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
during the Revolutionary War served as a lieutenant in the Chesterfield Troop of Horse and later as a colonel of militia
member of the Virginia house of delegates 1778-1785
elected as a Republican to the Sixth Congress (March 4, 1799-March 3, 1801)
died in Invermay, Mecklenburg County, Va., November 14, 1822
interment on his estate near Invermay, Mecklenburg County, Va.

GOODWIN, Henry Charles, a Representative from New York
born in De Ruyter, Madison County, N.Y., June 25, 1824
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1846 and commenced practice in Hamilton, N.Y.
district attorney of Madison County 1847-1850
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-third Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of Gerrit Smith and served from November 7, 1854, to March 3, 1855
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1859)
resumed the practice of law
died in Hamilton, N.Y., November 12, 1860
interment in Madison Street Cemetery.

GORDON, Robert Bryarly, a Representative from Ohio
born at St. Marys, Auglaize County, Ohio, August 6, 1855
attended the public schools
postmaster of St. Marys 1885-1889
auditor of Auglaize County 1890-1896
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1896
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-sixth and Fifty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1899-March 3, 1903)
engaged in the flour and grain business at St. Marys, Ohio
superintendent of the document room of the House of Representatives 1911-1913
Sergeant at Arms of the House of Representatives 1913-1919
died in Washington, D.C., January 3, 1923
interment in Elm Grove Cemetery, St. Marys, Ohio.

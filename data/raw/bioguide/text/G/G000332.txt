GORSKI, Martin, a Representative from Illinois
born in Poland, October 30, 1886
immigrated in 1889 to the United States with his parents, who settled in Chicago, Ill.
attended the public and high schools
was graduated from business college and from Chicago (Ill.) Law School in 1917
was admitted to the bar in 1917 and commenced practice in Chicago, Ill.
assistant StateÂ’s attorney 1918-1920
master in chancery of the superior court of Cook County, Ill., 1929-1942
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-eighth, Seventy-ninth, and Eightieth Congresses and served from January 3, 1943, until his death
had been reelected to the Eighty-first Congress
died in Chicago, Ill., December 4, 1949
interment in Resurrection Cemetery (Village of Justice).

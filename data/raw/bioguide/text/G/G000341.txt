GOULD, Herman Day, a Representative from New York
born in Sharon, Litchfield County, Conn., January 16, 1799
pursued an academic course
engaged in mercantile pursuits
president of the Delhi National Bank 1839-1849
unsuccessful candidate for election in 1840 to the Twenty-seventh Congress and in 1844 to the Twenty-ninth Congress
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-first Congress (March 4, 1849-March 3, 1851)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1850
resumed business interests in Delhi, N.Y., and died there January 26, 1852
interment in Woodland Cemetery.

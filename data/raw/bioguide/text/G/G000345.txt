GOURDIN, Theodore, a Representative from South Carolina
born near Kingstree, Williamsburg County, S.C., March 20, 1764
was educated in Charleston, S.C., and in Europe
engaged in planting
elected as a Republican to the Thirteenth Congress (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1815)
resumed agricultural pursuits
died in Pineville, S.C., January 17, 1826
interment in Episcopal Cemetery, St. Stephen, S.C.

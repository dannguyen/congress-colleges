GRANGER, Bradley Francis, a Representative from Michigan
born in Lowville, Lewis County, N.Y., March 12, 1825
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1847 and commenced practice in Tecumseh, Mich.
moved to Ann Arbor, Mich., and resumed practice
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1861-March 3, 1863)
engaged in the practice of law until his death in Ann Arbor, Mich., November 4, 1882
interment in Forest Hill Cemetery.

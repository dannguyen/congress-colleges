GRANGER, Daniel Larned Davis, a Representative from Rhode Island
born in Providence, R.I., May 30, 1852
attended the common schools
was graduated from Brown University, Providence, R.I., in 1874 and from the law department of Boston University in 1877
was admitted to the bar in 1877 and commenced practice in Providence, R.I.
reading clerk of the State house of representatives 1887-1890
treasurer of Providence from January 1890 to January 1901
mayor in 1901 and 1902
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-eighth, Fifty-ninth, and Sixtieth Congresses and served from March 4, 1903, until his death in Washington, D.C., February 14, 1909
interment in Swan Point Cemetery, Providence, R.I.

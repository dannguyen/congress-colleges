GRANT, Abraham Phineas, a Representative from New York
born in New Lebanon, Columbia County, N.Y., April 5, 1804
attended the public schools and was graduated from Hamilton College, Clinton, N.Y.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1828 and commenced practice in Oswego, N.Y.
district attorney of Oswego County in 1835
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1837-March 3, 1839)
resumed the practice of law
died in Oswego, N.Y., December 11, 1871
interment in Riverside Cemetery.

GRANTLAND, Seaton, a Representative from Georgia
born in New Kent County, Va., on June 8, 1782
pursued an academic course
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Milledgeville, Ga.
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress and reelected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1839)
presidential elector on the Whig ticket in 1840
died at his home, Woodville, near Milledgeville, Ga., October 18, 1864
interment in Milledgeville Cemetery.

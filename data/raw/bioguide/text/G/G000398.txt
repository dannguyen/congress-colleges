GRAY, John Cowper, a Representative from Virginia
born in Southampton County, Va., in 1783
pursued an academic course
member of the State house of delegates 1804-1806 and 1821-1823
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of James Johnson and served from August 28, 1820, to March 3, 1821
unsuccessful candidate in 1820 for reelection to the Seventeenth Congress
died May 18, 1823.

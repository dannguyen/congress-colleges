GREENE, George Woodward, a Representative from New York
born in Mount Hope, Orange County, N.Y., July 4, 1831
pursued classical studies and was graduated from the University of Pennsylvania at Philadelphia
taught school
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1860 and commenced practice in Goshen, N.Y.
school commissioner for Orange County
judge of the Orange County Courts 1861-1864
presented credentials as a Democratic Member-elect to the Forty-first Congress and served from March 4, 1869, to February 17, 1870, when he was succeeded by Charles H. Van Wyck, who contested his election
member of the State assembly 1885-1888
died in New York City July 21, 1895
interment in The Plains Cemetery, Otisville, N.Y.

GREENWOOD, Alfred Burton, a Representative from Arkansas
born in Franklin County, Ga., July 11, 1811
pursued classical studies at Lawrenceville, Ga.
was graduated from the University of Georgia at Athens
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1832 and commenced practice in Bentonville, Ark
member of the State house of representatives 1842-1845
State prosecuting attorney 1845-1851
circuit judge of Arkansas 1851-1853
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third, Thirty-fourth, and Thirty-fifth Congresses (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1859)
chairman, Committee on Indian Affairs (Thirty-fifth Congress)
Commissioner of Indian Affairs from May 13, 1859, to April 13, 1861
served in the Confederate House of Representatives 1862-1865
died in Bentonville, Ark., October 4, 1889
interment in Odd Fellows Cemetery.

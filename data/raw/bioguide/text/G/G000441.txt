GREGG, Alexander White, a Representative from Texas
born in Centerville, Leon County, Tex., January 31, 1855
attended the common schools of Texas, and was graduated from King College, Bristol, Tenn., in 1874
studied law at the University of Virginia at Charlottesville
was admitted to the bar in 1878 and commenced practice in Palestine, Tex.
member of the State senate 1886-1888
resumed the practice of law
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-eighth and to the seven succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1903-March 3, 1919)
chairman, Committee on War Claims (Sixty-third through Sixty-fifth Congresses)
was not a candidate for renomination
died in Palestine, Anderson County, Tex., April 30, 1919
interment in East Hill Cemetery.

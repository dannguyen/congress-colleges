GREGG, James Madison, a Representative from Indiana
born in Patrick County, Va., June 26, 1806
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1830 and began practice in Danville, Ind.
county surveyor of Hendricks County 1834-1837
clerk of the circuit court 1837-1845
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1859)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1858 to the Thirty-sixth Congress
resumed the practice of law in Danville, Ind.
member of the State house of representatives in 1862
died in Danville, Ind., on June 16, 1869
interment in South Cemetery.

GRIFFIN, John King, a Representative from South Carolina
born near Clinton, Laurens County, S.C., August 13, 1789
pursued an academic course
engaged as a planter
served in the State house of representatives 1816-1819
member of the State senate 1820-1824 and again in 1828
elected as a Nullifier to the Twenty-second through Twenty-fifth Congresses and reelected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1831-March 3, 1841)
died near Clinton, S.C., August 1, 1841
interment in Little River Church Cemetery.

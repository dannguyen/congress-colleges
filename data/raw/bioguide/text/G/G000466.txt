GRIFFIN, Samuel, a Representative from Virginia
born in 1746 in Richmond County, Va.
pursued classical studies
studied law, was admitted to the bar and practiced
colonel in the Revolutionary War
was wounded at Harlem Heights October 12, 1776
served on the State board of war
member of the State house of delegates 1786-1788
elected as a Pro-Administration supporter to the First Congress, reelected as an Anti-Administration candidate to the Second Congress, and returned as a Pro-Administration candidate to the Third Congresses (March 4, 1789-March 3, 1795)
died November 3, 1810.

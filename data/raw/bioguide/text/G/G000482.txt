GRISWOLD, Gaylord, a Representative from New York
born in Windsor, Hartford County, Conn., December 18, 1767
pursued classical studies and was graduated from Yale College in 1787
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1790 and commenced practice in Windsor, Conn.
moved to Herkimer, N.Y., in 1792
member of the State assembly 1796-1798
elected as a Federalist to the Eighth Congress (March 4, 1803-March 3, 1805)
resumed the practice of law in Herkimer, N.Y., and died there March 1, 1809
interment in Oak Hill Cemetery.

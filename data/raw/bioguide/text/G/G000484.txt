GRISWOLD, Harry Wilbur, a Representative from Wisconsin
born on a farm near West Salem, La Crosse County, Wis., May 19, 1886
attended the West Salem public and high schools and the college of agriculture of the University of Wisconsin at Madison
engaged in agricultural pursuits, specializing in the breeding of cattle
member of the West Salem School Board 1912-1929 and of the Wisconsin Board of Vocational Education 1930-1936
served in the State senate 1932-1936
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-sixth Congress and served from January 3, 1939, until his death in Washington, D.C., July 4, 1939
interment in Hamilton Cemetery, West Salem, Wis.

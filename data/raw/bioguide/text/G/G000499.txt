GROTBERG, John, a Representative from Illinois
born in Winnebago, Minn., March 23, 1925
graduated from Valley City High School, Valley City, N.D.
attended University of Chicago
B.S., George Williams College, 1961
corporate Director of Financial Development, YMCA of Metropolitan Chicago
Illinois State representative, 1973-1977
Illinois State senator, 1977-1985
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-ninth Congress and served from January 3, 1985, until his death at his home in St. Charles, Ill., on November 15, 1986
interment in Union Cemetery.

GROUT, Jonathan, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Lunenburg, Worcester County, Mass., July 23, 1737
served in the expedition against Canada 1757-1760
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Petersham, Mass.
served in the Revolutionary War
member of the State house of representatives in 1781, 1784, and 1787
served in the State senate in 1788
member of the State constitutional convention in 1788
elected as an Anti-Administration candidate to the First Congress (March 4, 1789-March 3, 1791)
returned to Lunenburg, Mass. (now Vermont), in 1803
died in Dover, N.H., September 8, 1807
interment in Pine Hill Cemetery.

GUARINI, Frank Joseph, Jr., a Representative from New Jersey
born in Jersey City, Hudson County, N.J., August 20, 1924
graduated from Lincoln High School, 1942
commissioned a naval officer at Columbia University, 1944
served in the United States Navy aboard the U.S.S. Mount McKinley, 1944-1946
B.A., Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H., 1947
J.D., New York University School of Law, 1950
LL.M., New York University School of Law, 1955
graduate work, Hague Academy of International Law, The Hague, Netherlands
admitted to the New Jersey bar in 1951 and commenced practice in Jersey City
served in the New Jersey State senate, 1965-1972
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-sixth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1993)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
is a resident of New York, N.Y.

GUILL, Ben Hugh, a Representative from Texas
born in Smyrna, Rutherford County, Tenn., September 8, 1909
graduated from West Texas State College, Canyon, Tex., 1933
teacher
business executive
United States Navy, 1942-1945
real estate agent
elected as a Republican to the Eighty-first Congress by special election to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Eugene Worley, (May 6, 1950-January 3, 1951)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Eighty-second Congress in 1950
delegate to Republican National Convention, 1952
executive assistant to the United States Postmaster General, Washington, D.C., 1953-1955
member of the Federal Maritime Board, United States Department of Commerce, 1955-1959
professional advocate
died on January 15, 1994, in Pampa, Tex.
interment in Fairview Cemetery, Pampa, Tex.

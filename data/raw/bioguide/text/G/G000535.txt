GUTIÉRREZ, Luis V., a Representative from Illinois
born in Chicago, Cook County, Ill., December 10, 1953
B.A., Northeastern Illinois University, DeKalb, Ill., 1974
teacher
social worker, Illinois state department of children and family services
administrative assistant, Chicago, Ill., mayor’s office subcommittee on infrastructure, 1984-1985
co-founder, West Town-26th Ward Independent Political Organization, 1985
alderman, Chicago, Ill., city council, 1986-1993, president pro tem, 1989-1992
delegate, Democratic National Committee, 1984
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-present).

GRAVES, Samuel, a Representative from Missouri
born in Tarkio, Atchison County, Mo., November 7, 1963
graduated from Tarkio High School, Tarkio, Mo., 1982
B.A., University of Missouri, Columbia, Mo., 1986
member of the Missouri state house of representatives, 1992-1994
member of the Missouri state senate, 1994-2000
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present)
chair, Committee on Small Business (One Hundred Twelfth and One Hundred Thirteenth Congresses).

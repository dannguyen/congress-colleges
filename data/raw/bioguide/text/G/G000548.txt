GARRETT, Scott, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Englewood, Bergen County, N.J., July 9, 1959
graduated from High Point Regional High School, Sussex, N.J., 1977
B.A., Montclair State University, Montclair, N.J., 1981
J.D., Rutgers University Law School, Camden, N.J., 1984
lawyer, private practice
member of the New Jersey state general assembly, 1990-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

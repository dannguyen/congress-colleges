GINGREY, Phil, a Representative from Georgia
born in Augusta, Richmond County, Ga., July 10, 1942
graduated from St. Thomas Aquinas High School, Augusta, Ga., 1960
B.S., Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, Ga., 1965
M.D., Medical College of Georgia, Augusta, Ga., 1969
physician
member of the Marietta, Ga., board of education, 1993-1997
member of the Georgia state senate, 1999-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-January 3, 2015)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate in 2014.

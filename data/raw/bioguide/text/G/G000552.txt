GOHMERT, Louie, a Representative from Texas
born in Pittsburg, Camp County, Tex., August 18, 1953
graduated from Mount Pleasant High School, Mount Pleasant, Tex., 1971
B.A., Texas A&M University, College Station, Tex., 1975
J.D., Baylor University, Waco, Tex., 1977
United States Army, 1978-1982
lawyer, private practice
district judge, Smith County, Tex., 1992-2002
chief justice, twelfth circuit court, 2002-2003
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Ninth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2005-present).

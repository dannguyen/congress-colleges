GRAYSON, Alan, a Representative from Florida
born in the Bronx, Bronx County, N.Y., March 13, 1958
graduated from Bronx High School of Science, Bronx, N.Y.
A.B., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1978
M.P.P., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1983
J.D, Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1983
assistant, U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals, Washington, D.C.
lawyer, private practice
businessman
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 2006
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

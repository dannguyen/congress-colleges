GUTHRIE, Brett, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Florence, Lauderdale County, Ala., February 18, 1964
graduated from Bradshaw High School, Florence, Ala., 1982
B.S., United States Military Academy, West Point, N.Y., 1987
M.P.P.M., Yale University, New Haven, Conn., 1997
United States Army, 1987-1990
United States Army Reserve, 1990-2002
business executive
member of the Kentucky state senate, 1999-2008
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2009-present).

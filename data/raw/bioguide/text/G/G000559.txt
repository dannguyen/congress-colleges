GARAMENDI, John, a Representative from California
born in Camp Blanding, Clay County, Fla., January 24, 1945
B.A., University of California, Berkeley, Calif., 1966
M.B.A., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1970
businessman
rancher
United States Peace Corps, 1966-1968
member of the California state assembly 1974-1976
member of the California state senate 1976-1990
California state insurance commissioner, 1991-1994, 2002-2006
deputy secretary, United States Department of the Interior, 1995-1998
lieutenant governor of California, 2007-2009
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Ellen Tauscher, and reelected to the two succeeding Congresses (November 3, 2009-present).

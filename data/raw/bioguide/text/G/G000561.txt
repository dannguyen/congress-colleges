GOODWIN, Carte P., a Senator from West Virginia
born on February 27, 1974, in Mt. Alto, West Virginia
B.A., Marietta College, 1996
J.D., Emory University School of Law, 1999
law clerk, United States Court of Appeals, Fourth Judicial Circuit 1999-2000
lawyer, private practice
general counsel to West Virginia Governor Joe Manchin, III 2005-2009
resumed private practice of law
appointed as a Democrat to the United States Senate on July 16, 2010, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Robert C. Byrd
took the oath of office on July 20, 2010
and served from July 16, 2010, to November 15, 2010, a successor having been chosen in a special election
not a candidate for election to the unexpired portion of the term.

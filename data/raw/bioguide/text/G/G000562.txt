GARDNER, Cory, a Senator and a Representative from Colorado
born in Yuma, Yuma County, Colo., on August 22, 1974
graduated from Yuma High School, Yuma, Colo., 1993
B.A., Colorado State University, Fort Collins, Colo., 1997
J.D., University of Colorado, Boulder, Colo., 2001
agricultural advocate
staff, United States Senator Wayne Allard of Colorado, 2002-2005
member of the Colorado state house of representatives, 2005-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the House of Representatives but was elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 2014 for the term ending January 3, 2021.

GIBSON, Christopher, a Representative from New York
born in Rockville Centre, Nassau County, N.Y., May 13, 1964
graduated from Ichabod Crane High School, Kinderhook, N.Y.
B.A., Siena College, Loudonville, N.Y., 1986
M.P.A., Cornell University, Ithaca, N.Y., 1995
Ph.D., Cornell University, Ithaca, N.Y., 1998
New York National Guard, 1981-1986
United States Army, 1986-2010
faculty, United States Military Academy, West Point, N.Y.
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

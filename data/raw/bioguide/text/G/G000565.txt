GOSAR, Paul, a Representative from Arizona
born in Rock Springs, Sweetwater County, Wyo., November 22, 1958
graduated from Pinedale High School, Pinedale, Wyo., 1977
B.S., Creighton University, Omaha, Nebr., 1981
D.D.S., Creighton University, Omaha, Nebr., 1985
dentist
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

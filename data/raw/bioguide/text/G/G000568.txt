GRIFFITH, H. Morgan, a Representative from Virginia
born in Philadelphia, Philadelphia County, Pa., March 15, 1958
graduated from Andrew Lewis High School, Salem, Va.
B.A., Emory & Henry College, Emory, Va., 1980
J.D., Washington & Lee University School of Law, Lexington, Va., 1983
lawyer, private practice
member of Virginia state house of representatives, 1994-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

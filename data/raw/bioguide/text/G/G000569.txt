GRIMM, Michael G., a Representative from New York
born in New York, New York County, N.Y., February 7, 1970
graduated from Archbishop Molloy High School, Briarwood, New York, 1988
B.B.A, Baruch College, City University of New York, N.Y., 1994
J.D. New York Law School, New York, N.Y., 2002
United States Marine Corps, 1989-1990
United States Marine Corps Reserve, 1990-1997
Federal Bureau of Investigation, 1997-2006, business owner
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2015)
re-elected to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress, but did not take his seat, resigning on January 5, 2015, prior to the opening of the new Congress.

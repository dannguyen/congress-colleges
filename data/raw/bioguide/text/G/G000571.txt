GABBARD, Tulsi, a Representative from Hawaii
born in Leloaloa, American Samoa, April 12, 1981
B.S.B.A., Hawaii Pacific University, Honolulu, Hawaii, 2009
member of the Hawaii state house of representatives, 2002-2004
Hawaii Army National Guard, 2003-present
staff, Senator Daniel Akaka of Hawaii
member of the Honolulu, Hawaii, city council, 2010-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

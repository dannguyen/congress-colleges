HAHN, John, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in New Hanover Township, Montgomery County, Pa., October 30, 1776
attended the common schools
studied medicine and practiced
elected as a Republican to the Fourteenth Congress (March 4, 1815-March 3, 1817)
resumed the practice of medicine and also engaged in agricultural pursuits
died in New Hanover Township February 26, 1823
interment in Falkner Swamp Graveyard.

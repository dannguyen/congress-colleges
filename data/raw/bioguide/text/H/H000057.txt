HALL, Joshua Gilman, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Wakefield, Carroll County, N.H., November 5, 1828
attended Gilmanton Academy, and was graduated from Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H., in 1851
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1855 and practiced in Wakefield and Dover, N.H.
solicitor of the county of Strafford 1862-1874
mayor of Dover in 1866 and 1867
member of the State senate 1871 and 1872
served in the State house of representatives in 1874
attorney of the United States for the district of Hampshire from April 1874 to February 1879
elected as a Republican to the Forty-sixth and Forty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1879-March 3, 1883)
resumed the practice of his profession
died in Dover, Strafford County, N.H., on October 31, 1898
interment in Pine Hill Cemetery.

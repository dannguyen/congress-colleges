HALL, Ralph Moody, a Representative from Texas
born in Fate, Rockwall County, Tex., May 3, 1923
graduated from Rockwall High School, Rockwall, Tex.
attended Texas Christian University, Fort Worth, Tex., 1943
attended University of Texas, Austin, Tex., 1946-1947
LL.B., Southern Methodist University, Dallas, Tex., 1951
United States Navy, 1942-1945
lawyer, private practice
business executive
Rockwall County, Tex., judge, 1950-1962
member of the Texas state senate, 1962-1972
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-seventh and to the eleven succeeding Congresses
changed from a Democrat to a Republican on January 5, 2004 (January 3, 1981-January 5, 2004)
served as a Republican in the One Hundred Eighth, and reelected to the five succeeding Congresses (January 5, 2004-January 3, 2015)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014
chair, Committee on Science, Space, and Technology (One Hundred Twelfth Congress).

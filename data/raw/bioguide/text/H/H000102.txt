HAMILTON, Andrew Holman, a Representative from Indiana
born in Fort Wayne, Ind., June 7, 1834
attended the common schools and was graduated from Wabash College, Crawfordsville, Ind., in 1854
studied law at Harvard University
was admitted to the bar in 1859 and commenced practice in Fort Wayne, Ind.
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-fourth and Forty-fifth Congresses (March 4, 1875-March 3, 1879)
resumed the practice of law
died in Fort Wayne, Ind., May 9, 1895
interment in Lindenwood Cemetery.

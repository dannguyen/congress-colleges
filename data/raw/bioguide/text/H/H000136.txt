HAMMONS, David, a Representative from Maine
born in Cornish, Maine, May 12, 1808
attended the common schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1836 and commenced practice in Lovell, Oxford County, Maine
member of the State senate in 1840 and 1841
elected as a Democrat to the Thirtieth Congress (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1849)
continued the practice of law until his death in Bethel, Oxford County, Maine, on November 7, 1888
interment in Woodland Cemetery.

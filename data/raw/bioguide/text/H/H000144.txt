HANCE, Kent Ronald, a Representative from Texas
born in Dimmitt, Castro County, Tex., November 14, 1942
attended the public schools of Dimmitt
B.B.A., Texas Tech University, 1965
LL.B., University of Texas School of Law, 1968
admitted to the Texas bar in 1968
lawyer, private practice
professor, Texas Tech University, 1968-1973
member of the Texas state senate, 1974-1978
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-sixth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1985)
was not a candidate for reelection to the Ninety-ninth Congress in 1984, but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate
changed party affiliation to Republican in 1985
was an unsuccessful candidate for the Republican nomination for governor of Texas in 1986 and 1990
railroad commissioner of Texas, 1987-1990
chancellor of Texas Tech University system, 2006-2014.

HANCHETT, Luther, a Representative from Wisconsin
born in Middlebury, Portage County, Ohio, October 25, 1825
attended the common schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1846 and commenced practice in Fremont, Ohio
moved to Portage County, Wis., in 1849
engaged in lumber and mining enterprises
county attorney two years
member of the State senate 1856-1860
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-seventh Congress and served from March 4, 1861, until his death in Plover, Portage County, Wis., November 24, 1862
interment in Plover Cemetery.

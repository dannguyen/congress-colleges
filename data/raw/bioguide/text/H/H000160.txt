HANNA, John, a Representative from Indiana
born near Indianapolis, Ind., September 3, 1827
pursued classical studies
was graduated from the Indiana Asbury (now De Pauw) University, Greencastle, Ind., in 1850
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Greencastle
mayor of Greencastle 1851-1854
moved to Kansas
was a member of its Territorial legislature in 1857 and 1858
returned to Indiana
United States district attorney 1861-1869
elected as a Republican to the Forty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1877-March 3, 1879)
died in Plainfield, Ind., October 24, 1882
interment in Forest Hill Cemetery, Greencastle, Ind.

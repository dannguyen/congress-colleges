HARMANSON, John Henry, a Representative from Louisiana
born in Norfolk, Va., January 15, 1803
pursued classical studies and was graduated from Jefferson College, Washington, Miss.
moved to Avoyelles Parish, La., in 1830 and engaged in agricultural pursuits
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
member of the State senate in 1844
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-ninth, Thirtieth, and Thirty-first Congresses and served from March 4, 1845, until his death in New Orleans, La., October 24, 1850
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Post Office Department (Twenty-ninth Congress)
interment in Moreau Plantation Cemetery, Pointe Coupee Parish, La.

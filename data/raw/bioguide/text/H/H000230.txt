HARRINGTON, Michael Joseph, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Salem, Essex County, Mass., September 2, 1936

lawyer, private practice

member of the Massachusetts Legislature, 1964-1969

elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-first Congress by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of United States Representative William H. Bates, and reelected to the four succeeding Congresses (September 30, 1969-January 3, 1979)
was not a candidate for reelection to the Ninety-sixth Congress in 1978
is a resident of Beverly, Mass.

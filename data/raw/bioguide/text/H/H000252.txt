HARRIS, Sampson Willis, a Representative from Alabama
born in Elbert County, Ga., February 23, 1809
obtained his early education from his mother and was graduated from the University of Georgia at Athens in 1828
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1830 and commenced practice in Athens, Ga.
member of the State house of representatives in 1834 and 1835
moved to Wetumpka, Ala., in 1838
elected solicitor of the eighth circuit in 1841
member of the State senate in 1844 and 1845
elected as a Democrat to the Thirtieth and to the four succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1857)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1856
died in Washington, D.C., April 1, 1857
interment in Oconee Cemetery, Athens, Ga.

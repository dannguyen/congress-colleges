HARRISON, John Scott, (son of President William Henry Harrison of Ohio, grandson of Benjamin Harrison of Virginia, father of President Benjamin Harrison of Indiana, and great-grandfather of William Henry Harrison [1896- ]), a Representative from Ohio
born in Vincennes, Knox County, Ind., October 4, 1804
completed preparatory studies
studied medicine but abandoned the profession
engaged in agricultural pursuits
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-third Congress and reelected as an Opposition Party candidate to the Thirty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1857)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection
retired to his estate Point Farm, near North Bend, Ohio, and died there May 25, 1878
interment in the Harrison Tomb, North Bend, Ohio.

HARRISON, Samuel Smith, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Virginia in 1780
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
moved to Kittanning, Armstrong County, Pa.
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-third and Twenty-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1833-March 3, 1837)
resumed the practice of law
died in Kittanning, Pa., April 1853
interment in Old Kittanning Cemetery.

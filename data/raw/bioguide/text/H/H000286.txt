HART, Emanuel Bernard, a Representative from New York
born in New York City October 27, 1809
attended the public schools and prepared for college
engaged in mercantile pursuits
colonel in the militia
member of the board of aldermen in 1845
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-second Congress (March 4, 1851-March 3, 1853)
appointed by President Buchanan surveyor of the port of New York and served from 1857 to 1861
member of the city board of assessors
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1868 and practiced
president of Mount Sinai Hospital 1870-1876
commissioner of immigration 1870-1873
excise commissioner in 1879
treasurer of the Society for the Relief of Poor Hebrews
died in New York City August 29, 1897
interment in Cypress Hills Cemetery, Brooklyn, N.Y.

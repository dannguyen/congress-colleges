HART, Thomas Charles, a Senator from Connecticut
born in Davidson, Genesee County, Mich., June 12, 1877
attended various public schools in Michigan
graduated from the United States Naval Academy, Annapolis, Md., in 1897
served in the Regular Navy from graduation until 1945, when placed on the inactive list as an admiral (retired)
his naval career covered service afloat during the Spanish-American War and both World Wars
upon retirement, settled in Sharon, Conn.
appointed on February 15, 1945, as a Republican to the United States Senate to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Francis T. Maloney and served from February 15, 1945, to November 5, 1946
was not a candidate for election to the vacancy in 1946
resided in Sharon, Conn., until his death there on July 4, 1971
interment in Arlington National Cemetery, Arlington, Va.

HARTLEY, Fred Allan, Jr., a Representative from New Jersey
born in Harrison, Hudson County, N.J., February 22, 1902
attended the public schools, Rutgers Prep, and Rutgers University, New Brunswick, N.J.
library commissioner of Kearny, N.J., in 1923 and 1924
police and fire commissioner 1924-1928
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-first and to the nine succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1929-January 3, 1949)
chairman, Committee on Education and Labor (Eightieth Congress)
co-sponsor of the Taft-Hartley Act of 1947
was not a candidate for renomination in 1948
engaged as a business consultant
died in Linwood, N.J., May 11, 1969
interment in Fairmount Cemetery, Newark, N.J.

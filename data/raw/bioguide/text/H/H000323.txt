HASTERT, John Dennis, a Representative from Illinois
born in Aurora, Kane County, Ill., January 2, 1942
B.A., Wheaton College, Wheaton, Ill., 1964
M.S., Northern Illinois University, DeKalb, Ill.,1967
teacher
athletic coach
business owner
member of the Illinois state house of representatives, 1980-1986
elected as a Republican to the One Hundredth and to the ten succeeding Congresses until his resignation on November 26, 2007 (January 3, 1987-November 26, 2007)
Speaker of the House (One Hundred Sixth through One Hundred Ninth Congresses).

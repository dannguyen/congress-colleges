HASTINGS, Alcee Lamar, a Representative from Florida
born in Altamonte Springs, Seminole County, Fla., September 5, 1936
graduated Crooms Academy, Sanford, Fla, 1953
B.A., Fisk University, Nashville, Tenn., 1958
attended Howard University School of Law, Washington, D.C., 1958-1960
J.D., Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University, Tallahassee, 1963
lawyer, private practice
Broward County, Fla., circuit court judge, 1977-1979
United States district judge for the Southern District of Florida, 1979-1989
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-present).

HASTINGS, Richard Norman (Doc), a Representative from Washington
born in Spokane, Spokane County, Wash., February 7, 1941
graduated from Pasco High School, Pasco, Wash., 1959
attended Columbia Basin College, Pasco, Wash., 1959-1961
attended Central Washington State University, Ellensburg, Wash., 1964
United States Army Reserves, 1963-1969
small businessman
member of the Washington state house of representatives, 1979-1987
delegate, Republican National Convention, 1976-1984
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 1992
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and reelected to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014
chair, Committee on Standards of Official Conduct (One Hundred Ninth Congress)
chair, Committee on Natural Resources (One Hundred Twelfth and One Hundred Thirteenth Congresses).

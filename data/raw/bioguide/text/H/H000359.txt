HAVENS, Jonathan Nicoll, a Representative from New York
born on Shelter Island, Suffolk County, N.Y., June 18, 1757
pursued classical studies and was graduated from Yale College in 1777
member of the State assembly 1786-1795
town clerk 1783-1787
elected to the State convention which ratified the Federal Constitution January 8, 1788
took a great interest in popular education and was chairman of the committee for establishing public schools in New York in 1795
justice of the peace of Suffolk County in 1795
elected as a Republican to the Fourth, Fifth, and Sixth Congresses and served from March 4, 1795, until his death on Shelter Island, N.Y., October 25, 1799
interment in the south burial ground of the Presbyterian Church.

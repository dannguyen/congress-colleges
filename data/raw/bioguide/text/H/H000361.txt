HAWES, Aylett, (uncle of Richard Hawes, Albert Gallatin Hawes, and Aylett Hawes Buckner), a Representative from Virginia
born in Culpeper County, Va., April 21, 1768
pursued classical studies
studied medicine and finished his education in Edinburg, Scotland
returned to Virginia and practiced medicine
also engaged as a planter
member of the State house of delegates 1802-1806
elected as a Republican to the Twelfth, Thirteenth, and Fourteenth Congresses (March 4, 1811-March 3, 1817)
resumed the practice of medicine and was also an extensive landowner
died on his farm in Rappahannock County, Va., August 31, 1833
interment on a farm near Sperryville, Rappahannock County, Va.

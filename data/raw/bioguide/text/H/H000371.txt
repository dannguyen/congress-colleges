HAWKINS, Joseph, a Representative from New York
born in that State on November 14, 1781
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Henderson, N.Y., and also engaged in agricultural pursuits
elected as an Anti-Jacksonian to the Twenty-first Congress (March 4, 1829-March 3, 1831)
died in Henderson, Jefferson County, N.Y., April 20, 1832
interment in Clark Cemetery.

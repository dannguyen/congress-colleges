HAZARD, Nathaniel, a Representative from Rhode Island
born in Newport, R.I., in 1776
was graduated from Brown University, Providence, R.I., in 1792
member of the State house of representatives in 1818 and 1819 and served as speaker
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth Congress and served from March 4, 1819, until his death in Washington, D.C., December 17, 1820
interment in the Congressional Cemetery.

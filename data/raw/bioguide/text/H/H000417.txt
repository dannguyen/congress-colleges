HAZELTINE, Ira Sherwin, a Representative from Missouri
born in Andover, Windsor County, Vt., July 13, 1821
attended the common schools and pursued an academic course
moved to Richland Center, Wis., in 1842
taught school in Natchez, Miss., for three years
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Richland Center, Wis.
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1860
member of the State house of representatives 1867-1869
engaged in farming near Springfield, Greene County, Mo., in 1870
elected as a Greenbacker to the Forty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1881-March 3, 1883)
unsuccessful for reelection in 1882
laid out and named the city of Richland Center, Wis., in 1851 and practiced law there
died near Springfield, Mo., January 13, 1899
interment in Hazelwood Cemetery, Springfield, Mo.

HEFLEY, Joel M., a Representative from Colorado
born in Ardmore, Carter County, Okla., April 18, 1935
B.A., Oklahoma Baptist University, Shawnee, Okla., 1957
M.S., Oklahoma State University, Stillwater, Okla., 1962
Gates Fellow, Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1984
management consultant
executive director, Community Planning and Research Council, 1966-1986
executive director, Pikes Peak Health Planning, 1971-1973
member of the Colorado state house of representatives, 1977-1978
member of the Colorado state senate, 1979-1986
elected as a Republican to the One Hundredth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1987-January 3, 2007)
chair, Committee on Standards (One Hundred Seventh and One Hundred Eighth Congresses)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Tenth Congress in 2006.

HENRY, Robert Pryor, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Henrys Mills, Scott County, Ky. (then a part of Virginia), November 24, 1788
pursued classical studies and was graduated from Transylvania College, Lexington, Ky.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1809 and commenced practice in Georgetown, Ky.
prosecuting attorney in 1819
served in the War of 1812
moved to Hopkinsville in 1817
elected as a Jackson Republican to the Eighteenth Congress and reelected as a Jacksonian candidate to the Nineteenth Congress and served from March 4, 1823, until his death in Hopkinsville, Ky., August 25, 1826
interment in Pioneer Cemetery.

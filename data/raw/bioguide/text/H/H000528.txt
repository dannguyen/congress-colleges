HERGER, Walter William (Wally), a Representative from California
born in Yuba City, Calif., May 20, 1945
graduated from East Nicolaus High School, Nicolaus, Calif.
attended California State University, Sacramento, Calif.
cattle rancher
businessman
member, East Nicolaus High School Board of Trustees, Nicolaus, Calif., 1977-1980
member of the California state assembly, 1980-1986
elected as a Republican to the One Hundredth and to the twelve succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1987-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

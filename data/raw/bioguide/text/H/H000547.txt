HERTEL, Dennis Mark, a Representative from Michigan
born in Detroit, Wayne County, Mich., December 7, 1948
attended the public schools
graduated from Denby High School, Detroit, 1967
B.A., Eastern Michigan University, Ypsilanti, 1971
J.D., Wayne State University, Detroit, 1974
admitted to the Michigan bar in 1975 and commenced practice in Detroit
served in the Michigan house of representatives, 1975-1980
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1981-January 3, 1993)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
is a resident of Harper Woods, Mich.

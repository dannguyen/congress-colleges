HILL, Clement Sidney, a Representative from Kentucky
born near Lebanon, Marion County, Ky., February 13, 1813
pursued academic studies
attended St. Marys College, St. Mary, Ky.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1837 and commenced practice in Lebanon, Ky.
member of the State house of representatives in 1839
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
resumed the practice of law in Lebanon, Ky., where he died January 5, 1892
interment in St. Augustines Cemetery.

HILL, John, (cousin of John Thomas Harris), a Representative from Virginia
born in New Canton, Buckingham County, Va., July 18, 1800
completed preparatory studies and was graduated from Washington Academy (now Washington and Lee University), Lexington, Va., in 1818
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1821 and practiced
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1839-March 3, 1841)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1840 to the Twenty-seventh Congress
resumed the practice of law
member of the Virginia constitutional convention 1850-1851
Commonwealth attorney for several years
county judge of Buckingham County 1870-1879
died at Buckingham Court House, Va., April 19, 1880
interment in the Presbyterian Cemetery.

HINCHEY, Maurice Dunlea, a Representative from New York
born in New York, N.Y., October 27, 1938
graduated from Saugerties High School, Saugerties, N.Y., 1956
B.S., State University of New York at New Paltz, New Paltz, N.Y., 1968, M.A., State University of New York at New Paltz, New Paltz, N.Y., 1970
United States Navy, 1956-1959
member of the New York state assembly, 1975-1993
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2013)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

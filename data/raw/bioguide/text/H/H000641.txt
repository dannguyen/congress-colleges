HINSON, Jon Clifton, a Representative from Mississippi
born in Tylertown, Walthall County, Miss., March 16, 1942
attended the Walthall County public schools
B.A., University of Mississippi, 1964
United States Marine Corps Reserve, 1964-1970
aide to United States Representative Charles Griffin of Mississippi, 1968-1973
aide to United States Representative Thad Cochran of Mississippi, 1973-1977
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-sixth and to the Ninety-seventh Congresses (January 3, 1979-April 13, 1981)
resigned on April 13, 1981
died on July 21, 1995, in Silver Spring, Md.
remains were cremated.

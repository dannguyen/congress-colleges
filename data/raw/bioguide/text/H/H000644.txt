HISE, Elijah, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Allegheny County, Pa., July 4, 1802
moved with his parents to Russellville, Logan County, Ky., when young
completed preparatory studies
attended Transylvania University, Lexington, Ky.
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice
member of the State house of representatives in 1829
unsuccessful Democratic candidate for Lieutenant Governor in 1836
Chargé dAffaires to Guatemala March 31, 1848, to June 21, 1849
chief justice of the court of appeals of Kentucky
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-ninth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Henry Grider
reelected to the Fortieth Congress and served from December 3, 1866, until his death in Russellville, Ky., May 8, 1867
interment in Maple Grove Cemetery.

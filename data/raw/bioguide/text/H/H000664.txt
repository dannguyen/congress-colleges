HOBLITZELL, Fetter Schrier, a Representative from Maryland
born in Cumberland, Md., October 7, 1838
attended the primary schools and was graduated from the Allegany Academy, Cumberland, Md.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1859 and commenced practice in Baltimore, Md.
during the Civil War served as a private in the First Maryland Regiment of Infantry, Confederate Army
resumed the practice of law
member of the State house of delegates in 1870 and 1876
reelected in 1878 and served as speaker
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-seventh and Forty-eighth Congresses (March 4, 1881-March 3, 1885)
city counselor of Baltimore in 1888 and 1889
resumed the practice of law
died in Baltimore, Md., May 2, 1900
interment in Loudon Park Cemetery.

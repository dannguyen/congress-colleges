HOEKSTRA, Peter, a Representative from Michigan
born in Groningen, The Netherlands, October 30, 1953
graduated from Holland Christian High School, Holland, Mich.
B.A., Hope College, Holland, Mich., 1975
M.B.A., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1977
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Third and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2011)
chair, Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence (One Hundred Eighth and One Hundred Ninth Congresses)
not a candidate for reelection in 2010, but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination for governor of Michigan.

HOLBROCK, Greg John, a Representative from Ohio
born in Hamilton, Butler County, Ohio, June 21, 1906
graduated from St. Xavier High School
attended Notre Dame University, South Bend, Ind.
Ph.D., Xavier University, Cincinnati, Ohio, 1928
J.D., University of Cincinnati, Cincinnati, Ohio, 1932
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-seventh Congress (January 3, 1941-January 3, 1943)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Seventy-eighth Congress in 1942
United States Navy, 1943-1946
delegate, Democratic National Conventions, 1948 and 1960
chairman, Butler County Democratic Executive Committee, 1950-1966
died on September 4, 1992, in Hamilton, Ohio
interment in St. Stephen's Mausoleum, Hamilton, Ohio.

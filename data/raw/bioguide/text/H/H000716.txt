HOLLAND, Edward Everett, a Representative from Virginia
born near Suffolk, Nansemond County, Va., February 26, 1861
attended private schools, Richmond (Va.) College, and was graduated from the University of Virginia at Charlottesville
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1882 and commenced practice in Suffolk, Va.
mayor of Suffolk 1885-1887
Commonwealth attorney for Nansemond County 1887-1907
elected president of the Farmers Bank of Nansemond in 1892
member of the State senate 1907-1911
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-second and to the four succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1911-March 3, 1921)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1920
resumed his banking pursuits
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1920 and 1924
member of the State senate 1930-1941
died in Suffolk, Va., on October 23, 1941
interment in Cedar Hill Cemetery, Suffolk, Va.

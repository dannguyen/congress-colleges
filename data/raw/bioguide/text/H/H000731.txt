HOLMAN, Rufus Cecil, a Senator from Oregon
born in Portland, Oreg., October 14, 1877
attended the public schools
school teacher 1896-1898
engaged in agricultural pursuits, steamboating, bookkeeping, accounting, and auditing 1899-1910
in 1910 engaged in the manufacture of record books and paper boxes and in the ice and cold storage business in Portland, Oreg.
member of a variety of civic commissions
State treasurer of Oregon 1931-1939
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate and served from January 3, 1939, to January 3, 1945
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1944
resumed management of Portland Paper Box Co., Portland, Oreg., and of a farm near Molalla, Oreg.
died in Portland, Oreg., November 27, 1959
interment in Riverview Cemetery.

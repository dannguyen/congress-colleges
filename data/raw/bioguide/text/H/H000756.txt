HOOK, Enos, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Waynesburg, Greene County, Pa., December 3, 1804
received a limited schooling
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1826 and commenced practice in Waynesburg, Pa.
member of the State house of representatives in 1837 and 1838
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth and Twenty-seventh Congresses and served from March 4, 1839, to April 18, 1841, when he resigned
died in Waynesburg, Pa., July 15, 1841
interment in Green Mount Cemetery.

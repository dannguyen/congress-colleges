HOOLEY, Darlene, a Representative from Oregon
born in Williston, Williams County, N. Dak., April 4, 1939
B.S., Oregon State University, Corvallis, 1961
teacher, 1961-1975
West Linn, Oreg., city council, 1977-1980
member of the Oregon state house of representatives, 1980-1986
Clackamas County, Oreg., board of commissioners, 1987-1996
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2009)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

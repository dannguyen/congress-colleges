HOOPER, Benjamin Stephen, a Representative from Virginia
born near Buckingham, Buckingham County, Va., March 6, 1835
attended the common schools
engaged in mercantile pursuits and the manufacture of tobacco
served in the Confederate Army during the Civil War
elected as a Readjuster to the Forty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1883-March 3, 1885)
resumed mercantile pursuits at Farmville, Va.
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1888
died in Farmville, Prince Edward County, Va., on January 17, 1898
interment in the Farmville Cemetery.

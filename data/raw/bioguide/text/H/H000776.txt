HOPKINS, Larry Jones, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Detroit, Wayne County, Mich., October 25, 1933
attended the public schools of Wingo, Ky.
attended Murray State University, 1951-1954
served in the United States Marine Corps, 1954-1956
stockbroker, Hilliard & Lyons, Inc.
county clerk, Fayette, Ky., 1969
served in the Kentucky State house of representatives, 1972-1976
State senate, 1976-1978
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-sixth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1993)
unsuccessful candidate in 1991 for governor of Kentucky
was not a candidate for renomination in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
director, Tobacco Division, Agricultural Marketing Service, United States Department of Agriculture
is a resident of Lexington, Ky.

HORN, Henry, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Philadelphia, Pa., in 1786
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced law in Philadelphia
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-second Congress (March 4, 1831-March 3, 1833)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Twenty-third Congress in 1832
resumed the practice of law in Philadelphia
collector of customs at Philadelphia from May 12, 1845, until August 4, 1846
died in Flourtown, Montgomery County, Pa., January 12, 1862
interment in Woodlands Cemetery, Philadelphia, Pa.

HORSEY, Outerbridge, a Senator from Delaware
born near Laurel, Sussex County, Del., March 5, 1777
received a liberal education
studied law
admitted to the bar in 1807 and commenced practice in Wilmington, Del.
member, State house of representatives 1800-1802
attorney general of Delaware 1806-1810
elected in 1810 as a Federalist to the United States Senate to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Samuel White
reelected in 1815 and served from January 12, 1810, to March 3, 1821
was not a candidate for reelection
chairman, Committee on the District of Columbia (Sixteenth Congress)
retired to his wifes estate, Needwood, near Petersville, Frederick County, Md., and died there June 9, 1842
interment in St. John's Cemetery, Frederick, Md.

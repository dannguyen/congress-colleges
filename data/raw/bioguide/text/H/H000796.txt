HORSFORD, Jerediah, a Representative from New York
born in Charlotte, Chittenden County, Vt., March 8, 1791
attended the common schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
served during the War of 1812
missionary to the Seneca Indians at Moscow, N.Y., in 1815
held several local offices
member of the State assembly in 1831
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-second Congress (March 4, 1851-March 3, 1853)
served as colonel of light infantry in the State militia
moved to Livonia, N.Y., in 1863
resumed agricultural pursuits
died in Livonia, Livingston County, N.Y., January 14, 1875
interment in Moscow Cemetery, Moscow (now Leicester), N.Y.

HOTCHKISS, Julius, a Representative from Connecticut
born in Waterbury, Conn., July 11, 1810
attended the common schools
engaged in manufacturing pursuits
mayor of Waterbury in 1852
member of the State house of representatives in 1851 and 1858
elected as a Democrat to the Fortieth Congress (March 4, 1867-March 3, 1869)
Lieutenant Governor of Connecticut in 1870
died in Middletown, Conn., December 23, 1878
interment in Pine Grove Cemetery.

HOUGH, David, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Norwich, Conn., March 13, 1753
attended the common schools
ship carpenter
moved to Lebanon, N.H., in 1778
member of the State house of representatives in 1788, 1789, and 1794
justice of the peace
colonel of militia
delegate to the State constitutional convention in 1783
commissioner of valuation in 1798
elected as a Federalist to the Eighth and Ninth Congresses (March 4, 1803-March 3, 1807)
engaged in agricultural pursuits
died in Lebanon, N.H., April 18, 1831
interment in the cemetery in the southern vicinity of Lebanon.

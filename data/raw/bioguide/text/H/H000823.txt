HOUSTON, Henry Aydelotte, a Representative from Delaware
born in Dagsboro Hundred, Sussex County, Del., July 10, 1847
attended the public schools and Newark Academy
engaged in agricultural pursuits
moved to Missouri in 1872
returned to Delaware and settled in Millsboro in 1875 and taught school for five years
engaged in mercantile pursuits
member of the Sussex County School Commission
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1903-March 3, 1905)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1904
engaged in lumber manufacturing and banking in Millsboro, Del.
died in Milford, Del., April 5, 1925
interment in Brotherhood Cemetery, Millsboro, Del.

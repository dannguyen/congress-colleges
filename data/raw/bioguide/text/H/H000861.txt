HOWELL, Elias, (father of James Bruen Howell), a Representative from Ohio
born in New Jersey in 1792
attended the public schools
moved to Newark, Ohio, in 1819
member of the State senate 1830-1832
elected as an Anti-Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1837)
was not a candidate for renomination
died near Newark, Ohio, in May 1844.

HUBARD, Edmund Wilcox, a Representative from Virginia
born near Farmville, Buckingham County, Va., February 20, 1806
attended private schools and the University of Virginia at Charlottesville
engaged in agricultural pursuits
justice of the peace
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-seventh, Twenty-eighth, and Twenty-ninth Congresses (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1847)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1846
resumed agricultural pursuits
colonel of a militia regiment in 1864
appraiser of the Confederate Government to regulate the value of the Confederate dollar
died at his home near Farmville, Buckingham County, Va., December 9, 1878
interment in the family cemetery near his home.

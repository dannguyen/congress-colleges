HUBBARD, Demas, Jr., a Representative from New York
born in Winfield, Herkimer County, N.Y., January 17, 1806
attended the public schools and pursued an academic course
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Smyrna, N.Y., in 1835
member of the State assembly 1838-1840
supervisor of Smyrna 1859-1864
elected chairman of the board of supervisors of Chenango County, N.Y.
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1865-March 3, 1867)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1866
resumed the practice of his profession
died in Smyrna, Chenango County, N.Y., September 2, 1873
interment in Smyrna East Cemetery.

HUBBARD, Elbert Hamilton, (son of Asahel Wheeler Hubbard), a Representative from Iowa
born in Rushville, Rush County, Ind., August 19, 1849
attended the public schools and was instructed by a private tutor
was graduated from Yale College in 1872
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1874 and commenced practice in Sioux City, Iowa
member of the State house of representatives in 1882
served in the State senate 1900-1902
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-ninth and to the three succeeding Congresses and served from March 4, 1905, until his death in Sioux City, Iowa, June 4, 1912
interment in Floyd Cemetery.

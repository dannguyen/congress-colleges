HUFFINGTON, Michael, a Representative from California
born in Dallas, Tex., September 3, 1947
attended public and private schools
B.A., B.S., Stanford University, 1970, M.B.A., Harvard University, 1972
chairman of a film production company
deputy assistant secretary for negotiations policy, Department of Defense, 1986-1987
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Third Congress (January 3, 1993-January 3, 1995)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1994, but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate.

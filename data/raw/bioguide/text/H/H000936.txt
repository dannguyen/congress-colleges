HULBURD, Calvin Tilden, a Representative from New York
born in Stockholm, St. Lawrence County, N.Y., June 5, 1809
completed preparatory studies
was graduated from Middlebury College, Vermont
attended Yale College Law School
was admitted to the bar in 1833
member of the New York assembly 1842-1844 and in 1862
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-eighth, Thirty-ninth, and Fortieth Congresses (March 4, 1863-March 3, 1869)
chairman, Committee on Public Expenditures (Thirty-eighth through Fortieth Congresses)
superintendent of construction of the New York post office
died in Brasher Falls, N.Y., on October 25, 1897
interment in Fairview Cemetery.

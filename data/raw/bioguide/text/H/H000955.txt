HUMPHREY, James Morgan, a Representative from New York
born in Holland, Erie County, N.Y., September 21, 1819
attended the common schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1847 and commenced practice in East Aurora, Erie County, N.Y.
district attorney for Erie County 1857-1859
member of the State senate in 1863 and 1864
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-ninth and Fortieth Congresses (March 4, 1865-March 3, 1869)
was not a candidate in 1869 for renomination
appointed to the superior court of Buffalo, N.Y., in 1871 and served until January 1, 1873
practiced his profession in Buffalo from 1873 to 1894, when he retired
died in Buffalo, N.Y., February 9, 1899
interment in Forest Lawn Cemetery.

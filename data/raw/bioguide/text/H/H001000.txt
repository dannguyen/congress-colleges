HUNTSMAN, Adam, a Representative from Tennessee
born in Charlotte County, Va.,, February 11, 1786
moved to Jackson, Tenn.
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1837)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1836 to the Twenty-fifth Congress
died August 23, 1849
interment in Old Salem Cemetery, Madison County, Tenn.

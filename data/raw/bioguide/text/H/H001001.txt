HUOT, Joseph Oliva, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Laconia, Belknap County, N.H., August 11, 1917
educated at Sacred Heart Parochial School and Laconia High School
supervisor in tabulating department of a manufacturer of knitting machines 1935-1956
member of Laconia Board of Education 1953-1959
advertising manager of newspaper 1956-1964
general manager of a weekly newspaper 1959-1964
served as mayor of Laconia 1959-1963
Democratic candidate for Congress in 1962
delegate, Democratic National Convention, 1964
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-ninth Congress (January 3, 1965-January 3, 1967)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1966 to the Ninetieth Congress
was a resident of Laconia, N.H., until his death there August 5, 1983
interment in Sacred Heart Cemetery, Laconia, N.H.

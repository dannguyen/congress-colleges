HUTTO, Earl Dewitt, a Representative from Florida
born in Midland City, Dale County, Ala., May 12, 1926
attended the Dale County public schools
B.S., Troy State University, 1949
graduate work in broadcasting, Northwestern University, Evanston, Ill., 1951
served in United States Navy, 1944-1946
worked as sports director and president of radio stations, 1954-1974
owner of an advertising agency, 1973-1979
elected to Florida house of representatives, 1972, reelected in 1974 and 1976
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-sixth and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1995)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourth Congress.

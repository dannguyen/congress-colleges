HONDA, Mike, a Representative from California
born in Walnut Grove, Solano County, Calif., June 27, 1941
graduated from San Jose High School, San Jose, Calif., 1959
B.A., San Jose State University, San Jose, Calif., 1968
M.A., San Jose State University, San Jose, Calif., 1974
United States Peace Corps, 1965-1967
member of the San Jose, Calif. planning commission, 1971-1981
member of the San Jose, Calif., unified school board, 1981-1990
member of the Santa Clara County, Calif., board of supervisors, 1990-1996
member of the California state assembly, 1997-2000
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present).

HALL, John, a Representative from New York
born in Baltimore, Md., July 23, 1948
graduated from Notre Dame High School, Elmira, N.Y.
attended Notre Dame University, Notre Dame, Ind., 1964-1965
attended Loyola College, Baltimore, Md., 1965-1966
musician
songwriter
business owner
member of the Ulster County, N.Y. legislature, 1990-1991
Saugerties Central School District board of education, Saugerties, N.Y., 1996-1998
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

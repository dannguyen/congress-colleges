HARE, Phil, a Representative from Illinois
born in Galesburg, Knox County, Ill., February 21, 1949
graduated from Alleman High School, Rock Island, Ill., 1967
attended Black Hawk Community College, Moline, Ill., 1967-1968
United States Army Reserve, 1969-1975
factory worker, 1969-1982
staff, United States Representative Lane Evans of Illinois, 1983-2006
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

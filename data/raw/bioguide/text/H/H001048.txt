HUNTER, Duncan Duane, (son of Duncan Lee Hunter), a Representative from California
born in San Diego, San Diego County, Calif., December 7, 1976
graduated from Granite Hills High School, El Cajon, Calif.
B.S., San Diego State University, San Diego, Calif., 2001
United States Marine Corps, 2002-2005
United States Marine Corps Reserve, 2005-present
real estate developer
businessman
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2009-present).

HAGAN, Kay, (niece of Lawton M. Chiles, Jr.), a Senator from North Carolina
born in Shelby, N.C., May 26, 1953
B.A., Florida State University, 1975
J.D., Wake Forest University, 1978
banker
North Carolina State senator, 1999-2009
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 2008, and served from January 3, 2009, to January 3, 2015
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 2014.

HERRERA BEUTLER, Jaime, a Representative from Washington
born in Glendale, Los Angeles County, Calif.
November 3, 1978
attended Seattle Pacific University, Seattle, Wash., 1996-1998
A.A., Bellevue Community College, Bellevue, Wash., 2003
B.A., University of Washington, Seattle, Wash., 2004
staff, United States Representative Cathy McMorris Rodgers of Washington, 2005-2007
member of Washington state house of representatives, 2007-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

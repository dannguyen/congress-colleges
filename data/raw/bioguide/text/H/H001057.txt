HUELSKAMP, Tim, a Representative from Kansas
born in Fowler, Meade County, Kans., November 11, 1968
B.A., College of Santa Fe, Santa Fe, N.M., 1991
Ph.D., American University, Washington, D.C., 1995
farmer
legislative analyst
member of Kansas state senate, 1996-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

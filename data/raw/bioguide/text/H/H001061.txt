HOEVEN, John, a Senator from North Dakota
born in Bismarck, N.D., March 13, 1957
graduated Dartmouth College, B.A., 1979
graduated Northwestern University, M.B.A., 1981
bank president and CEO
governor of North Dakota 2000-2010
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 2010 for the term ending January 3, 2017.

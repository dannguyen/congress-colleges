HORSFORD, Steven, a Representative from Nevada
born in Las Vegas, Clark County, Nev., April 29, 1973
graduated from Clark High School, Las Vegas, Nev.
attended University of Nevada, Reno, Nev.
professional advocate
business executive
member of the Nevada state senate, 2004-2012
majority leader, Nevada state senate, 2009-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress (January 3, 2013-January 3, 2015)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

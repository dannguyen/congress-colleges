HUFFMAN, Jared, a Representative from California
born in Independence, Jackson County, Mo., February 18, 1964
graduated from William Chrisman High School, Independence, Mo., 1982
B.A., University of California, Santa Barbara, Calif., 1986
J.D., Boston College, Newton, Mass., 1990
lawyer, private practice
senior attorney, Natural Resources Defense Council, 2001-2006
member of the Marin County, Calif., municipal water district board of directors, 2001-2006
member of the California state assembly, 2006-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

HARDY, Cresent Leo, a Representative from Nevada
born in Mesquite, Clark County, Nev., June 23, 1957
graduated from Virgin Valley High School, Mesquite, Nev., 1975
attended Dixie State College, St. George, Utah
business owner
member of the Mesquite, Nev., city council, 1997-2002
member of the Nevada state assembly, 2011-2014
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

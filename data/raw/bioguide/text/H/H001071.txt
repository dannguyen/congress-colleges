HICE, Jody Brownlow, a Representative from Georgia
born in Atlanta, Fulton County, Ga., April 22, 1960
graduated from Tucker High School, Tucker, Ga., 1978
B.A., Asbury College, Wilmore, Ky., 1982
M.Div., Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary, Fort Worth, Tex., 1986
D.Min., Luther Rice University and Seminary, Lithonia, Ga., 1988
pastor
talk radio host
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States House of Representatives in 2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

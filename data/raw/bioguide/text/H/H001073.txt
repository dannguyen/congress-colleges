HURD, William Ballard, a Representative from Texas
born in San Antonio, Bexar County, Tex., August 19, 1977
graduated from John Marshall High School, San Antonio, Tex., 1995
B.S., Texas Agricultural and Mechanical University, College Station, Tex., 2000
business owner
operations officer, Central Intelligence Agency, 2000-2009
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

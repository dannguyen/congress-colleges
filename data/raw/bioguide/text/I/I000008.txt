IKIRT, George Pierce, a Representative from Ohio
born near West Beaver, Columbiana County, Ohio, November 3, 1852
attended the public schools of New Lisbon, Ohio
taught school and studied law, but on account of ill health was compelled to abandon both
attended the Columbus Medical College
moved to Cincinnati
was graduated from the Cincinnati College of Medicine and Surgery in 1877 and practiced five years
went to New York City in 1882 and was graduated from the Bellevue Hospital Medical College in 1883
again resumed practice in East Liverpool, Ohio
unsuccessful candidate for election in 1888 to the Fifty-first Congress
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-third Congress (March 4, 1893-March 3, 1895)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1894
resumed the practice of medicine in East Liverpool, Ohio, and died there February 12, 1927
interment in Riverview Cemetery.

ISSA, Darrell, a Representative from California
born in Cleveland, Cuyahoga County, Ohio, November 1, 1953
B.A., Siena Heights College, Adrian, Mich., 1976
United States Army, 1970-1980
business owner
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present)
chair, Committee on Oversight and Government Reform (One Hundred Twelfth and One Hundred Thirteenth Congresses).

ISRAEL, Steve, a Representative from New York
born in Brooklyn, Kings County, N.Y., May 30, 1958
attended Nassau Community College, Garden City, N.Y., 1978
attended Syracuse University, Syracuse, N.Y., 1978-1979
B.A., George Washington University, Washington, D.C., 1982
staff, United States Representative Richard Ottinger of New York, 1980-1983
member of the town board, Huntington, N.Y., 1993-2001
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present).

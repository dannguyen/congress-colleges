JACKSON, Jabez Young, (son of James Jackson [1757-1806] and uncle of James Jackson [1819-1887]), a Representative from Georgia
born in Savannah, Ga., in July 1790
resided at Clarkesville
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of James M. Wayne
reelected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth Congress and served (October 5, 1835-March 3, 1839)
died in Clarkesville, Habersham County, Ga.
death date unknown.

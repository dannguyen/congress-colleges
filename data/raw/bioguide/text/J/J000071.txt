JEFFORDS, Elza, a Representative from Mississippi
born in Ironton, Lawrence County, Ohio, May 23, 1826
attended the common schools in Portsmouth, Ohio
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1847 and commenced practice in Portsmouth, Ohio
served in the Army of the Tennessee from June 1862 to December 1863 as clerk in the QuartermasterÂ’s Department
judge of the high court of errors and appeals in Mississippi 1868 and 1869
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1872
elected as a Republican to the Forty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1883-March 3, 1885)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1884
died in Vicksburg, Miss., on March 19, 1885
interment in Cedar Hill Cemetery, near Vicksburg.

JENRETTE, John Wilson, Jr., a Representative from South Carolina
born in Conway, Horry County, S.C., May 19, 1936
graduated from Loris High School, Loris, S.C., 1954
B.A., Wofford College, Wofford, S.C., 1958
LL.B., University of South Carolina Law School, Columbia, S.C., 1962
admitted to the South Carolina bar, 1962
served as North Myrtle Beach city judge, 1962-1965
city attorney, 1965-1969
member of the South Carolina house of representatives, 1964-1972
unsuccessful candidate for election to the Ninety-third Congress in 1972
delegate, Democratic National Conventions, 1974, 1980
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-fourth and to the two succeeding Congresses, until his resignation on December 10, 1980, (November 5, 1975-December 10, 1980)
president of an advertising and public relations firm in Florence and Myrtle Beach, S.C.
is a resident of North Myrtle Beach, S.C.

JENSEN, Benton Franklin, a Representative from Iowa
born in Marion, Linn County, Iowa, December 16, 1892
attended the rural and high schools
employed by a lumber company as yardman and assistant auditor 1914-1917
during the First World War served as a second lieutenant in 1918
manager of a lumber company 1919-1938
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-sixth and to the twelve succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1939-January 3, 1965)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1964 to the Eighty-ninth Congress
returned to Exira, Iowa
died in Washington, D.C., February 5, 1970
interment in Exira Cemetery, Exira, Iowa.

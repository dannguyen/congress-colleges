JEWETT, Luther, a Representative from Vermont
born in Canterbury, Windham County, Conn., December 24, 1772
was graduated from Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H., in 1795
studied medicine and practiced in Putney, Vt.
member of the State house of representatives
elected as a Federalist to the Fourteenth Congress (March 4, 1815-March 3, 1817)
moved to St. Johnsbury, Caledonia County, Vt.
studied theology
was ordained as a minister and officiated in Newbury, Vt., 1821-1828
returned to St. Johnsbury and published the Farmers Herald 1828-1832 and the Free Masons Friend 1830-1832
died in St. Johnsbury, Vt., March 8, 1860
interment in Mount Pleasant Cemetery.

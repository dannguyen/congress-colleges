JOHNSON, Harvey Hull, a Representative from Ohio
born in West Rutland, Rutland County, Vt., September 7, 1808
attended the common schools and Middlebury Academy
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1833 and commenced practice in Akron, Ohio
postmaster of Akron in 1837
moved to Ashland, Ohio, about 1848
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1854
moved to Minnesota in 1855 and settled in Winona
resumed the practice of law
president of the Winona & St. Peter Railroad during its construction to Rochester
moved to Owatonna, Steele County, Minn., in 1865 and engaged in the practice of law
mayor and city justice 1867-1870
died in Owatonna, Minn., February 4, 1896
interment in Forest Hill Cemetery.

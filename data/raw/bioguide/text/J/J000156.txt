JOHNSON, Joseph Travis, a Representative from South Carolina
born in Brewerton, Laurens County, S.C., February 28, 1858
attended the common schools and was graduated from Erskine College, Due West, S.C., in 1879
taught school for several years
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1883
practiced law in Laurens and later in Spartanburg
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses and served from March 4, 1901, until April 19, 1915, when he resigned
Federal judge of the western district of South Carolina from 1915 until his death in Spartanburg, S.C., May 8, 1919
interment in Oakwood Cemetery.

JOHNSTON, Harry A., II, a Representative from Florida
born in West Palm Beach, Palm Beach County, Fla., December 2, 1931
B.A., Virginia Military Institute, 1953
served in the United States Army as a first lieutenant, Fourth Armor Division, 1953-1955
J.D., University of Florida Law School, 1958
admitted to the bar in 1958 and commenced practice in West Palm Beach
Florida state senator, 1974-1986
president, 1984-1986
unsuccessful candidate for nomination for Governor of Florida, 1986
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred First and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1989-January 3, 1997)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress.

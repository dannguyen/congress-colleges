JOHNSTON, William, a Representative from Ohio
born in Ireland in 1819
immigrated to the United States and settled in Ohio
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced in Mansfield, Ohio, from 1859 to 1863
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1863-March 3, 1865)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1864 to the Thirty-ninth Congress
resumed the practice of law
died in Mansfield, Ohio, May 1, 1866
interment in Mansfield Cemetery.

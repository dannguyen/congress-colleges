JONES, Ben, a Representative from Georgia
born in Tarboro, Edgecombe County, N.C., August 30, 1941
graduated from Woodrow Wilson High School, Portsmouth, Va., 1959
attended the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, N.C., 1961-1964
professional entertainer
unsuccessful candidate for election to the One Hundredth Congress in 1986
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred First and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 1989-January 3, 1993)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992
unsuccessful candidate for election to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in Virginia in 2002.

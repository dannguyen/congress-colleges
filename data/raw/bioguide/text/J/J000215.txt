JONES, Daniel Terryll, a Representative from New York
born in Hebron, Tolland County, Conn., August 17, 1800
received a liberal schooling
was graduated from the medical department of Yale College in 1826 and began the practice of his profession in Amboy, Oswego County, N.Y.
moved to Baldwinsville, N.Y., in 1841
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-second and Thirty-third Congresses (March 4, 1851-March 3, 1855)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1854
chairman of the Republican State convention at Syracuse, N.Y., in 1858
resumed the practice of medicine
died in Baldwinsville, Onondaga County, N.Y., March 29, 1861
interment in Riverside Cemetery.

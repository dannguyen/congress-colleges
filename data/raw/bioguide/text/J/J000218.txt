JONES, Francis, a Representative from Tennessee
birth date unknown
received a limited schooling
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Winchester, Tenn.
solicitor general of the third Tennessee district in 1815
elected as a Republican to the Fifteenth, Sixteenth, and Seventeenth Congresses (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1823)
resumed the practice of his profession in Winchester, Franklin County, Tenn., and died there
death date unknown.

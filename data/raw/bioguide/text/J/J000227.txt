JONES, James, a Representative from Georgia
born in Maryland, birth date unknown
moved to Georgia with his uncle, Colonel Marbury, in 1740
attended the academy in Augusta
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced in Savannah
first lieutenant of East Company, Chatham County Regiment of Militia, in 1790
member of the State house of representatives, 1796-1798
member of the State constitutional convention, May 1798
elected as a Federalist to the Sixth Congress (March 4, 1799-January 11, 1801)
died on January 11, 1801, in Washington, D.C.
interment in the Congressional Cemetery.

JONES, Walter Beaman, Sr., (father of Walter Beaman Jones, Jr.), a Representative from North Carolina
born in Fayetteville, Hoke County, N.C., August 19, 1913
attended Elise Academy, Hemp, N.C.
B.S., North Carolina State University, Raleigh, N.C., 1934
businessman
mayor of Farmville, N.C., 1949-1953
member of the North Carolina state general assembly, 1955-1959
member of the North Carolina state senate, 1965
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-ninth Congress, by special election to fill the vacancy caused by the death of United States Representative Herbert C. Bonner, and reelected to the thirteen succeeding Congresses (February 5, 1966-September 15, 1992)
chairman, Committee on Merchant Marine and Fisheries (Ninety-seventh through One Hundred Second Congresses)
died on September 15, 1992, in Norfolk, Va.

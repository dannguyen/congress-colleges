JANKLOW, William J., a Representative from South Dakota
born in Chicago, Cook County, Ill., September 13, 1939
B.S., University of South Dakota, Vermillion, S.Dak., 1964
LL.B., University of South Dakota, Vermillion, S.Dak., 1966
United States Marine Corps, 1955-1959
South Dakota state attorney general, 1974-1978
Governor of South Dakota, 1979-1987, 1994-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth Congress and served until resignation on January 20, 2004 (January 3, 2003-January 20, 2004)
died on January 12, 2012, in Sioux Falls, S.Dak.

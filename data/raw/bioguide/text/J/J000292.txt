JOHNSON, Bill, a Representative from Ohio
born in Roseboro, Sampson County, N.C., November 10, 1954
B.S., Troy University, Troy, Ala., 1979
M.S., Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, Ga., 1984
United States Air Force, 1973-1999
business owner
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

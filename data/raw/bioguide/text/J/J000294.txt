JEFFRIES, Hakeem, a Representative from New York
born in Brooklyn, Kings County, N.Y., August 4, 1970
B.A., Binghamton University, Vestal, N.Y., 1992
M.P.P., Georgetown University, Washington, D.C., 1994
J.D., New York University, New York, N.Y., 1997
law clerk, United States District Court for the Southern District of New York, 1997-1998
lawyer, private practice
member of the New York state assembly, 2007-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

JOYCE, David, a Representative from Ohio
born in Cleveland, Cuyahoga County, Ohio, March 17, 1957
graduated from West Geauga High School, Chesterland, Ohio, 1976
B.S., University of Dayton, Dayton, Ohio, 1979
J.D., University of Dayton, Dayton, Ohio, 1982
Cuyahoga County, Ohio, public defender, 1983-1984
Geauga County, Ohio, public defender, 1985-1988
Geauga County, Ohio, prosecutor, 1988-2013
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

JENKINS, Evan H., a Representative from West Virginia
born in Huntington, Cabell County, W.Va., September 12, 1960
B.S., University of Florida, Gainesville, Fla., 1983
J.D., Cumberland School of Law, Samford University, Birmingham, Ala., 1987
lawyer, private practice
businessman
instructor, Marshall University, Huntington, W.Va.
member of the West Virginia state house of delegates, 1994-1998
member of the West Virginia state senate, 2002-2014
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

KALANIANAOLE, Jonah Kuhio, a Delegate from the Territory of Hawaii
born in Koloa, island of Kauai, Hawaii, March 26, 1871
attended the Royal School and Punahou College, Honolulu
studied four years in St. Matthews College, California
was a student at the Royal Agricultural College in England and was graduated from a business college in England
created a prince by royal proclamation in 1884
occupied a position in the Department of the Interior of the Hawaiian Government
took part in the revolution of the Hawaiians in 1895 and was sentenced to one years imprisonment
visited Africa during the years 1899-1902 and fought in the British Army in the Boer War
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-eighth and to the nine succeeding Congresses and served from March 4, 1903, until his death in Waikiki, near Honolulu, Hawaii, on January 7, 1922
interment in Royal Mausoleum, Nuuanu.

KARTH, Joseph Edward, a Representative from Minnesota
born in New Brighton, Ramsey County, Minn., August 26, 1922
attended public schools and the University of Nebraska School of Engineering
interrupted his education during the Second World War to serve in the United States Army, with service in the European Theater of Operations
employed by the Minnesota Mining & Manufacturing Company
international representative of OCAW-AFL-CIO 1947-1958
member of the Minnesota house of representatives, 1950-1958
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-sixth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1959-January 3, 1977)
was not a candidate for reelection to the Ninety-fifth Congress in 1976
established a consulting firm
died on May 29, 2005, in Scottsdale, Ariz.

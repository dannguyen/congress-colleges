KASICH, John Richard, a Representative from Ohio
born in McKees Rocks, Allegheny County, Pa., May 13, 1952
attended the public schools in McKees Rocks
B.A., Ohio State University, Columbus, 1974
administrative assistant to State senator, 1975-1977
elected to the Ohio Legislature, 1979-1982
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-eighth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-January 3, 2001)
chairman, Committee on the Budget (One Hundred Fourth through One Hundred Sixth Congresses)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Seventh Congress
Governor of Ohio, 2011-present

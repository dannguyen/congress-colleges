KEMPTHORNE, Dirk, a Senator from Idaho
born in San Diego, San Diego County, Calif., October 29, 1951
graduated, University of Idaho 1976
campaign manager for gubernatorial candidate Phil Batt 1982
executive assistant to the director, Idaho Department of Lands 1976-1978
executive vice president, Idaho Home Builders Association 1978-1981
state public affairs manager, FMC Corporation 1983-1986
mayor of Boise, Idaho 1985-1992
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 1992, and served from January 3, 1993, to January 3, 1999
not a candidate for reelection in 1998
governor of Idaho 1999-2006
chairman of the National Governors Association 2003-2004.

KENNEDY, Michael Joseph, a Representative from New York
born in New York City October 25, 1897
attended the Sacred Heart Parochial School, New York City
hotel clerk 1914-1921
clerk of the New York City Board of Elections 1921-1923
served as marshal of the city of New York 1923-1938
became engaged in the insurance business in 1939
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-sixth and Seventy-seventh Congresses (January 3, 1939-January 3, 1943)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1942
resumed the insurance business in New York City
was killed in an airplane accident at the Washington (D.C.) National Airport on November 1, 1949
interment in Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, N.Y.

KENNEDY, William, a Representative from North Carolina
born near Washington, N.C., July 31, 1768
was graduated from the University of Pennsylvania at Philadelphia in 1782
studied law
was admitted to the bar
elected as a Republican to the Eighth Congress (March 4, 1803-March 3, 1805)
elected to the Eleventh Congress (March 4, 1809-March 3, 1811)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Twelfth Congress but was subsequently elected to the same Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Thomas Blount
reelected to the Thirteenth Congress and served from January 30, 1813, to March 3, 1815
died in Washington, Beaufort County, N.C., on October 11, 1834
interment in Kennedy Cemetery, near Washington, N.C.

KILGORE, David, a Representative from Indiana
born in Harrison County, Ky., April 3, 1804
moved with his father to Franklin County, Ind., in 1819
attended the common schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1830 and commenced practice in Yorktown, Ind.
member of the State house of representatives 1833-1836, 1838, 1839, and 1855, and served as speaker in 1855
president judge of the Yorktown circuit 1839-1846
delegate to the State constitutional convention in 1850
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-fifth and Thirty-sixth Congresses (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1861)
delegate to the Union National Convention which met in Philadelphia August 14, 1866
died near Yorktown, Delaware County, Ind., January 22, 1879
interment in Mount Pleasant Cemetery, near Yorktown, Ind.

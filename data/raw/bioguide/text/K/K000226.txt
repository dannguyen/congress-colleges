KINSELLA, Thomas, a Representative from New York
born in County Wexford, Ireland, December 31, 1832
immigrated to the United States and settled in New York City
attended the common schools
moved to Cambridge, N.Y., in 1851 and learned the printerÂ’s trade
worked for the Cambridge Post
moved to Brooklyn in 1858
became editor of the Brooklyn Daily Eagle September 7, 1861
postmaster of Brooklyn in 1866
member of the city water commission and board of education
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-second Congress (March 4, 1871-March 3, 1873)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1872
established the Brooklyn Sunday Sun in 1874, afterward combined with the Daily Eagle, which he edited until his death in Brooklyn, N.Y., February 11, 1884
interment in Holy Cross Cemetery.

KIPP, George Washington, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Green Township, Pike County, Pa., March 28, 1847
attended the public schools
engaged in the lumber business for thirty-five years
county commissioner of Wayne County, Pa., in 1880
elected as a Democrat to the Sixtieth Congress (March 4, 1907-March 3, 1909)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1908, being an unsuccessful candidate for State treasurer
resumed his former business pursuits
elected to the Sixty-second Congress and served from March 4, 1911, until his death, before Congress assembled, on Vancouver Island, British Columbia, July 24, 1911
interment in Oak Hill Cemetery, Towanda, Bradford County, Pa.

KIRKPATRICK, Littleton, a Representative from New Jersey
born in New Brunswick, N.J., October 19, 1797
was graduated from Princeton College in 1815
studied law in Washington, D.C.
was admitted to the bar in 1821 and commenced practice in New Brunswick, N.J.
master in court chancery in 1824
surrogate of Middlesex County 1831-1836
mayor of New Brunswick in 1841 and 1842
trustee of Rutgers College 1841-1859
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1845)
chairman, Committee on Revisal and Unfinished Business (Twenty-eighth Congress)
died in Saratoga Springs, N.Y., August 15, 1859
interment in Presbyterian Cemetery, New Brunswick, N.J.
reinterment in Van Liew Cemetery in 1921.

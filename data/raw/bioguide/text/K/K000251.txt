KITCHIN, William Hodges, (father of Claude Kitchin and William Walton Kitchin, and grandfather of A. Paul Kitchin), a Representative from North Carolina
born in Lauderdale County, Ala., December 22, 1837
moved with his parents to North Carolina in 1841
attended Emory and Henry College, Emory, Va.
left college in April 1861 to enlist in the Confederate Army
was promoted to the rank of captain in 1863 and served throughout the Civil War
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1869 and practiced in Scotland Neck, N.C.
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1879-March 3, 188l)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1880 to the Forty-seventh Congress
died in Scotland Neck, Halifax County, N.C., February 2, 1901
interment in the Baptist Cemetery.

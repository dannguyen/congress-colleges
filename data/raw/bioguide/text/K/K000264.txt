KLEPPE, Thomas Savig, a Representative from North Dakota
born in Kintyre, Emmons County, N. Dak., July 1, 1919
graduated from Valley City High School, Valley City, N. Dak., 1936
attended Valley City Teachers College, 1937
United States Army, warrant officer, 1942-1946
mayor of Bismarck, N. Dak., 1950-1954
served on subcommittee of Federal Intergovernmental Relations Committee on Local Government (ex-Hoover Commission)
president and treasurer of Gold Seal Co., 1946-1964
vice president, J.M. Dain &Co., Minneapolis investment banking firm
Republican nominee for United States Senate in 1964
delegate, North Dakota Republican convention, 1966
former treasurer of the Republican Party of North Dakota
elected as a Republican to the Ninetieth and to the Ninety-first Congresses (January 3, 1967-January 3, 1971)
was not a candidate in 1970 for reelection but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate
Administrator of the Small Business Administration, 1971-1975
Secretary of the Interior, 1975-1977
faculty, University of Wyoming, 1977
died on March 2, 2007, in Bethesda, Md.

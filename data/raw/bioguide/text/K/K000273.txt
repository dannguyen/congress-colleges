KLUCZYNSKI, John Carl, a Representative from Illinois
born in Chicago, Ill., February 15, 1896
attended the public and parochial schools
during the First World War served overseas as a corporal with the Eighth Field Artillery in 1918 and 1919
engaged in the catering business in Chicago, Ill., since 1920
member of the State house of representatives 1933-1948
elected to the State senate in 1948 and served until December 1949, having become a candidate for Congress
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-second Congress
reelected to the twelve succeeding Congresses, and served from January 3, 1951, until his death January 26, 1975
in Chicago, Ill.
interment in Resurrection Mausoleum, Justice, Ill.

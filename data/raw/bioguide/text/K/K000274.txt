KLUG, Scott L., a Representative from Wisconsin
born in Milwaukee, Wis., January 16, 1953
attended private schools
B.A., Lawrence University, Appleton, Wis., 1975
M.J.S., Northwestern University, Evanston, Ill., 1976
M.B.A., University of Wisconsin, Madison, Wis., 1990
television anchor and reporter in Seattle, Wash., Washington, D.C., and Madison, Wis.
vice president for business development, investment firm
publisher
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Second and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1991-January 3, 1999)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1998 to the One Hundred Sixth Congress
is a resident of Madison, Wis.

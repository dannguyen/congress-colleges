KNAPP, Chauncey Langdon, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Berlin, Vt., February 26, 1809
completed preparatory studies
learned the art of printing and engaged in newspaper work in Montpelier
for a number of years was coproprietor and editor of the State Journal
secretary of state of Vermont 1836-1849
moved to Massachusetts and located in Lowell
editor of the Lowell News and other papers
secretary of the state senate in 1851
elected as a candidate of the American Party to the Thirty-fourth Congress and as a Republican to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1855-March 3, 1859)
editor of the Lowell Daily Citizen 1859-1882
died in Lowell, Mass., May 31, 1898
interment in Lowell Cemetery.

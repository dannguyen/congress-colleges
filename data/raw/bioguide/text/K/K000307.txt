KOLTER, Joseph Paul, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in McDonald, Ohio on September 3, 1926
graduated from New Brighton High School, New Brighton, Pa., 1944
B.S., Geneva College, Beaver Falls, Pa., 1950
served in the United States Army, 1944-1947
accountant, teacher, New Brighton city councilman, 1961-1965
State representative, 1969-1982
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-eighth and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-January 3, 1993)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
is a resident of New Brighton, Pa.

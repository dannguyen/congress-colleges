KUNKEL, Jacob Michael, a Representative from Maryland
born in Frederick, Frederick County, Md., July 13, 1822
attended the Frederick Academy for Boys and was graduated from the University of Virginia at Charlottesville in 1843
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Frederick in 1846
served in the State senate 1850-1856
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-fifth and Thirty-sixth Congresses (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1861)
resumed the practice of law in his native city
delegate to the Loyalist Convention in Philadelphia in 1866
died in Frederick, Md., April 7, 1870
interment in Mount Olivet Cemetery, Frederick, Md.

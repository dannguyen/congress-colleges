KUYKENDALL, Dan Heflin, a Representative from Tennessee
born in Cherokee, San Saba County, Tex., July 9, 1924
attended public schools at Cherokee, Tex.
B.S., Texas A.&M. University, 1947
served as lieutenant, pilot in the United States Army Air Corps, 1942-1945
joined Procter & Gamble Co., in 1947 and worked in Corpus Christi and Houston, Tex., and Memphis, Tenn.
co-chairman, Shelby County Republican Party, 1963-1964
member of the board of directors of the charter group drafting new charter for city of Memphis in 1965-1966
Republican nominee for the United States Senate from Tenneessee in 1964
elected as a Republican to the Ninetieth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1967-January 3, 1975)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1974 to the Ninety-fourth Congress
died on June, 12, 2008, in Memphis, Tenn.

KYLE, John Curtis, a Representative from Mississippi
born near Sardis, Panola County, Miss., July 17, 1851
attended Bethel College, Tennessee, and was graduated from the Cumberland University Law School in 1874
was admitted to the bar in 1874 and commenced practice in Sardis
mayor of Sardis 1879-1881
member of the State senate 1881-1885
member of the Mississippi Railroad Commission 1886-1890
chairman of the Democratic State executive committee in 1888
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-second, Fifty-third, and Fifty-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1891-March 3, 1897)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1896
resumed the practice of law and also engaged in banking in Sardis
retired from active business pursuits in 1912
died in Sardis, Miss., July 6, 1913
interment in Rosehill Cemetery.

KING, Steve, a Representative from Iowa
born in Storm Lake, Buena Vista County, Iowa, May 28, 1949
graduated from Denison Community High School, Denison, Iowa
attended Northwest Missouri State University, Maryville, Mo., 1967-1970
business owner
member of the Iowa state senate, 1996-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

KLINE, John, a Representative from Minnesota
born in Allentown, Lehigh County, Pa., September 6, 1947
B.A., Rice University, Houston, Tex., 1969
M.P.A., Shippensburg University of Pennsylvania, Shippensburg, Pa., 1988
United States Marine Corps, 1969-1994
leadership consultant
unsuccessful candidate for the One Hundred Sixth Congress in 1998
unsuccessful candidate for the One Hundred Seventh Congress in 2000
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present)
chair, Committee on Education and the Workforce (One Hundred Twelfth through One Hundred Fourteenth Congresses).

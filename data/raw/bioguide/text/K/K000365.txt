KAGEN, Steven, a Representative from Wisconsin
born in Appleton, Outagamie County, Wis., December 12, 1949
attended Appleton East High School, Appleton, Wis., B.S., University of Wisconsin, Madison, Wis., 1972
M.D., University of Wisconsin, Madison, Wis., 1976
physician
founder, Kagen Allergy Clinics
professor, Medical College of Wisconsin, Milwaukee, Wis.
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth Congress and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

KLEIN, Ron, a Representative from Florida
born in Cleveland, Cuyahoga County, Ohio, July 10, 1957
B.A., Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio, 1979
J.D., Case Western Reserve University, Cleveland, Ohio, 1982
lawyer, private practice
professional advocate
member of the Florida house of representatives, 1992-1996
member of the Florida senate, 1996-2006
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth Congress and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

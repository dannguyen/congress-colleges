KLOBUCHAR, Amy, a Senator from Minnesota
born in Plymouth, Minn., May 25, 1960
attended the public schools in Plymouth
graduated magna cum laude Yale University 1982
J.D., University of Chicago 1985
practiced law in Minnesota, becoming partner at the law firms of Dorsey & Whitney and Gray Plant Mooty
Hennepin County attorney 1999-2006
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 2006 for the term commencing January 3, 2007
reelected in 2012 for the term ending January 3, 2019.

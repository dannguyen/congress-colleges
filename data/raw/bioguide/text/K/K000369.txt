KISSELL, Larry, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Pinehurst, Moore County, N.C., January 31, 1951
graduated from East Montgomery High School, Biscoe, N.C., 1969
B.A., Wake Forest University, Winston-Salem, N.C., 1973
teacher
businessman
unsuccessful candidate for the United States House of Representatives in 2006
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

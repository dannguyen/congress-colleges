KILROY, Mary Jo, a Representative from Ohio
born in Cleveland, Cuyahoga County, Ohio, April 30, 1949
B.A., Cleveland State University, Cleveland, Ohio, 1977
J.D., Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio, 1980
lawyer, private practice
Columbus (Ohio) board of education, 1992-1999
president, Columbus (Ohio) board of education, 1999
unsuccessful candidate for the Ohio state senate, 1996
Franklin County (Ohio) board of commissioners, 2001-2008
president, Franklin County (Ohio) board of commissioners, 2005-2007
unsuccessful candidate for the United States House of Representatives in 2006
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

KELLY, Mike, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Pittsburgh, Allegheny County, Pa., May 10, 1948
B.A., Notre Dame University, South Bend, Ind., 1970
business owner
member of the Butler Pa., area school board, 1992-1995
member of the Butler County, Pa., council, 2006-2009
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

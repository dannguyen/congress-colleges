KINZINGER, Adam, a Representative from Illinois
born in Kankakee, Kankakee County, Illinois, February 27, 1978
graduated from Normal West High School, Normal, Ill., 1996
B.A., Illinois State University, Normal, Ill., 2000
sales representative
United States Air Force/Illinois Air National Guard, 2003-present
member of the McLean County, Ill., board, 1998-2003
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

KENNEDY, Joseph P. III, (son of Joseph Patrick Kennedy, II, great-nephew of Edward Moore Kennedy and John Fitzgerald Kennedy, and great-great-grandson of John Francis Fitzgerald, first cousin once removed of Patrick Joseph Kennedy), a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Brighton, Suffolk County, Mass., October 4, 1980
B.S., Stanford University, Stanford, Calif., 2003
J.D., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 2009
United States Peace Corps, 2004-2006
research analyst
Middlesex, Mass. county assistant district attorney, 2011-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

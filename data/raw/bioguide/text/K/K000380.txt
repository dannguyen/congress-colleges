KILDEE, Dan, (nephew of Dale Edward Kildee), a Representative from Michigan
born in Flint, Genesee County, Mich., August 11, 1958
attended, University of Michigan, Flint, Mich.
B.S., Central Michigan University, Mount Pleasant, Mich.
founder, Genesee County Land Bank
co-founder and chief executive, Center for Community Progress
member of the Flint, Mich., board of education, 1977-1985
member of the Genesee County, Mich., board of commissioners, 1985-1997
Genesee County, Mich., treasurer, 1997-2009
unsuccessful nominee for Governor of Michigan in 2010
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

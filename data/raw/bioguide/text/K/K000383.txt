KING, Angus Stanley, Jr., a Senator from Maine
born in Alexandria, Va., March 31, 1944
graduated Dartmouth College, B.A., 1966
graduated University of Virginia, J.D., 1969
lawyer
counsel, U.S. Senate Subcommittee on Alcoholism and Narcotics
television program host and co-producer
company executive
energy conservation company owner
governor of Maine 1995-2003
elected as an Independent to the United States Senate in 2012 for the term ending January 3, 2019.

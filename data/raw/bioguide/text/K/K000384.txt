KAINE, Timothy Michael (Tim), a Senator from Virginia
born in St. Paul, Minn., February 26, 1958
graduated University of Missouri, B.A., 1979
graduated Harvard University, J.D., 1983
lawyer
Richmond City Council 1994-1998
mayor of Richmond, Va., 1998-2001
lieutenant governor of Virginia 2002-2006
governor of Virginia 2006-2010
chairman of the Democratic National Committee 2009-2011
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 2012 for the term ending January 3, 2019.

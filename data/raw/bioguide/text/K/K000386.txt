KATKO, John, a Representative from New York
born in Camillus, Onondaga County, N.Y., November 9, 1962
graduated from Bishop Ludden High School, Syracuse, N.Y., 1980
B.A., Niagara University, Lewiston, N.Y., 1984
J.D., Syracuse University, Syracuse, N.Y., 1988
lawyer, private practice
staff, United States Securities and Exchange Commission, 1991-1995
staff, United States Justice Department, 1995-2014
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

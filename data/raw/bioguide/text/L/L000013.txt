LADD, George Washington, a Representative from Maine
born in Augusta, Kennebec County, Maine, September 28, 1818
attended the common schools and Kents Hill Seminary
engaged in the drug business in Bangor, Maine
later engaged in the lumber, commission, and wholesale grocery business in Bangor
was also interested in railroad development
elected as a Greenback candidate to the Forty-sixth and Forty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1879-March 3, 1883)
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Post Office Department (Forty-sixth Congress)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1882 to the Forty-eighth Congress
died in Bangor, Penobscot County, Maine, January 30, 1892
interment in Mount Hope Cemetery.

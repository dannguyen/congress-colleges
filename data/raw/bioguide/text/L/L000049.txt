LANDGREBE, Earl Fredrick, a Representative from Indiana
born in Valparaiso, Porter County, Ind., January 21, 1916
attended Union Township Elementary and Wheeler High School
elected to the Indiana State senate, 1959-1968
owner and operator, Landgrebe Motor Transport, Inc.
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-first and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1969-January 3, 1975)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1974 to the Ninety-fourth Congress
was a resident of Valparaiso, Ind., until his death there June 29, 1986
interment in Blachly Cemetery, Crown Point, Ind.

LANDIS, Charles Beary, (brother of Frederick Landis), a Representative from Indiana
born in Millville, Butler County, Ohio, July 9, 1858
attended the public schools of Logansport, Ind., and was graduated from Wabash College, Crawfordsville, Ind., in 1883
editor of the Logansport Journal 1883-1887 and at the time of his nomination for Congress was editor of the Delphi (Ind.) Journal
president of the Indiana Republican Editorial Association in 1894 and 1895
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-fifth and to the five succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1897-March 3, 1909)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection
resumed newspaper work in Delphi, Ind.
died in Asheville, N.C., where he had gone because of impaired health, April 24, 1922
interment in Mount Hope Cemetery, Logansport, Ind.

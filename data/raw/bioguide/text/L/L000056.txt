LANDY, James, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Northern Liberties District, Philadelphia, Pa., October 13, 1813
attended the public schools
studied law, but abandoned the same and engaged in mercantile pursuits
member of the board of school commissioners in 1845
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1859)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection
elected chief commissioner of highways in 1862
died in Philadelphia, Pa., July 25, 1875
interment in Monument Cemetery.

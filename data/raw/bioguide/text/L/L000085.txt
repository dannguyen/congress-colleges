LANSING, Frederick, a Representative from New York
born in Manheim, Herkimer County, N.Y., February 16, 1838
attended the Little Falls Academy, New York
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1859 and practiced in Watertown, N.Y.
served during the Civil War in the Eighth New York Cavalry
acting adjutant of that regiment from June 23 to October 11, 1863
member of the State senate 1881-1885
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-first Congress (March 4, 1889-March 3, 1891)
died in Watertown, N.Y., January 31, 1894
interment in Brookside Cemetery.

LARGENT, Steve, a Representative from Oklahoma
born in Tulsa, Tulsa County, Okla., September 28, 1954
graduated from Putnam City High School, Okla.
B.S., University of Tulsa, Tulsa, Okla., 1976
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth Congress
appointed to the One Hundred Third Congress by the Governor of Oklahoma under the provisions of Oklahoma Statute, Section 26-12-101 (B) and without objection by the House (H. Res. 585, 103rd Congress) to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative James Inhofe
reelected to the three succeeding Congresses and served until his resignation on February 15, 2002 (November 29, 1994-February 15, 2002)
unsuccessful candidate for Governor of Oklahoma in 2002.

LATHAM, Thomas, a Representative from Iowa
born in Hampton, Franklin County, Iowa, July 14, 1948
graduated from Cal Community College, Latimer, Iowa, 1966
attended Wartburg College, Waverly, Iowa, 1966-1967, and Iowa State University, Ames, Iowa, 1967-1970
business owner
delegate, Republican National Convention, 1992
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

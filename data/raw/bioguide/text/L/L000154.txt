LAZEAR, Jesse, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Rich Hill Township, Greene County, Pa., December 12, 1804
received a limited schooling
taught school
engaged in mercantile pursuits
recorder for Greene County 1829-1832
bank cashier of the Farmers & Drovers Bank, Waynesburg, Pa., 1835-1867
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-seventh and Thirty-eighth Congresses (March 4, 1861-March 3, 1865)
chairman, Committee on Expenditures on Public Buildings (Thirty-seventh Congress)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1864
delegate to the Union National Convention at Philadelphia in 1866
retired to his country home, Windsor Mill Farm, in Baltimore County, Md., in 1867
president of the Baltimore & Powhatan Railroad Co. 1871-1874
died at his country home September 2, 1877
interment in Green Mount Cemetery, Waynesburg, Pa.

LE FEVER, Jacob, (father of Frank Jacob Le Fevre), a Representative from New York
born in New Paltz, Ulster County, N.Y., April 20, 1830
attended New Paltz Academy and Amenia Seminary
supervisor of the town in 1861 and 1862
member of the State assembly 1863-1865 and again in 1867
delegate to many Republican State conventions
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1888
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-third and Fifty-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1893-March 3, 1897)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1896 to the Fifty-fifth Congress
president of the Huguenot National Bank until his death in New Paltz, N.Y., on February 4, 1905
interment in New Paltz Rural Cemetery.

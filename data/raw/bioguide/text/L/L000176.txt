LEAKE, Shelton Farrar, a Representative from Virginia
born near Hillsboro, Albemarle County, Va., November 30, 1812
completed preparatory studies
taught school
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1835 and commenced practice in Charlottesville, Va.
member of the State house of delegates in 1842 and 1843
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
resumed the practice of law
elected Lieutenant Governor of Virginia in 1851
elected as an Independent Democrat to the Thirty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1859-March 3, 1861)
again resumed the practice of law
died in Charlottesville, Va., on March 4, 1884
interment in Maplewood Cemetery.

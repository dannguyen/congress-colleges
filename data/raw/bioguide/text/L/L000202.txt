LEE, Robert Emmett, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Pottsville, Schuykill County, Pa., October 12, 1868
attended the common schools
apprenticed to the blacksmiths trade
engaged in mercantile pursuits in Pottsville
county treasurer in 1905
unsuccessful candidate for election in 1908 to the Sixty-first Congress
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-second and Sixty-third Congresses (March 4, 1911-March 3, 1915)
chairman, Committee on Mileage (Sixty-second Congress)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1914 to the Sixty-fourth Congress
resumed his former business activities in Pottsville
unsuccessful candidate for election in 1916 to the Sixty-fifth Congress
died in Pottsville, Pa., November 19, 1916
interment in St. Patricks Cemetery.

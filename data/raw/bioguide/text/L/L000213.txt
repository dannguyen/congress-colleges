LEFFERTS, John, a Representative from New York
born in Brooklyn, N.Y., December 17, 1785
attended the public schools
elected as a Republican to the Thirteenth Congress (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1815)
delegate to the State constitutional convention of 1821
member of the State senate 1820-1825
died in Brooklyn, N.Y., September 18, 1829
interment in Greenwood Cemetery.

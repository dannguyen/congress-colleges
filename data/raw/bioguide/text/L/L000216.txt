LEFTWICH, Jabez, a Representative from Virginia
born in Bedford County near Liberty (now Bedford), Va., September 22, 1765
attended the rural schools
member of the State house of delegates 1801-1809
inspector general with the rank of colonel on the staff of his brother, General Joel Leftwich, during the War of 1812
elected as a Republican to the Seventeenth Congress and reelected as a Crawford Republican to the Eighteenth Congress (March 4, 1821-March 3, 1825)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1824 to the Nineteenth Congress
moved to Madison County, Ala., in 1825
engaged in agricultural and mercantile pursuits
member of the Alabama house of representatives
died near Huntsville, Ala., June 22, 1855
interment in Maple Hill Cemetery.

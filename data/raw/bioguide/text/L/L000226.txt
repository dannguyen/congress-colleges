LEHMAN, William, a Representative from Florida
born in Selma, Dallas County, Ala., October 5, 1913
graduated from Dallas Academy and Selma High School, Selma, Ala., 1930
B.S., University of Alabama, Tuscaloosa, Ala., 1934
attended Oxford University, Oxford, England, 1965
auto dealer
teacher, Miami Norland Junior High School, Miami, Fla., 1963-1964
instructor, Miami Dade Junior College, Miami, Fla., 1965-1966
member, Dade County, Fla., school board, 1966-1972
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-third and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1973-January 3, 1993)
was not a candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992
died on March 16, 2005, in Miami Beach, Fla.

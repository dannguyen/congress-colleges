LEITER, Benjamin Franklin, a Representative from Ohio
born in Leitersburg, Md., October 13, 1813
received a limited schooling
taught school in Maryland 1830-1834
moved to Ohio and taught school 1834-1842
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1842 and commenced practice in Canton, Stark County, Ohio
justice of the peace
mayor for ten years
member of the state house of representatives in 1848 and 1849 and served as speaker in the latter year
elected as an Opposition Party candidate to the Thirty-fourth and reelected as a Republican to the Thirty-fifth Congresses (March 4, 1855-March 3, 1859)
died in Canton, Ohio, June 17, 1866
interment in West Lawn Cemetery.

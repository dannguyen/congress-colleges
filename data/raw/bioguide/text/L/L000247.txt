LEONARD, John Edwards, (grandnephew of John Edwards of Pennsylvania), a Representative from Louisiana
born in Fairville, Chester County, Pa., September 22, 1845
attended the public schools
was graduated from Phillips Exeter Academy, Exeter, N.H., in 1863 and from Harvard University in 1867
studied law in Germany
returned to the United States and was admitted to the bar in Louisiana in 1870 and commenced practice at Monroe, Ouachita Parish
district attorney of the thirteenth judicial district of Louisiana in 1871 and 1872
elected associate justice of the State supreme court in 1876
resumed the practice of his profession in Monroe, La.
elected as a Republican to the Forty-fifth Congress and served from March 4, 1877, until his death in Havana, Cuba, March 15, 1878

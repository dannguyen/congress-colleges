LEVIN, Carl, (brother of Sander Martin Levin), a Senator from Michigan
born in Detroit, Wayne County, Mich., June 28, 1934
attended Detroit public schools
graduated, Swarthmore College, Swarthmore, Pa., 1956
graduated, Harvard Law School 1959
admitted to the Michigan bar in 1959 and commenced practice in Detroit
assistant attorney general and general counsel for the Michigan civil rights commission 1964-1967
special assistant attorney general for the State of Michigan and chief appellate defender for the city of Detroit 1968-1969
member, Detroit city council 1969-1973
president, Detroit city council 1974-1977
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 1978
reelected in 1984, 1990, 1996, 2002, and again in 2008, and served from January 3, 1979, to January 3, 2015
chair, Committee on Armed Services (One Hundred Seventh Congress [January 3-20, 2001
June 6, 2001-January 3, 2003]
One Hundred Tenth to One Hundred Thirteenth Congresses)
was not a candidate for reelection in 2014.

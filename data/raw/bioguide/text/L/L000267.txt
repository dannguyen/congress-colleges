LEVY, David A., a Representative from New York
born in Johnson County, Ind., December 18, 1953
B.A., Hofstra University, 1973, J.D., 1979
admitted to the bar in 1980 and commenced the practice of law
utility company executive
member, Hempstead town council, 1989-1993
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Third Congress (January 3, 1993-January 3, 1995)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Fourth Congress.

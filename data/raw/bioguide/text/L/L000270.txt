LEWIS, Abner, a Representative from New York
born in Rutland County, Vt., August 17, 1801
attended the public schools
member of the State assembly in 1838 and 1839
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
Chautauqua county judge 1847-1852
practiced law in Winona, Minn., where he died on October 11, 1879
interment in Woodlawn Cemetery.

LEWIS, William J., a Representative from Virginia
born in Augusta County, Va., July 4, 1766
attended the common schools
member of the State house of delegates
elected as a Republican to the Fifteenth Congress (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1819)
died at Mount Athos plantation, near Lynchburg, Campbell County, Va., November 1, 1828
interment in a vault blasted out of a solid rock at the summit of Mount Athos, Virginia.

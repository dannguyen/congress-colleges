LIBBEY, Harry, a Representative from Virginia
born in Wakefield, Carroll County, N.H., November 22, 1843
attended the common schools
moved to Virginia and settled in Hampton in 1863
engaged in mercantile pursuits
appointed one of the presiding justices of Elizabeth City County, Va., in 1869
elected as a Readjuster to the Forty-eighth Congress and reelected as a Republican to the Forty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1883-March 3, 1887)
engaged in the oyster industry
served as chairman of the Republican county committee
appointed postmaster of Hampton, Va., January 18, 1907, and served until his death in Hampton, Elizabeth City County, Va., on September 30, 1913

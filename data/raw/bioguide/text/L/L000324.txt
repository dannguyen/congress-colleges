LINDSAY, George Henry, (father of George Washington Lindsay), a Representative from New York
born in New York City January 7, 1837
moved with his parents to Brooklyn, N.Y., in 1843
attended the public schools
engaged in the real estate and investment business
member of the State assembly 1882-1886
coroner of Kings County 1886-1892
appointed assistant tax commissioner in 1898
delegate to various national and State conventions
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1901-March 3, 1913)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1912
lived in retirement until his death in Brooklyn N.Y., May 25, 1916
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

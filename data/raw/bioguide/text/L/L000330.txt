LINDSLEY, William Dell, a Representative from Ohio
born in New Haven, Conn., December 25, 1812
attended the common schools
moved to Buffalo, N.Y., in 1832 and soon after to Erie County, Ohio, settling near Sandusky
engaged in agricultural pursuits
served as captain in the Ohio Militia from 1840 to 1843 and as brigadier general in 1843
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1854 to the Thirty-fourth Congress
resumed agricultural pursuits
died in Perkins Township, Erie County, Ohio, March 11, 1890
interment in Oakland Cemetery, Sandusky, Ohio.

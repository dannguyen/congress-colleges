LINEHAN, Neil Joseph, a Representative from Illinois
born in Chicago, Ill., September 23, 1895
attended the public schools
was graduated from John L. Marsh School in 1913
engaged in electrical business since 1919
during the First World War served in France with the Three Hundred and Fortieth Infantry, Eighty-fifth Division, United States Army
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-first Congress (January 3, 1949-January 3, 1951)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1950 to the Eighty-second Congress and for election in 1952 to the Eighty-third Congress
Director of Price Stabilization, Chicago district, 1951
resumed electrical engineering business
died in Chicago, Ill., August 23, 1967
interment in St. Mary Cemetery.

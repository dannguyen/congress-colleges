LINN, Archibald Ladley, a Representative from New York
born in New York City on October 15, 1802
was a member of the class of 1820 at Union College, Schenectady, N.Y.
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Schenectady
county judge of Schenectady County from January 17, 1840, to February 9, 1845
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1843)
chairman, Committee on Public Expenditures (Twenty-seventh Congress)
member of the State assembly in 1844
died in Schenectady, N.Y., October 10, 1857.

LONGLEY, James, a Representative from Maine
born in Lewiston, Maine, July 7, 1951
graduated Phillips Academy
graduated Holy Cross and University of Maine Law School
personal assistant to the late Governor James B. Longley
U.S. Marines, 1976
attorney and small business person
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth Congress (January 3, 1995-January 3, 1997)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress
unsuccessful candidate for governor in 1998.

LOVE, John, a Representative from Virginia
birth date unknown
pursued an academic course
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1801 and commenced practice in Alexandria, Va.
member of the State house of delegates, 1805-1807
elected as a Republican to the Tenth and Eleventh Congresses (March 4, 1807-March 3, 1811)
chairman, Committee on District of Columbia (Eleventh Congress)
served in the State senate, 1816-1820
resumed the practice of law
died in Alexandria, Va., August 17, 1822.

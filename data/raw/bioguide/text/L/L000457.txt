LOVE, Peter Early, a Representative from Georgia
born near Dublin, Laurens County, Ga., July 7, 1818
was graduated from Franklin College (now a part of the University of Georgia), Athens, Ga., in 1829 and from the Philadelphia College of Medicine in 1838
practiced medicine while studying law
was admitted to the bar in 1839 and commenced practice in Thomasville, Thomas County, Ga.
solicitor general of the southern district of Georgia in 1843
member of the State senate in 1849
elected judge of the State superior court for the southern circuit in 1853
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-sixth Congress and served from March 4, 1859, until his retirement on January 23, 1861
resumed the practice of law in Thomasville, Ga.
member of the State house of representatives in 1861
died in Thomasville, Ga., November 8, 1866
interment in the Old Cemetery.

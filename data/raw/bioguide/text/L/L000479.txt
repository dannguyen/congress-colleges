LOWERY, William David, a Representative from California
born in San Diego, San Diego County, Calif., May 2, 1947
attended the public schools
graduated from Point Loma High School, 1965
attended San Diego State University
partner, advertising and public relations firm
member, San Diego City Council, 1977-1980
deputy mayor, San Diego, 1979-1980
delegate, Republican National Convention, 1980
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1981-January 3, 1993)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
is a resident of San Diego, Calif.

LOWNDES, Lloyd, Jr., a Representative from Maryland
born in Clarksburg, Harrison County, Va. (now West Virginia), February 21, 1845
attended the common schools
was graduated from Allegheny College, Meadville, Pa., in 1865 and from the law department of the University of Pennsylvania at Philadelphia in 1867
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Cumberland, Md.
elected as a Republican to the Forty-third Congress (March 4, 1873-March 3, 1875)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1874 to the Forty-fourth Congress
engaged in banking
Governor of Maryland 1895-1899
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1898
died in Cumberland, Md., January 8, 1905
interment in Rose Hill Cemetery.

LUKEN, Charles J., (son of Thomas Andrew Luken), a Representative from Ohio
born in Cincinnati, Ohio, July 18, 1951
B.A., University of Notre Dame, 1973
J.D., University of Cincinnati College of Law, 1976
was admitted to the bar in 1976 and commenced practice in Cincinnati
member, Cincinnati City Council, 1981-1984
mayor of Cincinnati, 1985-1990
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Second Congress (January 3, 1991-January 3, 1993)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
is a resident of Cincinnati, Ohio.

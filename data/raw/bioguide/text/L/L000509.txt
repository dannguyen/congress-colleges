LUKENS, Donald Edgar, a Representative from Ohio
born in Harveysburg, Warren County, Ohio, February 11, 1931
attended grade school in Harveysburg and graduated from Waynesville, Ohio, high school
B.A., Ohio State University, Colombus, Ohio, 1954
attended University of Maryland, College Park, Md., 1955-1956
United States Air Force, 1964-1960
United States Air Force Reserve, 1971-1983
minority counsel, House Committee on Rules, 1961
elected as a Republican to the Ninetieth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 1967-January 3, 1971)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1970 but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination for Governor of Ohio
member of the Ohio state senate, 1971-1986
elected to the One Hundredth and to the succeeding Congress until his resignation on October 24, 1990 (January 3, 1987-October 24, 1990)
died in Dallas, Dallas County, Tex., May 22, 2010.

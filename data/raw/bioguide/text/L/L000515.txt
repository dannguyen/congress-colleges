LUNDIN, Frederick, a Representative from Illinois
born in the parish of Vestra Tollstad, Hastholmen, Sweden, May 18, 1868
immigrated to the United States and settled in Chicago, Ill., in 1880
completed academic studies
president of Lundin & Co., manufacturing chemists
member of the State senate 1894-1898
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-first Congress (March 4, 1909-March 3, 1911)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1910 to the Sixty-second Congress
resumed manufacturing interests until retirement in 1916
died in Beverly Hills, Calif., August 20, 1947
interment in Forest Home Cemetery, Forest Park, Ill.

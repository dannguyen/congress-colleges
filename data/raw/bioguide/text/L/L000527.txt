LYMAN, Joseph Stebbins, a Representative from New York
born in Northfield, Franklin County, Mass., February 14, 1785
attended the common schools
was graduated from Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H., in 1806
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Cooperstown, N.Y.
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth Congress (March 4, 1819-March 3, 1821)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1821
died in Cooperstown, Otsego County, N.Y., March 21, 1821
interment in Greenfield, Franklin County, Mass.

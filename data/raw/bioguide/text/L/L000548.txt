L'ENGLE, Claude, a Representative from Florida
born in Jacksonville, Fla., October 19, 1868
attended the public schools and the Duval High School
engaged in mercantile pursuits
became editor and publisher of Dixie, a weekly newspaper
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-third Congress (March 4, 1913-March 3, 1915)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1914 to the Sixty-fourth Congress
again engaged in journalism
died in Jacksonville, Duval County, Fla., November 6, 1919
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

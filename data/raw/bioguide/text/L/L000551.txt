LEE, Barbara, a Representative from California
born in El Paso, El Paso County, Tex., July 16, 1946
graduated from San Fernando High School, San Fernando, Calif., 1964
B.A., Mills College, Oakland, Calif., 1973
M.S.W., University of California, Berkeley, Calif., 1975
staff, United States Representative Ronald V. Dellums of California, 1975-1987
member of the California state assembly, 1991-1997
member of the California state senate, 1997-1998
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Ron Dellums, and reelected to the nine succeeding Congresses (April 7, 1998-present).

LARSON, John B., a Representative from Connecticut
born in Hartford, Hartford County, Conn., July 22, 1948
graduated East Hartford High School, Hartford, Conn., 1967
B.A., Central Connecticut University, New Britain, Conn., 1971
member of the East Hartford, Conn., board of education, 1977-1979
member of the East Hartford, Conn., town council, 1979-1983
senior fellow at the Yale Bush Center for Child Development and Social Policy, New Haven, Conn.
teacher
businessman
member of the Connecticut state senate, 1986-1998, President Pro Tempore of the Connecticut state senate, 1990-1998
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Sixth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1999-present)
chair, House Democratic Caucus (One Hundred Eleventh and One Hundred Twelfth Congresses).

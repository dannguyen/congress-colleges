LARSEN, Richard Ray (Rick), a Representative from Washington
born in Arlington, Snohomish County, Wash., June 15, 1965
B.A., Pacific Lutheran University, Tacoma, Wash., 1987
M.P.A., University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, Minn., 1990
member of the Snohomish, Wash., county council, 1998-2000
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present).

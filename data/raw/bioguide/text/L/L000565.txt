LOEBSACK, Dave, a Representative from Iowa
born in Sioux City, Woodbury County, Iowa, December 23, 1952
graduated from East High School, Sioux City, Iowa, 1970
B.S., Iowa State University, Ames, Iowa, 1974
M.A., Iowa State University, Ames, Iowa, 1976
Ph.D., University of California, Davis, Calif., 1985
professor
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2007-present).

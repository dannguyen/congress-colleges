LATTA, Robert E., (son of Delbert L. Latta), a Representative from Ohio
born in Bluffton, Allen County, Ohio, April 18, 1956
B.A., Bowling Green State University, Bowling Green, Ohio, 1978
J.D., University of Toledo, Toledo, Ohio, 1981
lawyer, private practice
Wood County, Ohio, commissioner, 1991-1996
member of the Ohio state senate, 1997-2000
member of the Ohio state house of representatives, 2001-2007
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Tenth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of United States Representative Paul Gillmor, and reelected to the four succeeding Congresses (December 11, 2007-present).

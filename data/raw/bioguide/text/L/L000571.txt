LUMMIS, Cynthia M., a Representative from Wyoming
born in Cheyenne, Laramie County, Wyo., September 10, 1954
B.S., University of Wyoming, Laramie, Wyo., 1976
B.A., University of Wyoming, 1978
J.D., University of Wyoming College of Law, Laramie, Wyo., 1985
rancher
lawyer, private practice
staff, Wyoming Governor Jim Geringer, 1994-1996
member of Wyoming state house of representatives, 1979-1983, 1985-1993
member of Wyoming state senate, 1993-1995
Wyoming state treasurer, 1999-2007
Wyoming state lands and investment acting director, 1997-1998
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2009-present).

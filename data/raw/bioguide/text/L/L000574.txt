LANDRY, Jeff, a Representative from Louisiana
born in Saint Martinville, Saint Martin Parish, Louisiana, December 23, 1970
graduated from Saint Martinville Senior High School, Saint Martinville, La., 1988
B.S., University of Southwestern Louisiana, Lafayette, La, 1999
J.D., Loyola University, New Orleans, La., 2004
Louisiana Army National Guard, 1987-1998
lawyer
police officer
business owner
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

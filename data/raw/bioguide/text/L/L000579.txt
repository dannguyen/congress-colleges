LOWENTHAL, Alan, a Representative from California
born in Manhattan, New York County, N.Y., March 8, 1941
B.A., Hobart College, Geneva, N.Y., 1962
M.A., Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio, 1965
Ph.D., Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio, 1967
professor
psychologist
member of the Long Beach Calif., city council, 1992-1998
member of the California state assembly, 1998-2004
member of the California state senate, 2004-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

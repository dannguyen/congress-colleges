MacGREGOR, Clark, a Representative from Minnesota
born in Minneapolis, Hennepin County, Minn., July 12, 1922
graduated from Washburn High School, Minneapolis, Minn.
A.B., Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H., 1946
LL.B., University of Minnesota Law School, 1948
United States Army, Office of Strategic Services, 1942-1945
lawyer, private practice
delegate, Republican National Conventions, 1964 and 1968
elected as a Republican to the Eighty-seventh and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1961-January 3, 1971)
not a candidate for reelection to the Ninety-second Congress in 1970, but was an unsuccessful nominee to the United States Senate
business executive
died on February 10, 2003, in Pompano Beach, Fla.

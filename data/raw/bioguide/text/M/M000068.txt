MAHONEY, Peter Paul, a Representative from New York
born in New York City June 25, 1848
educated in the common schools of New York City
engaged in the dry-goods business for several years
moved to Brooklyn, N.Y., and engaged in the sale of liquor
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-ninth and Fiftieth Congresses (March 4, 1885-March 3, 1889)
was not a candidate in 1888 for reelection to the Fifty-first Congress
became ill while attending the inauguration ceremonies of President Benjamin Harrison March 4, 1889, and died in Washington, D.C., March 27, 1889
interment in Calvary Cemetery, Long Island City, Queens County, N.Y.

MALBY, George Roland, a Representative from New York
born in Canton, St. Lawrence County, N.Y., September 16, 1857
attended Canton Union School and St. Lawrence University, Canton, N.Y.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1881 and commenced the practice of law in Ogdensburg, St. Lawrence County, N.Y.
justice of the peace of Oswegatchie
member of the State assembly in 1890-1895
elected leader of his party in that body in 1893 and served as speaker in 1894
served in the State senate 1895-1907
elected as a Republican to the Sixtieth, Sixty-first, and Sixty-second Congresses and served from March 4, 1907, until his death in New York City July 5, 1912
interment in Ogdensburg Cemetery, Ogdensburg, N.Y.

MARION, Robert, a Representative from South Carolina
born 1766 in Berkeley District, S.C.
pursued an academic course, and was graduated from the University of the State of Pennsylvania (now the University of Pennsylvania) at Philadelphia in 1784
owned and managed plantation at Belle Isle, S.C.
justice of quorum, St. Stephens Parish
justice of the peace, Charleston, S.C.
served in the State house of representatives, 1790-1796, and in the State senate, 1802-1805
elected as a Republican to the Ninth, Tenth, and Eleventh Congresses and served from March 4, 1805, until his resignation on December 4, 1810
died on his plantation in St. Stephens Parish, March 22, 1811.

MARKELL, Henry, (son of Jacob Markell), a Representative from New York
born in Stone Arabia, Montgomery County, N.Y., February 7, 1792
attended the common schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
elected as an Adams candidate to the Nineteenth and Twentieth Congresses (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1829)
died in Palatine, N.Y., on August 30, 1831
interment in the cemetery at St. Johnsville, Montgomery County, N.Y.

MARSHALL, Alexander Keith, a Representative from Kentucky
born at Buck Pond, near Versailles, Woodford County, Ky., on February 11, 1808
completed preparatory studies
settled in Nicholasville, Ky.
was graduated from the medical department of the University of Pennsylvania at Philadelphia in 1844
engaged in the practice of medicine at Nicholasville
member of the State constitutional convention held in Frankfort in 1849
elected as a candidate of the American Party to the Thirty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1855-March 3, 1857)
moved to Missouri, but returned to Kentucky and settled in Fayette County
engaged in agricultural pursuits
died near East Hickman, Ky., April 28, 1884
interment in Lexington Cemetery, Lexington, Ky.

MARSHALL, Alfred, a Representative from Maine
born in New Hampshire about 1797
member of the State house of representatives in 1827, 1828, 1834, and 1835
served as a general in the State militia
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1843)
collector at Belfast, Maine, 1846-1849
engaged in mercantile pursuits and the hotel business
died in China, Kennebec County, Maine, October 2, 1868
interment in Village Cemetery.

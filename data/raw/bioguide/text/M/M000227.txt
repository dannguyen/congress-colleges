MASON, William, a Representative from New York
born in Lebanon, New London County, Conn., September 10, 1786
studied medicine in Vermont and practiced in Preston, N.Y.
surgeon of the Chenango County Company, New York Volunteers, in 1812
clerk of Chenango County in 1820-1821
member of the State assembly in 1821 and 1822
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1837)
died in Norwich, N.Y., January 13, 1860
interment in Mount Hope Cemetery.

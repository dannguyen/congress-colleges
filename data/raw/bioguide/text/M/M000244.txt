MATHIS, Marvin Dawson, a Representative from Georgia
born in Nashville, Berrien County, Ga., November 30, 1940
attended the Nashville public schools
attended South Georgia College in Douglas, Ga.
news director, WALB-TV, Albany, Ga., 1964-1970
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-second and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1971-January 3, 1981)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate in 1980
private advocate
is a resident of Upper Marlboro, Md.

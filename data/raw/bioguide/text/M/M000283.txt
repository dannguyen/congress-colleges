MAYNARD, Harry Lee, a Representative from Virginia
born in Portsmouth, Va., June 8, 1861
attended the common schools of Norfolk County
was graduated from the Virginia Agricultural and Mechanical College at Blacksburg in 1880
engaged in the real estate business and the promotion of public utilities
member of the State house of delegates in 1889 and 1890
served in the State senate 1893-1901
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-seventh and to the four succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1901-March 3, 1911)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1910
moved to New York City and engaged in the insurance and real estate business
died in Fort Totten, N.Y., October 23, 1922
interment in Oak Grove Cemetery, Portsmouth, Va.

McCARTHY, Carolyn, a Representative from New York
born Carolyn Cook in Brooklyn, Kings County, N.Y., January 5, 1944
graduated from Mineola High School, Garden City Park, N.Y., 1962
graduated from Glen Cove Nursing School, Glen Cove, N.Y., 1964
licensed practical nurse in ICU section, Glen Cove Hospital, Glen Cove, N.Y.
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2015), was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

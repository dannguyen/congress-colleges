McGOVERN, James P., a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Worcester, Worcester County, Mass., November 20, 1959
graduated from Worcester Academy, Worcester, Mass.
B.A., American University, Washington, D.C., 1981
M.P.A., American University, Washington, D.C., 1984
presidential campaign staff, United States Senator George McGovern of South Dakota, 1984
staff, United States Representative Joseph Moakley of Massachusetts, 1982-1996
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-present).

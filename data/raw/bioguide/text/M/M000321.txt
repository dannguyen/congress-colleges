McCARTY, Richard, a Representative from New York
born in Coeymans, Albany County, N.Y., February 19, 1780
attended the common schools
county clerk of Greene County 1811-1813
flour inspector of the state of New York
elected as a Republican to the Seventeenth Congress (March 4, 1821-March 3, 1823)
president of the Lafayette Bank in New York City
was one of the committee appointed to receive General Lafayette when he visited the United States in 1824 and 1825
died in New York City May 18, 1844
interment in Adams Cemetery, Coxsackie, Greene County, N.Y.

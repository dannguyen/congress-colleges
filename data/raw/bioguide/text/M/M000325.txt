McCLEAN, Moses, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Gettysburg, Pa., June 17, 1804
pursued an academic course
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1825 and commenced practice in Gettysburg, Pa.
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
resumed the practice of law in Gettysburg, Pa.
member of the State house of representatives in 1855
continued the practice of law until his death in Gettysburg, Pa., September 30, 1870
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

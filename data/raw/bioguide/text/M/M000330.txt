McCLELLAN, George, a Representative from New York
born in Schodack, Rensselaer County, N.Y., October 10, 1856
attended the public schools and the local academies at Spencertown and Chatham, N.Y.
was graduated from the Albany Law School in 1880
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Chatham, N.Y.
police justice for two terms
president of the Columbia County Agriculture Society for ten years
served as postmaster of Chatham
surrogate of Columbia County 1907-1913
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-third Congress (March 4, 1913-March 3, 1915)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1914 to the Sixty-fourth Congress
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1920
resumed the practice of his profession in Chatham, N.Y.
moved to Kinderhook, Columbia County, and died there February 20, 1927
interment in Nassau Cemetery at Nassau, Rensselaer County, N.Y.

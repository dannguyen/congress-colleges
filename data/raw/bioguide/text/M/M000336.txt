McCLENACHAN, Blair, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Ireland, birth date unknown
immigrated to the United States at an early age and settled in Philadelphia, Pa.
engaged in mercantile pursuits and in banking and shipping
one of the founders of and served with the First Troop of Philadelphia Cavalry during the Revolutionary War
in 1780 he subscribed a large sum of money to help the American forces and aided the Continental Congress with money and credit
member of the State house of representatives, 1790-1795
elected as a Republican to the Fifth Congress (March 4, 1797-March 3, 1799)
died in Philadelphia, Pa., May 8, 1812

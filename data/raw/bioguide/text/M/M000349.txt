McCOLLISTER, John Yetter, a Representative from Nebraska
born in Iowa City, Johnson County, Iowa, June 10, 1921
graduated from Washington High School, Sioux Falls, S.Dak, 1939.
B.S., University of Iowa, Iowa City, Iowa, 1943
lieutenant, United States Naval Reserve, 1943-1946
president, McCollister &Co., Omaha, Nebr., 1960-1971, 1979-1986
Douglas County (Nebr.) Commissioner, two terms, 1965-1970
delegate, Nebraska State Republican conventions, 1960-1970
delegate, Republican National Convention, 1968
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-second, Ninety-third, and Ninety-fourth Congresses (January 3, 1971-January 3, 1977)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1976, but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate
died on November 1, 2013, in Omaha, Nebr.

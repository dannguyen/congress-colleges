McCONNELL, Felix Grundy, a Representative from Alabama
born in Nashville, Tenn., April 1, 1809
moved with his parents to Fayetteville, Lincoln County, Tenn., in 1811
received a limited education and became a saddler
moved to Talladega, Talladega County, Ala., in 1834
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1836 and commenced practice in Talladega, Ala.
member of the State house of representatives in 1838
served in the State senate 1839-1843
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-eighth and Twenty-ninth Congresses and served from March 4, 1843, until his death in Washington, D.C., September 10, 1846
interment in the Congressional Cemetery.

McCORMICK, John Watts, a Representative from Ohio
born near Gallipolis, Gallia County, Ohio, December 20, 1831
attended the common schools, the Ohio Wesleyan University at Delaware, and the Ohio University at Athens
engaged in agricultural pursuits and stock raising
taught school and later became a Methodist minister
delegate to the Ohio constitutional convention in 1873
elected as a Republican to the Forty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1883-March 3, 1885)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1884 to the Forty-ninth Congress
trustee of Rio Grande College 1883-1885
resumed agricultural pursuits
died in Gallipolis, Ohio, June 25, 1917
interment in Mount Zion Cemetery near Gallipolis, Ohio.

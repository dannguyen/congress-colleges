McCOY, Robert, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Carlisle, Pa., birth date unknown
attended the common schools
prothonotary of Cumberland County
brigadier general of militia
state canal commissioner
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-second Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of United States Representative William Ramsey (November 22, 1831-March 3, 1833)
died in Wheeling, Va. (now West Virginia), June 7, 1849.

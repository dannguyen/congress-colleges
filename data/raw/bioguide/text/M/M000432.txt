McEWEN, Bob, a Representative from Ohio
born in Hillsboro, Highland County, Ohio, January 12, 1950
attended the public schools
B.B.A., University of Miami, Coral Gables, Fla., 1972
business executive
served in the Ohio house of representatives 1974-1980
delegate, Republican National Conventions, 1974-1978
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1981-January 3, 1993)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Third Congress
is a resident of Hillsboro, Ohio.

McHALE, Paul F., Jr., a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Bethlehem, Northampton County, Pa., July 26, 1950
B.A., Lehigh University, 1972, J.D., Georgetown University School of Law, 1977
served in the United States Marine Corps, 1972-1974, and in the Marine Corps Reserve, 1974 to present
unsuccessful candidate for nomination in 1980 to the Ninety-seventh Congress
member, Pennsylvania state house of representatives, 1983-1991
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 1999)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Sixth Congress in 1998
Assistant Secretary of Defense, Homeland Security, 2003-2009.

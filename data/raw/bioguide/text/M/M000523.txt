McKINNEY, Cynthia Ann, a Representative from Georgia
born in Atlanta, Fulton County, Ga., March 17, 1955
graduated St. Joseph High School
B.A., University of Southern California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1978
attended Fletcher School of Law and Diplomacy, Medford, Mass.
diplomatic fellow, Spellman College, Atlanta, Ga., 1984
faculty member, Clark Atlanta University and Agnes Scott College
member of the Georgia state house of representatives, 1988-1992
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2003)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in 2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Ninth Congress (January 3, 2005-January 3, 2007)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the One Hundred Tenth Congress in 2006
unsuccessful Green Party candidate for election for President of the United States in 2008.

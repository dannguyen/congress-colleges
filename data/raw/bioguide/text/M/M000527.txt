McKINNEY, Stewart Brett, a Representative from Connecticut
born in Pittsburgh, Allegheny County, Pa., January 30, 1931
graduated, Kent School, Kent, Conn., 1949
attended Princeton University, 1949-1951
B.A., Yale University, 1958
sergeant, United States Air Force, 1951-1955
elected State representative, Connecticut general assembly in 1966
reelected in 1968
serving as minority leader, 1969, 1970
director, Bridgeport Hospital, Bridgeport Child Guidance Clinic, Rehabilitation Center of Eastern Fairfield County, and Bridgeport Chamber of Commerce
delegate, Connecticut State Republican conventions, 1968-1970
delegate, Republican National Convention, 1972
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-second and to the eight succeeding Congresses and served from January 3, 1971, until his death in Washington, D.C., May 7, 1987
was a resident of Green Farms, Conn.

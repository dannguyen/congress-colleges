McMASTER, William Henry, a Senator from South Dakota
born in Ticonic, Monona County, Iowa, May 10, 1877
attended the public schools at Sioux City, Iowa
graduated from Beloit (Wis.) College in 1899
moved to Yankton, Yankton County, S.Dak., in 1901 and engaged in banking
member, State house of representatives 1911-1912
member, State senate 1913-1916
lieutenant governor of South Dakota 1917-1920, Governor 1921-1924
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate and served from March 4, 1925, to March 3, 1931
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1930
moved to Dixon, Ill., in 1933 and engaged in banking until his death there on September 14, 1968
interment in Oakwood Cemetery.

McPHERSON, John Rhoderic, a Senator from New Jersey
born in York, Livingston County, N.Y., May 9, 1833
attended the common schools and pursued an academic course
moved to Jersey City, N.J., in 1859
engaged in agricultural pursuits and was also a dealer in livestock
member, board of aldermen of Jersey City 1864-1870, and served as president of the board for three years
member, State senate 1871-1873
presidential elector on the Democratic ticket in 1876
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 1877
reelected in 1883 and 1889 and served from March 4, 1877, to March 3, 1895
chairman, Committee on Naval Affairs (Forty-sixth through Fifty-third Congresses)
died in Jersey City, N.J., October 8, 1897
interment in Oak Hill Cemetery, Washington, D.C.

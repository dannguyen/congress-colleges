MEBANE, Alexander, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Hawfields, N.C., November 26, 1744
attended the common schools of Orange County
delegate to the Provincial Congress of North Carolina in 1776
justice of the peace in 1776 and sheriff of Orange County in 1777
auditor of the Hillsboro district in 1783 and 1784
member of the Hillsboro convention in 1788 and of the Fayetteville convention in 1789
member of the state house of commons 1787-1792
elected as an Anti-Administration candidate to the Third Congress (March 4, 1793-March 3, 1795)
died at Hawfields, Orange County, N.C., July 5, 1795.

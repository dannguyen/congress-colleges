MELLISH, David Batcheller, a Representative from New York
born in Oxford, Worcester County, Mass., January 2, 1831
attended the public schools
became a printer in Worcester
taught school in Massachusetts, Maryland, and Pennsylvania
proofreader in New York City
reporter on the New York Tribune
stenographer to the police board of New York City for ten years
appointed assistant appraiser of merchandise for the port of New York in 1871
elected as a Republican to the Forty-third Congress and served from March 4, 1873, until his death in Washington, D.C., on May 23, 1874
interment in Hillside Cemetery, Auburn, Mass.

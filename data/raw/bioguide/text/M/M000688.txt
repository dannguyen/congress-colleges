MICA, Daniel Andrew, a Representative from Florida
born in Binghamton, Broome County, N.Y., February 4, 1944
attended Horace Mann School, Binghamton, N.Y., 1950
graduated, Miami Edison High School, Fla., 1961
attended the University of Florida, Gainesville, 1961
B.A., Florida Atlantic University, Boca Raton, 1966
teacher, Palm Beach, Fla., and Montgomery County, Md., 1966-1968
administrative assistant to Rep. Paul G. Rogers, 1968-1978
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-sixth and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1989)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1988 but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate
is a resident of West Palm Beach, Fla.

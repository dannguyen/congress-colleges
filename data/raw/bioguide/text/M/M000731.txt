MILLER, Jacob Welsh, a Senator from New Jersey
born in German Valley, Morris County, N.J., August 29, 1800
attended the public schools
studied law
admitted to the bar in 1823 and practiced in Morristown, N.J.
elected to the State general assembly in 1832
served in the State council 1838-1840
elected as a Whig to the United States Senate in 1840
reelected in 1846 and served from March 4, 1841, to March 3, 1853
chairman, Committee on the District of Columbia (Twenty-seventh and Twenty-eighth Congresses)
died in Morristown, N.J., September 30, 1862
interment in St. Peter's Parish Churchyard.

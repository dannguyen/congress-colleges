MILLER, John Ripin, a Representative from Washington
born in New York City, May 23, 1938
attended public schools and Friends Seminary, New York City
B.A., Bucknell University, Lewisburg, Pa., 1959
M.A., Yale University, 1964
LL.B., Yale University Law School, 1964
served in the United States Army, 1960, and Reserves, 1961-1968
admitted to the bar in 1965 and began practice in Seattle, Wash.
adjunct professor, University of Puget Sound, Wash., 1981-1984
assistant attorney general of State of Washington, 1965-1968
member and president, Seattle City Council, 1972-1980
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-ninth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1985-January 3, 1993)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
is a resident of Seattle, Wash.

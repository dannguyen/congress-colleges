MILLER, Joseph, a Representative from Ohio
born in Virginia September 9, 1819
attended the common schools
moved to Ohio and settled in Chillicothe
was graduated from Miami University, Oxford, Ohio, in 1839
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1841 and commenced practice in Chillicothe, Ohio
prosecuting attorney of Ross County, Ohio, 1844-1848
member of the State house of representatives in 1856
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1859)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1858 to the Thirty-sixth Congress
appointed United States judge for Nebraska Territory March 5, 1859
died in Cincinnati, Ohio, on May 27, 1862
interment in Grandview Cemetery, Chillicothe, Ohio.

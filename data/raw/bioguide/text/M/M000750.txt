MILLER, Pleasant Moorman, a Representative from Tennessee
born in Lynchburg, Campbell County, Va., birth date unknown
moved to Rogersville, Hawkins County, Tenn., in 1796, and thence to Knoxville, Knox County, Tenn., 1800
one of the commissioners for the government of Knoxville, 1801 and 1802
elected as a Republican to the Eleventh Congress (March 4, 1809-March 3, 1811)
moved to west Tennessee about 1824, and was chancellor of that division in 1836 and 1837
died in 1849
interment in Trenton, Gibson County, Tenn.

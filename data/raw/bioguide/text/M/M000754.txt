MILLER, Smith, a Representative from Indiana
born near Charlotte, N.C., May 30, 1804
moved to Gibson County, Ind., with his parents who settled in Patoka in 1813
received a limited schooling
engaged in agricultural pursuits
member of the State house of representatives 1835-1839 and in 1846
served in the State senate 1841-1844 and 1847-1850
delegate to the State constitutional convention in 1850
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third and Thirty-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1857)
resumed agricultural pursuits
delegate to the Democratic National Convention at Charleston, S.C., in 1860
died near Patoka, Ind., March 21, 1872
interment in Robb Cemetery.

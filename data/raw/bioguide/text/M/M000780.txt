MILLSON, John Singleton, a Representative from Virginia
born in Norfolk, Va., October 1, 1808
pursued an academic course
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1829 and commenced practice in Norfolk
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-first and to the five succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1849-March 3, 1861)
chairman, Committee on Revolutionary Pensions (Thirty-second Congress)
resumed the practice of law
died in Norfolk, Va., March 1, 1874
interment in Cedar Grove Cemetery.

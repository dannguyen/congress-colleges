MONTGOMERY, William, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Guilford County, N.C., December 29, 1789
studied medicine and practiced his profession in Albrights, Orange County, N.C.
member of the state senate 1824-1827 and 1829-1834
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress and reelected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth and Twenty-sixth Congresses (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1841)
chairman, Committee on the Post Office and Post Roads (Twenty-fifth Congress)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1840
died in Albrights, N.C., November 27, 1844.

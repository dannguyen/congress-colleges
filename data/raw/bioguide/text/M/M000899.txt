MOORE, Heman Allen, a Representative from Ohio
born in Plainfield, Washington County, Vt., August 27, 1809
pursued an academic course
studied law in Rochester, N.Y.
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Columbus, Ohio
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-eighth Congress and served from March 4, 1843, until his death in Columbus, Ohio, April 3, 1844
interment in Green Lawn Cemetery.

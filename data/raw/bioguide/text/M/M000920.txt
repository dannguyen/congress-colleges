MOORE, Thomas Love, a Representative from Virginia
born near Charles Town, Jefferson County, Va. (now West Virginia), birth date unknown
pursued an academic course
studied law and practiced
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative George F. Strother
reelected as a Republican to the Seventeenth Congress (November 13, 1820-March 3, 1823)
resumed the practice of law at Warrenton, Va.
made the principal speech upon the visit of General Lafayette to Warrenton on August 23, 1825
died in Warrenton, Fauquier County, Va., in 1862
interment in Warrenton Cemetery.

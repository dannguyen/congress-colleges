MORGAN, Stephen, a Representative from Ohio
born in Jackson County, Ohio, January 25, 1854
attended the common schools, Central College, Worthington, Ohio, and the Normal University, Lebanon, Ohio
taught in the public schools of Jackson County for a number of years
school examiner for nine years and principal of Oak Hill Academy for fifteen years
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-sixth, Fifty-seventh, and Fifty-eighth Congresses (March 4, 1899-March 3, 1905)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1904 to the Fifty-ninth Congress
moved to Columbus, Ohio, and retired from public life
died at Magnetic Springs, Union County, Ohio, February 9, 1928
interment in Horeb Cemetery, near Oak Hill, Jackson County, Ohio.

MORRIS, Joseph, (father of James Remley Morris), a Representative from Ohio
born in Greene County, Pa., October 16, 1795
attended the public schools
sheriff of Greene County in 1824
moved to Woodsfield, Monroe County, Ohio, in 1829 and engaged in mercantile pursuits
member of the State house of representatives in 1833 and 1834
treasurer of Monroe County
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-eighth and Twenty-ninth Congresses (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1847)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1846
resumed business interests
died in Woodsfield, Ohio, October 23, 1854
interment in Morris Cemetery, near Woodsfield.

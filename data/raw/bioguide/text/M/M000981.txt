MORRIS, Joseph Watkins, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Sulphur, Henry County, Ky., on February 26, 1879
moved to New Castle, Ky., with his father in 1889
attended the public schools and was graduated from the New Castle High School in 1899
engaged in mercantile pursuits at New Castle
secretary to Representative J. Campbell Cantrill 1909-1923
delegate to every Democratic State convention since 1904
chairman of the Democratic State campaign committee in 1923
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-eighth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of J. Campbell Cantrill and served from November 30, 1923, to March 3, 1925
was not a candidate for renomination in 1924
State revenue agent for Kentucky 1925-1927
manager of a bus terminal in Louisville, Ky., from 1929 until his death in Louisville, Ky., December 21, 1937
interment in Odd Fellows Cemetery, Carrollton, Ky.

MORRISON, James Hobson, a Representative from Louisiana
born in Hammond, Tangipahoa Parish, La., December 8, 1908
attended the public schools
was graduated from the law department of Tulane University at New Orleans, La., LL.B., and J.D., 1934
was admitted to the bar in 1934 and commenced practice in Hammond, La.
unsuccessful candidate for Governor in 1939 and again in 1944
delegate to Democratic National Conventions, 1956 and 1960
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-eighth and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1943-January 3, 1967)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1966 to the Ninetieth Congress
resumed the practice of law
died in Hammond, La., on July 20, 2000.

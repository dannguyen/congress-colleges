MOSELEY, William Abbott, a Representative from New York
born in Whitesboro, Oneida County, N.Y., October 20, 1798
was graduated from Yale College in 1816
studied medicine and practiced
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced in Buffalo, N.Y.
member of the State assembly in 1835
served in the State senate 1838-1841
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-eighth and Twenty-ninth Congresses (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1847)
resumed the practice of law
died in New York City on November 19, 1873
interment in Forest Lawn Cemetery, Buffalo, N.Y.

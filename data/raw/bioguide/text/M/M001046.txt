MOULTON, Mace, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Concord, N.H., May 2, 1796
attended the public schools
sheriff of Hillsborough County in 1845
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
State councilor in 1848 and 1849
engaged in banking
died in Manchester, Hillsborough County, N.H., May 5, 1867
interment in Valley Cemetery.

MUHLENBERG, Henry Augustus, (son of Henry Augustus Philip Muhlenberg and grandson of Joseph Hiester), a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Reading, Pa., July 21, 1823
pursued classical studies
was graduated from Dickinson College, Carlisle, Pa., in 1841
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1844 and commenced practice in Reading, Pa.
member of the State senate 1849-1852
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress and served from March 4, 1853, until his death in Washington, D.C., January 9, 1854
interment in Charles Evans Cemetery, Reading, Pa.

MUMFORD, George, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Rowan County, N.C., birth date unknown
attended the common schools
member of the State house of commons, 1810 and 1811
elected as a Republican to the Fifteenth Congress (March 4, 1817-December 31, 1818)
died on December 31, 1818, in Washington, D.C.
interment in the Congressional Cemetery.

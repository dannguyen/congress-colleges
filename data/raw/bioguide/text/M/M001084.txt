MURFREE, William Hardy, (uncle of David W. Dickinson), a Representative from North Carolina
born in Hertford County, N.C., October 2, 1781
was graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 1801
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Edenton, N.C.
also interested in agricultural pursuits
member of the State house of representatives in 1805 and 1812
elected as a Republican to the Thirteenth and Fourteenth Congresses (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1817)
chairman, Committee on Public Expenditures (Fourteenth Congress)
moved from Murfreesboro, N.C., to his estate in Williamson County, Tenn., in 1823 and died there on January 19, 1827
interment in Murfree Cemetery, northwest of Franklin, Williamson County, Tenn.

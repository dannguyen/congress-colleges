MURPHEY, Charles, a Representative from Georgia
born near Anderson, Anderson County, S.C., May 9, 1799
attended the country schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1825 and commenced practice in Decatur, Ga.
clerk of the superior court of De Kalb County, Ga., 1825-1827
member of the State house of representatives 1839-1841
served in the State senate in 1842, 1845, 1849-1850, 1855-1856
elected as a Unionist to the Thirty-second Congress (March 4, 1851-March 3, 1853)
resumed the practice of law
delegate to the Democratic National Convention at Baltimore in 1860
died in Decatur, Ga., January 16, 1861
interment in Decatur City Cemetery.

MANZULLO, Donald A., a Representative from Illinois
born in Rockford, Winnebago County, Ill., March 24, 1944
graduated from Auburn High School, Rockford, Ill., 1962
B.A., American University, Washington, D.C., 1967
J.D., Marquette University School of Law, Milwaukee, Wis., 1970
lawyer, private practice
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the One Hundred Second Congress in 1990
member, Republican National Committee
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Third and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2013)
chair, Committee on Small Business (One Hundred Seventh through One Hundred Ninth Congresses)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

MATHESON, James David (Jim), a Representative from Utah
born in Salt Lake City, Salt Lake County, Utah, March 21, 1960
A.B., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1982
M.B.A., University of California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1987
business consultant
business owner
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

McCOLLUM, Betty, a Representative from Minnesota
born in Minneapolis, Hennepin County, Minn., July 12, 1954
graduated from South St. Paul High School, St. Paul, Minn., 1972
graduated from University of St. Catherine, St. Paul, Minn.
member of the Minnesota state house of representatives, 1992-2001
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present).

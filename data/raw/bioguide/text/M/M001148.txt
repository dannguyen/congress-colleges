MEEK, Kendrick B., (son of Carrie Meek), a Representative from Florida
born in Miami, Dade County, Fla., September 6, 1966
B.S., Florida A&M University, Tallahassee, Fla., 1989
member of the Florida state house of representatives, 1994-1998
member of the Florida state senate, 1998-2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth Congress and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-January 3, 2011)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress, but was an unsuccessful candidate for the United States Senate in 2010.

MILLER, Candice S., a Representative from Michigan
born in Detroit, Wayne County, Mich., May 7, 1954
graduated from Lakeshore High School, St. Clair Shores, Mich.
attended Macomb County Community College, Warren, Mich.
attended Northwood Institute
trustee, Harrison Township Board, Harrison Township, Mich., 1979-1980
Harrison Township supervisor, Harrison Township, Mich., 1980-1992
treasurer, Macomb County, Mich., 1992-1994
Michigan secretary of state, 1994-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present)
chair, Committee on House Administration (One Hundred Thirteenth and One Hundred Fourteenth Congresses).

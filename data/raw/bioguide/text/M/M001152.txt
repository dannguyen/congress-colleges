MUSGRAVE, Marilyn N, a Representative from Colorado
born in Greeley, Weld County, Colo., January 27, 1949
B.A., Colorado State University, 1972
businesswoman
teacher
member of the Fort Morgan, Colo., school board, 1990-1994
member of the Colorado state house of representatives, 1994-1998
member of the Colorado state senate, 1998-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the two succeeding Congress (January 3, 2003-January 3, 2009)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

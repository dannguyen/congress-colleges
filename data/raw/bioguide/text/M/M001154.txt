MILLER, Brad, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Fayetteville, N.C., on May 19, 1953
B.A., University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, N.C., 1975
M.S., London School of Economics, London, England, 1978
J.D., Columbia University, New York, N.Y., 1979
lawyer, private practice
member of the North Carolina state house of representatives, 1992-1994
member of the North Carolina state senate, 1996-2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth Congress and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

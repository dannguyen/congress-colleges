MARCHANT, Kenny, a Representative from Texas
born in Bonham, Fannin County, Tex., February 23, 1951
B.A., South Nazarene University, Bethany, Okla., 1974
attended Nazarene Theological Seminary, Kansas City, Mo., 1975-1976
real estate developer
member of the Carrollton, Tex., city council, 1980-1984
mayor of Carrollton, Tex., 1984-1987
member of the Texas state house of representatives, 1987-2004
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Ninth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2005-present).

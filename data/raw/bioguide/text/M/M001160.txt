MOORE, Gwendolynne S. (Gwen), a Representative from Wisconsin
born in Racine, Racine County, Wis., April 18, 1951
graduated from Northern Division High School, Milwaukee, Wis., 1969
B.A., Marquette University, Milwaukee, Wis., 1978
housing officer, Wisconsin Housing and Development Authority
member of the Wisconsin state assembly, 1989-1992
member of the Wisconsin state senate, 1993-2004, president pro tempore, 1997-1998
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Ninth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2005-present).

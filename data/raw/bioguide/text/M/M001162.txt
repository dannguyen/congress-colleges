MARTINEZ, Melquiades R. (Mel), a Senator from Florida
born in Sagua La Grande, Cuba, on October 23, 1946
immigrated to the United States in 1962
lived with foster families until reunited with his family in Orlando, Fla., in 1966
J.D., College of Law, Florida State University 1973
practiced law in Orlando, Fla., for twenty-five years
secretary, Department of Housing and Urban Development 2001-2005
elected as a Republican to the U.S. Senate in 2004 and served from January 3, 2005, until his resignation on September 9, 2009.

MAHONEY, Tim, a Representative from Florida
born in Aurora, Kane County, Ill., August 15, 1956
B.A., West Virginia University, Morgantown, W.Va., 1978
M.B.A., George Washington University, Washington, D.C., 1983
businessman
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth Congress (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2009)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

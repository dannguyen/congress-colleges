MARKEY, Betsy, a Representative from Colorado
born in Cresskill, Bergen County, N. J.
April 27, 1956
B. S., University of Florida, Gainesville, Fla., 1978
M. P. A., American University, Washington, D. C., 1983
business owner
legislative aide
aide to United States Senator Ken Salazar, 2005-2007
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

born in Charleston, Charleston County, S.C., September 16, 1959
B.S., United States Naval Academy, Annapolis, Md., 1981
United States Navy, 1981-2001
businessman
engineer
staff, United States House of Representatives Armed Services Committee
unsuccessful candidate for United States House of Representatives in 2006

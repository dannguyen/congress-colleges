McMAHON, Michael E., a Representative from New York
born in Staten Island, Richmond County, N.Y., September 12, 1957
graduated from Monsignor Farrell High School, Staten Island, N.Y.
B.A., New York University, New York, N.Y., 1980
A.A. Heidelberg U., Heidelberg, Germany, 1983
J.D. New York University Law School, New York, N.Y., 1985
lawyer, private practice
member, New York city council, 2002-2008
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010
Staten Island, Richmond County, N.Y. district attorney, 2015-present.

MINNICK, Walt, a Representative from Idaho
born in Walla Walla, Walla Walla County, Wash., September 20, 1942
B.A., Whitman College, Walla Walla, Wash., 1964
M.B.A., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1966
J.D., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1969
United States Army, 1970-1972
lawyer, private practice
businessman
staff, President Richard M. Nixon
unsuccessful candidate for the United States Senate in 1996
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

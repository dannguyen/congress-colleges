MURPHY, Scott, a Representative from New York
born in Columbia, Boone County, Mo., January 26, 1970
graduated from David H. Hickman High School, Columbia, Mo., 1988
A.B., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1992
businessman
professional advocate
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress, by special election to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Kirsten Gillibrand (March 31, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

MARINO, Thomas A., a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Williamsport, Lycoming County, Pa., August 15, 1952
graduated from Williamsport Area High School, Williamsport, Pa., 1970
A.A., Williamsport Area Community College, Williamsport, Pa., 1983
B.A., Lycoming College, Williamsport, Pa., 1985
J.D., Dickinson School of Law, Carlisle, Pa., 1988
manufacturing manager
lawyer, private practice
Lycoming County,Pa., district attorney, 1992-2002
United States attorney for the Middle District of Pennsylvania, 1992-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

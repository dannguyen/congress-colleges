MULVANEY, Mick, a Representative from South Carolina
born in Alexandria, Va., July 21, 1967
graduated from Charlotte Catholic High School, Charlotte, N.C., 1985
B.S.F.S., Georgetown University, Washington, D.C., 1989
J.D., University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, N.C., 1992
lawyer, private practice
real estate developer
member of the South Carolina state house of representatives, 2007-2009
member of the South Carolina state senate, 2009-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

MENG, Grace, a Representative from New York
born in Queens, Queens County, N.Y., October 1, 1975
A.B., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1997
J.D., Yeshiva University, New York City, N.Y., 2002
lawyer, private practice
member of the New York state assembly, 2009-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

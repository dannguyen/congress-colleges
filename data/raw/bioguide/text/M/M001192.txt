McALLISTER, Vance M., a Representative from Louisiana
born in Oak Grove, West Carroll Parish, La., January 7, 1974
graduated from Forest High School, Forest, La., 1992
attended, University of Louisiana, Monroe
United States Army, 1992-1994
Louisiana National Guard
business owner
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Rodney Alexander (November 16, 2013-January 3, 2015)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

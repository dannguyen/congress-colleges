MacARTHUR, Thomas Charles, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Hebron, Tolland County, Conn., October 16, 1960
graduated from RHAM High School, Hebron, Conn., 1978
B.A., Hofstra University, Hempstead, N.Y., 1982
insurance executive
member of the Randolph, N.J., city council, 2011-2013
deputy mayor of Randolph, N.J., 2012
mayor of Randolph, N.J., 2013-2014
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

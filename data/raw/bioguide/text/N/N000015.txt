NEAL, Richard Edmund, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Springfield, Hampden County, Mass., February 14, 1949
graduated from Springfield Technical High School, Springfield, Mass., 1968
B.A., American International College, Springfield, Mass., 1972
M.B.A., University of Hartford, West Hartford, Conn., 1976
staff, Mayor William C. Sullivan, Springfield, Mass., 1973-1978
member of the Springfield, Mass., city council, 1978-1984
Mayor of Springfield, Mass., 1984-1988
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred First and to the thirteen succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1989-present).

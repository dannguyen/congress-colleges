NEWTON, Cherubusco, a Representative from Louisiana
born in Greensburg, St. Helena Parish, La., May 15, 1848
attended private schools in Bastrop, La., and the Louisiana State University, then at Alexandria, La.
taught school
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1870 and commenced practice in Bastrop, La.
member of the State senate 1879-1883
declined a judgeship in 1885
elected as a Democrat to the Fiftieth Congress (March 4, 1887-March 3, 1889)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1888
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1888
resumed the practice of law in Bastrop, La., for several years, and then moved to Monroe, Ouachita Parish, La., where he continued the practice of law until his death on May 26, 1910
interment in the New Cemetery, Bastrop, La.

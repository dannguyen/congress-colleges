NEWTON, Eben, a Representative from Ohio
born in Goshen, Conn., October 16, 1795
attended the common schools
moved to Portage County, Ohio, in 1814 and engaged in agricultural pursuits
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1823 and commenced practice in Canfield, Mahoning County, Ohio
member of the State senate 1842-1851
presiding judge of the court of common pleas 1844-1851
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-second Congress (March 4, 1851-March 3, 1853)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1852 to the Thirty-third Congress
served as president of the Ashtabula & New Lisbon Railroad 1856-1859
again served in the State senate 1862-1864
resumed the practice of law and also engaged in agricultural pursuits
died in Canfield, Ohio, on November 6, 1885
interment in Canfield Village Cemetery.

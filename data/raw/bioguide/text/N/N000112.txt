NIVEN, Archibald Campbell, a Representative from New York
born in Newburgh, Orange County, N.Y., December 8, 1803
completed preparatory studies
surrogate of Sullivan County 1828-1840
adjutant general of New York in 1844
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
district attorney of Sullivan County 1847-1850
member of the State senate in 1864 and 1865
died in Monticello, Sullivan County, N.Y., February 21, 1882
interment in Rock Ridge Cemetery.

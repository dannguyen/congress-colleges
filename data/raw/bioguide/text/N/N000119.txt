NOBLE, Warren Perry, a Representative from Ohio
born near Berwick, Luzerne County, Pa., June 14, 1820
moved to Ohio
attended the common schools
taught school
was graduated from Wadsworth Academy, Wadsworth, Ohio, in 1840
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1843 and commenced practice in Tiffin, Ohio
member of the State house of representatives 1846-1850
prosecuting attorney of Seneca County 1851-1854
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-seventh and Thirty-eighth Congresses (March 4, 1861-March 3, 1865)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Thirty-ninth Congress
resumed the practice of law in Tiffin, Ohio, and died there July 9, 1903
interment in Green Lawn Cemetery.

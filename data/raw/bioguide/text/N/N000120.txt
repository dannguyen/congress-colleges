NOBLE, William Henry, a Representative from New York
born in New Milford, Litchfield County, Conn., September 22, 1788
moved to Ballston Spa, then to Cato, N.Y., and later to Rochester, N.Y.
received a limited education
was a tanner by trade and later a farmer
member of the New York State assembly 1828-1830
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1837-March 3, 1839)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1838 to the Twenty-sixth Congress
inspector of Auburn Prison 1843-1845
died in Rochester, N.Y., February 5, 1850.

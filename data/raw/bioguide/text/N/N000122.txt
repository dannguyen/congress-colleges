NOELL, John William, (father of Thomas Estes Noell), a Representative from Missouri
born in Bedford County, Va., February 22, 1816
attended the rural schools
at the age of seventeen moved to Missouri and settled near Perryville
engaged in milling and storekeeping
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1843 and commenced practice in Perryville, Mo.
clerk of the circuit court for Perry County 1841-1850
member of the state senate 1851-1855
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-sixth and Thirty-seventh Congresses
reelected as an Unconditional Unionist to the Thirty-eighth Congress and served from March 4, 1859, until his death in Washington, D.C., March 14, 1863

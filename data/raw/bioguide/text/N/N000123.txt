NOELL, Thomas Estes, (son of John William Noell), a Representative from Missouri
born in Perryville, Perry County, Mo., April 3, 1839
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1858 and commenced practice in Perryville, Mo., the same year
during the Civil War was appointed a military commissioner in 1861
served as major in the State militia from July 1861 to April 1862
appointed captain unassigned in Company C, Nineteenth Infantry, United States Army, and served from April 1, 1862, until his resignation on February 20, 1865, to take his seat in the House of Representatives
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-ninth Congress
reelected as a Democrat to the Fortieth Congress and served from March 4, 1865, until his death in St. Louis, Mo., on October 3, 1867
interment in St. Mary's Cemetery, Perryville, Mo.

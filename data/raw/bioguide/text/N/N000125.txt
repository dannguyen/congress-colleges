NOLAN, Mae Ella, (wife of John Ignatius Nolan), a Representative from California
born in San Francisco, Calif., September 20, 1886
attended the public schools, St. VincentÂ’s Convent and Ayres Business College of San Francisco
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-seventh and to the Sixty-eighth Congresses by special election, January 23, 1923 to fill the vacancies caused by the death of her husband, John Ignatius Nolan, who had been reelected in 1922, and she served from January 23, 1923, to March 3, 1925
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Post Office Department (Sixty-eighth Congress)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1924 to the Sixty-ninth Congress
moved to Sacramento in her later years where she died July 9, 1973
interment in Holy Cross Cemetery, Colma, Calif.

NORTHWAY, Stephen Asa, a Representative from Ohio
born in Christian Hollow, Onondaga County, N.Y., June 19, 1833
moved with his parents in 1840 to the township of Orwell, Ashtabula County, Ohio
attended the district school, Kingsville Academy, and Orwell Academy
taught school
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1859 and commenced practice in Jefferson, Ashtabula County, Ohio
prosecuting attorney of Ashtabula County 1861-1865
member of the State house of representatives in 1865 and 1866
resumed the practice of law
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-third, Fifty-fourth, and Fifty-fifth Congresses and served from March 4, 1893, until his death in Jefferson, Ashtabula County, Ohio, on September 8, 1898
interment in Oakdale Cemetery.

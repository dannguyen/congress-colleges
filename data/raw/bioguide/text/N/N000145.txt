NORTON, Daniel Sheldon, a Senator from Minnesota
born in Mount Vernon, Knox County, Ohio, on April 12, 1829
pursued classical studies and graduated from Kenyon College, Gambier, Ohio
served in the Mexican War
after the war returned to Ohio
studied law
admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Mount Vernon in 1852
moved to St. Paul, Minn., in 1855, and then to Winona, Minn., in 1856, where he continued the practice of law
member, State house of representatives 1857-1860
member, State senate 1861-1864
elected as a Unionist to the United States Senate and served from March 4, 1865, until his death in Washington, D.C. on July 13, 1870
interment in Greenmount Cemetery, Baltimore, Md.

NORWOOD, Charles W., Jr., a Representative from Georgia
born in Valdosta, Lowndes County, Ga., July 27, 1941
graduated from Baylor Military High School, Chattanooga, Tenn., 1959
B.A., Georgia Southern University, Statesboro, Ga., 1964
D.D.S., Georgetown University Dental School, Washington, D.C, 1967
United States Army, 1967-1969
dentist
business owner
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and to the six succeeding Congresses, served until his death on February 13, 2007 (January 3, 1995-February 13, 2007).

NUTE, Alonzo, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Milton, Strafford County, N.H., February 12, 1826
attended the common schools
moved to Natick, Mass., in 1842
returned to New Hampshire in 1848 and engaged in the manufacture of boots and shoes in Farmington
in the spring of 1861, entered the Union Army in the Sixth Regiment, New Hampshire Volunteer Infantry
member of the New Hampshire house of representatives in 1866
served in the State senate in 1867 and 1868
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1876
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-first Congress (March 4, 1889-March 3, 1891)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1890
died in Farmington, Strafford County, N.H., December 24, 1892
interment in Pine Grove Cemetery.

NEUGEBAUER, Randy, a Representative from Texas
born in St. Louis, St. Louis County, Mo., December 24, 1949
graduated from Coronado High School, Lubbock, Tex., 1968
graduated from Texas Tech University, Lubbock, Tex., 1972
businessman
member of the Lubbock, Tex., city council, 1992-1998
mayor pro tempore, Lubbock, Tex., 1994-1996
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Larry Combest, and reelected to the six succeeding Congresses (June 3, 2003-present).

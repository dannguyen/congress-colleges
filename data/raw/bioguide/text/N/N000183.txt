NYE, Glenn C., III, a Representative from Virginia
born in Philadelphia, Philadelphia County, Pa., September 9, 1974
graduated from Norfolk Academy
B. S., Georgetown University, Washington, D. C., 1996
foreign service officer, United States Department of State
staff member, United States Agency for International Development
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2010.

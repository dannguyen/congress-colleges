NOEM, Kristi, a Representative from South Dakota
born in Watertown, Codington County, S.Dak., November 30, 1971
graduated from Hamlin High School, Hayti, S.Dak., 1990
attended Mount Marty College, Yankton, S.Dak.
attended Northern State University, Aberdeen, S.Dak., 1990-1992
B.A., South Dakota State University, Brookings, S.Dak., 2011
farmer
rancher
member of the South Dakota state house of representatives, 2007-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

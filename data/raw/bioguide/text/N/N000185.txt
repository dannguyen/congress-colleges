NUGENT, Richard, a Representative from Florida
born in Evergreen Park, Cook County, Illinois, on May 26, 1951
B.A., Saint Leo College, Saint Leo, Fla., 1990
M.P.A., Troy State University, Troy, Ala., 1995
Illinois Air National Guard, 1969-1975
deputy sheriff, Hernando County, Fla.
sheriff of Hernando County, Fla., 2000-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

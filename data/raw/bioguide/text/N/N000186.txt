NUNNELEE, Alan, a Representative from Mississippi
born in Tupelo, Lee County, Miss., October 9, 1958
B.S., Mississippi State University, Mississippi State, Miss., 1980
business owner
member of the Mississippi state senate, 1995-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses
served until his death on February 6, 2015 (January 3, 2011-February 6, 2015)
died on February 6, 2015, in Tupelo, Miss.

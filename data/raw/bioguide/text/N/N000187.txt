NEGRETE McLEOD, Gloria, a Representative from California
born in Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, Calif., September 6, 1941
A.A., Chaffey Community College, Rancho Cucamonga, Calif.
instructional aide
member of the Chaffey Community College board, 1995-2000
president of the Chaffey Community College board, 2000
member of the California state assembly, 2000-2006
member of the California state senate, 2006-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress (January 3, 2013-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014, but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to San Bernadino County, Calif., board of supervisors.

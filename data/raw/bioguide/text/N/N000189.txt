NEWHOUSE, Daniel Milton, a Representative from Washington
born in Sunnyside, Yakima County, Wash., July 10, 1955
graduated from Sunnyside High School, Sunnyside, Wash., 1973
B.S., Washington State University, Pullman, Wash., 1977
farmer
member of the Washington state house of representatives, 2003-2009
director of the Washington state department of agriculture, 2009-2013
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

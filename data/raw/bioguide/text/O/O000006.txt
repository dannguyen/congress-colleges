OBERSTAR, James Louis, a Representative from Minnesota
born in Chisholm, St. Louis County, Minn., September 10, 1934
graduated from Chisholm High School, Chisholm, Minn., 1952
B.A., College of St. Thomas, St. Paul, Minn., 1956
M.A., College of Europe, Bruges, Belgium, 1957
staff for United States Representative John Anton Blatnik of Minnesota, 1963-1974
administrator of the Committee on Public Works, United States House of Representatives, 1971-1974
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-fourth and to the seventeen succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1975-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010
chair, Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure (One Hundred Tenth and One Hundred Eleventh Congresses)
died on May 3, 2014, in Potomac, Md.

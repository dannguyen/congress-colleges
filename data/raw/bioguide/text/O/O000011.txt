O'BRIEN, James, a Representative from New York
born in County Kings, Ireland, March 13, 1841
attended the common schools
immigrated to the United States around 1850 and settled in New York City
alderman of the city of New York in 1864 and 1866
sheriff of the city and county of New York in 1867
served in the State senate in 1872 and 1873
unsuccessful candidate for mayor of the city of New York in 1873
unsuccessful candidate for election in 1874 to the Forty-fourth Congress
elected as an Independent Democrat to the Forty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1879-March 3, 1881)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1880
engaged as a broker until his death in New York City March 5, 1907
interment in Calvary Cemetery, Long Island, N.Y.

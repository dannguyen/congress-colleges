O'BRIEN, Jeremiah, a Representative from Maine
born in Machias, Washington County, Maine, on January 21, 1778
attended the common schools
engaged in lumber manufacturing and in shipping
member of the State senate 1821-1824
elected as an Adams-Clay Republican to the Eighteenth Congress, and reelected as an Adams candidate to the Nineteenth, and Twentieth Congresses (March 4, 1823-March 3, 1829)
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Department of the Navy (Nineteenth Congress)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1828 to the Twenty-first Congress
member of the state house of representatives 1832-1834
resumed his former lumber manufacturing and shipping business
died in Boston, Mass., May 30, 1858

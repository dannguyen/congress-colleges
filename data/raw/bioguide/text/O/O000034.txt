O'DANIEL, Wilbert Lee (Pappy), a Senator from Texas
born in Malta, Morgan County, Ohio, March 11, 1890
reared on a cattle ranch near Arlington, Kans.
attended the public schools and business college in Hutchinson, Kans.
engaged in the flour milling and merchandising business, Fort Worth, Tex., 1909-1938
radio personality
elected Governor of Texas in 1938, reelected in 1940, and served until August 3, 1941, when he resigned, having been elected to the Senate
elected on June 28, 1941, as a Democrat to the United States Senate to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Morris Sheppard
reelected in 1942 and served from August 4, 1941, until January 3, 1949
was not a candidate for renomination in 1948
unsuccessful candidate for the gubernatorial nomination in 1956
owned and operated several life insurance companies in Texas
died in Dallas, Tex., May 11, 1969
interment in Hillcrest Memorial Park.

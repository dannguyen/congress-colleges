ODELL, Moses Fowler, a Representative from New York
born in Tarrytown, Westchester County, N.Y., February 24, 1818
completed preparatory studies
appointed entry clerk in the New York customhouse in 1845 and became public appraiser
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-seventh and Thirty-eighth Congresses (March 4, 1861-March 3, 1865)
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Department of the Treasury (Thirty-seventh Congress)
appointed Navy agent at the city of New York in 1865 and served until his death in Brooklyn, N.Y., June 13, 1866
interment in Greenwood Cemetery.

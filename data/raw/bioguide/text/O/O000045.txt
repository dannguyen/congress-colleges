OGLE, Alexander, (father of Charles Ogle and grandfather of Andrew Jackson Ogle), a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Frederick, Frederick County, Md., August 10, 1766
completed preparatory studies
moved to Somerset, Pa., in 1795
member of the State house of representatives in 1803, 1804, 1807, 1808, and 1811
served as major general in the State militia
prothonotary, recorder of deeds, and clerk of courts 1812-1817
elected as a Republican to the Fifteenth Congress (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1819)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1818
again a member of the State house of representatives 1819-1823
served in the State senate in 1827 and 1828
died in Somerset, Pa., October 14, 1832
interment in Union Cemetery.

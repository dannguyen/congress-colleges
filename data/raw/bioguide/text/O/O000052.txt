O'HAIR, Frank Trimble, a Representative from Illinois
born near Paris, Edgar County, Ill., March 12, 1870
attended the common schools and was graduated from the law department of De Pauw University, Greencastle, Ind., in 1893
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Paris, Ill.
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-third Congress (March 4, 1913-March 3, 1915)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1914 to the Sixty-fourth Congress
resumed the practice of his profession in Paris, Ill., until his death there August 3, 1932
interment in Edgar Cemetery.

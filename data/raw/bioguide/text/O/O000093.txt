O'NEILL, Charles, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Philadelphia, Pa., March 21, 1821
was graduated from Dickinson College, Carlisle, Pa., in 1840
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1843 and commenced practice in Philadelphia
member of the State house of representatives 1850-1852 and in 1860
served in the State senate in 1853
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-eighth and to the three succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1863-March 3, 1871)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1870 to the Forty-second Congress
elected to the Forty-third and to the ten succeeding Congresses and served from March 4, 1873, until his death in Philadelphia, Pa., on November 25, 1893
interment in West Laurel Hill Cemetery, Montgomery County, Pa.

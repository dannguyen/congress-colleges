ORTIZ, Solomon P., a Representative from Texas
born in Robstown, Nueces County, Tex., June 3, 1937
attended Robstown High School, Robstown, Tex.
attended Del Mar College, Corpus Christi, Tex., 1965-1967
United States Army, 1960-1962
insurance agent
Nueces County, Tex., constable, 1965-1968
Nueces County, Tex., commissioner, 1969-1976
Nueces County, Tex., sheriff, 1976-1982
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-eighth and to the thirteen succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

OTEY, Peter Johnston, a Representative from Virginia
was born in Lynchburg, Campbell County, Va., December 22, 1840
attended private schools in Lynchburg
was graduated from the Virginia Military Institute at Lexington in 1859
engaged in civil engineering
joined the Confederate Army in 1861 and served throughout the Civil War
organized and built the Lynchburg & Durham Railroad and became president of the company
engaged in banking and was general manager of the Rivermont Land Co.
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-fourth and to the three succeeding Congresses and served from March 4, 1895, until his death
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1896
died in Lynchburg, Va., May 4, 1902
interment in the Presbyterian Cemetery.

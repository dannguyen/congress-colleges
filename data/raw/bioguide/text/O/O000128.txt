OTIS, John, a Representative from Maine
born in Leeds, Maine, August 3, 1801
attended the common schools, and was graduated from Bowdoin College, Brunswick, Maine, in 1823
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Hallowell, Maine, in 1826
member of the State house of representatives in 1841
appointed a member of the Northeastern Boundary Commission in 1842
served in the State senate in 1842
again a member of the State house of representatives in 1846 and 1847
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-first Congress (March 4, 1849-March 3, 1851)
died in Hallowell, Kennebec County, Maine, October 17, 1856
interment in Hallowell Cemetery.

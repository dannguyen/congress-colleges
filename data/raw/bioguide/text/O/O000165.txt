OSBORNE, Thomas, a Representative from Nebraska
born in Hastings, Adams County, Nebr., February 23, 1937
graduated from Hastings High School, Hastings, Nebr.
B.S., Hastings College, Hastings, Nebr., 1959
M.A., University of Nebraska, Lincoln, Nebr., 1963
Ph.D., University of Nebraska, Lincoln, Nebr., 1965
Nebraska Army National Guard, 1960-1966
head football coach, University of Nebraska, 1972-1997
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2007)
not a candidate for reelection, but was an unsuccessful candidate for the Republican nomination for Governor of Nebraska in 2006.

OWENS, William, a Representative from New York
born in Brooklyn, Kings County, N.Y., January, 20, 1949
B.S., Manhattan College, Riverdale, N.Y., 1971
J.D., Fordham University, New York, N.Y., 1974
United States Air Force, 1975-1979
United States Air Force Reserve, 1979-1982
lawyer, private practice
faculty, State University of New York, Plattsburgh, N.Y., 1978-1986
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative John McHugh, and to the two succeeding Congresses (November 3, 2009-present)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

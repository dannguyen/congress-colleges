O'ROURKE, Beto, a Representative from Texas
born in El Paso, El Paso County, Tex., September 26, 1972
attended El Paso High School and graduated from Woodberry Forest School, Woodberry Forest, Va.
B.A., Columbia University, New York, N.Y., 1995
business owner
member of the El Paso, Tex., city council, 2005-2011
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

PACE, Stephen, a Representative from Georgia
born in Terrell County, Ga., near Dawson, March 9, 1891
attended the public schools and Georgia School of Technology at Atlanta
was graduated from the law department of the University of Georgia at Athens in 1914
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Americus, Ga.
also engaged in agricultural pursuits
served in the State house of representatives 1917-1920
was a member of the State senate in 1923 and 1924
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-fifth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1937-January 3, 1951)
did not seek renomination in 1950
resumed the practice of law in Americus, Ga., and practiced until his death there April 5, 1970
interment in Sunset Memorial Gardens.

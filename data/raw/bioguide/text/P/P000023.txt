PAGE, Sherman, a Representative from New York
born in Cheshire, Conn., May 9, 1779
attended the common schools
taught school in Coventry, N.Y., in 1799
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1805 and commenced practice in Unadilla, Otsego County, N.Y.
member of the State assembly in 1827
judge of the court of common pleas in Otsego County
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-third and Twenty-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1833-March 3, 1837)
chairman, Committee on Public Expenditures (Twenty-fourth Congress)
died in Unadilla, N.Y., September 27, 1853
interment in St. MatthewÂ’s Cemetery.

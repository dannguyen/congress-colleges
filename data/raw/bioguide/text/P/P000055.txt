PARKER, Andrew, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Cumberland County, Pa., May 21, 1805
attended the common schools
was graduated from Dickinson College, Carlisle, Pa., in 1824
studied law in Carlisle
was admitted to the bar in 1826 and commenced practice in Lewistown, Pa.
appointed deputy attorney general of Mifflin County
moved to Mifflintown in 1831, where he practiced law
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-second Congress (March 4, 1851-March 3, 1853)
continued the practice of law in Mifflintown, Juniata County, Pa., until his death there on January 15, 1864
interment in the Presbyterian Cemetery.

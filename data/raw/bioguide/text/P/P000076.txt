PARMENTER, William, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Boston, Mass., March 30, 1789
attended the public schools and the Boston Latin School
member of the State house of representatives in 1829
served in the State senate in 1836
selectman of Cambridge in 1836
manager and agent of the New England Crown Glass Co., 1824-1836
president of the Middlesex Bank
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth and to the three succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1837-March 3, 1845)
chairman, Committee on Naval Affairs (Twenty-eighth Congress)
naval officer at the port of Boston 1845-1849
died in East Cambridge, Mass., February 25, 1866
interment in Cambridge Cemetery.

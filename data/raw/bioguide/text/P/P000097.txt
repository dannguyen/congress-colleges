PASHAYAN, Charles, Jr., a Representative from California
born in Fresno, Fresno County, Calif., March 27, 1941
graduated from Bullard High School, 1959
B.A., Pomona College, Claremont, Calif., 1963
J.D., Hastings Law School, University of California, San Francisco, 1968
captain, United States Army, 1968-1970
admitted to the California bar in 1969 and to the U.S. Supreme Court bar in 1977
special assistant to General Counsel, United States Department of Health, Education, and Welfare, 1973-1975
B.Litt., Oxford University, England, 1977
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-sixth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1979-January 3, 1991)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1990 to the One Hundred Second Congress
is a resident of Fresno, Calif.

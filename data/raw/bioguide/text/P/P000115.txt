PATTERSON, George Robert, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Lewistown, Mifflin County, Pa., November 9, 1863
attended the public schools and Lewistown (Pa.) Academy
engaged in mercantile pursuits in 1880
moved to Ashland, Schuylkill County, in 1886 and engaged in the wholesale grain and feed business
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1900 and 1904
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-seventh, Fifty-eighth, and Fifty-ninth Congresses and served from March 4, 1901, until his death in Washington, D.C., March 21, 1906

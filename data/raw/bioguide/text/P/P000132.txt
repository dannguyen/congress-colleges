PATTERSON, William, a Representative from Ohio
born in Maryland in 1790
moved to Mansfield, Ohio
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
held several local offices
associate judge of the court of common pleas in 1820 and 1827
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twenty-third and Twenty-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1833-March 3, 1837)
died in Van Wert, Van Wert County, Ohio, on August 17, 1868
interment in Mansfield Cemetery, Mansfield, Ohio.

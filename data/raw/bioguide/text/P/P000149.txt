born in Newark, Essex County, N.J., July 16, 1934
graduated from Barringer High School, Newark, N.J., 1952
B.A., Seton Hall University, South Orange, N.J., 1957
teacher
insurance executive
president, Young MenÂ’s Christian Association of the United States (YMCA), 1970-1973
member, Essex County, N.J., board of chosen freeholders, 1972-1978
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the Ninety-seventh Congress in 1980
member of the Newark, N.J., Municipal Council, 1982-1988

died on March 6, 2012, in Livingston, N.J.

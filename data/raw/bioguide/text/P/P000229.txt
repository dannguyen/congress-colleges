PERKINS, Carl Christopher, (son of Carl Dewey Perkins), a Representative from Kentucky
born in Washington, D.C., August 6, 1954
graduated from Fort Hunt High School, Alexandria, Va., 1972
B.A., Davidson College, Davidson, N.C., 1976
J.D., University of Louisville, Louisville, Ky., 1978
lawyer, private practice
member of the Kentucky state house of representatives, 1981-1984
elected simultaneously as a Democrat to the Ninety-eighth and to the Ninety-ninth Congress by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of his father, United States Representative Carl Dewey Perkins, and reelected to the three succeeding Congresses (November 6, 1984-January 3, 1993)
was not a candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Third Congress in 1992
is a resident of Hindman, Ky.

PETERSEN, Andrew Nicholas, a Representative from New York
born near Thisted, Denmark, March 10, 1870
immigrated to the United States in 1873 with his parents, who settled in Boston, Mass.
moved to New York City in 1879
attended the public schools
learned the patternmakerÂ’s trade
president of the Brooklyn Foundry Co. 1900-1952
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1921-March 3, 1923)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1922 to the Sixty-eighth Congress
resumed manufacturing pursuits in Brooklyn, N.Y.
died in East Rockaway, N.Y., September 28, 1952
interment in Cypress Hills Abbey, Brooklyn, N.Y.

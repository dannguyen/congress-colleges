PETERSON, Collin Clark, a Representative from Minnesota
born in Fargo, Cass County, N.D., June 29, 1944
B.A., Moorhead State University, Moorhead, Minn., 1966
United States Army National Guard, 1963-1969
certified public accountant
member of the Minnesota state senate, 1977-1986
unsuccessful candidate for election to the Ninety-ninth Congress in 1984 and to the One Hundredth Congress in 1986
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States House of Representatives in 1988
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Second and to the twelve succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1991-present)
chair, Committee on Agriculture (One Hundred Tenth and One Hundred Eleventh Congresses).

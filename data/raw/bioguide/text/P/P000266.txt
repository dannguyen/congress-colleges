PETRIE, George, a Representative from New York
born at Little Falls, Herkimer County, N.Y., September 8, 1793
attended the common schools
elected as an Independent Democrat to the Thirtieth Congress (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1849)
employed in the Post Office Department, Washington, D.C., from January 1, 1869, until August 31, 1875, when he resigned
died at Little Falls, N.Y., May 8, 1879
interment in Church Street Cemetery.

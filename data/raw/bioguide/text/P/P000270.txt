PETTIGREW, Ebenezer, a Representative from North Carolina
born near Plymouth, Tyrrell County, N.C., March 10, 1783
studied under tutors at home and later attended the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill
engaged in planting
member of the state senate in 1809 and 1810
elected as an Anti-Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1837)
resumed agricultural pursuits
died at Magnolia plantation on Lake Scuppernong, Tyrrell County, N.C., July 8, 1848
interment in the family cemetery.

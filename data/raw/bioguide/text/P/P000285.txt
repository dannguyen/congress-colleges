PFEIFER, Joseph Lawrence, a Representative from New York
born in Brooklyn, Kings County, N.Y., February 6, 1892
attended St. Nicholas Parochial School, St. Leonards Academy, and St. Francis College, Brooklyn, N.Y.
was graduated from Long Island (N.Y.) Medical College in 1914
was licensed to practice the same year
lecturer and author on surgical topics
during the First World War served on the medical advisory board, instructing medical officers going overseas
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-fourth and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1935-January 3, 1951)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1950 to the Eighty-second Congress
resumed the practice of medicine
retired
resided in Brooklyn, N.Y., where he died April 19, 1974
interment in St. Johns Cemetery, Middle Village, N.Y.

PICKETT, Owen Bradford, a Representative from Virginia
born in Richmond, Va., August 31, 1930
attended public schools
B.S., Virginia Polytechnic Institute, Blacksburg, 1952
LL.B., University of Richmond Law School, 1955
admitted to the Virginia State bar in 1955 and commenced practice in Richmond
member, Virginia house of delegates, 1972-1986
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundredth and six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1987-January 3, 2001)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Seventh Congress
died on October 27, 2010, in Virginia Beach, Va.

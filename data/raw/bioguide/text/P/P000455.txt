POST, Jotham, Jr., a Representative from New York
born near Westbury, Nassau County, N.Y., April 4, 1771
was graduated from Columbia College, New York City, in 1792
studied medicine but did not practice
engaged in the drug-importing business in New York City
member of the board of aldermen
served in the State assembly 1795 and 1805-1808
director of the New York Hospital 1798-1802
elected as a Federalist to the Thirteenth Congress (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1815)
died in New York City, May 15, 1817.

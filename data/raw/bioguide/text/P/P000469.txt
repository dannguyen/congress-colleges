POTTER, William Wilson, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born at Potters Mills, Pa., December 18, 1792
completed preparatory studies in Bellefonte, Centre County, Pa., and was graduated from Dickinson College, Carlisle, Pa.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1814 and practiced his profession
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth and Twenty-sixth Congresses and served from March 4, 1837, until his death, before the assembling of the Twenty-sixth Congress, in Bellefonte, Pa., on October 28, 1839
interment in Union Cemetery, Bellefonte, Pa.

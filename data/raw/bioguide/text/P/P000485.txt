POWELL, Walter Eugene, a Representative from Ohio
born in Hamilton, Butler County, Ohio, April 25, 1931
attended Hamilton public schools
A.B., Heidelberg College, Tiffin, Ohio, 1953
M.Ed., Miami (Ohio) University, 1961
educator in Butler County, Ohio, for twenty-four years
clerk, city of Fairfield, Ohio, 1956-1957
member, Fairfield City Council, 1958
member, Ohio house of representatives, 1961-1967
member, Ohio State senate, 1967-1971
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-second and to the Ninety-third Congresses (January 3, 1971-January 3, 1975)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1974 to the Ninety-fourth Congress
is a resident of Middletown, Ohio.

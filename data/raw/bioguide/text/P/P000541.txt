PRINDLE, Elizur H., a Representative from New York
born in Newtown, Conn., May 6, 1829
completed preparatory studies
attended the local academy at Homer, N.Y.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1854 and practiced
moved to New York and practiced law in Norwich, Chenango County
was district attorney of Chenango County, N.Y., 1859-1863
member of the State assembly in 1863
member of the State constitutional convention in 1867 and 1868
elected as a Republican to the Forty-second Congress (March 4, 1871-March 3, 1873)
resumed the practice of law
died in Norwich, N.Y., October 7, 1890
interment in Mount Hope Cemetery.

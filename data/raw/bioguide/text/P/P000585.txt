PLATTS, Todd, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in York, York County, Pa., March 5, 1962
attended York Suburban High School, York, Pa., 1980
B.S., Shippensburg University, Shippensburg, Pa., 1984
J.D., Pepperdine University School of Law,Pepperdine University, Malibu, Calif., 1991
member of the Pennsylvania state house of representatives, 1992-2000
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012
judge, Common Pleas Court, York County, Pa., nineteenth district, 2014-present.

PUTNAM, Adam, a Representative from Florida
born in Bartow, Polk County, Fla., July 31, 1974
B.S., University of Florida, Gainesville, Fla., 1995
farmer
rancher
member of the Florida state house of representatives, 1996-2001
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2011)
House Republican Conference Chair (One Hundred Tenth Congress)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010
Commissioner of Agriculture, Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services, 2011-present.

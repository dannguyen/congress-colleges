PENCE, Mike, a Representative from Indiana
born in Columbus, Bartholomew County, Ind., June 7, 1959
graduated from Hanover College, Hanover, Ind., 1981
J.D., Robert H. McKinney School of Law, Indiana University, Indianapolis, Ind., 1986
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 1986 and 1988
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2013)
House Republican Conference Chair (One Hundred Eleventh Congress)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundredth Thirteenth Congress in 2012
Governor of Indiana, 2013-present.

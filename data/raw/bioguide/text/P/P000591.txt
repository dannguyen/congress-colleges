PRICE, Tom, a Representative from Georgia
born in Lansing, Ingham County, Mich., October 8, 1954
graduated from Dearborn High School, Dearborn, Mich., 1972
A.B., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1976
M.D., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1979
physician
member of the Georgia state senate, 1997-2004
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Ninth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2005-present)
chair, Committee on Budget (One Hundred Fourteenth Congress).

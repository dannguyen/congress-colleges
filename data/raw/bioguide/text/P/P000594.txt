PAULSEN, Erik, a Representative from Minnesota
born in Bakersfield, Kern County, Calif., May 14, 1965
graduated from Chaska High School, Chaska, Minn., 1983
B.A., St. Olaf College, Northfield, Minn., 1987
businessman
staff, United States Senator Rudy Boschwitz of Minnesota
member of the Minnesota state house of representatives, 1995-2008, majority leader, 2003-2007
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2009-present).

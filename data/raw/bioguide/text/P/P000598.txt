POLIS, Jared, a Representative from Colorado
born in Boulder, Boulder County, Colo., May 12, 1975
A.B., Princeton University, Princeton, N. J., 1996
business owner
entrepreneur
member of the Colorado state board of education, 2001-2007
chairman of the Colorado state board of education, 2004-2005
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2009-present).

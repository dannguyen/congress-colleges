PERRIELLO, Tom, a Representative from Virginia
born in Charlottesville, Albemarle County, Va., October 9, 1974
B. A. Yale University, 1996
J. D., Yale University, New Haven, Conn., 2001
entrepreneur
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress (January 3, 2009-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

PALAZZO, Steven, a Representative from Mississippi
born in Gulfport, Harrison County, Miss., February 21, 1970
graduated from Saint John High School, Gulfport, Miss., 1988
B.S., University of Southern Mississippi, Hattiesburg, Miss., 1994
M.P.A., University of Southern Mississippi, Hattiesburg, Miss., 1996
United States Marine Corps Reserve, 1988-1996
Mississippi Army National Guard, 2007-present
accountant
member of the Mississippi state house of representatives, 2007-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

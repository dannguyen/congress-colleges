POMPEO, Mike, a Representative from Kansas
born in Orange, Orange County, Calif., December 30, 1963
B.S., United States Military Academy, West Point, N.Y., 1986
J.D., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1994
United States Army, 1986-1991
business executive
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

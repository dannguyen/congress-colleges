PETERS, Scott, a Representative from California
born in Springfield, Clark County, Ohio, June 17, 1958
B.A., Duke University, Durham, N.C., 1980
J.D., New York University, New York, N.Y., 1984
lawyer, private practice
economist, United States Environmental Protection Agency, 1980-1981
deputy attorney, city of San Diego, Calif., 1991-1996
member of the San Diego, Calif., city council, 2000-2008
president of the San Diego, Calif., city council, 2006-2008
commissioner, San Diego unified port district, 2009-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

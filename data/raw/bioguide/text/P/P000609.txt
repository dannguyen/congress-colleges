PALMER, Gary James, a Representative from Alabama
born in Haleyville, Winston County, Ala., May 14, 1954
graduated from Hackleburg High School, Hackleburg, Ala., 1972
attended Northwest Alabama Junior College, Phil Campbell, Ala., 1972-1974
B.S., University of Alabama, Tuscaloosa, Ala., 1977
engineer
professional advocate
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

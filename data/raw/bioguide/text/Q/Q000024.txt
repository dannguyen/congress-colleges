QUAYLE, Ben, (son of James Danforth Quayle), a Representative from Arizona
born in Fort Wayne, Allen County, Indiana, November 3, 1976
B.A., Duke University, Durham, N.C., 1998
J.D., Vanderbilt University, Nashville, Tenn., 2002
business owner
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

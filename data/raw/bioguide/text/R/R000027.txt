RAMSEY, John Rathbone, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Wyckoff, Bergen County, N.J., April 25, 1862
attended the public schools and a private school in Parkersburg, W.Va., where he lived from 1872 to 1879
studied law in Hackensack, N.J.
was admitted to the bar in 1883 and commenced practice in Hackensack, N.J.
county clerk of Bergen County 1895-1910
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1908
president of the Hackensack Brick Co. 1909-1933
director of several banks
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-fifth and Sixty-sixth Congresses (March 4, 1917-March 3, 1921)
was an unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1920
resumed the manufacture of brick
died in Hackensack, N.J., April 10, 1933
interment in Hackensack Cemetery.

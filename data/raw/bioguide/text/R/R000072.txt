RAVENEL, Arthur, Jr., a Representative from South Carolina
born in Charleston, S.C., March 29, 1927
B.S., College of Charleston, 1950
served in the U.S. Marine Corps, 1945-1946
realtor and general contractor
member, South Carolina State house of representatives, 1953-1958
South Carolina State senate, 1980-1986
elected as a Republican to the One Hundredth and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1987-January 1995)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1994, but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination for Governor of South Carolina.

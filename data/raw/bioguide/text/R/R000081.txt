RAY, William Henry, a Representative from Illinois
born in Amenia, Dutchess County, N.Y., December 14, 1812
moved to Oneida County, N.Y., in 1813 with his parents who settled in Utica
attended the common schools
moved to Rushville, Ill., in 1834
engaged in mercantile pursuits
also interested in banking
member of the first State board of equalization 1867-1869
elected as a Republican to the Forty-third Congress (March 4, 1873-March 3, 1875)
resumed his former business pursuits in Rushville, Schuyler County, Ill., and died there January 25, 1881
interment in Rushville Cemetery.

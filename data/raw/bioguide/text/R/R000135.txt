REESE, David Addison, a Representative from Georgia
born in Charlotte, N.C., March 3, 1794
attended the public schools and was instructed in the classics by a private tutor
studied medicine
was graduated from the Jefferson Medical College, Philadelphia, Pa., and commenced practice in Elberton, Ga.
moved to Monticello, Ga., and continued the practice of medicine
member of the State senate in 1829, 1830, 1834, 1835, and 1836
member of the board of trustees of the University of Georgia at Athens for twenty-five years
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
moved to Auburn, Ala., and resumed the practice of medicine
died in Auburn December 16, 1871
interment in Hopewell Cemetery, West Point, Troup County, Ga.

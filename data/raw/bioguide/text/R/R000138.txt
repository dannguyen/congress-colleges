REEVES, Henry Augustus, a Representative from New York
born in Sag Harbor, N.Y., December 7, 1832
attended private schools in Sag Harbor, the Southampton Academy, the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor for three years, and was graduated from Union College, Schenectady, N.Y., in 1852
studied law
was admitted to the bar
edited the Republican Watchman in Greenport from 1858 until his death
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-first Congress (March 4, 1869-March 3, 1871)
resumed newspaper interests
supervisor of Southold Town 1872-1894
member of the State assembly in 1887
member of the State commission in lunacy 1889-1897
died in Greenport, Suffolk County, N.Y., March 4, 1916
interment in Southampton Cemetery, Southampton, N.Y.

REYNOLDS, Edwin Ruthvin, a Representative from New York
born at Fort Ann, N.Y., February 16, 1816
pursued classical studies
was principal of Albion Academy, Orleans County, N.Y., for six years
was county superintendent 1843-1845
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1843 and commenced practice in Albion, N.Y., in 1846
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-sixth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Silas M. Burroughs and served from December 5, 1860, to March 3, 1861
judge and surrogate of Orleans County 1864-1868
resumed the practice of law
died in Albion, N.Y., July 4, 1908
interment in Mount Albion Cemetery.

REYNOLDS, James B., a Representative from Tennessee
born in County Antrim, Ireland, in 1779
attended the common schools
immigrated to the United States and settled in Clarksville, Tenn.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1804 and practiced
elected as a Republican to the Fourteenth Congress (March 4, 1815-March 3, 1817)
elected as a Jackson Republican to the Eighteenth Congress (March 4, 1823-March 3, 1825)
resumed the practice of law
died in Clarksville, Tenn., June 10, 1851
interment in the City Cemetery.

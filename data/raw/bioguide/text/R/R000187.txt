RHODES, George Milton, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Reading, Berks County, Pa., February 24, 1898
attended Reading public schools
during the First World War served in the United States Army
printer, Reading Eagle Co., 1913-1927
business manager, Reading Labor Advocate, 1927-1942
A.F. of L. labor representative
editor and manager of The New Era, 1942-1949
president of Federated Trades Council, A.F. of L. Central Labor Union 1928-1951
member of the Reading Housing Authority 1938-1948
delegate to Socialist National Conventions in 1928 and 1932, and to Democrat National Conventions in 1952 and 1956
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-first and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1949-January 3, 1969)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1968 to the Ninety-first Congress
resided in Reading, Pa., where he died October 23, 1978
interment in Forest Hills Memorial Park, Reiffton, Pa.

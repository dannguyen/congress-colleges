RICE, John Blake, a Representative from Illinois
born in Easton, Talbot County, Md., May 28, 1809
received a limited schooling
went on the stage in New York in 1839
moved to Chicago, Ill., in 1847 and was manager of a theater
also managed theaters in Bangor, Maine, Buffalo, N.Y., and Milwaukee, Wis.
retired from the stage in 1857 and from theatrical management in 1861
mayor of Chicago 1865-1869
elected as a Republican to the Forty-third Congress and served from March 4, 1873, until his death in Norfolk, Va., December 17, 1874
interment in Rosehill Cemetery, Chicago, Ill.

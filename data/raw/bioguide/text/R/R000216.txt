RICHARDS, John, a Representative from New York
born in Wales April 13, 1765
immigrated to the United States and settled in Johnsburg, Warren County, N.Y.
received a limited schooling
member of the state assembly from January 29, 1811, to April 8, 1811
state surveyor 1810-1812
delegate to the state constitutional convention in 1821
elected as a Crawford Republican to the Eighteenth Congress (March 4, 1823-March 3, 1825)
died at Lake George, Warren County, N.Y., April 18, 1850
interment in John Richards Cemetery, Lake George, N.Y.

RICHARDS, Mark, a Representative from Vermont
born in Waterbury, Conn., July 15, 1760
received a limited schooling
enlisted during the Revolutionary War in 1776
settled in Boston after the Revolution and engaged in mercantile and mechanical pursuits
moved to Westminster, Vt., in 1796
member of the State house of representatives 1801-1805
sheriff of Windham County 1806-1810
member of the GovernorÂ’s council in 1816
elected as a Republican to the Fifteenth and Sixteenth Congresses (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1821)
again a member of the State house of representatives 1824-1826, 1828, and 1832-1834
Lieutenant Governor of Vermont in 1830 and 1831
died in Westminster, Vt., August 10, 1844
interment in the Bradley tomb, Old Cemetery.

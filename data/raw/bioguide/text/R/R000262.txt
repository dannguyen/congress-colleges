RINALDO, Matthew John, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Elizabeth, Union County, N.J., September 1, 1931
B.S., Rutgers University, New Brunswick, N.J., 1953
M.B.A., Seton Hall University Graduate School of Business Administration, South Orange, N.J., 1959
D.P.A., New York University, School of Public Administration, 1979
member, Union Township Zoning Board of Adjustment, 1962-1963
Union County Board of Freeholders, 1963-1964
State senate, 1967-1972
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-third and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1973-January 3, 1993)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1992 to the One Hundred Third Congress
died on October 13, 2008, in West Caldwell, N.J.

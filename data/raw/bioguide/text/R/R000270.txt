RISLEY, Elijah, a Representative from New York
born in Connecticut on May 7, 1787
completed preparatory studies
moved to Fredonia, Chautauqua County, N.Y., in 1807
engaged in mercantile pursuits
sheriff of Chautauqua County 1825-1828
supervisor of town of Pomfret in 1835
engaged in the culture of garden seeds 1833-1853
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-first Congress (March 4, 1849-March 3, 1851)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1850
major general in the State militia
died in Fredonia, Chautauqua County, N.Y., January 9, 1870
interment in the East Main Street Cemetery.

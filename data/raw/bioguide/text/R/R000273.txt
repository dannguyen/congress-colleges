RITCHIE, David, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Canonsburg, Washington County, Pa., August 19, 1812
was graduated from Jefferson College, Canonsburg, Pa., in 1829, and subsequently at Heidelberg, Germany
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1835 and commenced practice in Pittsburgh, Pa.
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-third Congress, reelected as an Opposition Party candidate to the Thirty-fourth Congress, and elected as a Republican to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1859)
chairman, Committee on Revolutionary Claims (Thirty-fourth Congress)
was appointed associate judge of the court of common pleas of Allegheny County in 1862 and served nine months
resumed the practice of his profession
died in Pittsburgh, Pa., January 24, 1867.

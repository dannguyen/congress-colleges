RITCHIE, James Monroe, (father of Byron Foster Ritchie), a Representative from Ohio
born in Dunfermline, Scotland, July 28, 1829
immigrated to the United States in 1832 with his parents, who settled in St. Lawrence County, N.Y.
his early schooling was limited and he received instruction at home from his father and mother
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1858 and commenced practice in Toledo, Ohio
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1880
elected as a Republican to the Forty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1881-March 3, 1883)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1882
again resumed the practice of his profession in Toledo, Ohio, and died there August 17, 1918
interment in Grafton Cemetery, Grafton, Lorain County, Ohio.

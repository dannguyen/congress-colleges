RITTER, John, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Exeter, Pa., February 6, 1779
received a limited schooling
apprenticed as a printer
member of the State constitutional convention in 1836
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-eighth and Twenty-ninth Congresses (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1847)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1846
editor and publisher of the Adler, a German newspaper, at Reading
died in Reading, Berks County, Pa., November 24, 1851
interment in the Charles Evans Cemetery.

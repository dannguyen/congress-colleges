RIXEY, John Franklin, a Representative from Virginia
born in Culpeper County, Va., August 1, 1854
attended the common schools, Bethel Academy, and the University of Virginia at Charlottesville
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1876 and commenced practice in Culpeper, Va.
Commonwealth attorney for Culpeper County, Va., 1879-1891
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-fifth and to the four succeeding Congresses and served from March 4, 1897, until his death in Washington, D.C., February 8, 1907, before the close of the Fifty-ninth Congress
had been reelected to the Sixtieth Congress
interment in Fairview Cemetery, Culpeper, Va.

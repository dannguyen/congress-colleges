ROBINSON, John Seaton, a Representative from Nebraska
born in Wheeling, W.Va., May 4, 1856
attended the public schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar by the supreme court of West Virginia in 1880
moved to Madison, Nebr., in 1884
prosecuting attorney of Madison County 1886-1888 and 1890-1892
judge of the ninth judicial district 1893-1895
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-sixth and Fifty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1899-March 3, 1903)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1902
died in Madison, Nebr., on May 25, 1903
interment in Crownhill Cemetery.

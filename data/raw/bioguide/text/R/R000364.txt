ROCKHILL, William, a Representative from Indiana
born in Burlington, N.J., February 10, 1793
attended the public schools
moved to Fort Wayne, Ind., in 1822
engaged in agricultural pursuits
commissioner of Allen County, Ind., in 1825 justice of the peace
member of the first city council of Fort Wayne and also city assessor
member of the State house of representatives 1834-1837
served in the State senate 1844-1847
elected as a Democrat to the Thirtieth Congress (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1849)
resumed agricultural pursuits
died at Fort Wayne, Allen County, Ind., January 15, 1865
interment in Lindenwood Cemetery.

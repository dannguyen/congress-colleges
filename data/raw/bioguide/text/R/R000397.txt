ROGERS, John, a Representative from New York
born in Caldwell, N.Y., May 9, 1813
completed preparatory studies
moved to Black Brook, Clinton County, in 1832 and engaged in the manufacture of iron
supervisor of the town of Black Brook for ten years and held other local offices
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-second Congress (March 4, 1871-March 3, 1873)
resumed manufacturing activities
died at the Rogers Place, near Fort Edward, Washington County, N.Y., May 11, 1879
interment in the family burial ground on his estate at Moreau, near Fort Henry, N.Y.

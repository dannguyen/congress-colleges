ROGERS, Paul Grant, (son of Dwight L. Rogers), a Representative from Florida
born in Ocilla, Irwin County, Ga., June 4, 1921
B.A., University of Florida, Gainesville, Fla.,1942
United States Army, 1942-1946
attended George Washington University School of Law, Washington, D.C.
J.D., University of Florida, Gainesville, Fla., 1948
lawyer, private practice
member, board of directors, Merck & Co. and Mutual Life Insurance Co. of New York
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-fourth Congress by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of his father, United States Representative Dwight L. Rogers, and reelected to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 4, 1955-January 3, 1979)
was not a candidate for reelection to the Ninety-sixth Congress in 1978
died on October 13, 2008, in Washington, D.C.

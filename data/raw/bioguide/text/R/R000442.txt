ROSENTHAL, Benjamin Stanley, a Representative from New York
born in New York City June 8, 1923
attended public schools, Long Island University, and City College
United States Army, 1943-1946
LL.B., Brooklyn Law School, 1949
LL.M., New York University, 1952
admitted to the New York bar in 1949 and commenced practice in New York City
admitted to the Supreme Court bar in 1954
elected as a Democrat to the Eighty-seventh Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Lester Holtzman, and reelected to the eleven succeeding Congresses (February 20, 1962-January 4, 1983)
died on January 4, 1983, in Washington, D.C.
interment at Beth-David Cemetery, Elmont, N.Y.

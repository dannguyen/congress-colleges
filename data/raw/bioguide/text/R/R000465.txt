ROUKEMA, Margaret Scafati, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Newark, Essex County, N.J., September 19, 1929
graduated from West Orange High School, West Orange, N.J., 1947
B.A., Montclair State College, Montclair, N.J., 1951
graduate work, Montclair State College, Montclair, N.J., 1951-1953, and Rutgers University, New Brunswick, N.J., 1975
teacher
Ridgewood, N.J., board of education, 1970-1973
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 1978
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-seventh and to the ten succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1981-January 3, 2003)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in 2002
died on November 12, 2014, in Wyckoff, N.J.

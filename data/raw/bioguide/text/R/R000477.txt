ROWE, Peter, a Representative from New York
born in Crescent, Saratoga County, N.Y., March 10, 1807
completed preparatory studies and was graduated from Schenectady (N.Y.) Academy
engaged in mercantile pursuits
chief auditor of the New York Central Railroad
mayor of Schenectady 1846-1850
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
died in Schenectady, N.Y., April 17, 1876
interment in Vale Cemetery.

ROWLAND, Charles Hedding, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Hancock, Washington County, Md., December 20, 1860
moved to Huntingdon County, Pa., in 1866 and to Houtzdale, Pa., in 1874
attended the public schools
president of the Moshannon Coal Mining Co. and of the Pittsburgh & Susquehanna Railroad Co.
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-fourth and Sixty-fifth Congresses (March 4, 1915-March 3, 1919)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1918
died in Philipsburg, Centre County, Pa., on November 24, 1921
interment in the Philipsburg Cemetery.

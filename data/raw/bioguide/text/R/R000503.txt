RUGGLES, Nathaniel, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Roxbury, Mass., November 11, 1761
pursued preparatory studies
was graduated from Harvard University in 1781
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced law in Roxbury, Mass.
appointed judge of the general sessions in 1807
chief justice of Massachusetts in 1808
was elected as a Federalist to the Thirteenth, Fourteenth, and Fifteenth Congresses (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1819)
died in Roxbury, Mass., December 19, 1819.

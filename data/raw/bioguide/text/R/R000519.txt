RUSS, John, a Representative from Connecticut
was born in Ipswich, Mass., on October 29, 1767
completed preparatory studies
moved to Hartford, Conn.
engaged in mercantile pursuits
elected as a Republican to the Sixteenth and Seventeenth Congresses (March 4, 1819-March 3, 1823)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1823
unsuccessful candidate for election in 1823 to the state house of representatives
elected to the state house of representatives in 1824
elected judge of the Hartford Probate Court in 1824 and served until 1830
resumed his former business pursuits
died in Hartford, Conn., June 22, 1833
interment in the Old North Cemetery.

RUSSELL, Joseph, a Representative from New York
born in New York and resided in Warrensburg, N.Y., 1800
received a limited schooling
Warrensburg town supervisor, 1829-1830, 1831-1834, 1839-1840
sheriff of Warren County, November 1834-November 1837
member of the New York state assembly, 1840
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
again elected to the Thirty-second Congress (March 4, 1851-March 3, 1853)
died in 1875
interment in Glen Falls Cemetery.

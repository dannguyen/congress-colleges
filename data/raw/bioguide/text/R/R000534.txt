RUSSELL, Joshua Edward, a Representative from Ohio
born near Sidney, Shelby County, Ohio, on August 9, 1867
attended the common schools and Sidney High School
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1893 and commenced practice in Sidney
member of the city board of education in 1894 and 1895
city solicitor 1895-1899
member of the State senate 1905-1908
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1915-March 3, 1917)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1916 to the Sixty-fifth Congress
resumed the practice of law
died in Sidney, Ohio, June 21, 1953
interment in Graceland Cemetery.

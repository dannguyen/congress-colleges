REHBERG, Denny, a Representative from Montana
born in Billings, Yellowstone County, Mont., October 5, 1955
graduated from West High School, Billings, Mont.
attended Montana State University, Billings, Mont., 1973-1974
B.A., Washington State University, Pullman, Wash., 1977
rancher
staff, United States Representative Ron Marlenee of Montana, 1979-1982
member, Montana state house of representatives, 1984-1991
Montana lieutenant governor, 1991-1996
unsuccessful candidate for the United States Senate in 1996
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2013)
not a candidate for reelection in 2012, but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate.

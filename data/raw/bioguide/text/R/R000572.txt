ROGERS, Mike, a Representative from Michigan
born in Livingston County, Mich., June 2, 1963
graduated from Howell High School, Howell, Mich., 1981
B.S., Adrian College, Adrian, Mich., 1985
United States Army, 1985-1989
Federal Bureau of Investigation, 1989-1994
member of the Michigan state senate, 1995-2000, and majority floor leader, 1999-2000
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014
chair, Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence (One Hundred Twelfth and One Hundred Thirteenth Congresses).

ROSS, Michael Avery, a Representative from Arkansas
born in Texarkana, Miller County, Ark., on August 2, 1961
graduated from Hope High School, Hope, Ark.
B.A., University of Arkansas, Little Rock, Ark., 1987
businessman
staff, office of Lt. Governor of Arkansas, 1985-1989
member of the Arkansas state senate, 1991-2000
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Seventh and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

RENZI, Rick, a Representative from Arizona
born in Fort Monmouth, Monmouth County, N.J., June 11, 1958
graduated from Buena High School, Sierra Vista, Ariz.
B.S., Northern Arizona University, Flagstaff, Ariz., 1980
J.D., Catholic University, Washington, D.C., 2002
businessman
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth Congress and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-January 3, 2009)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

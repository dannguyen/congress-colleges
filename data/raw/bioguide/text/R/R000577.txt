RYAN, Timothy J., a Representative from Ohio
born in Niles, Trumbull County, Ohio, July 16, 1973
graduated from John F. Kennedy High School, Warren, Ohio, 1991
B.S., Bowling Green State University, Bowling Green, Ohio, 1995
J.D., Franklin Pierce Law Center, Rindge, N.H., 2000
staff, United States Representative James A. Traficant of Ohio
member of the Ohio state senate, 2000-2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

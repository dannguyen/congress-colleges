ROE, David P. (Phil), a Representative from Tennessee, born in Clarksville, Montgomery County, Tenn., July 21, 1945
B.S., Austin Peay State University, Clarksville, Tenn., 1967
M.D., University of Tennessee, Knoxville, Tenn., 1970
United States Army, 1973-1974
physician
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States House of Representatives in 2006
member of the Johnson City, Tenn., board of commissioners, 2003-2008
vice mayor of Johnson City, Tenn., 2003-2007
mayor of Johnson City, Tenn., 2007-2009
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh and to the three succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2009-present).

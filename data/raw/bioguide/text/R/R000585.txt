REED, Thomas W. II, a Representative from New York
born in Joliet, Will County, Ill., November 18, 1971
graduated from Horseheads High School, Horseheads, N.Y., 1989
B.A., Alfred University, Alfred, N.Y., 1993
J.D., Ohio Northern University, Ada, Ohio, 1996
lawyer, private practice
business owner
Mayor of Corning, N.Y., 2007-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Eric J.J. Massa, and to the three succeeding Congresses (November 2, 2010-present).

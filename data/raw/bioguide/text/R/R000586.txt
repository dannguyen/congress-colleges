RENACCI, Jim, a Representative from Ohio
born in Monongahela, Washington County, Pa., December 3, 1958
graduated from Ringgold High School, Monongahela, Pa., 1976
B.S., Indiana University of Pennsylvania, Indiana, Pa., 1980
business owner
member of the Wadsworth, Ohio, board of zoning appeals, 1994-1995
Wadsworth, Ohio, city council president, 1999-2003
mayor of Wadsworth, Ohio, 2004-2008
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

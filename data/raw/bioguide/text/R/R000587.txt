RIBBLE, Reid, a Representative from Wisconsin
born in Neenah, Winnebago County, Wis., April 5, 1956
graduated from Appleton East High School, Appleton, Wis., 1974
attended Grand Rapids School of Bible and Music, Grand Rapids, Mich.
business owner
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

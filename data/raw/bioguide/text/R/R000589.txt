RIGELL, E. Scott, a Representative from Virginia
born in Titusville, Brevard County, Fla., May 28, 1960
A.A., Brevard Community College, Cocoa, Fla., 1981
B.B.A., Mercer University, Macon, Ga., 1983
M.B.A., Regent University, Virginia Beach, Va., 1990
United States Marine Corps Reserve, 1978-1984
entrepreneur
member of the Virginia Motor Vehicle Dealer Board, 1995-1999
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

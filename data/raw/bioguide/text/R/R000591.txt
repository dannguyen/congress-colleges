ROBY, Martha, a Representative from Alabama
born in Montgomery, Montgomery County, Ala., July 27, 1976
B.M., New York University, New York, N. Y., 1998
J.D., Samford University, Birmingham, Ala., 2001
lawyer, private practice
member of the Montgomery, Ala., city council, 2003-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

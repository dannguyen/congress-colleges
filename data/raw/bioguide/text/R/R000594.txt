RUNYAN, Jon, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Flint, Genessee County, Mich., November 27, 1973
graduated from Carman-Ainsworth High School, Flint, Mich.
attended University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1992-1995
attended University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School of Business, Philadelphia, Pa.
professional athlete
television commentator
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2011-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

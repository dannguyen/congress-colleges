RUIZ, Raul, a Representative from California
born in Zacatecas, Mexico, August 25, 1972
graduated from Coachella Valley High School, Coachella, Calif., 1990
B.S., University of California, Los Angles, Calif., 1994
M.D. and M.P.P., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 2001
M.P.H., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 2007
physician
associate dean, University of California-Riverside School of Medicine, Riverside, Calif., 2011-2012
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

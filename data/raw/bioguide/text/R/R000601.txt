RATCLIFFE, John, a Representative from Texas
born in Chicago, Cook County, Ill., October 20, 1965
B.A., University of Notre Dame, Notre Dame, Ind., 1987
J.D., Southern Methodist University, Dallas, Tex., 1989
lawyer, private practice
professor
United States attorney for the Eastern District of Texas, 2007-2008
mayor of Heath, Tex., 2004-2012
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

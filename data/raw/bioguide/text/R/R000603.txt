ROUZER, David, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Landstuhl, Germany, February 16, 1972
graduated from Northern High School, Durham, N.C.
B.S., North Carolina State University, Raleigh, N.C., 1994
businessman
associate administrator and rural administrator, United States Department of Agriculture, 2005-2006
staff, United States Senator Jesse Helms of North Carolina, 1996-2000
staff, United States Senator Elizabeth Dole of North Carolina, 2003-2005
member of the North Carolina state senate, 2009-2012
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 2012
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

ROUNDS, Marion Michael (Mike), a Senator from South Dakota
born in Huron, Beadle County, S. Dak., October 24, 1954
B.S., South Dakota State University, 1977
insurance and real estate executive
member of the South Dakota state senate 1991-2000, serving as majority leader 1995-2000
governor of South Dakota 2003-2011
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 2014 for the term ending January 3, 2021.

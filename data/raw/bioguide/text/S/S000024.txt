SAMPLE, Samuel Caldwell, a Representative from Indiana
born in Elkton, Cecil County, Md., on August 15, 1796
attended the rural school

studied law

elected prosecuting attorney in 1834
elected judge of the ninth judicial circuit in 1836 and served until 1843, when he resigned

elected as a Whig to the Twenty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1845)

interment in the City Cemetery.

SANDFORD, James T., a Representative from Tennessee
born in Virginia, birth date unknown
attended the common schools
moved to Columbia, Tenn.
engaged in agricultural pursuits
elected as a Jackson Republican to the Eighteenth Congress (March 4, 1823-March 3, 1825)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Nineteenth Congress in 1825
contributed a part of his wealth to the establishment of Jackson College at Columbia, Tenn.
death date unknown.

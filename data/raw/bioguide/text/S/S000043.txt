SANDLIN, John Nicholas, a Representative from Louisiana
born near Minden, Webster Parish, La., on February 24, 1872
attended the public schools
studied law, was admitted to the bar in 1896 and commenced practice in Minden, La.
prosecuting attorney for the second district of Louisiana 1904-1910
judge of the second judicial district of Louisiana 1910-1920
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1916
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1921-January 3, 1937)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1936, but was an unsuccessful candidate for nomination for United States Senator
engaged in the practice of law
died Minden, La., December 25, 1957
interment in Minden Cemetery.

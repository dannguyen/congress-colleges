SANFORD, Stephen, (son of John Sanford [1803-1857] and father of John Sanford [1851_1939]), a Representative from New York
born in Mayfield, Fulton County, N.Y., May 26, 1826
attended the common schools and local academy at Amsterdam, N.Y., Georgetown College, Washington, D.C., for two years, and the United States Military Academy at West Point, N.Y.
engaged in the carpet manufacturing business from 1844 until his death
elected as Republican to the Forty-first Congress (March 4, 1869-March 3, 1871)
declined to be a candidate for renomination
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1876
died in Amsterdam, N.Y., February 13, 1913
interment in Green Hill Cemetery.

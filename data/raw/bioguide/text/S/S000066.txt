SARPALIUS, William, a Representative from Texas
born in Los Angeles, January 10, 1948
grew up in Houston, Tex. and on Cal Farleys Boys Ranch near Amarillo, Tex.
president, Texas Future Farmers of America Association, 1967-1968
A.D., Clarendon College, 1970
B.A., Texas Tech University, 1972
staff assistant and teacher, Cal Farleys Boys Ranch, 1972-1978
agribusinessman
M.A., West Texas State University, 1978
Texas State senator, 1981-1989
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred First and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1989-January 3, 1995)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourth Congress.

SCHAFFER, Robert W., a Representative from Colorado
born in Cincinnati, Hamilton County, Ohio, July 24, 1962
attended Archbishop Moeller High School, Cincinnati, Ohio
B.A., University of Dayton, Dayton, Ohio, 1984
member of the Colorado state senate, 1987-1996
small business owner, 1990-1994
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2003)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in 2002.

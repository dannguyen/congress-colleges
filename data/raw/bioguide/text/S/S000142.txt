SCHROEDER, Patricia Scott, a Representative from Colorado
born Patricia Nell Scott in Portland, Multnomah County, Oreg., July 30, 1940
graduated from Roosevelt High School, Des Moines, Iowa, 1958
B.A., University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, Minn., 1961
J.D., Harvard Law School, Cambridge, Mass., 1964
lawyer, private practice
lawyer, National Labor Relations Board, 1964-1966
teacher, 1969-1972
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-third and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1973-January 3, 1997)
chair, Select Committee on Children, Youth and Families (One Hundred Second and One Hundred Third Congresses)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress in 1996.

SCHUNEMAN, Martin Gerretsen, a Representative from New York
born in Catskill, Albany (now Greene) County, N.Y., February 10, 1764
educated by his father
justice of the peace of Albany County in 1792
engaged in mercantile pursuits and owned an inn at Madison
supervisor for Catskill in Albany and Greene Counties in 1797, 1799, and 1802
member of the State assembly from Ulster County 1798-1800 and from Greene County in 1803
delegate from Greene County to the State constitutional convention in 1801
elected as a Republican to the Ninth Congress (March 4, 1805-March 3, 1807)
resumed his former business pursuits
died in Catskill, N.Y., February 21, 1827
interment in the Old Cemetery, Madison (now Leeds), N.Y.

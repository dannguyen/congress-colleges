SCHWABE, Max, (brother of George Blaine Schwabe), a Representative from Missouri
born in Columbia, Boone County, Mo., December 6, 1905
attended the University of Missouri, Columbia, Mo.
insurance agent
farmer
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-eighth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1943-January 3, 1949)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Eighty-first Congress in 1948
Missouri State director, Farmers Home Administration, United States Department of Agriculture, 1953-1961
died on July 31, 1983, in Columbia, Mo.
interment in Columbia Cemetery, Columbia, Mo.

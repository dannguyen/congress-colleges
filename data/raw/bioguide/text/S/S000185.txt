SCOTT, Robert C., a Representative from Virginia
born in Washington, D.C., April 30, 1947
graduated from Groton School, Groton, Mass.
A.B., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1969
J.D., Boston College School of Law, Boston, Mass., 1973
lawyer, private practice
United States Army Reserve, 1970-1974
Massachusetts Army National Guard, 1974-1976
member of the Virginia state house of delegates, 1978-1983
member of the Virginia state senate, 1983-1993
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 1986
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-present).

SCUDDER, Henry Joel, (uncle of Townsend Scudder), a Representative from New York
born in Northport, Suffolk County, N.Y., on September 18, 1825
attended the district school and Huntington Academy
was graduated from Trinity College, Hartford, Conn., in 1846
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1848 and practiced in New York City
commissioned captain in the Thirty-seventh Regiment, New York National Guard, in 1862 and served throughout the Civil War
elected as a Republican to the Forty-third Congress (March 4, 1873-March 3, 1875)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1874
trustee of Trinity College for over twenty years
resumed the practice of law in New York City, where he died February 10, 1886
interment in the family cemetery at Northport, Suffolk County, N.Y.

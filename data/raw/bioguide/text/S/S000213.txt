SEASTRAND, Andrea, a Representative from California
born in Chicago, Ill., August 5, 1941
graduated DePaul University, B.A., 1963
elementary school teacher
California Federation of Republican Women, president
member California State Assembly, 1990-1994
assistant minority leader
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth Congress (January 3, 1995-January 3, 1997)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress.

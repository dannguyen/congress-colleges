SEAVER, Ebenezer, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Roxbury, Mass., July 5, 1763
was graduated from Harvard University in 1784
engaged in agricultural pursuits
member of the State house of representatives 1794-1802
elected as a Republican to the Eighth and to the four succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1803-March 3, 1813)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1812 to the Thirteenth Congress
member of the State constitutional convention in 1820
again a member of the State house of representatives in 1822, 1823, and 1826
died in Roxbury, Mass., March 1, 1844.

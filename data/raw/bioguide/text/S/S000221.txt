SEDGWICK, Charles Baldwin, a Representative from New York
born in Pompey, Onondaga County, N.Y., March 15, 1815
attended Pompey Hill Academy, and Hamilton College, Clinton, N.Y.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1848 and commenced practice in Syracuse, N.Y.
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-sixth and Thirty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1859-March 3, 1863)
chairman, Committee on Naval Affairs (Thirty-seventh Congress)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1862
engaged for the next two years in codifying naval laws for the Navy Department at Washington, D.C.
resumed the practice of law in Syracuse, N.Y., where he died February 3, 1883
interment in Oakwood Cemetery.

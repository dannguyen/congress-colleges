SESSIONS, Pete, a Representative from Texas
born in Waco, McLennan County, Tex., March 22, 1955
B.S., Southwestern University, Georgetown, Tex., 1978
business executive
professional advocate
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-present)
chair, Committee on Rules (One Hundred Thirteenth and One Hundred Fourteenth Congresses).

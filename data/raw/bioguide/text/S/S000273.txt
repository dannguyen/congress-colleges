SHACKELFORD, John Williams, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Richlands, Onslow County, N.C., November 16, 1844
attended the common schools and Richlands (N.C.) Academy
during the Civil War entered the Confederate Army at the age of seventeen and served throughout the war, attaining the rank of lieutenant
member of the State house of representatives 1872-1878
served in the State senate 1878-1880
was elected to preside over the convention that nominated him for Congress in 1880
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-seventh Congress, and served from March 4, 1881, until his death in Washington, D.C., January 18, 1883
interment in the Wallace Graveyard, Richlands, N.C.

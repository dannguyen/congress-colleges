SHARP, Philip Riley, a Representative from Indiana
born in Baltimore, Md., July 15, 1942
attended Washington Elementary School, Elwood, Ind.
graduated from Wendell Willkie High School, Elwood, 1960
attended DePauw University, Greencastle, Ind., 1961
B.S., Georgetown University School of Foreign Service, Washington, D.C., 1964
graduate work, Exeter College, Oxford University, 1966
Ph.D., Georgetown University, 1974
aide to United States Senator Vance Hartke, 1964-1969
assistant and later associate professor, Ball State University, Muncie, Ind., 1969-1974
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-fourth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1975-January 3, 1995)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourth Congress.

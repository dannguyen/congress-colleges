SHAW, Henry Marchmore, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Newport, R.I., November 20, 1819
completed preparatory studies
was graduated from the medical department of the University of Pennsylvania at Philadelphia in 1838 and began practice in Indiantown, Camden County, N.C.
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Thirty-fourth Congress
elected to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1859)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Thirty-sixth Congress
served as a colonel in the Confederate Army during the Civil War and was killed near New Bern, N.C., November 1, 1864
interment in the cemetery at Shawboro, Currituck County, N.C.

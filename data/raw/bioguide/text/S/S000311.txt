SHAW, Tristram, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Hampton, Rockingham County, N.H., May 23, 1786
completed preparatory studies
held several local offices in Exeter, N.H.
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth and Twenty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1839-March 3, 1843)
died in Exeter, Rockingham County, N.H., March 14, 1843
interment in Bride Hill Cemetery, Hampton, N.H.

SHERBURNE, John Samuel, a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Portsmouth, N.H., in 1757
was graduated from Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H., in 1776 and from the law department of Harvard University
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Portsmouth, N.H., in 1776
served in the Revolutionary Army and attained the rank of major
elected as an Anti-Administration candidate to the Third Congress and reelected as a Republican to the Fourth Congress (March 4, 1793-March 3, 1797)
United States district attorney for New Hampshire 1801-1804
appointed judge of the United States District Court for the District of New Hampshire and served from May 1804 until his death in Portsmouth, N.H., August 2, 1830.

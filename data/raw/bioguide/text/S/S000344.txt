SHERMAN, Brad, a Representative from California
born in Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, Calif., October 24, 1954
B.A., University of California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1974
J.D., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1979
lawyer, private practice
certified public accountant
member of the California state board of equalization, 1990-1995
chair, California state board of equalization, 1991-1995
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-present).

born in Greenville, Ulster County, N.Y., February 16, 1813
attended the public schools
tanner and farmer
held several local offices
major in the State militia
elected as a Whig to the Thirtieth Congress (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1849)
member of the State senate in 1854

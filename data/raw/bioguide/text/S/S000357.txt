SHERWOOD, Samuel Burr, a Representative from Connecticut
born in Northfield Society (later Weston), Conn., November 26, 1767
graduated from Yale College in 1786
studied law
was admitted to the bar and began practice in that part of Fairfield which is now Westport, Conn.
member of the State house of representatives 1809-1815
served in the State senate in 1816
elected as a Federalist to the Fifteenth Congress (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1819)
resumed the practice of his profession until 1831, when he retired from professional life
died in Westport, Fairfield County, Conn., on April 27, 1833
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

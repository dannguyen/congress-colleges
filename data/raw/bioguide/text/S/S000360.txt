SHIELDS, Ebenezer J., a Representative from Tennessee
born in Georgia, December 22, 1778
moved to Tennessee in 1809 and settled on Robertson Fork Creek near Lynnville, Giles County
completed preparatory studies
was graduated from the University of Nashville, Tennessee, in 1827
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Pulaski, Tenn.
member of the state house of representatives 1833-1835
elected as an Anti-Jacksonian to the Twenty-fourth Congress and reelected as a Whig to the Twenty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1835-March 3, 1839)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1838 to the Twenty-sixth Congress
resumed the practice of law in Pulaski, Tenn.
moved to Memphis, Tenn., in 1844 and continued the practice of his profession
died near La Grange, Fayette County, Tex., April 21, 1846.

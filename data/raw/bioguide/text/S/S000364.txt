SHIMKUS, John M., a Representative from Illinois
born in Collinsville, Madison County, Ill., February 21, 1958
graduated from Collinsville High School, Collinsville, Ill.
B.S., West Point Military Academy, West Point, N.Y., 1980
teaching certificate, Christ College, Irvine, Calif., 1990
M.B.A., Southern Illinois University, Edwardsville, Ill., 1997
United States Army, 1980-1986
United States Army Reserve, 1986-2008
Collinsville Township, Ill., trustee, 1989-1993
Madison County, Ill., treasurer, 1990-1996
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-present).

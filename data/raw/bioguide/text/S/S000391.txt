SHULL, Joseph Horace, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born at Martins Creek, Northampton County, Pa., August 17, 1848
attended the public schools and Blair Hall, Blairstown, N.J.
took a special course at Lafayette College, Easton, Pa.
was graduated from the University of New York and in 1873 from the Bellevue Hospital Medical College, both in New York City
taught in the public schools of Pennsylvania for four years
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1879 and commenced practice in Stroudsburg, Monroe County, Pa.
editor of the Monroe Democrat 1881-1886
member of the State senate 1886-1891
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1903-March 3, 1905)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1904
resumed the practice of law and medicine
was a contract surgeon during the First World War
died in Stroudsburg, Pa., August 9, 1944
interment in Stroudsburg Cemetery.

SHUSTER, E. G. (Bud), (father of William Shuster), a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Glassport, Allegheny County, Pa., January 23, 1932
B.S., University of Pittsburgh, Pa., 1954
M.B.A., Duquesne University, Pittsburgh, Pa., 1960
Ph.D., American University, Washington, D.C., 1967
United States Army, 1954-1956
delegate, Republican National Conventions, 1976, 1980, 1984, and 1988
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-third and to the fourteen succeeding Congresses, served until his resignation on February 3, 2001 (January 3, 1973-February 3, 2001)
chairman, Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure (One Hundred Fourth through One Hundred Sixth Congresses).

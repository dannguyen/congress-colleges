SILVESTER, Peter Henry, (grandson of Peter Silvester), a Representative from New York
born in Kinderhook, Columbia County, N.Y., February 17, 1807
attended Kinderhook Academy, and was graduated from Union College, Schenectady, N.Y., in 1827
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1830 and practiced his profession in Coxsackie, N.Y.
elected as a Whig to the Thirtieth and Thirty-first Congresses (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1851)
retired and lived on one of his farms in Coxsackie, Greene County, N.Y., until his death on November 29, 1882
interment in Kinderhook Cemetery, Kinderhook, Columbia County, N.Y.

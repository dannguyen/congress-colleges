SIMPSON, Edna Oakes, (wife of Sidney E. Simpson), a Representative from Illinois
born in Carrollton, Greene County, Ill., October 26, 1891
elected as a Republican to the Eighty-sixth Congress (January 3, 1959-January 3, 1961)
did not seek renomination in 1960
was a resident of Carrollton, Ill., until her death in Alton, Ill., on May 15, 1984.

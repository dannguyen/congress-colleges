SINGLETON, Thomas Day, a Representative from South Carolina
born near Kingstree, S.C., birth date unknown
attended the common schools
member, State house of representatives, 1826-1833
elected as a Nullifier to the Twenty-third Congress and served without having qualified, from March 3, 1833, until his death in Raleigh, N.C., November 25, 1833, while en route to Washington, D.C.
interment in Congressional Cemetery, Washington, D.C.

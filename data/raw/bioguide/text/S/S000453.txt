SISISKY, Norman, a Representative from Virginia
born in Baltimore, Md., June 9, 1927
graduated from John Marshall High School, Richmond, Va., 1944
B.S., Virginia Commonwealth University, Richmond, 1949
United States Navy, 1945-1946
member of the Virginia state house of delegates, 1974-1982
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-eighth and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-March 29, 2001)
died on March 29, 2001, in Richmond, Va.
interment in Temple Beth-El Cemetery, Richmond, Va.

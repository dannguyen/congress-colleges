SKELTON, Isaac Newton, IV (Ike), a Representative from Missouri
born in Lexington, Lafayette County, Mo., December 20, 1931
attended the Wentworth Military Academy, Lexington, Mo.
graduated from Lexington High School, Lexington, Mo., 1949
attended the University of Edinburgh, Scotland, 1953
A.B., University of Missouri, Columbia, Mo., 1953
LL.B., University of Missouri, Columbia, Mo., 1956
lawyer, private practice
Lafayette County, Mo., prosecuting attorney, 1957-1960
special assistant attorney general, Missouri state attorney general, 1961-1963
member of the Missouri state senate, 1971-1977
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-fifth and to the sixteen succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1977-January 3, 2011)
chair, Committee on Armed Services (One Hundred Tenth and One Hundred Eleventh Congresses)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010
died in Arlington, Va., October 28, 2013.

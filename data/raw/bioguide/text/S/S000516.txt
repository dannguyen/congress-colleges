SMITH, Ballard, a Representative from Virginia
born in Hanover County, Va., birth date unknown
served as lieutenant in the Army during the Revolutionary War
member of the State house of delegates 1810-1813
elected as a Republican to the Fourteenth, Fifteenth, and Sixteenth Congresses (March 4, 1815-March 3, 1821)
again a member of the State house of delegates, 1824-1826, 1836, and 1837
death date unknown.

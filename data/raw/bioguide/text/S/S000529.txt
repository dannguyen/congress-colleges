SMITH, Edward Henry, a Representative from New York
born in Smithtown, Long Island, Suffolk County, N.Y., May 5, 1809
attended private schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
served as justice of the peace in the township of Smithtown 1833-1843, assessor 1840-1843, and supervisor 1856-1860
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1861-March 3, 1863)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1862
resumed farming in Suffolk County, N.Y.
died in Smithtown, N.Y., August 7, 1885

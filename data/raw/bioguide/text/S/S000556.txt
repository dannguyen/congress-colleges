SMITH, Isaac, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Chester County, Pa., January 4, 1761
attended the common schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits near Level Corners, Lycoming County, Pa.
member of the State house of representatives 1806-1808
elected as a Republican to the Thirteenth Congress (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1815)
resumed agricultural pursuits and also engaged in the occupation of millwright
died on his farm at Level Corners, near Jersey Shore, Lycoming County, Pa., April 4, 1834
interment in the Pine Creek Presbyterian Churchyard
reinterment in Jersey Shore Cemetery, Jersey Shore, Lycoming County, Pa.

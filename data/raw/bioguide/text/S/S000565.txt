SMITH, John, (father of Worthington Curtis Smith
great-great grandfather of William Warren Scranton), a Representative from Vermont
born in Barre, Mass., August 12, 1789
attended the common schools
moved to St. Albans, Vt.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1810 and commenced practice in St. Albans, Vt.
StateÂ’s attorney for Franklin County 1826-1832
member of the State house of representatives 1827-1837, and served as speaker from 1831 to 1833
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1839-March 3, 1841)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1840 to the Twenty-seventh Congress
engaged in the construction of railroads
died in St. Albans, Vt., November 26, 1858
interment in Greenwood Cemetery.

SMITH, Nathaniel, (brother of Nathan Smith and uncle of Truman Smith), a Representative from Connecticut
born in Woodbury, Conn., January 6, 1762
attended the common schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits and was also a cattle dealer
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1787 and commenced the practice of his profession in Woodbury, Conn.
member of the State house of representatives 1789-1795
elected as a Federalist to the Fourth and Fifth Congresses (March 4, 1795-March 3, 1799)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1798
served in the State senate 1800-1805
judge of the supreme court of Connecticut 1806-1819
delegate to the Hartford Convention of 1814
died in Woodbury, Litchfield County, Conn., March 9, 1822
interment in the Episcopal Church Cemetery.

SMITH, Robert Freeman, a Representative from Oregon
born in Portland, Multnomah County, Oreg., June 16, 1931
attended public schools in Burns, Oreg.
B.A., Willamette University, Salem, Oreg., 1953
rancher
elected to the Oregon house of representatives, 1960-1972
Oregons representative on Presidents Public Land Review Commission, 1965-1969
delegate, Republican National Convention, 1980
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-eighth and the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-January 3, 1995)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourth Congress
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fifth Congress (January 3, 1997-January 3, 1999)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1998 to the One Hundred Sixth Congress
chairman, Committee on Agriculture (One Hundred Fifth Congress).

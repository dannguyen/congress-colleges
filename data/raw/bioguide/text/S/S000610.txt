SMITH, Samuel, a Representative from Pennsylvania
birth date unknown
associate judge of Erie County, Pa., 1803-1805, when he resigned
elected as a Republican to the Ninth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative John B. C. Lucas
reelected as a Republican to the Tenth and Eleventh Congresses (November 7, 1805-March 3, 1811)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Twelfth Congress in 1810
death date unknown.

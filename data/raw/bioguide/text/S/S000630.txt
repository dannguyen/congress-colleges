SMITH, William Alexander, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Warren County, N.C., January 9, 1828
attended the common schools
engaged in agricultural pursuits
member of the State constitutional convention in 1865
member of the State senate in 1870
president of the North Carolina Railroad in 1868 and of the Yadkin River Railroad
elected as a Republican to the Forty-third Congress (March 4, 1873-March 3, 1875)
died in Richmond, Va., May 16, 1888
interment in Hollywood Cemetery.

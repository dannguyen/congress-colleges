SNEED, William Henry, a Representative from Tennessee
born in Davidson County, Tenn., August 27, 1812
completed preparatory studies
moved with his fatherÂ’s family to Rutherford County, Tenn.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1834 and commenced practice in Murfreesboro, Tenn.
member of the State senate 1843-1845
moved to Knoxville, Tenn., in 1845 and resumed the practice of law
elected as the candidate of the American Party to the Thirty-fourth Congress (March 4, 1855-March 3, 1857)
chairman, Committee on Mileage (Thirty-fourth Congress)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1856 and also for nomination as circuit judge
resumed the practice of law
died in Knoxville, Tenn., September 18, 1869
interment in the Old Gray Cemetery.

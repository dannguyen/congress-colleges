SNODGRASS, Charles Edward, (nephew of Henry Clay Snodgrass), a Representative from Tennessee
born near Sparta, White County, Tenn., December 28, 1866
attended the common schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Crossville, Tenn., in 1888
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-sixth and Fifty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1899-March 3, 1903)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1902
judge of the fifth judicial circuit of Tennessee
appointed and subsequently elected judge of the court of appeals upon the reorganization of that court and served from 1925 to 1934
retired to private life in Crossville, Tenn., where he died August 3, 1936
interment in the Crossville City Cemetery.

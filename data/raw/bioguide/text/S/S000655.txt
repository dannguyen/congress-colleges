SNODGRASS, Henry Clay, (uncle of Charles Edward Snodgrass), a Representative from Tennessee
born near Sparta, White County, Tenn., March 29, 1848
attended Sparta Academy
studied law at Cumberland University, Lebanon, Tenn.
was admitted to the bar in 1870 and commenced practice in Sparta, Tenn.
also engaged in agricultural pursuits
during the Civil War served as a private in the Confederate Army
attorney general of the fifth judicial circuit 1878-1884
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-second and Fifty-third Congresses (March 4, 1891-March 3, 1895)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1894 to the Fifty-fourth Congress
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1896
resumed the practice of his profession in Sparta, White County, Tenn.
moved to Gould, Okla., and engaged in agricultural pursuits
died in Altus, Okla., April 22, 1931
interment in Altus Cemetery.

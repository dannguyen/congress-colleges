SNODGRASS, John Fryall, a Representative from Virginia
born in Berkeley County, Va. (now West Virginia), March 2, 1804
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1843 and commenced practice in Parkersburg, Va.
delegate to the State constitutional convention in 1850 and 1851
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress and served from March 4, 1853, until his death in Parkersburg, Va. (now West Virginia), June 5, 1854
interment in Riverview Cemetery, Parkersburg, Wood County, Va. (Now W. Va.).

SOLLERS, Augustus Rhodes, a Representative from Maryland
born near Prince Frederick, Calvert County, Md., May 1, 1814
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1836 and commenced practice in Prince Frederick
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1843)
resumed the practice of law
delegate to the State constitutional convention in 1851
elected to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
resumed the practice of law at Prince Frederick, and died near there November 26, 1862

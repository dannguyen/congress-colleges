SOMES, Daniel Eton, a Representative from Maine
born in Meredith (now Laconia), N.H., May 20, 1815
received an academic education
moved to Biddeford, Maine, in 1846
established the Eastern Journal, later known as the Union and Journal
engaged in the manufacture of loom harnesses, reed twine, and varnishes
mayor of Biddeford 1855-1857
president of the City Bank of Biddeford 1856-1858
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1859-March 3, 1861)
member of the peace convention of 1861 held in Washington, D.C., in an effort to devise means to prevent the impending war
engaged in the practice of patent law in Washington, D.C., until his death in that city on February 13, 1888
interment in Rock Creek Cemetery.

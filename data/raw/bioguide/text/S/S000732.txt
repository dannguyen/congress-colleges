SPERRY, Lewis, a Representative from Connecticut
born at East Windsor Hill, town of South Windsor, Conn., January 23, 1848
attended the district school and Monson Academy, Monson, Mass.
was graduated from Amherst College, Amherst, Mass., in 1873
studied law
was admitted to the bar in March 1875 and commenced practice in Hartford, Conn.
member of the State house of representatives in 1876
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-second and Fifty-third Congresses (March 4, 1891-March 3, 1895)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1894 to the Fifty-fourth Congress
again resumed the practice of his profession in Hartford, Conn.
died at East Windsor Hill, town of South Windsor, Hartford County, Conn., June 22, 1922
interment in South Windsor Cemetery.

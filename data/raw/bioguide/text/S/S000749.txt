SPRATT, John McKee, Jr., a Representative from South Carolina
born in Charlotte, Mecklenburg County, N.C., November 1, 1942
graduated from York High School, York, S.C., 1960
A.B., Davidson College, Davidson, N.C., 1964
M.A., Marshall Scholar, Corpus Christi College, Oxford University, Oxford, England, 1966
LL.B., Yale Law School, New Haven, Conn., 1969
United States Army, 1969-1971
lawyer, private practice
banker
business owner
delegate, South Carolina state Democratic convention, 1972-1980
delegate, Democratic National Convention, 1964
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-eighth and to the thirteen succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010
chair, Committee on the Budget (One Hundred Tenth and One Hundred Eleventh Congresses).

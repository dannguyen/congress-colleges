STANLY, John, (father of Edward Stanly), a Representative from North Carolina
born in New Bern, N.C., April 9, 1774
received his early education from private tutors
attended Princeton University
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1799 and practiced
clerk and master in equity
member of the State house of commons in 1798 and 1799
elected as a Federalist to the Seventh Congress (March 4, 1801-March 3, 1803)
elected to the Eleventh Congress (March 4, 1809-March 3, 1811)
resumed the practice of his profession
again a member of the State house of commons 1812-1815, 1818, 1819, 1823-1825, and 1826
died in New Bern, N.C., August 2, 1834
interment in Episcopal Cemetery.

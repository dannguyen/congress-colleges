STARKWEATHER, George Anson, a Representative from New York
born in Preston, Conn., May 19, 1794
attended the common schools and was graduated from Union College, Schenectady, N.Y., in 1819
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced in Cooperstown, N.Y.
elected as a Democrat to the Thirtieth Congress (March 4, 1847-March 3, 1849)
again resumed the practice of his profession in Milwaukee, Wis., 1853-1868
died in Cooperstown, N.Y., October 15, 1879
interment in Lakewood Cemetery.

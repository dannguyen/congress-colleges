STEARNS, Clifford Bundy, a Representative from Florida
born in Washington, D.C., April 16, 1941
attended Hearst Elementary School, 1946-1952
graduated from Woodrow Wilson High School, Washington, D.C., 1959
B.S., George Washington University, Washington, D.C., 1963
attended the University of California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1965
United States Air Force, 1963-1967
businessman
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred First and to the eleven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1989-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

STEBBINS, Henry George, a Representative from New York
born in Ridgefield, Conn., September 15, 1811
attended private schools
moved to New York
engaged in banking
colonel of the Twelfth Regiment
president of the Dramatic Fund Association
president of the Academy of Music
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-eighth Congress and served from March 4, 1863, until his resignation on October 24, 1864
engaged as a stock broker
president of the New York Stock Exchange
appointed president of the Central Park Commission
died in New York City December 9, 1881
interment in Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn, N.Y.

STEELE, William Gaston, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Somerville, Somerset County, N.J., December 17, 1820
attended the public schools and Somerville Academy
engaged in banking
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-seventh and Thirty-eighth Congresses (March 4, 1861-March 3, 1865)
engaged in the brokerage business
died in Somerville, Somerset County, N.J., April 22, 1892
interment in Somerville City Cemetery.

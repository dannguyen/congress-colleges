STEPHENS, Abraham P., a Representative from New York
born near New City, Rockland County, N.Y., February 18, 1796
justice of the peace
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-second Congress (March 4, 1851-March 3, 1853)
died in Nyack, Rockland County, N.Y., November 25, 1859
interment in Oak Hill Cemetery.

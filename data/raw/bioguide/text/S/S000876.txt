STEVENS, Bradford Newcomb, a Representative from Illinois
born in Boscawen, Merrimack County, N.H., January 3, 1813
attended schools in New Hampshire and at Montreal, Canada, and was graduated from Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H., in 1835
taught school six years in Hopkinsville, Ky., and New York City
moved to Bureau County, Ill., in 1846
engaged in mercantile and agricultural pursuits
county surveyor
mayor of Tiskilwa, Ill.
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-second Congress (March 4, 1871-March 3, 1873)
resumed mercantile and agricultural pursuits
died in Tiskilwa, Bureau County, Ill., November 10, 1885
interment in Mount Bloom Cemetery.

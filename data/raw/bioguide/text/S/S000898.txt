STEWART, Alexander, a Representative from Wisconsin
born in Fredericton, York County, New Brunswick, Canada, September 12, 1829
attended the common schools of his native city
moved to Marathon County, Wis., in 1849, and settled where the city of Wausau is now located
engaged in the lumber business
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1884
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-fourth, Fifty-fifth, and Fifty-sixth Congresses (March 4, 1895-March 3, 1901)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1900 to the Fifty-seventh Congress
resided in Washington, D.C., until his death on May 24, 1912
interment in Pine Grove Cemetery, Wausau, Wis.

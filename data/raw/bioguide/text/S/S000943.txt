STODDARD, Ebenezer, a Representative from Connecticut
born in Union, Tolland County, Conn., May 6, 1785
attended Woodstock Academy in 1802 and in 1803, and was graduated from Brown University, Providence, R.I., in 1807
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1810 and commenced practice in West Woodstock, Conn.
elected as Republican to the Seventeenth Congress and reelected as an Adams-Clay Republican candidate to the Eighteenth Congresses (March 4, 1821-March 3, 1825)
served in the State senate 1825-1827
Lieutenant Governor of Connecticut in 1833 and 1835-1837
practiced law
died in West Woodstock, Conn., on August 19, 1847
interment in Bungay Cemetery.

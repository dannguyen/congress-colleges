STONE, Michael Jenifer, (brother of Thomas Stone and grandfather of Frederick Stone), a Representative from Maryland
born at Equality, near Port Tobacco, Charles County, Md., in 1747
completed preparatory studies
member of the state House of Delegates 1781-1783
member of the state ratification convention in 1788
elected as an Anti-Administration candidate to the First Congress (March 4, 1789-March 3, 1791)
appointed judge of the first judicial district of Maryland in 1791
died in Charles County, Md., in 1812
interment on his estate, ``Equality,'' near Port Tobacco, Charles County, Md.

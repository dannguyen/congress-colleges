STOWER, John G., a Representative from New York
born in Madison, Madison County, N.Y., ca. 1791
completed preparatory studies
sergeant in the 129th Regiment of the New York State Militia, 1812
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twentieth Congress (March 4, 1827-March 3, 1829)
member of the New York state senate, 1833 and 1834
died on December 20, 1850
interment in Madison Street Cemetery, Hamilton, N.Y.

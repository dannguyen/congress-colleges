STRADER, Peter Wilson, a Representative from Ohio
born in Shawnee, Warren County, N.J., November 6, 1818
moved with his parents to Lebanon, Ohio, in 1819
attended the common schools
worked in a printing office for three years
moved to Cincinnati, Ohio, in 1835
connected with the steamboat interests on the Ohio and Mississippi Rivers as a clerk and an engineer 1835-1848
general ticket agent of the Little Miami Railroad 1848-1867
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-first Congress (March 4, 1869-March 3, 1871)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1870
resumed his former business interests
moved to Ashtabula, Ohio, in 1876, where he died February 25, 1881
interment in Spring Grove Cemetery, Cincinnati, Ohio.

STRATTON, John, a Representative from Virginia
born at Old Castle, near Eastville, Northampton County, Va., August 19, 1769
attended the common schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
member of the State house of delegates 1789-1792
elected as a Federalist to the Seventh Congress (March 4, 1801-March 3, 1803)
died in Norfolk, Va., May 10, 1804
interment in St. Pauls Church Cemetery.

STRATTON, John Leake Newbold, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Mount Holly, Burlington County, N.J., November 27, 1817
attended private schools at Mount Holly
prepared for college at Mendham
graduated from Princeton College, 1836
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-sixth and to the succeeding Congress (March 4, 1859-March 3, 1863)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1862
delegate to the Union National Convention of Conservatives at Philadelphia, 1866
president of the FarmersÂ’ National Bank of Mount Holly in 1875
died on May 17, 1889, Mount Holly, N.J.
interment in St. Andrews Cemetery.

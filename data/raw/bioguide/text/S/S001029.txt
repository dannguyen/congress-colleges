STRUDWICK, William Francis, a Representative from North Carolina
born at Stag Park, near Wilmington, New Hanover County, N.C., ca. 1765
received a limited education
farmer
delegate to the North Carolina state convention, 1789
member of the North Carolina state senate, 1792-1793 and 1797
elected as a Federalist to the Fourth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Absalom Tatom (November 28, 1796-March 3, 1797)
member of the North Carolina state house of representatives, 1801-1803
died in North Carolina in 1812
interment in a private cemetery on his estate at ``Hawfields,'' Orange County, N.C.

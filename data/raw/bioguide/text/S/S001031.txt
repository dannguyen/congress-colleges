STUART, Andrew, a Representative from Ohio
born near Pittsburgh, Pa., August 3, 1823
moved to Pittsburgh with his mother in 1834
received limited schooling
worked in a newspaper office
moved to Steubenville, Ohio, in 1850
was editor of the American Union 1850-1857
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection
engaged in the shipping business on the Gulf of Mexico and in the transportation of mails and supplies from Leavenworth, Kans., to Santa Fe, N.Mex.
resided in Washington, D.C., from 1869 until his death, April 30, 1872
interment in Union Cemetery, Steubenville, Ohio.

STUPAK, Bart T., a Representative from Michigan
born in Milwaukee, Milwaukee County, Wis., February 29, 1952
graduated from Gladstone High School, Gladstone, Mich., 1970
A.A., Northwestern Michigan Community College, 1972
B.S., Saginaw Valley State College, University Center, Mich., 1977
J.D., Thomas M. Cooley Law School, Lansing, Mich., 1981
patrolman, Escanaba, Mich., police department, 1972-1973
trooper, Michigan department of state police, 1973-1984
member of the Michigan state house of representatives, 1989-1990
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Third and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1993-January 3, 2011)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

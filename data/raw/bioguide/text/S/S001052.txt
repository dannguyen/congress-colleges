SULLIVAN, George, (son of John Sullivan and nephew of James Sullivan), a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Durham, N.H., August 29, 1771
was graduated from Harvard University in 1790
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Exeter, Rockingham County, N.H., in 1793
member of the State house of representatives in 1805
attorney general of New Hampshire in 1805 and 1806
elected as a Federalist to the Twelfth Congress (March 4, 1811-March 3, 1813)
again a member of the State house of representatives in 1813
served in the State senate in 1814 and 1815
again attorney general of the State 1816-1835
died in Exeter, N.H., April 14, 1838
interment in the Old Cemetery (Winter Street).

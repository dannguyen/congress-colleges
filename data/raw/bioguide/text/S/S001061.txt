SULLIVAN, Timothy Daniel, a Representative from New York
born in New York City July 23, 1862
attended public schools
businessman, real estate and theatrical
member of the New York state assembly, 1886-1894
member of the New York state senate 1894-1903, 1909-1912
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-eighth and to the succeeding Congress (March 4, 1903-July 27, 1906)
resigned on July 27, 1906
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-third Congress in 1912, but never took his seat
struck and killed on August 31, 1913, by a locomotive near Pelham Parkway, New York, N.Y.
interment in Calvary Cemetery, Long Island City, N.Y.

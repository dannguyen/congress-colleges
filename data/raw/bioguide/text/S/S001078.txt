SUNUNU, John E., a Representative and a Senator from New Hampshire
born in Boston, Suffolk County, Mass., September 10, 1964
graduated from Salem High School, Salem, N.H. 1982
B.S., M.S., Massachusetts Institute of Technology 1987
M.B.A., Harvard Graduate School of Business 1991
elected as a Republican to the House of Representatives for the One Hundred Fifth and the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2003)
was not a candidate for reelection in 2002
elected as a Republican to the U.S. Senate in 2002, and served from January 3, 2003, to January 3, 2009
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 2008.

SWARTZ, Joshua William, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Lower Swatara Township, Dauphin County, Pa., June 9, 1867
raised on his fatherÂ’s farm
attended the rural schools, Lebanon Valley College, and Williamsport Commercial School
was graduated from the law department of Dickinson College, Carlisle, Pa., in 1892
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Harrisburg, Pa.
member of the State house of representatives 1915-1917
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1925-March 3, 1927)
declined to become a candidate for reelection in 1926
resumed the practice of law until his death in Harrisburg, Pa., May 27, 1959
interment in Paxtang Cemetery, Paxtang, Pa.

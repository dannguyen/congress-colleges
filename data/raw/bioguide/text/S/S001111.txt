SWEETSER, Charles, a Representative from Ohio
born in Dummerston, Vt., January 22, 1808
moved with his parents to Delaware, Ohio, in 1817
attended the public schools
engaged in mercantile pursuits
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1832 and commenced practice in Delaware, Delaware County, Ohio
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-first and Thirty-second Congresses (March 4, 1849-March 3, 1853)
chairman, Committee on Public Expenditures (Thirty-second Congress)
resumed the practice of law
died in Delaware, Ohio, April 14, 1864
interment in Oak Grove Cemetery.

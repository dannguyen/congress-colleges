SWETT, Richard, (son-in-law of Thomas Peter Lantos), a Representative from New Hampshire
born in Bryn Mawr, Montgomery County, Pa., May 1, 1957
attended public schools in Meredith and Laconia, N.H.
B.A., Yale University, New Haven, Conn., 1979
architect
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Second and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 1991-January 3, 1995)
was not a candidate for reelection to the House of Representatives but was an unsuccessful candidate in 1996 for election to the United States Senate
United States Ambassador to Denmark, 1998-2001
is a resident of Bow, N.H.

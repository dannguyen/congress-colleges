SHAYS, Christopher H., a Representative from Connecticut
born in Stamford, Fairfield County, Conn., October 18, 1945
graduated Darien High School, Darien, Conn., 1964
B.A., Principia College, Elsah, Ill., 1968
M.B.A., New York University, New York, N.Y., 1974
M.P.A., New York University, New York, N.Y., 1978
Peace Corps, 1968-1970
executive aide to Trumbull, Conn., first selectman Larry Heimann, 1971-1972
member of the Connecticut state house of representatives, 1975-1987
elected as a Republican to the One Hundredth Congress by special election to fill the vacancy caused by the death of United States Representative Stewart B. McKinney and reelected to the ten succeeding Congresses (August 18, 1987-January 3, 2009)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate in 2012.

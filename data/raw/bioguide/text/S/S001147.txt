SHOWS, Clifford Ronald, a Representative from Mississippi
born in Moselle, Jones County, Miss., January 26, 1947
graduated from Moselle High School, Moselle, Miss., 1965
B.S., University of Southern Mississippi, Hattiesburg, 1971
farmer
teacher
elected circuit clerk, Jefferson Davis County, Miss., 1976
member of the Mississippi state senate, 1980-1988
elected as Mississippi state Southern District Highway Commissioner, 1988-1998
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Sixth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 1999-January 3, 2003)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eighth Congress in 2002
is a resident of Bassfield, Miss.

SIMPSON, Michael K., a Representative from Idaho
born in Burley, Cassia County, Idaho, September 8, 1950
graduated from Blackfoot High School, Blackfoot, Idaho, 1968
graduated from Utah State University, Logan, Utah, 1972
D.M.D., Washington University School of Dental Medicine, St. Louis, Mo., 1978
dentist
member of the Idaho state house of representatives, 1984-1998, speaker, 1993-1998
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Sixth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1999-present).

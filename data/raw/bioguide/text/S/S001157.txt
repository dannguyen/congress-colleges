SCOTT, David, a Representative from Georgia
born in Aynor, Horry County, S.C., June 27, 1945
graduated from Campbell High School, Daytona Beach, Fla., 1963
B.A., Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University, Tallahassee, Fla., 1967
M.B.A., Wharton School of Finance, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, Pa., 1969
business owner
member of the Georgia state house of representatives, 1974-1982
member of the Georgia state senate, 1982-2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

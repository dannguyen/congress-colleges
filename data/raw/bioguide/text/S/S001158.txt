SALAZAR, John, (brother of Kenneth Salazar), a Representative from Colorado
born in Alamosa, Alamosa County, Colo., July 21, 1953
B.S., Adams State College, Alamosa, Colo., 1981
United States Army, 1973-1976
farmer
rancher
business owner
member of the Colorado Agricultural Commission, 1999-2002
member of the Colorado state house of representatives, 2003-2004
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Ninth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2005-January 3, 2011)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

SHULER, Heath, a Representative from North Carolina
born in Swain County, N.C., December 31, 1971
graduated from Swain County High School, Bryson City, N.C., 1991
B.A., University of Tennessee, Knoxville, Tenn., 2001
professional athlete
business owner
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2013)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

SUTTON, Betty, a Representative from Ohio
born in Barberton, Summit County, Ohio, July 31, 1963
B.A., Kent State University, Kent, Ohio, 1985
J.D., University of Akron, Akron, Ohio, 1990
lawyer, private practice
Barberton, Ohio, city council, 1991-1992
member, Ohio state house of representatives, 1993-2000
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2007-January 3, 2013)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress in 2012.

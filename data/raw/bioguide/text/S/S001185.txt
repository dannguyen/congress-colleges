SEWELL, Terri, a Representative from Alabama
born in Huntsville, Madison County, Alabama, on January 1, 1965
graduated from Selma High School, Selma, Ala., 1982
A.B., Princeton University, Princeton, N.J., 1986
M.A., Oxford University, Oxford, U.K., 1988
J.D., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1992
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

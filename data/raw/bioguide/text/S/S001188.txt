STUTZMAN, Marlin, a Representative from Indiana
born in Sturgis, Saint Joseph County, Mich., August 31, 1976
graduated from Lake Area Christian High School, Sturgis, Mich., 1994
attended Trine State University, Angola, Ind.
attended Glen Oaks Community College, Centreville, Mich.
farmer
business owner
member of the Indiana state house of representatives, 2002-2008
member of the Indiana state senate, 2009-2010
unsuccessful candidate for nomination to the United States Senate in 2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Mark Souder, and simultaneously elected to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress
reelected to the two succeeding Congresses (November 2, 2010-present).

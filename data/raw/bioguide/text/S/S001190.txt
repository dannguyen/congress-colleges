SCHNEIDER, Brad, a Representative from Illinois
born in Denver, Denver County, Colo., August 20, 1961
graduated from Cherry Creek High School, Denver, Colo., 1979
B.S., Northwestern University, Evanston, Ill., 1983
M.B.A., Northwestern University, 1985
businessman
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress (January 3, 2013-January 3, 2015)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

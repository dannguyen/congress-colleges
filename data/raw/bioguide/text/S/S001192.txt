STEWART, Chris, a Representative from Utah
born in Logan, Cache County, Utah, July 15, 1960
graduated from Sky View High School, Smithfield, Utah, 1978
B.S., Utah State University, Logan, Utah, 1984
United States Air Force, 1984-1998
author
business executive
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

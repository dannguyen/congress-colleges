born in Albany, Albany County, N.Y., July 2, 1984
graduated from Albany Academy for Girls, Albany, N.Y., 2002
A.B., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 2006
staff, President George W. Bush administration, 2006-2009

businesswoman

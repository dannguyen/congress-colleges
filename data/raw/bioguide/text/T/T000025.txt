TALIAFERRO, Benjamin, a Representative from Georgia
born in Virginia in 1750
completed preparatory studies
served in the Revolutionary War as a lieutenant in the rifle corps commanded by General Morgan
was promoted to captain
captured by the British at Charleston in 1780
settled in Georgia in 1785
member of the state senate and its president
delegate to the state constitutional convention in 1798
elected as a Federalist to the Sixth Congress and reelected as a Republican to the Seventh Congress and served from March 4, 1799, until his resignation in 1802
judge of the superior court
trustee of Georgia University
died in Wilkes County, Ga., September 3, 1821.

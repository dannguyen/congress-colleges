TANNER, John S., a Representative from Tennessee
born in Halls, Lauderdale County, Tenn., September 22, 1944
graduated from Union City High School, Union City, 1962
B.S., University of Tennessee, Knoxville, Tenn., 1966
J.D., University of Tennessee, Knoxville, Tenn., 1968
United States Navy, 1968-1972
Tennessee Army National Guard, 1974-2000
lawyer, private practice
member of the Tennessee state house of representatives, 1976-1986
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred First and to the ten succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1989-January 3, 2011)
not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Twelfth Congress in 2010.

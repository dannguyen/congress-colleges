TATE, Randy J., a Representative from Washington
born in Puyallup, Pierce County, Wash., November 23, 1965
A.A., Tacoma Community College
B.A., Western Washington University, Bellingham, 1988
member, Washington State house of representatives, 1988-1994
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth Congress (January 3, 1995-January 3, 1997)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress.

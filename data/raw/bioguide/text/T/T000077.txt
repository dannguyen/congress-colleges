TAYLOR, George, a Representative from New York
born in Wheeling, Va. (now West Virginia), October 19, 1820
completed preparatory studies
studied medicine and law
was admitted to the bar and practiced in Indiana
moved to Alabama in 1844, and to Brooklyn, N.Y., in 1848, where he continued the practice of law
held several local offices
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1859)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1858 to the Thirty-sixth Congress
resumed the practice of law in Washington, D.C., until his death there January 18, 1894
interment in Rock Creek Cemetery.

TAYLOR, Robert, a Representative from Virginia
born at Orange Court House, Va., April 29, 1763
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1783 and commenced practice at Orange Court House, Va.
held several local offices
member of the state senate 1804-1815, and served as president pro tempore
elected as an Adams candidate to the Nineteenth Congress (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1827)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1826
devoted his attention to the management of his plantation
died on his estate, Meadow Farm, in Orange County, Va., July 3, 1845
interment in the family burying ground at ``Meadow Farm.''

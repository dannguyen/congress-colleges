TAYLOR, William Penn, a Representative from Virginia
born in Fredericksburg, Spotsylvania County, Va., birth date unknown
received a limited schooling
held several local offices
elected as an Anti-Jacksonian to the Twenty-third Congress (March 4, 1833-March 3, 1835)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Twenty-fourth Congress in 1834
died at Hayfield, Caroline County, Va., near Fredericksburg, Va.
death date unknown
interment in the family graveyard at "Hayfield."

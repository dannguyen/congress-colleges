TEMPLE, William, a Representative from Delaware
born in Queen Anne County, Md., February 28, 1814
completed preparatory studies
moved to Smyrna, Del., and engaged in mercantile pursuits
member of the State house of representatives in 1844 and served as speaker
served in the State senate 1845-1854
Acting Governor of Delaware from May 1846 to January 1847
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-eighth Congress and served from March 4, 1863, until his death before the convening of the Congress
died in Smyrna, Del., May 28, 1863
interment in the Episcopal Cemetery.

TEMPLETON, Thomas Weir, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Plymouth, Luzerne County, Pa., November 8, 1867
attended the public schools and was graduated from Wyoming Seminary, Kingston, Pa.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1899 but did not practice
prothonotary of Luzerne County 1904-1907
engaged in business as a florist at Kingston
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1917-March 3, 1919)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1918
superintendent of grounds and buildings at the State capitol in 1920-1923
resumed the florist business in Kingston, Pa.
died in Plymouth, Pa., September 5, 1935
interment in Edgehill Cemetery, West Nanticoke, Pa.

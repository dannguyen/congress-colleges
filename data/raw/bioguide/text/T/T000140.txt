THACHER, Thomas Chandler, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Yarmouth Port, Mass., July 20, 1858
attended the public schools
was graduated from Adams Academy, Quincy, Mass., in 1878 and from Harvard University in 1882
became engaged in the wool business at Boston in 1882
president of the Barnstable County Agricultural Society
president of the Cape Cod Pilgrim Memorial Association
chairman of the Yarmouth Port Planning Board
served as chairman of the Provincetown Tercentenary Commission in 1920
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-third Congress (March 4, 1913-March 3, 1915)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1914
writer on business topics and also engaged in his former business pursuits
died in Boston, Mass., April 11, 1945
interment in Woodside Cemetery, Yarmouth Port, Mass.

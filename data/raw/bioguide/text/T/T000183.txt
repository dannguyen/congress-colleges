THOMAS, Richard, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in West Whiteland, Pa., December 30, 1744
educated at home by private teachers
served in the Revolutionary Army as colonel of the First Regiment, Chester County Volunteers
elected as a Federalist to the Fourth, Fifth, and Sixth Congresses (March 4, 1795-March 3, 1801)
engaged in agricultural pursuits
died in Philadelphia, Pa., January 19, 1832
interment in the Friends Western Burial Ground.

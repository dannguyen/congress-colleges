THOMPSON, Hedge, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Salem, N.J., January 28, 1780
pursued an academic course
was graduated from the medical department of the University of Pennsylvania at Philadelphia in 1802 and practiced his profession in Salem
member of the General Assembly of New Jersey in 1805
served in the State council in 1819
appointed associate judge of Salem County, N.J., in 1815 and again in 1824
served as collector for Salem County from 1826 to 1828
elected to the Twentieth Congress and served from March 4, 1827, until his death in Salem, N.J., on July 23, 1828

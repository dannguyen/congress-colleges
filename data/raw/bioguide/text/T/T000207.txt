THOMPSON, John, a Representative from New York
born in Rhinebeck, Dutchess County, N.Y., July 4, 1809
was graduated from Union College, Schenectady, N.Y., and later from Yale College
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Poughkeepsie, N.Y.
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1857-March 3, 1859)
resumed the practice of law
died in New Hamburg, Dutchess County, N.Y., June 1, 1890
interment in Poughkeepsie Rural Cemetery, Poughkeepsie, N.Y.

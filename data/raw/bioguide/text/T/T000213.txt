THOMPSON, Philip Rootes, a Representative from Virginia
born near Fredericksburg, Va., March 26, 1766
was educated by private tutors
was graduated from the College of William and Mary, Williamsburg, Va.
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Fairfax, Va.
member of the State house of delegates 1793-1797
elected as a Republican to the Seventh, Eighth, and Ninth Congresses (March 4, 1801-March 3, 1807)
resumed the practice of law
died in Kanawha County, Va. (now West Virginia), July 27, 1837
interment at Coals Mouth (now St. Albans), W.Va.

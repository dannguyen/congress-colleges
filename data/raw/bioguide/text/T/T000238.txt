THORNBERRY, William McClellan (Mac), a Representative from Texas
born in Clarendon, Donley County, Tex., July 15, 1958
graduated from Clarendon High School, Clarendon, Tex., 1976
B.A., Texas Tech University, Lubbock, Tex., 1980
J.D., University of Texas School of Law, Austin, Tex., 1983
staff, United States Representative Thomas G. Loeffler of Texas, 1983-1985
staff, United States Representative Larry Combest of Texas, 1985-1988
deputy assistant Secretary of State for Legislative Affairs, United States Department of State, 1988-1989
rancher
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and to the ten succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-present)
chair, Committee on Armed Services (One Hundred Fourteenth Congress).

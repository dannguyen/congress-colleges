TIBBATTS, John Wooleston, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Lexington, Ky., June 12, 1802
pursued classical studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1826 and commenced practice in Newport, Campbell County, Ky.
held several local offices
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-eighth and Twenty-ninth Congresses (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1847)
served as colonel in the Mexican War
resumed the practice of law in Newport, Ky., and died there July 5, 1852
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

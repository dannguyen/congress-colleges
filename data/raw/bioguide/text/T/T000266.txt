TIERNEY, John F., a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Salem, Essex County, Mass., September 18, 1951
graduated from Salem High School, Salem, Mass.
B.A., Salem State College, Salem, Mass., 1973
J.D., Suffolk University, Boston, Mass., 1976
member of the Salem, Mass., chamber of commerce, 1976-1996
President, Salem, Mass. chamber of commerce, 1995
trustee, Salem State College, Salem, Mass., 1992-1997
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fifth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1997-January 3, 2015)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

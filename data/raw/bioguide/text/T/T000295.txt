TOLAN, John Harvey, a Representative from California
born in St. Peter, Nicollet County, Minn., January 15, 1877
attended the public schools
moved to Anaconda, Mont., in 1897
was graduated from the law department of the University of Kansas at Lawrence in 1902
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced the practice of law in Anaconda, Mont.
attorney of Deer Lodge County, Mont., 1904-1906
moved to Oakland, Calif., in 1914 and continued the practice of law
elected as a Democrat to the Seventy-fourth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1935-January 3, 1947)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1946 to the Eightieth Congress
died in Westwood, Calif., on June 30, 1947
interment in Holy Sepulchre Cemetery, Hayward, Calif.

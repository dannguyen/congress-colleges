TORKILDSEN, Peter Gerard, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Milwaukee, Wis., January 28, 1958
B.A., University of Massachusetts, Amherst, Mass., 1980
M.P.A., John F. Kennedy School of Government, Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1990
service coordinator, Visiting Nurse Association of Boston, 1982-1984
member of the Massachusetts state house of representatives, 1985-1990
delegate, Republican National Convention, 1988, 1996, and 2008
commissioner, Massachusetts department of labor and industries, 1991-1992
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Third and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 1993-January 3, 1997)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fifth Congress in 1996
unsuccessful candidate for election to the One Hundred Sixth Congress in 1998
commissioner, Massachuetts labor relations commission, 2001-2003
executive director, Massachusetts workforce investment board, 2004-2007
chairman, Massachusetts Republican Party, 2007-2009
businessman.

TOU VELLE, William Ellsworth, a Representative from Ohio
born in Celina, Mercer County, Ohio, November 23, 1862, attended the public schools and was graduated from Celina High School in 1879
appointed postmaster of Celina, Ohio, on May 27, 1885, and served until June 14, 1888, when a successor was appointed
was graduated from Cincinnati Law School in 1889
was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Celina
elected as a Democrat to the Sixtieth and Sixty-first Congresses (March 4, 1907-March 3, 1911)
did not seek renomination in 1910
resumed the practice of law
president of the First National Bank of Celina
died in Celina, Ohio, August 14, 1951
interment in North Grove Cemetery.

TOWELL, David Gilmer, a Representative from Nevada
born in Bronxville, Westchester County, N.Y., June 9, 1937
attended Bronxville and New York City schools
B.A., University of Pacific, Stockton, Calif., 1960
served in the Nevada Air National Guard, 1960-1966
real estate broker
elected chairman, Douglas County Republican Central Committee, 1970
delegate, Nevada State Republican conventions, 1968, 1970, and 1972
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-third Congress (January 3, 1973-January 3, 1975)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1974 to the Ninety-fourth Congress
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate in 1976
resumed real estate business
died on June 10, 2003, in Reno, Nev.

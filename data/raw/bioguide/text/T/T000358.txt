TREADWAY, Allen Towner, a Representative from Massachusetts
was born in Stockbridge, Berkshire County, Mass., on September 16, 1867
attended the public schools, and was graduated from Amherst (Mass.) College in 1886
engaged in the hotel business
member of the State house of representatives in 1904
member of the State senate 1908-1911, and served as president 1909-1911
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-third and to the fifteen succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1913-January 3, 1945)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1944
resided in Stockbridge, Mass., and Washington, D.C.
died in Washington, D.C., February 16, 1947
interment in Stockbridge Cemetery, Stockbridge, Mass.

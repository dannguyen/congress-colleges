TRIMBLE, Carey Allen, a Representative from Ohio
born in Hillsboro, Highland County, Ohio, September 13, 1813
attended Pestalostian School in Philadelphia, Pa., and StubbÂ’s Classical School in Newport, Ky.
was graduated from Ohio University at Athens in 1833 and from Cincinnati Medical College in 1836
taught for four years
practiced medicine in Chillicothe, Ohio
elected as a Republican to the Thirty-sixth and Thirty-seventh Congresses (March 4, 1859-March 3, 1863)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection
resumed medical practice
moved to Columbus, Ohio, where he died May 4, 1887
interment in Grand View Cemetery, Chillicothe, Ohio.

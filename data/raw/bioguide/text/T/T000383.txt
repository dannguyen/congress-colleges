TROUT, Michael Carver, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Hickory Township, Mercer County, Pa., September 30, 1810
received a very limited education
employed as a hatter for three years and then became a carpenter and contractor
served as president of the Hickory Township School Board for twenty years
elected burgess of Sharon in 1841
recorder of Mercer County, Pa., 1842-1845
prothonotary 1846-1851
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection
engaged in iron manufacturing, banking, and coal mining
died in Hickory Township, Pa., June 25, 1873
interment in Morefield Cemetery, Hickory Township, near Sharon, Pa.

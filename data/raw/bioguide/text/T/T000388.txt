TRUMBO, Andrew Alkire, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Montgomery (now Bath) County, Ky., September 15, 1797
attended the common schools
employed in the county clerkÂ’s office
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Owingsville, Ky., in 1824
clerk of Bath County in 1830
Commonwealth attorney for Bath County in 1830
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1845-March 3, 1847)
presidential elector on the Democratic ticket in 1848
resumed the practice of law
moved to Franklin County, Ky.
died in Frankfort, Ky., August 21, 1871
interment in the City Cemetery, Owingsville, Ky.

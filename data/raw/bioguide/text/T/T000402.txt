TUCKER, Starling, a Representative from South Carolina
born in Halifax County, N.C., in 1770
moved to Mountain Shoals (now Enoree), S.C.
received a limited education
held several local offices
member of the State house of representatives
elected as a Republican to the Fifteenth Congress
reelected to the Sixteenth through Nineteenth Congresses and reelected as a Jacksonian to the Twentieth and Twenty-first Congresses (March 4, 1817-March 3, 1831)
died in Mountain Shoals (now Enoree), S.C., January 3, 1834
interment in the private burial ground on the family estate west of Enoree, S.C.

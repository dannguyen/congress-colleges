TULLY, Pleasant Britton, a Representative from California
born in Henderson County, Tenn., on March 21, 1829
moved to Arkansas with his father, who settled in Phillips County in 1838
attended public and private schools
moved to California in 1853 and engaged in mining
resided in Gilroy, Calif., after 1857
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced
delegate at large to the State constitutional convention in 1879
elected as a Democrat to the Forty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1883-March 3, 1885)
resumed the practice of law
died in Gilroy, Santa Clara County, Calif., March 24, 1897
interment in the Masonic Cemetery.

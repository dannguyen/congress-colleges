TWICHELL, Ginery, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Athol, Mass., August 26, 1811
attended the common schools
proprietor of several stagecoach lines
in 1848 engaged in railroading
president of the Boston & Worcester Railway in 1857
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1864
elected as a Republican to the Fortieth, Forty-first, and Forty-second Congresses (March 4, 1867-March 3, 1873)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1872
president of the Atchison, Topeka & Santa Fe Railway Co. 1870-1874
president of the Boston, Barre & Gardner Railroad Co. 1873-1878
died in Brookline, Norfolk County, Mass., July 23, 1883
interment in the Rural Cemetery, Worcester, Mass.

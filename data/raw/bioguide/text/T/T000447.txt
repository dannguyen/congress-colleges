TYLER, Asher, a Representative from New York
born in Bridgewater, Oneida County, N.Y., May 10, 1798
was graduated from Hamilton College, Clinton, N.Y., in 1817
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Ellicottville, Cattaraugus County, N.Y., in 1836
agent of the Devereaux Land Co., with headquarters at Ellicottville, and subsequently served in a like capacity for the Erie Co.
held several local offices
elected as a Whig to the Twenty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1845)
moved to Elmira in 1846 and engaged in railroad operations
one of the incorporators of the Elmira Rolling Mill Co.
died in Elmira, N.Y., August 1, 1875
interment in Woodlawn Cemetery.

TERRY, Lee Raymond, a Representative from Nebraska
born in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebr., January 29, 1962
graduated from Omaha Northwest High School, Omaha, Nebr., 1980
B.A., University of Nebraska, Lincoln, Nebr., 1984
J.D., Creighton Law School, Omaha, Nebr., 1987
member of the Omaha, Nebr., city council, 1991-1998
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Sixth and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1999-January 3, 2015)
was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014.

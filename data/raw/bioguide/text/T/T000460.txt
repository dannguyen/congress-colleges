THOMPSON, Michael, a Representative from California
born in St. Helena, Napa County, Calif., January 24, 1951
graduated from St. Helena High School, St. Helena, Calif.
B.A., California State University, Chico, Calif., 1982
M.A., California State University, Chico, Calif., 1996
United States Army, 1969-1972
California state assembly fellow, 1982-1983
member of the California state senate, 1990-1998
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Sixth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1999-present).

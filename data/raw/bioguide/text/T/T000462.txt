TIBERI, Patrick (Pat), a Representative from Ohio
born in Columbus, Franklin County, Ohio, October 21, 1962
graduated from Northland High School, Columbus, Ohio, 1981
B.A., Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio, 1985
member of the Ohio state house of representatives, 1992-2001
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Seventh and to the seven succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2001-present).

TURNER, Michael R., a Representative from Ohio
born in Dayton, Montgomery County, Ohio, January 11, 1960
graduated from Belmont High School, Dayton, Ohio, 1978
B.A., Ohio Northern University, Ada, Ohio, 1982
J.D., Case Western Reserve University, Cleveland, Ohio, 1985
M.B.A., University of Dayton, Dayton, Ohio, 1992
lawyer, private practice
mayor of Dayton, Ohio, 1994-2002
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

TSONGAS, Nicola S. (Niki), (wife of Paul Tsongas), a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Chico, Butte County, Calif., April 26, 1946
graduated from Narimasu American High School, Tokyo, Japan, 1964
attended Michigan State University, East Lansing, Mich.
B.A., Smith College, Northampton, Mass. 1968
J.D., Boston University, Boston, Mass., 1988
social worker
lawyer, private practice
dean of external affairs, Middlesex Community College, Lowell, Mass., 1997-2007
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Tenth Congress, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of Representative Martin Meehan, and reelected to the four succeeding Congresses (October 16, 2007-present).

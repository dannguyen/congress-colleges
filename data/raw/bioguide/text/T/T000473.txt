TAKAI, Mark, a Representative from Hawaii
born in Honolulu, Honolulu County, Hawaii, July 1, 1967
graduated from Pearl City High School, Pearl City, Hawaii, 1985
B.A., University of Hawaii, Manoa , Hawaii, 1990
M.P.H., University of Hawaii, Manoa, Hawaii, 1993
Hawaii Army National Guard, 1999-present
member of the Hawaii state house of representatives, 1994-2014
vice speaker, Hawaii state house of representatives, 2005-2006
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

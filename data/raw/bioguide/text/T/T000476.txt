TILLIS, Thomas Roland (Thom), a Senator from North Carolina
born in Jacksonville, Duval County, Fla., August 30, 1960
B.A., University of Maryland University College, 1997
management consultant
member of the Cornelius (N.C.) board of commissioners 2003-2005
member of the North Carolina house of representatives 2007-2014, serving as speaker of the house 2011-2014
elected as a Republican to the United States Senate in 2014 for the term ending January 3, 2021.

UNDERHILL, Walter, a Representative from New York
born in New York City September 12, 1795
completed preparatory studies
trustee of the house of refuge
treasurer of New York City for several years
served on the board of managers of the Society for the Reformation of Juvenile Delinquents in the city of New York 1845-1866, serving as treasurer 1857-1866
elected as a Whig to the Thirty-first Congress (March 4, 1849-March 3, 1851)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1850
president of the Mechanics & TradersÂ’ Insurance Co., New York City, from 1853 until his death in Whitestone, Long Island, N.Y., August 17, 1866
interment in Woodlawn Cemetery, New York City.

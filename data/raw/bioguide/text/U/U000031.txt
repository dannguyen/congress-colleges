UPTON, Frederick Stephen, a Representative from Michigan
born in St. Joseph, Berrien County, Mich., April 23, 1953
graduated from Shattuck School, Fairbault, Minn.
A.B., University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Mich., 1975
staff, United States Representative David Stockman of Michigan, 1976-1980
staff, United States Office of Management and Budget, 1981-1985
elected as a Republican to the One Hundredth and to the fourteen succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1987-present)
chair, Committee on Energy and Commerce (One Hundred Twelfth through One Hundred Fourteenth Congresses).

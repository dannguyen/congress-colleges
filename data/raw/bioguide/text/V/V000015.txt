VAN BUREN, John, a Representative from New York
born in Kingston, Ulster County, N.Y., May 13, 1799
was graduated from Union College, Schenectady, N.Y., in 1818
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Kingston, N.Y.
member of the State assembly in 1831
judge of Ulster County 1836-1841
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1843)
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Department of State (Twenty-seventh Congress)
resumed the practice of law
district attorney of Ulster County 1846-1850
died in Kingston, N.Y., January 16, 1855
interment in Old Houghtaling Cemetery.

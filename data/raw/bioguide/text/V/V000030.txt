VANDERVEER, Abraham, a Representative from New York
born in Kings County, N.Y., in 1781
attended the common schools
county clerk of Kings County 1816-1821 and 1822-1837
upon its organization was elected treasurer of the Brooklyn Savings Bank
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-fifth Congress (March 4, 1837-March 3, 1839)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1838
died in Brooklyn, N.Y., July 21, 1839
interment in Reformed Dutch Cemetery.

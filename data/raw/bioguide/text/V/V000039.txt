VAN GAASBECK, Peter, a Representative from New York
born in Ulster County, N.Y., September 27, 1754
attended the grammar schools and became a man of prominence in the county and State
engaged in mercantile pursuits in Kingston, N.Y.
was a captain and major in the Ulster County Militia during the Revolutionary War
elected to the Third Congress (March 4, 1793-March 3, 1795)
died in Kingston, N.Y., in 1797
interment in First Reformed Dutch Churchyard.

VAN SWEARINGEN, Thomas, a Representative from Virginia
born near Shepherdstown, Jefferson County, Va. (now West Virginia), May 5, 1784
attended the common schools
member of the State house of delegates 1814-1816
elected to the Sixteenth and Seventeenth Congresses and served from March 4, 1819, until his death in Shepherdstown, Va., August 19, 1822
interment in Elmwood Cemetery.

VARNUM, John, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Dracut, Middlesex County, Mass., June 25, 1778
was graduated from Harvard University in 1798
studied law
was admitted to the bar and commenced practice in Haverhill, Mass., in 1802
was elected as a Federalist to the State senate in 1811
moved to Lowell, Mass.
elected to the Nineteenth, Twentieth, and Twenty-first Congresses (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1831)
returned to Lowell, Mass., and later moved to Niles, Berrien County, Mich., where he died July 23, 1836
interment in Silverbrook Cemetery.

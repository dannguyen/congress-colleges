VENTO, Bruce Frank, a Representative from Minnesota
born in St. Paul, Ramsey County, Minn., October 7, 1940
attended the public schools of St. Paul
attended St. Thomas College, St. Paul, 1958-1959
A.A., University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, 1961
B.S., Wisconsin State University, River Falls, 1965
graduate studies, University of Minnesota, 1966
taught in Minneapolis, Minn., public schools, 1965-1976
served in Minnesota house of representatives, 1971-1976
elected as a Democrat-Farmer-Labor candidate to the Ninety-fifth and to the eleven succeeding Congresses
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Seventh Congress
served from January 3, 1977, until his death in St. Paul, Minn., on October 10, 2000
interment in Forest Lawn Memorial Park, Maplewood, Ramsey County, Minn.

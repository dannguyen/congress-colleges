VAN HOLLEN, Christopher, a Representative from Maryland
born in Karachi, Pakistan, January 10, 1959
B.A., Swarthmore College, Swarthmore, Pa., 1982
M.P.P., Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass., 1985
J.D., Georgetown University, Washington, D.C., 1990
lawyer, private practice
member of the Maryland state house of representatives, 1990-1994
member of the Maryland state senate, 1994-2002
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Eighth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2003-present).

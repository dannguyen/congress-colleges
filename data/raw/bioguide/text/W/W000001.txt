WACHTER, Frank Charles, a Representative from Maryland
born in Baltimore, Md., September 16, 1861
attended private schools and St. PaulÂ’s Evangelical School at Baltimore, Md.
learned the trade of clothing cutter and in 1892 engaged in the cloth-shrinking business
member of the jail board of Baltimore 1896-1898
unsuccessful candidate for police commissioner of Baltimore in 1898
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-sixth and to the three succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1899-March 3, 1907)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1906
resumed his former business pursuits in Baltimore
member of the board of managers of Maryland Penitentiary from 1909 until his death in Baltimore, Md., on July 1, 1910
interment in Loudon Park Cemetery.

WADSWORTH, Jeremiah, a Delegate and a Representative from Connecticut
born in Hartford, Conn., July 12, 1743
attended the common schools
went to sea in 1761
became first mate of a vessel and subsequently master
served as deputy and commissary general 1775-1778 during the Revolution
Member of the Continental Congress in 1788
member of the Connecticut ratification convention in 1788
elected to the First, Second, and Third Congresses (March 4, 1789-March 3, 1795)
was not a candidate for reelection
member of the State house of representatives in 1795 and of the State executive council 1795-1801
engaged in agricultural pursuits
died in Hartford, Conn., April 30, 1804
interment in Ancient Burying Ground.

WALDRON, Alfred Marpole, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Philadelphia, Pa., September 21, 1865
educated in the public schools of Philadelphia
engaged in the insurance business
member of the Philadelphia Select Council 1911-1924
member of the Republican city committee 1916-1936
delegate to the Republican National Conventions in 1924, 1928, and 1932
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-third Congress (March 4, 1933-January 3, 1935)
did not seek renomination in 1934
resumed the insurance business
died in Philadelphia, Pa., June 28, 1952
interment in North Cedar Hill Cemetery.

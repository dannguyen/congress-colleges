WALKER, Freeman, a Senator from Georgia
born in Charles City, Charles City County, Va., October 25, 1780
attended the common schools
moved to Augusta, Ga., in 1797
studied law
admitted to the bar in 1802 and commenced practice in Augusta
member, State house of representatives 1807-1811
mayor of Augusta 1818-1819
elected as a Democratic Republican to the United States Senate to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of John Forsyth and served from November 6, 1819, to August 6, 1821, when he resigned
mayor of Augusta 1823
died in Augusta, Richmond County, Ga., September 23, 1827
interment in the Walker Family Cemetery in Augusta, Ga.

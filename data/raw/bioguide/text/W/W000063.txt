WALKER, Lewis Leavell, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Lancaster, Garrard County, Ky., February 15, 1873
attended Lancaster (Ky.) Academy, Garrard College, Lancaster, Ky., and Central University, Richmond, Ky.
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1894 and commenced practice in Lancaster, Ky.
also engaged in banking
prosecuting attorney of Garrard County in 1901
city attorney of Lancaster 1907-1910
served as trustee of the University of Kentucky, at Lexington, Ky., 1908-1915
judge of the thirteenth judicial district of Kentucky in 1910 and 1911
elected as a Republican to the Seventy-first Congress (March 4, 1929-March 3, 1931)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1930
continued the practice of law in Lancaster, Ky., until his death there on June 30, 1944
interment in Lancaster Cemetery.

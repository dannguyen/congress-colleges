WALSH, James T., (son of William F. Walsh), a Representative from New York
born in Syracuse, Onondaga County, N.Y., June 19, 1947
graduated from Christian Brothers Academy, Syracuse, N.Y., 1966
B.A., St. Bonaventure University, St. Bonaventure, N.Y., 1970
United States Peace Corps, 1970-1972
business executive
member of the Syracuse, N.Y., common council, 1977-1985, president, 1985-1988
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred First and to the nine succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1989-January 3, 2009)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

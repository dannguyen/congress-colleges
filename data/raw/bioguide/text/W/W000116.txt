WALTON, Matthew, (cousin of George Walton and John Walton), a Representative from Kentucky
birth date unknown
received a limited schooling
member of the conventions held in Danville in 1785 and 1787
member of the first State constitutional convention, 1792
member of the Kentucky state house of representatives, 1792, 1795, and 1808
elected as a Republican to the Eighth and Ninth Congresses (March 4, 1803-March 3, 1807)
died in Springfield, Ky., January 18, 1819
interment in Springfield Cemetery.

WARD, Marcus Lawrence, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Newark, N.J., November 9, 1812
received a limited schooling
engaged in candle manufacturing
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1860 and 1864
unsuccessful candidate for Governor of New Jersey in 1862
elected Governor in 1865 and served from January 16, 1866, to January 18, 1869
was chairman of the Republican National Committee in 1866
elected as a Republican to the Forty-third Congress (March 4, 1873-March 3, 1875)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1874 to the Forty-fourth Congress
died in Newark, N.J., April 25, 1884
interment in Mount Pleasant Cemetery.

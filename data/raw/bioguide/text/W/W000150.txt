WARFIELD, Henry Ridgely, a Representative from Maryland
born in Anne Arundel County, Md., September 14, 1774
completed preparatory studies
held several local offices
settled in Frederick, Md.
elected to the Sixteenth, Seventeenth, and Eighteenth Congresses (March 4, 1819-March 3, 1825)
died in Frederick, Md., March 18, 1839.

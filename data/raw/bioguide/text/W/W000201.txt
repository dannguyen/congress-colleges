WATSON, Henry Winfield, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Bucks County, Pa., June 24, 1856
educated in private schools
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1881 and commenced the practice of his profession in Philadelphia
president of the Washington, Potomac & Chesapeake Railway Co.
director of several banks and of the Langhorne Water Co.
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-fourth and to the nine succeeding Congresses and served from March 4, 1915, until his death in Langhorne, Pa., on August 27, 1933
interment in the Brandywine Cemetery, Wilmington, Del.

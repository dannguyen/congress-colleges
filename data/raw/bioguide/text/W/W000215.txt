WAXMAN, Henry Arnold, a Representative from California
born in Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, Calif., September 12, 1939
attended Los Angeles public schools
B.A., University of California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1961
J.D., University of California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1964
lawyer, private practice
member of the California state assembly, 1969-1974
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-fourth and to the nineteen succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1975-January 3, 2015)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress in 2014
chair, Committee on Oversight and Government Reform (One Hundred Tenth Congress)
chair, Committee on Energy and Commerce (One Hundred Eleventh Congress).

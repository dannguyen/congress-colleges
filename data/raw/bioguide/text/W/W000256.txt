WEIS, Jessica McCullough, a Representative from New York
born Jessica McCullough, in Chicago, Ill., July 8, 1901
attended the Franklin School, Buffalo, N.Y.
graduate of Miss Wrights School, Bryn Mawr, Pa., and Madam Rieffels School, New York City
appointed to Inter-American Commission of Women
vice chairman, Monroe County Republican committee, 1937-1952
president, National Federation of Republican Women, in 1940 and 1941
member of the Republican National Committee 1944-1963
delegate at large to the Republican National Conventions, 1940, 1944, 1948, 1952, and 1956
member of the National Civil Defense Advisory Council, 1953, 1956, and 1960
elected as a Republican to the Eighty-sixth and Eighty-seventh Congresses (January 3, 1959-January 3, 1963)
was not a candidate for reelection to the Eighty-eighth Congress in 1962
died on May 1, 1963, in Rochester, N.Y.
interment in Mount Hope Cemetery.

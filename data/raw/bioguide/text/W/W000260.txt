WELBORN, John, a Representative from Missouri
born near Aullville, Lafayette County, Mo., on November 20, 1857
attended the public schools
studied law at Warrensburg (Mo.) State Normal School
was admitted to the bar in 1880 and practiced in Lexington, Lafayette County, Mo.
city recorder in 1890 and 1891
mayor of Lexington 1896-1920
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1900
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-ninth Congress (March 4, 1905-March 3, 1907)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1906 to the Sixtieth Congress
resumed the practice of law
died in Lexington, Mo., October 27, 1907
interment in Machpelah Cemetery.

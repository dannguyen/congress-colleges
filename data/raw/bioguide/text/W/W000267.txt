WELDON, David Joseph, a Representative from Florida
born in Amityville, Suffolk County, N.Y., August 31, 1953
graduated from Farmingdale High School, Farmingdale, N.Y., 1971
B.S., State University of New York, Stony Brook, N.Y., 1978
M.D., State University of New York, Buffalo, N.Y., 1981
United States Army, 1981-1987
United States Army Reserve, 1987-1992
physician, internal medicine
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and to the six succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-January 3, 2009)
was not a candidate for reelection to the One Hundred Eleventh Congress in 2008.

WELLBORN, Marshall Johnson, a Representative from Georgia
born near Eatonton, Putnam County, Ga., May 29, 1808
attended the University of Georgia at Athens
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1826 and practiced in Columbus, Ga.
held several local offices
member of the State house of representatives in 1833 and 1834
judge of the superior court of Georgia 1838-1842
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-first Congress (March 4, 1849-March 3, 1851)
studied theology and was ordained as a Baptist minister in 1864 and continued in the ministry until his death in Columbus, Ga., October 16, 1874
interment in Oakland Cemetery, Atlanta, Ga.

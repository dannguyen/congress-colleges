WESTBROOK, John, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Sussex County, N.J., January 9, 1789
moved with his parents to Pike County, Pa., in 1792 and settled near Dingmans Ferry
attended private schools
engaged in lumbering and agricultural pursuits
colonel in the State militia in 1812
sheriff of Pike County in 1817
member of the State house of representatives in 1833
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-seventh Congress (March 4, 1841-March 3, 1843)
declined to be a candidate for reelection in 1842 to the Twenty-eighth Congress
resumed agricultural pursuits
died near Dingmans Ferry, Pike County, Pa., October 8, 1852
interment in Laurel Hill Cemetery, Milford, Pa.

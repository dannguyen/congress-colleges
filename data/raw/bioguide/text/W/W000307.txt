WESTBROOK, Theodoric Romeyn, a Representative from New York
born in Fishkill, Dutchess County, N.Y., November 20, 1821
attended the common schools and was graduated from Rutgers College, New Brunswick, N.J., in 1838
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1843 and commenced practice in Kingston, Ulster County, N.Y.
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1854
resumed the practice of his profession at Kingston, N.Y.
elected a justice of the supreme court in 1873
died while holding court in Troy, Rensselaer County, N.Y., on October 6, 1885
interment in Wiltwyck Cemetery, Kingston, N.Y.

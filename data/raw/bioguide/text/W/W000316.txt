WEYMOUTH, George Warren, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in West Amesbury (now Merrimac), Mass., August 25, 1850
attended the public schools and the Merrimac High School
moved to Fitchburg, Mass., in 1882 and engaged in the carriage business
later became manager of the Simonds Rolling Machine Co.
trustee of the Fitchburg Savings Bank 1891-1901 and director of the Fitchburg National Bank 1892-1901
director in other corporations
member of the common council of Fitchburg in 1886
served in the State house of representatives in 1896
delegate to the Republican National Convention in 1896
elected as a Republican to the Fifty-fifth and Fifty-sixth Congresses (March 4, 1897-March 3, 1901)
was not a candidate for renomination in 1900
moved to Fairhaven
president of the Atlas Tack Corp. of Fairhaven, Mass., 1897-1910
killed in an automobile accident near Bingham, Maine, September 7, 1910
interment in Riverside Cemetery, Fairhaven, Bristol County, Mass.

WHEAT, Alan Dupree, a Representative from Missouri
born in San Antonio, Bexar County, Tex., October 16, 1951
attended elementary schools in Wichita, Kans., and in Seville, Spain
graduated from Airline High School, Bossier City, La., 1968
B.A., Grinnell College, Grinnell, Iowa, 1972
economist
legislative aide to county executive, Jackson County, Mo.
elected, Missouri general assembly, 1977-1982
delegate, Missouri State Democratic convention, 1978
elected as a Democrat to the Ninety-eighth and to the five succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1983-January 3, 1995)
was not a candidate for reelection in 1994, but was an unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate.

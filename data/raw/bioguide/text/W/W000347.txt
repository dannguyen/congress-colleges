WHITACRE, John Jefferson, a Representative from Ohio
born in Decatur, Burt County, Nebr., December 28, 1860
attended the public schools, Hiram (Ohio) College, and the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor
engaged as a manufacturer of hollow building tile
delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1912
unsuccessful candidate in 1908 to the Sixty-first Congress
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-second and Sixty-third Congresses (March 4, 1911-March 3, 1915)
resumed his former manufacturing pursuits
president of the Whitacre Engineering Co. and the Whitacre-Greer Fireproofing Co.
died in Miami, Fla., December 2, 1938
interment in Magnolia Cemetery, Magnolia, Ohio.

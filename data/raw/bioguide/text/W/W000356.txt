WHITE, Bartow, a Representative from New York
born in Yorktown, Westchester County, N.Y., November 7, 1776
attended the common schools and completed preparatory studies
studied medicine with his father, Dr. Ebenezer White, and commenced practice in Fishkill, N.Y., in 1800
elected to the Nineteenth Congress (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1827)
resumed the practice of medicine
presidential elector on the Whig ticket in 1840
died in Fishkill, Dutchess County, N.Y., December 12, 1862
interment in the Dutch Reformed Church Cemetery.

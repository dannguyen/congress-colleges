WHITE, Leonard, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Haverhill, Essex County, Mass., May 3, 1767
was graduated from Harvard University in 1787
member of the State house of representatives in 1809
held many local offices
elected as a Federalist to the Twelfth Congress (March 4, 1811-March 3, 1813)
town clerk of Haverhill
cashier of the Merrimack Bank of Haverhill 1814-1836
died in Haverhill, Mass., October 10, 1849
interment in Pentucket Cemetery.

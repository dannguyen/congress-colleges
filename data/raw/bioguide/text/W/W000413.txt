WHITFIELD, Wayne Edward (Ed), a Representative from Kentucky
born in Hopkinsville, Christian County, Ky., May 25, 1943
graduated from Madisonville High School, Madisonville, Ky., 1961
B.S., University of Kentucky, Lexington, Ky., 1965
attended Wesley Theological Seminary and American University, Washington, D.C.
J.D., University of Kentucky School of Law, Lexington, Ky., 1969
United States Army Reserve, 1967-1973
lawyer, private practice
business executive
business owner
attorney adviser to Chairman Edward J. Philbin of the Interstate Commerce Commission, 1991-1993
member of the Kentucky state house of representatives, 1973-1975
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourth and to the ten succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1995-present).

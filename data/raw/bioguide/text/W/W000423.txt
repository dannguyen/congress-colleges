WHITMORE, Elias, a Representative from New York
born in Pembroke, N.H., March 2, 1772
completed preparatory studies
moved to New York and settled in Windsor
engaged in mercantile pursuits
elected to the Nineteenth Congress (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1827)
resumed his former business pursuits in Windsor, N.Y., until his death in that city December 26, 1853
interment in the Village Cemetery.

WILDER, William Henry, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Belfast, Waldo County, Maine, May 14, 1855
moved with his parents to Massachusetts in 1866
attended the common schools
engaged in the mercantile and manufacturing business at Gardner, Mass.
president of the Wilder Industries (Inc.)
studied law
admitted to the bar in 1900
was admitted to practice before the United States Supreme Court in 1909
studied the monetary systems in Europe in 1909 and wrote many articles and pamphlets on monetary questions
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-second and Sixty-third Congresses and served from March 4, 1911, until his death in Washington, D.C., September 11, 1913
interment in Crystal Lake Cemetery, Gardner, Worcester County, Mass.

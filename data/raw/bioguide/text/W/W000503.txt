WILLIAMS, Henry, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Taunton, Bristol County, Mass., November 30, 1805
completed preparatory studies
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1829 and commenced practice in Taunton, Mass.
member of the State house of representatives in 1834
served in the State senate in 1836 and 1837
elected as a Democrat to the Twenty-sixth Congress (March 4, 1839-March 3, 1841)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1840 to the Twenty-seventh Congress
was elected to the Twenty-eighth Congress (March 4, 1843-March 3, 1845)
resumed the practice of law
died in Taunton, Mass., on May 8, 1887
interment in Mount Pleasant Cemetery.

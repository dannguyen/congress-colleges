WILLIAMS, John McKeown Snow, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Richmond, Henrico County, Va., August 13, 1818
moved to Boston, Mass.
attended the public schools
engaged in mercantile pursuits and was also a shipowner
member of the State house of representatives in 1856
served in the State senate in 1858
elected as a Republican to the Forty-third Congress (March 4, 1873-March 3, 1875)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1874 to the Forty-fourth Congress
resumed his former business pursuits
died in Cambridge, Middlesex County, Mass., March 19, 1886
interment in Mount Auburn Cemetery.

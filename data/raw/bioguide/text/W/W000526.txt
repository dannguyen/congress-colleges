WILLIAMS, Lemuel, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Taunton, Mass., June 18, 1747
was graduated from Harvard College in 1765
studied law
was admitted to the bar and practiced in Bristol and Worcester Counties, Mass.
town clerk of New Bedford, Mass., 1792-1800
elected as a Federalist to the Sixth, Seventh, and Eighth Congresses (March 4, 1799-March 3, 1805)
member of the State house of representatives in 1806
resumed the practice of law
died in Acushnet, Mass., November 8, 1828
interment in Acushnet Cemetery.

WILSON, Alexander, a Representative from Virginia
born in Virginia, birth date unknown
completed preparatory studies
member of the Virginia state house of delegates, 1803 and 1804
elected as a Republican to the Eighth Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of United States Representative Andrew Moore
reelected to the Ninth and Tenth Congresses (December 4, 1804- March 3, 1809)
death date unknown.

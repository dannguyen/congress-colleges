WILSON, Henry, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Dauphin, Dauphin County, Pa., in 1778
completed preparatory studies
studied law in Harrisburg, Pa.
was admitted to the bar December 21, 1812, and commenced practice in Allentown, Pa.
prothonotary and clerk of Lehigh County Courts 1815-1821
elected to the Eighteenth and Nineteenth Congresses and served from March 4, 1823, until his death in Allentown, Pa., August 14, 1826
interment in Union Cemetery.

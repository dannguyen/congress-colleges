WILSON, James, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Millerstown (now Fairfield), Pa., April 28, 1779
attended the common schools
learned the trade of cabinetmaker
engaged in mercantile pursuits and also interested in the real estate business
justice of the peace 1811-1822
elected to the Eighteenth, Nineteenth, and Twentieth Congresses (March 4, 1823-March 3, 1829)
again a justice of the peace 1830-1859
engaged in the real estate business
died in Gettysburg, Adams County, Pa., July 19, 1868
interment in Evergreen Cemetery.

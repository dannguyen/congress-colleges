WISE, James Walter, a Representative from Georgia
born near McDonough, Henry County, Ga., March 3, 1868
attended the common schools
studied law at Emory College, Oxford, Ga.
was admitted to the bar in 1892 and commenced practice in Fayetteville, Fayette County, Ga., in January 1893
member of the State house of representatives 1902-1908
was mayor of Fayetteville 1904-1906
solicitor general of the Flint judicial circuit 1908-1912
elected as a Democrat to the Sixty-fourth and to the four succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1915-March 3, 1925)
declined to be a candidate for renomination in 1924
owing to prolonged illness was unable to qualify for or attend the Sixty-eighth Congress
died in Atlanta, Ga., on September 8, 1925
interment in McDonough Cemetery, McDonough, Ga.

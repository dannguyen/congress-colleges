WITTE, William Henry, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Columbia, Morris County, N.J., October 4, 1817
moved to Springtown, Bucks County, Pa., and attended the common schools
moved to Philadelphia in 1840
engaged in mercantile pursuits and the real estate business
elected as a Democrat to the Thirty-third Congress (March 4, 1853-March 3, 1855)
engaged in newspaper work and resumed real estate interests
died in Philadelphia, Pa., November 24, 1876
interment in Durham Cemetery, Durham, Bucks County, Pa.

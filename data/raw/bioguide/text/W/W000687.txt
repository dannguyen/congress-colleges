WOOD, Abiel, a Representative from Massachusetts
born in Wiscasset, Maine (then a district of Massachusetts), July 22, 1772
attended the common schools
engaged in mercantile pursuits
member of the Massachusetts house of representatives 1807-1811, and again in 1816
elected as a Republican to the Thirteenth Congress (March 4, 1813-March 3, 1815)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1814 to the Fourteenth Congress
delegate to the constitutional convention of Maine in 1819
State councilor of Maine in 1820 and 1821
resumed mercantile pursuits and also engaged in shipping
bank commissioner for Maine until his death in Belfast, Waldo County, Maine, October 26, 1834
interment in Woodlawn Cemetery, Wiscasset, Lincoln County, Maine.

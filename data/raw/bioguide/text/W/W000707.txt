WOODARD, Frederick Augustus, a Representative from North Carolina
born near Wilson, Wilson County, N.C., February 12, 1854
attended private schools in Wilson County
studied law at the law school of Chief Justice Richmond Mumford Pearson
was admitted to the bar in 1873 and commenced practice in Wilson, N.C.
vice president of the First National Bank of Wilson
elected as a Democrat to the Fifty-third and Fifty-fourth Congresses (March 4, 1893-March 3, 1897)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection to the Fifty-fifth Congress
resumed the practice of law in Wilson, N.C., and died there May 8, 1915
interment in Maplewood Cemetery.

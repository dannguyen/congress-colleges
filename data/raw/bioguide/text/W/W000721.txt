WOODS, Frank Plowman, a Representative from Iowa
born near Sharon, Walworth County, Wis., December 11, 1868
attended the public schools and the Northern Indiana Normal School, Valparaiso, Ind.
moved to Estherville, Emmett County, Iowa, in 1887 and worked in a newspaper office for two years
engaged in the mortgage-loan business and private banking
chairman of the Republican State central committee in 1906 and 1907
elected as a Republican to the Sixty-first and to the four succeeding Congresses (March 4, 1909-March 3, 1919)
unsuccessful candidate for renomination in 1918
chairman of the Republican National Congressional Committee 1913-1918
resided in Altadena, Calif., until his death there April 25, 1944
interment in Mountain View Cemetery.

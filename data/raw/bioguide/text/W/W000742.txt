WORD, Thomas Jefferson, a Representative from Mississippi
born in Surry County, N.C., birth date unknown
member of the State house of commons in 1832
moved to Mississippi and settled in Pontotoc, Pontotoc County
contested the election of Samuel J. Gholson to the Twenty-fifth Congress, and the election was set aside by the House
subsequently elected as a Whig to fill the vacancy caused by this action (May 30, 1838-March 3, 1839)
death date unknown.

WORMAN, Ludwig, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Tinicum Township, Bucks County, Pa., in 1761
received a limited schooling
learned the tanning business
moved to Earl Township, Berks County, Pa., in 1784 and established a tannery
elected as a Federalist to the Seventeenth Congress and served from March 4, 1821, until his death
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1822 to the Eighteenth Congress
died in Earl Township, Berks County, Pa., October 17, 1822
interment in Earl Township Cemetery.

WURTS, John, a Representative from Pennsylvania
born in Flanders, Morris County, N.J., August 13, 1792
after his fatherÂ’s death in 1793 the family resided in Montville, Morris County, and subsequently moved to Philadelphia, Pa.
was graduated from Princeton College in 1813
studied law
was admitted to the bar in 1816 and commenced practice in Philadelphia, Pa.
member of the State house of representatives in 1817
served in the State senate in 1820
elected to the Nineteenth Congress (March 4, 1825-March 3, 1827)
was not a candidate for renomination
United States district attorney 1827-1831
member of the city council of Philadelphia
president of the Delaware & Hudson Canal Co. 1831-1858
went abroad for his health in 1859
died in Rome, Italy, on April 23, 1861
interment in the family cemetery at Pleasant Mills, near Batsto, Atlantic County, N.J.

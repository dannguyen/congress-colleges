WYNN, William Joseph, a Representative from California
born in San Francisco, Calif., June 12, 1860
attended the public schools of San Francisco
apprenticed to the machinistÂ’s trade and subsequently worked in the principal manufacturing establishments of San Francisco
member of the board of supervisors of the city and county of San Francisco from January 8, 1902, to March 4, 1903

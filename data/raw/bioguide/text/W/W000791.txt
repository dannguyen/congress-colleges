WALDEN, Greg, a Representative from Oregon
born in The Dalles, Wasco County, Ore., January 10, 1957
B.S., University of Oregon, Eugene, Ore., 1981
member of the Oregon state house of representatives, 1989-1995
member of the Oregon state senate, 1995-1997
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Sixth and to the eight succeeding Congresses (January 3, 1999-present).

WEBB, James H. (Jim), a Senator from Virginia
born in St. Joseph, Mo., February 9, 1946
graduated high school in Bellevue, Neb.
attended University of Southern California 1963-1964
graduated United States Naval Academy 1968
J.D., Georgetown University Law School 1975
served in the United States Marines 1968-1972
author, screenwriter, and journalist
lawyer
Secretary of the Navy 1987-1988
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 2006, and served from January 3, 2007, to January 3, 2013
was not a candidate for reelection to the Senate in 2012.

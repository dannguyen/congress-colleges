WARNER, Mark, a Senator from Virginia
born in Indianapolis, Ind., December 15, 1954
B.A., George Washington University, 1977
J.D., Harvard Law School, 1980
worked on the staff of Senator Christopher Dodd
engaged in the high-technology business
co-founded the company that became Nextel
Virginia Democratic Party chairman, 1993-1995
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States Senate in 1996
governor of Virginia, 2002-2006
chairman of the National Governors Association 2004-2005
elected as a Democrat to the United States Senate in 2008
reelected in 2014 for the term ending January 3, 2021.

WEBSTER, Daniel A., a Representative from Florida
born in Charleston, Kanawha County, West Virginia, on April 27, 1949
graduated from Maynard Evans High School, Orlando, Fla., 1967
B.E.E., Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, Ga., 1971
business owner
member of the Florida state house, 1980-1998, speaker of the house of representatives, 1996-1998
member of the Florida state senate, 1998-2008, majority leader, 2006-2008
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

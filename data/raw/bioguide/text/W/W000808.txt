WILSON, Frederica, a Representative from Florida
born in Miami, Miami-Dade County, Florida, on November 5, 1942
B.A., Fisk University, Nashville, Tenn., 1963
M.A., University of Miami, Coral Gables, Fla., 1972
elementary school principal
member of the Miami-Dade County school board
member of the Florida state house of representatives, 1998-2002
member of the Florida state senate, 2002-2010
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

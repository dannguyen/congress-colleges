WOMACK, Steve, a Representative from Arkansas
born in Russellville, Pope County, Ark., on February 18, 1957
graduated from Russellville High School, Russellville, Ark., 1975
B.A., Arkansas Tech University, Russellville, Ark., 1979
Arkansas Army National Guard, 1979-2009
radio station manager
consultant
member of the Rogers, Ark., city council, 1983-1984 and 1997-1998
Mayor of Rogers, Ark., 1999-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

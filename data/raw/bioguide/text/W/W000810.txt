WOODALL, Rob, a Representative from Georgia
born in Athens, Clarke County, Ga., February 11, 1970
graduated from Marist School, Atlanta, Ga., 1988
B.A., Furman University, Greenville, S.C., 1992
J.D., University of Georgia School of Law, Athens, Ga., 1998
staff, United States Representative John Linder, 1994-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

WEBER, Randy, a Representative from Texas
born in Pearland, Brazoria County, Tex., July 2, 1953
attended Alvin Community College in Alvin, Tex., 1971-1974
B.S., University of Houston-Clear Lake, Houston, Tex., 1977
business owner
member of the Pearland, Tex., city council, 1990-1996
member of the Texas state house of representatives, 2008-2012
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Thirteenth and to the succeeding Congress (January 3, 2013-present).

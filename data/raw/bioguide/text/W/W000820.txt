WALTERS, Mimi, a Representative from California
born in Pasadena, Los Angeles County, Calif., May 14, 1962
B.A., University of California, Los Angeles, Calif., 1984
sales representative
business executive
member of the Laguna Niguel, Calif., city council, 1996-2004
Mayor of Laguna Niguel, Calif., 2000
member of the California state assembly, 2005-2008
member of the California state senate, 2008-2015
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

WESTERMAN, Bruce Eugene, a Representative from Arkansas
born in Hot Springs, Hot Springs County, Ark., November 18, 1967
graduated from Fountain Lake High School, Hot Springs, Ark., 1986
B.S., University of Arkansas, Fayetteville, Ark., 1990
M.F., Yale Forestry School, New Haven, Conn., 2001
engineer
member of the Fountain Lake, Ark., school board, 2006-2010
member of the Arkansas state house of representatives 2010-2014
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

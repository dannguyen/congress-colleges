WATSON COLEMAN, Bonnie, a Representative from New Jersey
born in Camden, Camden County, N.J., February 6, 1945
B.A., Thomas Edison State College, Trenton, N.J., 1985
member of the New Jersey state assembly, 1998-2014
New Jersey state assembly majority leader, 2006-2009
member, New Jersey state democratic committee, 2002-2006
elected as a Democrat to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

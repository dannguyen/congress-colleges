YANCEY, Joel, a Representative from Kentucky
born in Albemarle County, Va., October 21, 1773
member of the State house of representatives 1809-1811
served in the State senate 1816-1820 and 1824-1827
elected as a Jacksonian to the Twentieth and Twenty-first Congresses (March 4, 1827-March 3, 1831)
chairman, Committee on Expenditures in the Post Office Department (Twenty-first Congress)
unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1830 to the Twenty-second Congress
died in Barren County, Ky., in April 1838
interment in that county.

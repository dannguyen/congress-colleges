YOUNG, Charles William (Bill), a Representative from Florida
born in Harmarville, Allegheny County, Pa., December 16, 1930
Army National Guard, 1948-1957
member of the Florida state senate, 1960-1970, and minority leader, 1966-1970
member, Florida Constitution Revision Commission, 1965-1967
delegate to Republican National Convention, 1968, 1972, 1976, and 1984
elected as a Republican to the Ninety-second and to the twenty-one succeeding Congresses
served until his death on October 18, 2013 (January 3, 1971-October 18, 2013)
chair, Committee on Appropriations (One Hundred Sixth through One Hundred Eighth Congresses)
died on October 18, 2013, in Bethesda, Md.

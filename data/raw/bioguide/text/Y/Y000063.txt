YODER, Kevin, a Representative from Kansas
born in Hutchison, Reno County, Kans., January 8, 1976
B.A., University of Kansas, Lawrence, Kans., 1999
J.D., University of Kansas, Lawrence, Kans., 2002
lawyer, private practice
member of the Kansas state house of representatives, 2002-2010
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

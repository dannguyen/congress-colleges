YOUNG, Todd, a Representative from Indiana
born in Lancaster, Lancaster County, Pa., August 24, 1972
graduated from Carmel High School, Carmel, Ind., 1990
B.S., United States Naval Academy, Annapolis, Md., 1995
M.B.A., University of Chicago, Chicago, Ill., 2000
M.A., University of London, London, England, 2001
J.D., Robert H. McKinney School of Law, Indiana University, Indianapolis, Ind., 2006
United States Navy, 1990-1991
United States Marine Corps, 1995-2000
staff, United States Senator Richard Lugar of Indiana, 2001-2003
Orange County, Ind., deputy prosecutor, 2007-2010
consultant
lawyer, private practice
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the two succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).

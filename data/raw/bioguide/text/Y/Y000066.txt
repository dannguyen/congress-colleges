YOUNG, David, a Representative from Iowa
born in Van Meter, Dallas County, Iowa, May 11, 1968
graduated from Johnston High School, Johnston, Iowa, 1986
B.A., Drake University, Des Moines, Iowa, 1991
businessman
staff, United States Senator Hank Brown of Colorado, 1993-1996
staff, campaign manager, United States Senator James Bunning of Kentucky, 1998-2006
Chief of Staff, United States Senator Charles Grassley of Iowa, 2006-2013
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).

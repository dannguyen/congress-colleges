ZELDIN, Lee M, a Representative from New York
born in East Meadow, Nassau County, N.Y., January 30, 1980
graduated from William Floyd High School, Brookhaven, N.Y., 1998
B.A., State University of New York, Albany, N.Y., 2001
J.D., Albany Law School, Albany, N.Y., 2003
United States Army, 2003-2007
United States Army Reserve, 2007-present
lawyer
unsuccessful candidate for election to the United States House of Representatives in 2008
member of the New York state senate, 2010-2014
elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress (January 3, 2015-present).
